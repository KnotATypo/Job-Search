About Australian Agribusiness

Australian Agribusiness is an integrated manufacturer, distributor, and provider of professional products and services. We’re dedicated to providing effective and responsible solutions that enhance human life, through protecting and nurturing the environment and supporting a sustainable future.

Australian Agribusiness establishes the strategic direction for our Crop Solutions, and Consumer and Professional Solutions business divisions. Our Crop Solutions division is home to Accensi, Australia’s leading independent toll manufacturer of agrochemical and crop solution products. Our Consumer and Professional Solutions divisions comprise of Nuturf, Globe Pest Solutions, and Amgrow, which respectively operate across the professional turf and greenspace management, pest management, and consumer home and garden sectors. Together, we create partnerships that leverage innovation and capability in our market segments.

About Accensi

Accensi:

Accensi is a leading independent toll manufacturer of agrochemical and crop solution products. With innovative production facilities located in key growing regions around Australia, Accensi provides services for both local and international markets. Accensi’s fully integrated business model and broad footprint ensures we are positioned to provide an end-to end service. To complement our strength in toll manufacturing, Accensi also delivers excellence in research and development, formulation optimisation, storage and handling, global procurement, distribution, and regulatory submissions.

About the Opportunity

Australian Agribusiness is currently seeking a permanent full time QA Officer to join the team at our Narangba Site.  Reporting to the QA Coordinator, the primary purpose of this role is to assist in driving the ongoing quality aspects that support operations and progress the betterment of manufacturing processes.

Your day-to-day responsibilities will include (but will not be limited to):

  • Writing procedures and work instructions to support Aus-Ag’s QMS
  • Reviewing and updating manufacturing and packing instructions
  • Creating manufacturing documentation for new products
  • Administration of quality data in our ERP system
  • Management of our document management system
  • Collation and review of quality compliance data
  • Assisting in generation and maintenance of BOMs in our ERP system.
  • Assisting with driving and developing manufacturing Operating Procedures (SOP) across the Accensi sites to promote safe work practices
  • Assisting in ensuring the proper disposal of chemical waste and hazardous goods in accordance with company procedure
