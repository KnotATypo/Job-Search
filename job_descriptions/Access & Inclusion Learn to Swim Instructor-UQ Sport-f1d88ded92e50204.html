About This Opportunity

UQ Sport has two access and inclusion programs that aim to assist clients of varied abilities who aspire to swim competitively or become better swimmers overall.

The UQ Can Swim Program is an entry-level program and runs for 9 weeks (Wednesday, Friday, Saturday, and Sunday) during Queensland school terms. UQ Can Swim classes are 30 minute one-on-one lessons.

Reporting to the Access and Inclusion Swimming Coordinator as well as the Aquatic Program Manager you will ideally be available on Saturdays and/or Sundays, and open to picking up other class times during the week.

IN THIS ROLE YOU WILL:

  • Assist with class setup and pack down.
  • Teaching one-on-one clients with a range of disabilities
  • Be flexible in your teaching style according to the characteristic of the client
  • Have the possibility to increase your working hours with the growing learn to swim program

TO BE SUCCESSFUL IN THIS ROLE YOU WILL HAVE:

  • Current First aid and CPR certificate
  • Working with Children (QLD Blue Card)
  • AUSTSWIM Teacher of Swimming and Water Safety (or equivalent Swim Australia qualification)
  • AUSTSWIM Teacher of Aquatics Access and Inclusion (or equivalent Swim Australia qualification) (required qualification to recieve uplifted hourly rate).
  • Interest in disability, Parasport, access and inclusion teaching and programs
  • Disability worker screening card (QLD Yellow Card) – Desirable
