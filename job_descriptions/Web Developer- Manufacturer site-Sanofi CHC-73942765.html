The Position:

The Web Developer for our Brisbane Manufacturing site is a newly created IT role with endless growth and development opportunities on a local and global level.

Our Team:

The Web Developer is a Digital Site Expert for our Manufacturing site and will be integrated into the CHC Digital M&S team based in Virginia, which is overseeing M&S solution portfolio, roadmap, and projects globally for CHC GBU (Global Business Unit). This mainly includes supporting existing and future solutions for integrated functions (i.e., Manufacturing ERP, Procurement, Laboratories, Analytics, Engineering, Network).

  • Identify opportunities for business-led delivery; coach employees to make use of technology and information
