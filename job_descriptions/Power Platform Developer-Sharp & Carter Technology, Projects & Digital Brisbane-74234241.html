About the role:
You will be responsible for the design, implementation and support of Azure applications and data integration pipelines between systems used by the organisation. You will work with others in IT and the organisation to understand application and integration needs and identify opportunities for automating and improving the movement and reliability of data.

Your contribution:
  • Work within the Microsoft Azure technology stack to deliver effective integration between external and internal data sources and the companies enterprise systems.
  • Assist in identifying business opportunities and improvements for stakeholders using industry best practices, latest product innovations and technical solutions delivered by the Applications team.
  • Build, maintain and consolidate their suite of custom Azure Power Platform applications ensuring that they meet business requirements, that they well are documented and well received by business users.
  • Build strong working relationships with stakeholders to gain deep understanding their application needs, and promote practices and solutions that enable self-service and ease of use.
  • Assist in the design, support and development of ETL and ELT data pipelines, Dataverse, data lake, warehousing and unification assets.
  • Deliver training and guidance to users to enable optimal system acceptance and utilisation by business users.
  • Work within, and in alignment with the defined IT strategic priorities and goals.
  • Work in accordance with all data governance and data security policies ensuring that users are also aware of such policies.
About You:
  • Experience of designing, implementing, monitoring, maintaining and optimising integrations between enterprise systems.
  • Extensive experience with databases and querying and programming in SQL.
  • Experience building data integrations in Azure (Azure Data Factory, Logic Apps, Azure Functions).
  • Experience working with multiple REST APIs and semi structured data sources
  • Experience working with Dynamics 365/Power Platform including Dataverse
  • Experience programming in .Net solutions (C#, database solution)
  • Knowledge of data integration techniques.
  • Proven experience working with project stakeholders to identify requirements.
  • Ability to work in Agile and SCRUM and knowledge of Azure DevOps.
