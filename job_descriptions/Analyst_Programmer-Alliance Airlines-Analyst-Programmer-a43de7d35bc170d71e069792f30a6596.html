About Alliance Airlines

Alliance airlines is Australia's largest charter airline providing charter services, wet and dry leasing to the mining, construction, government, entertainment and sporting industries.  We are currently operating 72 aircraft with another 30 purchased to be operational by 2026. With in-house maintenance and servicing we pride ourselves on delivering a great service with an impeccable safety record.  At Alliance Airlines, we like to operate with a mantra of what can we do to ensure the best outcomes for our people and clients. Through applying this, we have been able to achieve success and are expanding our team.

About the Role

We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Analyst / Programmer, who is passionate about delivering outstanding customer service, who is driven to deliver high quality solutions, applications, integrations and reporting in a time efficient manner using a security by design mindset.  You   have a continuous improvement mindset, are a flexible and solution-based thinker that has a great ‘can-do’ attitude. Ideally you have experience in modernising development frameworks and principles or establishing new ways of working.

As an Analyst / Programmer, you will join a small IT team, located at our HQ at Brisbane Airport Hangars and will engage directly with the business to gather requirements, create solutions and provide ongoing support and maintenance, across a number of business critical, internal ICT systems and integrations. We have exciting plans to transition our existing suite of applications to the latest, new technology stacks utilising .NET 8 and Vue3. This is a fantastic opportunity to get in at the start of this journey and to help shape the future of our applications.

Candidates, please note, this is a 100% on-site position (5 days a week).

Drug and Alcohol testing and police check clearance may be part of the recruitment process.
