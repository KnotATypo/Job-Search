Full job description

Brock Solutions is an innovative engineering solutions and professional services company specializing in the design, build and implementation of real-time operational solutions for broad based industrial/manufacturing and transportation/logistics organizations worldwide. Some interesting areas we are exploring include industrial automation, machine learning, IIOT, cyber security, AI, robotics, and other technologies. With approximately 800 employees globally, Brock Solutions is a privately held, employee owned organization with over 30 years in the real-time operational solutions space.

As Brock Solutions continues to grow and respond to the on-going requirements of major clients, we need to add talent to our base of outstanding professionals. We are currently seeking a Software Integration Engineer to join our team of experienced professionals.

Working with our other highly motivated developers, you will be involved in the entire life cycle of the software engineering process.

Employee-owned company with profit sharing, comprehensive benefits, company events and charitable initiatives, a diverse workplace, BBQs and Lunch and Learns, Sports teams, Group fitness initiatives, and a "We win as a Team" culture are just some of the reasons our employees love working at Brock!

Why Work at Brock?

  • Competitive compensation package
  • Profit sharing
  • Continuous career growth
  • Flexible working hours
  • Company events and celebrations
  • Travel Opportunities

Responsibilities may include:

  • Work with a multi-disciplinary team to deploy and configure software to customer test and production environemnts
  • Configure and set up server hardware and peripheral devices (printers, scanners, bluetooth devices)
  • Modify product code to meet customer's unique needs
  • Demonstrate complete integrated system with key external stakeholders during functional acceptance testing and on-site system testing
  • Root cause analysis of defects identified in production
  • Maintain, develop, and improve our automated test and deployment tools
  • Performing other duties as assigned by Project Manager
Qualifications Required:
  • Bachelors degree in IT, Software Engineering, Computer science or related fields
  • Preferred 1-3 years experience (co-op considered).
  • Must be an Australian citizen or permanent resident
  • Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written
  • Strong troubleshooting skills
  • Proficiency with SQL and knowledge of databases
  • Experience with an object-oriented programming language is required. C# is preferred but Java, C++ and other languages are also acceptable
  • Knowledge of Flyaway, Powershell, Azure Devops, Python and Ignition is an asset
  • The ability to travel and perform night/weekend work on occasion
  • Willing to travel up to 20% throughout the year
  • Must meet federal security clearance requirements
Other Job Information (if applicable): New grads are welcome to apply. If you have graduated within the last 3 years, please also submit a soft copy of your transcript (unofficial copy is fine).

Brock Solutions strives to create a respectful, accessible and inclusive work environment. Upon individual request, Brock Solutions will endeavour to remove barriers to the hiring process to accommodate those candidates with disabilities.

Please be advised that, outside of our official applicant tracking system, Brock Solutions will never solicit personal information upfront. We send formal offer documentation in writing first, and wait until your acceptance before asking for any additional personal information.
