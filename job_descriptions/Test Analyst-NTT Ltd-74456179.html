Want to be part of the team?

We are seeking a Test Analyst to join our team for an initial 4 month contract to assist testing and validating data solutions across a newly formed data platform. This position will require you to work across currrent data and analytics solutions leveraging databrick within AWS.

Your day at NTT

  • Assist with defining a test strategy
  • Analsye and review the data platform alongside data analysts, engineers and modellers
  • Analyse and translate platform architecture, system design, and business requirements into test plans, and test casess
  • Develop system, regression, performance and ETL test cases
  • Design and build a test automation process across all data flows and touch points
  • Consult with Data Engineers to quality control the deployment processes across environments with automated pipelines via GitHub Workflows and Actions
  • Develop and execute manual test cases and facilitate defect resolution and test status reporting using either Micro Focus ALM (Quality Centre) or Jira.

What would make you a good fit for this role?

  • ISTQB Certified highly desired
  • Experienced testing on a Reporting or Analytics data platform
  • Demonstrated experience with manual, regression, performance, ETL and test automation
  • Understanding of data modelling, Data Lakehouse and ETL/ELT processes
  • Have previously tested cloud based (AWS) reporting and analytics or datawarehouse solutions
  • Any experience with metadata driven testing methodologies and frameworks would be highly regarded
  • Have previously incorporated test automation with CI/CD for automated deployments
  • Familiarity with Databricks and Python
