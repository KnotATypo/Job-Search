About the Role

  • Coordination of the Clinical Coding Quality Program, including education, auditing and clinician liaison activities to ensure a high standard of coding accuracy, consistency and timeliness.
  • The Clinical Coding Quality Coordinator reports to the Director Health Information Management Services, QEII Jubilee Hospital, MSH.
  • The Clinical Coding Quality Coordinator reports to the Director Health Information Management Services, QEII Jubilee Hospital, MSH.
  • Utilise the 3M Codefinder software, relevant patient information systems, related pathology, and associated databases to assist in verification and allocation of ICD-10-AM codes.
