Now part of the Australian Finance Group (ASX:AFG) list of companies, Fintelligence is best known for providing our partners with an all of business platform highlighted by the following:

  • Inbuilt workflows using proprietary cloud-based code

  • Inbound lead analytics data and integrated report builder
