About Data42:

We are a small team of specialists that get called when problems are complex or span large areas of the meat supply chain. We have the experience and knowledge to answer the supply chain information questions when others don't even know where to begin.

Our solutions are used by some of the largest companies in the Australian meat and livestock industry, but you won't see our name as we do confidential development for our clients.

We are a leader in our field and have been providing software solutions and services to the meat and livestock industry for more than 25 years.
