Company Description

Gameloft Brisbane , part of the global Gameloft family, opened in 2014 and since then has released Zombie Anarchy, Ballistic Baseball, My Little Pony: Mane Merge, and multi-award-winning The Oregon Trail™. In 2022, Gameloft Brisbane was announced as the winner of the coveted ‘Studio of the Year’ at the Australian Game Developer Awards (AGDAs).

We believe the keys to success are a great studio culture and finding a balance in everything we do, whether it's working with great people on exciting projects or enjoying the incredible lifestyle of Queensland Australia.

We're setting our sights on an exciting new direction in game development, embracing the challenges and opportunities of cross-platform innovation. As pioneers in leading Brisbane's game development landscape, we're dedicated to crafting immersive experiences that transcend traditional boundaries and engage players across diverse platforms. As we venture into this exciting phase, we're also expanding our talented team to bring to life a range of unannounced projects.

Job Description

You will take ownership of every aspect of the game (development and product). You will guide a team of designers, programmers, and artists, and work with specialists in monetization, marketing, community management, customer care, and quality assurance to ensure our game is engaging and profitable.

Responsibilities

  • Lead the development, launch, and live operations of our cross-platform games.
  • Manage and mentor a production team, fostering a collaborative and high-performing culture.
  • Coordinate efforts across global teams to ensure alignment and efficiency.
  • Drive decision-making on product features and manage development through all phases.
  • Maintain the game's vision for success, balancing creativity with strategic objectives.
  • Regularly communicate progress and updates to stakeholders.

Qualifications

  • Minimum 8 years of experience in game development leadership roles.
  • Proven track record of shipping cross-platform games.
  • Strong understanding of gaming business performance indicators.
  • Blend of creativity and analytical skills with the confidence to make strategic decisions.
  • Effective in a fast-paced, changing environment.
