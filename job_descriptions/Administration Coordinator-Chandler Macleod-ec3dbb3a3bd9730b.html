Chandler Macleod in partnership with a well-established manufacturing group renowned for their high-profile brand products, are seeking a high-level Administration Coordinator to join their growing technical team to facilitate the expansion of the team and play a crucial role in optimising their project operations.

ABOUT THE ROLE:

  • Manage Various office activities
  • Identify and collaborate with suitable contractors for the installation of product samples for structural and fire testing.
  • Schedule and coordinate lab activities, overseeing material orders, contractors, and engineers.
  • Collaborate with the technical team, ensuring effective communication and coordination of various projects, ranging from product development to technical literature and support.
  • Liaise with external consultants, clients, and suppliers.
  • Propose and implement innovative ideas to enhance productivity and document management within the technical services team
  • Prepare Quotes and invoices for external clients
  • Manage purchase orders and oversee payment of invoices


ABOUT YOU:
  • Pronto and CAD experience highly desirable
  • Hold a tertiary qualification in a relevant field.
  • Demonstrate proven experience in project coordination, and team collaboration.
  • Exhibit excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Show a demonstrated ability to think strategically and implement innovative solutions.
  • Strategic thinker with a focus on scalability and efficiency.
  • Results-oriented with a proactive approach to problem-solving.
  • Possess strong organizational and multitasking abilities.
  • Demonstrate the ability to lead and effectively train teams.
