CRC - Capital Raising Coordinator

About VentureCrowd

VentureCrowd is Australia's leading digital investment platform, leveraging the power of crowdfunding for investments that back a better future. We are here to prove that investments can be accessible and impactful by providing founders and property developers with a platform to find and capitalise on their crowd; and investors the opportunity to directly contribute to ventures and property projects they believe in. Together, we're fostering a future that uplifts the crowd.

The Role

VentureCrowd is Australia’s leading digital investment platform for alternative assets and Australia's only multi-asset class crowd-sourced funding platform. VentureCrowd’s business involves the operation of wholesale and retail funds and retail crowdfunding transactions, where the underlying assets are unlisted private companies or property development projects.

This role undertakes the “front end” of the capital-raising funnel for the Australian and South-East Asian markets. The CRC's role is to call leads, qualify them and set up appointments for them with a Capital Manager, for those who qualify. The CRC enters these details in Salesforce CRM and digitally sets appointment times. (Appointment Setter)

The CRC will be involved in other activities, such as the improvement of customer relationships and processes to assist in that. For the right candidate we would consider this role to be Full-time or Part-time.
