Full job description

Do you want a high-flying corporate career working for the world’s largest air services provider, based in Brisbane ?


Are you passionate and knowledgeable in food safety compliance, and Biosecurity disciplines? If so, we have an exciting opportunity available for an experienced Food Safety & Compliance Manager to join our team in Brisbane.


More about this role:

You will be responsible for supporting the Unit Manager in maintaining compliance with food safety, Halal, and biosecurity disciplines. Additionally, you will manage food safety and compliance programs in conjunction with the General Manager – Quality Assurance and lead the team of Compliance Co-ordinators to ensure excellence in performance from both an execution and management perspectives.


In a nutshell, your main responsibilities are:

  • Manage Food Safety and compliance programmers in conjunction with the General Manager – Quality Assurance that positions dnata catering as an industry leader in airline catering.
  • Lead the team of Compliance Co-ordinators that will ensure excellence in performance, both from an execution and management perspective.
  • Providing specialist advice and information to external/internal stakeholders in regard to Food Safety, Halal, Environmental, Biosecurity and risk prevention with guidance and clear guidelines always available.
  • Seek out, quantify, and develop opportunities for process improvement.
  • Conduct relevant sections of the induction process and ensure all staff are inducted in accordance with Company and legislative requirements.


What do you need to be successful in securing this role?

  • Demonstrated success in a similar role.
  • Tertiary Qualification in Food related discipline
  • Lead or Internal Food Safety Auditor (RABQSA)
  • HACCP qualification
  • Experience in ISO22000:2018 certification
  • Practical experience in a large food manufacturing environment involving QA, HACCP, Food Safety
  • Ability to communicate related technical information to all levels of Staff within the business and externally.
  • Ability to influence decision makers in appropriate control measures resulting from risk assessment.
  • Eligibility to hold Aviation Security Identification Card
  • Available for intermittent travel to QLD based sites (Coolangatta / Maroochydore, Cairns)

Desirable

  • Halal compliance in a food manufacturing environment
  • Experience in Biosecurity waste management and Approved Arrangements
  • Certificate IV in Workplace Assessment & Training

What’s in it for you?

  • Be part of a close knit & dedicated team that is committed to safety.
  • Competitive pay rates and penalties.
  • Free onsite parking, meals, and uniforms.
  • Career development and progression, coupled with national relocation opportunities.
  • 5 weeks annual leave.
  • Discounts at hundreds of retailers, discounted health insurance and wellbeing resources including online exercise classes.

About Us:

We are Australia’s largest and most admired inflight catering and retail partner. Specialising in inflight catering and retail, food manufacturing, and airport lounge management, our group of diverse brands enables us to deliver extensive service capabilities.


Chosen by many of the world’s top airlines, we operate 16 facilities across 10 cities and employ over 4,000 people who help us to produce over 64 million meals, and service more than 250,000 flights, each year.


Parent company dnata – part of the Emirates Group – is one of the world’s largest air services providers. Offering ground handling, cargo, travel, and inflight catering and retail services, dnata ensures the aviation industry operates smoothly and efficiently.
