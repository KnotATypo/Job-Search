Full job description

Exciting role for Software Developer to work with our client based in Inner Northern Suburbs. You will work in large development team and be part of a fun, dynamic and creative culture. Free parking and many other benefits on offer.

Responsibilities:
  • Working across data pipelines and data mining activities
  • Helping to automate processes for greater efficiency
  • Integrating new sources of data into existing systems and processes
  • Working with other software developers
  • Working closely with Data Engineering team looking at quality and continuous improvement
  • Looking at delivery of high-quality data
  • Ensuring software and solutions meet policies and standards
  • Troubleshooting any issues identified in data processes and data feeds
  • Other duties as directed

Only candidates with the following skills should apply:
  • You will have a curious mindset and enjoy learning
  • Strong software development skills
  • Experience with automation of processes and data related processes
  • Experience with SQL and MySQL or Oracle
  • PHP experience and object oriented programming skills
  • Experience with version control GIT
  • Experience integrating with APIs and data infrastructure
  • Experience with JavaScript, Typescript, highly regarded
  • AWS experience
  • Excellent communication skills
