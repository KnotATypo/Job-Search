About us

Eneraque is a global brand specialising in engineering, design, manufacture and implementation of conventional and advanced energy solutions for the industrial, civil, mining, oil & gas, waste to energy and commercial industries.

We are looking for a Project Engineer to assist with managing and monitoring the completion of projects to ensure budget, quality, environmental and contractual obligations are achieved.

Duties include:

  • Assisting with preparation of schedules, cost estimates, and project baselines
  • Skilled in schedule control, and cost control by utilising Earned Value Management (EVM) techniques
  • Liaising with clients and suppliers
  • Coordination of various activities
  • Assisting with the procurement management by providing the required standards and specifications for the manufacturers and the suppliers
  • Monitoring and reporting on project progress against timelines and budget
  • Providing guidance on the project risks and opportunities
  • Supervising the fabrication process
  • Coordinate the preparation of documentation as required including the Quality Plan, Safety Plan, Environmental Plan, Inspection & Test Plan etc
  • Attend Risk Assessment meetings, HAZOPs, Failure Mode Effect Analysis(FEMA), Safety Integrity Level (SIL) etc
  • Assisting with Factory assembly, testing, and commissioning across Australia and New Zealand
  • Occasional site visits across Australia and New Zealand

About you

You will:

  • Be Degree qualified in Project Management, or Engineering
  • Be experienced in bespoke project management software packages
  • Have experience with construction works supervision, relevant Australian and New Zealand regulations, and standards
  • Be able to prioritise tasks ensuring you meet set deadlines
  • Ideally have experience in managing multiple projects simultaneously
  • Have excellent communication skills
  • Be available to travel across Australia and New Zealand
