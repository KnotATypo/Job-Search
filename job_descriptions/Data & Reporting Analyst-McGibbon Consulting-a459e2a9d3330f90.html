Full job description

  • Drive quality of reporting through data enhancement
  • Develop dashboard reporting

Our client is an established leader in its industry sector with a multi-geographically dispersed sales and service based network. At a Group level, this organisation is represented by diverse portfolio of brands. With a firm hold on the market, they have plans for continued growth, making it a great time to join this successful organisation.

As a Senior Data & Reporting Analyst your focus is on data enhancement activities bringing quality and efficiency to how data insights are derived and managed. Working with raw data, you will model, analyse and interpret various data sources, building reporting to drive decision making and support business improvement initiatives.

Day to day your role will:
  • Contribute to maturing data quality, profiling, interpretation, data sharing & reporting
  • Gather raw data from a range of sources
  • Engage stakeholders to understand reporting requirements
  • Implement data collection systems
  • Perform data cleansing and validation activities
  • Design and develop insights and performance reports and dashboards in PowerBi
  • Conduct analysis on trends and patterns
  • Develop requirements documents to support data onboarding and modelling
  • Work with technical stakeholders as the SME for data and reporting initiatives
  • Drive regulatory compliance
The ideal candidate will have Tertiary qualifications in Business, Finance or Commerce. You’ll bring previous experience in Data and Reporting Analytics gained in a large commercial complex environment.

In addition, you’ll demonstrate:
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • A specialisation in data management and dashboard development using PowerBi
  • Experience working with large data sets
  • Strong data management and reporting technology knowledge (SQL pref)
  • Ability to gather user requirements
  • Advanced systems skills (Exce)
You will be working with a professional team, who operate in a dynamic environment. Your effort will contribute to development of meaningful reporting which drives business decision making.

Competitive remuneration offered to successful candidate.

Please note as part of recruitment process candidates will be required to complete a pre-employment medicals and AFP Criminal History Checks.

Located North side, the role offers on-site parking.
