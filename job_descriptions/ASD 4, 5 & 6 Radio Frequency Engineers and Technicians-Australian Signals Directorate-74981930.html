About the Role

As a member of a team within Access Foundations, the successful applicant(s) will be involved in the design, development, testing and integration of RF systems and sub-systems to support ASD’s mission. This is achieved using one or more of the following skills:

  • Developing, integrating and configuring antennas, and RF sub-systems in hardware or software forms, including software-defined radio (SDR),
  • RF design and testing (using antenna theory, propagation theory, system performance optimisation), and
  • Integration, testing, and evaluation of system and subsystem components, using industry standard test equipment such as spectrum and logic analysers and channel simulators.

For these roles, ASD requires a mix of specialisation and generalisation across the Radio Frequency design and development skill sets. Whether you are a dedicated RF or software engineer with a passion for designing software based RF solutions, or an RF technician with a love of building, testing, fault finding or integrating bespoke and COTS technologies to deliver customers the products they need, these positions offer you an exciting and rewarding career opportunity.
