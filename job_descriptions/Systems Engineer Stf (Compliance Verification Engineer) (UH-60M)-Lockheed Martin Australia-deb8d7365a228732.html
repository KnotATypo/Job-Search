Job Highlights

Responsible to the Chief of the Office of Airworthiness, as the Compliance Verification Engineer (CVE) for UH-60M, you will be responsible for the compliance verification IAW Defence Aviation Safety Regulation (DASR) Guidance Material (GM) 21.A.239(a) para 3.1.3.

About us:

Lockheed Martin is a dynamic, growing, and energetic organisation offering stimulating, challenging work at the forefront of technology. Lockheed Martin is a leader in Australia’s National Security, and our collaboration with Australian industry spans the Aeronautics, Maritime, Surveillance and Defence domains. We employ approximately 114,000 staff worldwide, and over 1200 staff in Australia across multiple locations.

Following the announcement by the Australian Government to investigate the acquisition of UH-60M Black Hawk helicopters from the US Government, Lockheed Martin Australia is looking to establish a core team of experienced military Rotary Wing professionals to support the potential procurement and sustainment activities. The modern-day, 3rd Generation UH-60M Black Hawk helicopter is the world’s leading multi-role battlefield and special operations helicopter with a proven legacy of exceptional capability and reliability. Lockheed Martin Australia is looking forward to being the key contributor to the Australian UH-60M Black Hawk program and encourages you to come join us in delivering a sovereign sustainment capability.

We are pleased to invite applications for the position of Compliance Verification Engineer to join our UH-60M Black Hawk team.

Your responsibilities will include:

  • Independent checking of compliance demonstration documents, e.g. design definition documents, operating instructions, analysis, test programs test data and expositions
  • Independent checking of compliance demonstration documentation
  • Approval of revision of technical content in manuals approved by DASA

About you:

With your extensive DASR Part21J Compliance Verification experience (or equivalent), you will be a driven individual who is able to successfully manage to completion a broad range of tasks and, at times, conflicting demands. You will also ideally hold an Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF) Level 7 (Bachelor of Engineering or equivalent).

Special Requirements:

Applicants are required to be Australian Citizens and hold a current or re-instatable NV1 Defence Security Clearance or be eligible to gain this.

The location of the role will be Brisbane, QLD.

Employment may be contingent on the satisfactory result of criminal and/or other background screens, which require the collection and transfer of personal information.
