Your role

This is a terrific opportunity for an Associate to join a high-performing team and develop their expertise in front and/or back end work in the public infrastructure sector. Ideally you will have a minimum three years' experience in this area of the law.

You will have the opportunity to be trained by the leading Queensland construction team, work on market leading projects and disputes across asset classes including property, health, transport, resources and supporting Queensland's energy transition and Olympic infrastructure.

Your role may involve both:

  • advisory work on current major projects and transactions in the State; and
  • the management of disputes.

Our Team

MinterEllison is one of very few firms in the Australian market with a comprehensive integrated Construction, Infrastructure and Project Finance capability capable of handling the complete range of work across the full lifecycle of a project from finance arrangements and business case development to project documentation, contract administration and dispute resolution.

The team's Partners are recognised as leaders in the construction and PPP industry for their commerciality and their depth of knowledge and expertise. A position within the team therefore offers the opportunity to learn from the best. While this role is based in Brisbane, you will work on a range of projects in Queensland and nationally and there will also be close contact with other leading lawyers working in the broader national team on large scale projects within other jurisdictions. Key leaders and mentors will include market leading lawyers Jonathon Williams, Michael Creedon, Sian Keast, David Pearce, Andrew Orford and Julie Whitehead.

Our client base is diverse and they come to us for innovative solutions to their individual project needs, delivered by some of the most prominent lawyers in the sector. MinterEllison is an undoubtedly Tier 1 construction firm, with a proven and ongoing track-record of providing top-tier advice to Government agencies and the largest contractors and developers on their highest value new contracting negotiations and on litigation and arbitration. The team advises on major infrastructure projects and service transformation projects crossing education, healthcare, energy, aerospace and transportation sectors. The client base spans both public and private sectors - unquestionably among the largest project owners in Australia. The Minters team is consistently engaged for the most demanding matters in the market.

Examples of projects include Cross River Rail, Gold Coast Rapid Transit, Brisbane Metro, Melbourne Metro, Westgate Tunnel, Melbourne Airport Rail Link, Sydney Metro, Footscray Hospital, Toowoomba Hospital, Western Harbour Tunnel, Suburban Rail Loop, and many other complex infrastructure projects in the transport, health, education and correctional sectors.

What you need

We welcome applications from lawyers with a minimum 3-5 years PQE. Ideally, you will have major projects and infrastructure industry experience and are looking to cement your career in a Tier 1 practice.

You will also have:

  • a commercial approach to all matters
  • superior drafting ability and a strong attention to detail
  • the ability to work in a highly collaborative fast-paced environment
  • excellent communication skills, both written and verbal and
  • resilience and a commitment to excellence that matches our own.
