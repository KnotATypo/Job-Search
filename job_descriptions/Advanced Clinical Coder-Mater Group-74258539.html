What you’ll be doing

  • Accurately assign specific morbidity and procedural classification to patient episodes to meet data reporting requirements

About you

  • Up to date Clinical Coding knowledge using ICD-10-AM/ACHI/ACS
  • Experience using 3M Encoder
  • Strong organisation skills in planning, prioritising and managing workloads
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to work within a team environment
  • Understanding of private sector funding models (Desirable)
  • Experience using Webpas, iPM, and scanned medical records (Desirable)
