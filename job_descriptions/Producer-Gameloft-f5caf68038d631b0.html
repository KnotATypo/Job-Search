Company Description


Gameloft Brisbane , part of the global Gameloft family, opened in 2014 and since then has released Zombie Anarchy, Ballistic Baseball, My Little Pony: Mane Merge, and multi-award-winning The Oregon Trail™. In 2022, Gameloft Brisbane was announced as the winner of the coveted ‘Studio of the Year’ at the Australian Game Developer Awards (AGDAs).

We believe the keys to success are a great studio culture and finding a balance in everything we do, whether it's working with great people on exciting projects or enjoying the incredible lifestyle of Queensland Australia.

We're setting our sights on an exciting new direction in game development, embracing the challenges and opportunities of cross-platform innovation. As pioneers in leading Brisbane's game development landscape, we're dedicated to crafting immersive experiences that transcend traditional boundaries and engage players across diverse platforms. As we venture into this exciting phase, we're also expanding our talented team to bring to life a range of unannounced projects.


Job Description


As a Producer, you will play a pivotal role in the development and product management of our game. Working closely with a team of designers, programmers, and artists, you will be responsible for ensuring the successful execution of various aspects of the game lifecycle. Your role will involve collaborating with specialists in monetization, marketing, community management, customer care, and quality assurance to enhance the game's engagement and profitability.

Responsibilities

  • Overseeing the game team's activities throughout development, from conception to live operations
  • Coordinating and collaborating with cross-functional teams, including Production, Marketing, User Acquisition, QA, Business Performance, Legal, and other relevant groups
  • Participating in the decision-making process regarding product features and collaborating with the Game Design team to translate ideas into detailed concepts
  • Managing development through each phase of the production process: concept, mock-up, alpha, beta, gold, and updates
  • Ensuring the preservation of the game's vision throughout development to deliver a quality product with high levels of retention, revenue, and profitability
  • Communicating regular progress updates to stakeholders and sharing insights with other teams

Qualifications
  • 3+ years of experience as a Producer, Development Manager, or Project Manager is required
  • Proven track record of contributing to the release of at least one commercially successful game
  • Comprehensive understanding of the requirements for creating a commercially successful product, along with a solid grasp of gaming business-performance indicators and target audiences
  • Ability to seamlessly transition between Game Design meetings, marketing conference calls, and bug-fixing sessions
  • Blend of creative and analytical talents, enabling the identification of an optimal balance between creative and data-driven decision-making
  • Confidence in taking ownership of a game, defending strategy, scope, quality, and resource decisions
  • Openness to feedback and ability to thrive in a fast-paced, ever-changing environment
  • Proficiency in employing efficient production methods to complete tasks
  • Proven capability to keep teams motivated and engaged through transparent and effective communication
