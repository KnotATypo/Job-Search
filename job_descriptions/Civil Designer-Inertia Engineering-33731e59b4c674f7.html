Full job description

Looking for a role that gives you the freedom to expand your capabilities and knowledge into design and engineering avenues that interest you?

We are looking for an experienced Civil Designer to join our Land & Water team, you will be supported by a solid team across our civil and structural groups who have a strong pipeline of impressive projects. We provide opportunities to work closely with clients and contractors to achieve optimal outcomes. Intrigued?

Inertia is an engineering consultancy specialising in delivering structural, civil and water engineering solutions for clients within the built environment. We have built an enviable reputation for delivering high-quality, innovative solutions and are known for our multidisciplinary approach that delivers exceptional outcomes for our clients.

On Offer

  • Diverse range of projects throughout South East Queensland
  • Rewarding remuneration package to reflect industry experience
  • Flexible working arrangements
  • A dynamic role within a growing team and an opportunity to be part of a growing, successful business
  • An excellent company culture based on mutual respect, enjoying what we do and doing it well. Come and have a chat to see for yourself.

Requirements

  • 4 + years experience working as a civil designer.
  • Advanced level skills in the use of AutoCAD, Civil 3D and/or 12D design software.
  • Local experience in South East Queensland
  • Strong experience within earthworks, roadworks, stormwater, sewer and water reticulation
  • Proven ability in mentoring
  • Proven experience in meeting project timelines to budget

You:

  • Like working in an energetic and fun culture, while being committed to excellent project outcomes
  • Want to work on interesting projects that will challenge your skills and creativity
  • Have technical and mentoring skills to assist and develop Civil design personnel
  • Have a result-orientated approach and can effectively communicate within a team and with our partners.
  • You should have full working rights to live and work in Australia.

Us:

We like to have fun! The team have created a successful Cultural Committee with a Strive to Thrive Program with a ‘real’ focus on community, wellness, and growth. We provide an afternoon of trivia, a running club, mentoring program and a calendar of fun annual events.

We will:

  • Recognise your efforts and celebrate your achievements
  • Provide you with opportunities to use your creativity, experience and skills to deliver outstanding projects and succeed as a professional.
  • Support your desire to innovate, challenge the norm and push boundaries by enabling access to resources to enhance your knowledge and support your growth.
  • Foster a culture where teamwork is championed, collaboration is valued and balance is achieved by enjoying what we do and who we are.
