Key Responsibilities:
  • Assisting with preparation of Proposals, Reports, Applications, and other Submissions.
  • Assessment and documentation of clients’ requirements and preparation of contracts.
  • Assessment and monitoring of the status and performance of projects with a focus on delivery of contract requirements within specified timeframes.
  • Responsible for the project finance, including invoicing, cost tracking, variations etc.
  • Liaison with the client and approval authorities regarding performance and delivery of contract requirements.
  • Fostering of Business Development opportunities.
  • Act as the Superintendents representative for our construction contracts.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Civil Engineering or similar discipline.
  • Demonstrated work experience in the industry
Experience required:
  • A minimum of 4+ years’ experience in professional engineering services.
  • Experience with the development approval process with Local Authorities.
  • Exposure to a broad range of engineering issues and concepts.
  • Knowledge of systems and procedures relating to the role, including practical knowledge, principles and methods of engineering, and relevant codes, standards and engineering legislation.
  • Ability to use information technology, including AutoCAD, 12D Solutions, Microsoft Office Programs and any other applicable software packages.
