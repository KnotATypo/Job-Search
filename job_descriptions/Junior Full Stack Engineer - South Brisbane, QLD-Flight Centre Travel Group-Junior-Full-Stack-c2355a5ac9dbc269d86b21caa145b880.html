  • Craft high quality, secure, testable, maintainable code that is not over engineered.

Who you are...

  • Have up to 3 years professional experience in software development.
  • Have experience as a Full-Stack Engineer, delivering end-to-end experiences across front-end and backend.
  • Have proven experience in building user-friendly, responsive web applications for desktop and mobile.
  • Are experienced with modern JavaScript coding, testing, debugging, and automation techniques.
  • You are proficient in some of the following: TypeScript, Styled Components, HTML and JSS.

Desirable...

  • Any experience with observability tools, such as logging (Splunk, Kibana) and monitoring (New Relic).
