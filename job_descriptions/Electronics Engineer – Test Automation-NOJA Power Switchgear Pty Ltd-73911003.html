NOJA Power designs, manufactures, and supplies medium voltage switchgear products to over 100 countries globally.  The Group operates from our manufacturing campus and global headquarters in Murarrie, Brisbane with Factory and Offices in Brazil, Canada, UK & USA.

We are committed to an inclusive environment where people feel comfortable to be themselves.  We want our people to feel that their voices are heard, all cultures respected and that a variety of perspectives are not only welcome, but they are also essential to our success.

About the Role:

We are seeking an experienced Electronics Engineer to support Test Automation and Production of automated test systems for testing of Electrical Switchgear, Electronic Modules and PCB assemblies. The successful applicant will enjoy being part of an organization taking Australian technology to the world. You’ll be part of a supportive team, backed by your leader to grow and achieve your career aspirations in the team.  This role is a full-time position, Monday to Friday.

Reporting to the Electronics Engineering Manager, your key responsibilities will include but not limited to:

  • Design and implementation of new (and updated) test systems on NOJA production lines, in particular those requiring automation and programming
  • Support and improve NOJA’s data systems; data entry interfaces, machine communications, database structure and back-ends, reporting, data analysis.
  • Support the team taking on Electronics Engineering tasks, including testing, redesign, component qualification etc.
  • Coordinate your work closely with a team of Process, Mechanical, Mechatronic and other Electronic Engineers.
  • Implement, comply with, and enforce NOJA Power quality, environmental and workplace health and safety procedures.

What You’ll Bring:

  • Tertiary qualifications; Electronics, Mechatronics Engineering or similar;
  • Experience in:
    • Control and measurement systems
    • Production Processes
    • LABVIEW programming
    • Electronics Design & Engineering
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment
  • “Can-do” attitude with good work ethic
  • Excellent personal communication skills
  • Precise, clear technical documentation skills

What NOJA Offers:

  • A work environment where safety is always number one priority.
  • A permanent position working for NOJA Power.
  • A competitive salary reflective of your skills, experience and shift timings.
  • Career development and opportunities to grow your knowledge.
  • Job security, knowing you’re working for an award-winning successful manufacturer which is Australian owned, operated and based in Brisbane.

This role will provide you the challenges and rewards you have been searching for.
