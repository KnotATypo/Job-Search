About Our Client

This company is a globally recognised leader in its field, and has a long and successful history in delivering some of Australia's largest and most technically-complex infrastructure projects over the past 50 years. With an ever-increasing focus on Data and Business Intelligence, this Data Analyst/Power BI Developer position is a unique opportunity to join this small but growing Data & Analytics team in helping to make data a central asset to the wider organisation.

Job Description

The Data Analyst/Power BI Developer will have the following responsibilities:

  • Developing and customising various Power BI dashboards and reports to support business requirements.
  • Actively consulting with reporting line and the wider business to identify opportunities for enhancements to the database, dashboards and reports.
  • Consulting with Data Engineers to provide input into the new global Azure data warehouse as required to support database, dashboard and reporting needs.

The Successful Applicant

Skills and experience required:

  • Demonstrated commercial experience developing and customising dashboards/reports using Power BI.
  • Strong SQL Server skills, particularly:
    • T-SQL
    • SQL queries, functions, and stored procedures.
    • Query performance tuning.
    • SQL Server access provisioning.
  • Polished written and exceptional verbal communication skills.
  • Comfortable and confident when speaking with all levels of the business to understand requirements, and an ability to adapt to technical and non-technical conversations as needed.


Non-essential, but beneficial skills:

  • Experience working with Financial data and developing Financial reports.
  • Experience in a similar role in the construction industry/construction systems.
  • Understanding or familiarity with Azure data services, including Azure SQL databases, Azure Data Factory (ADF).
  • Exposure to Databricks, SSIS, and/or SSRS.
