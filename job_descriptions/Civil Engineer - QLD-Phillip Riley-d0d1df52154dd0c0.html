Phillip Riley are looking for an experienced Civil Engineer to join our team.

Duties include:

  • Plan, design and document ongoing progress of current works in accordance with project programming
  • Monitoring changes to designs, project costs, and measuring, valuing and assessing variations to designs
  • Analysing structural systems for loading
  • Maintain and provide cost analysis and reporting as required
  • Maintain Subcontractor and Supplier contracts and relationships
  • Implement and maintain project compliance in accordance with safety, quality, and environmental management systems

As the ideal candidate you will have:

  • You will have a minimum of 2 years’ experience in a similar role
  • Extensive technical knowledge,
  • Interpersonal skills.
