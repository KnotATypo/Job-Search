What you’ll do

Tabcorp is famous for winning moments. Behind (almost) all of them is great tech and a talented team of 700, passionate technology professionals. Together, we ensure our services are built sustainably to scale, handling over 1 billion bets annually.

Our Tech Teams deliver gaming moments that pack a punch! You will have the opportunity to innovate and inspire, leveraging technology to make good moments great. Game to join us?

This role will assist the Software Engineer Team to develop and deliver high quality software products and services to customers, instilling a customer first culture in the organisation and drive consistency across the Technology business.

Responsibilities will include:

  • Develop and implement innovative software solutions aligned with current industry standards and best practices.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to analyse requirements, design solutions, and develop computer applications that effectively meet business objectives.
  • Proactively identify and address technical issues, ensuring the seamless operation of software systems.
  • Stay informed about software releases and enhancements, evaluating their impact on system maintenance and making recommendations for upgrades as necessary.
  • Keep abreast of emerging approaches, techniques, and technologies in software development, deployment, and configuration to continuously improve practices.
  • Actively engage in workshops, stand-ups, product demonstrations, and retrospectives to contribute to team collaboration and continuous improvement.
  • Efficiently resolve incidents and requests within the Software Installations queue, adhering to SLAs and accurately documenting actions taken for future reference and audit purposes.

What you’ll bring

  • Accredited tertiary qualifications in a relevant field.
  • C# and C++ programming language experience.
  • Continued Learning Capability and ability to take direction from Software Engineers, Senior Software Engineers and Software Engineer Leads
  • Familiarity with technical solutions and commercial models within the technology sector
  • Understanding of relevant legislation and associated regulations.
  • Knowledge of gaming and entertainment industries advantageous..

What you’ll get

  • The flexibility of hybrid working with team anchor days.
  • Diverse leave options: cultural, volunteer, flexi, and 18-weeks parental leave.
  • Access to our health and wellbeing app and Employee Assistance Program.
  • Monthly employee social activities, competitions, awards and free tickets to events.
  • Access to digital learning modules for continuous upskilling.
  • Ongoing commitment to DE&I through leading policies & initiatives.
