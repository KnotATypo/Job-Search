About us

Disaster Relief Australia (DRA) brings hope to communities devastated by disaster. We unite the skills and experience of military veterans, emergency responders and motivated civilians to deploy Disaster Relief Teams around Australia and the world.  With our army of dedicated volunteers, we respond to disasters and deliver support to those in need.

At the heart of our mission is a commitment to serving those in need, powered by cutting-edge technology and a team of passionate IT professionals. We're on the lookout for a dynamic Power Apps, Power Automate, and Virtual Agents Developer to join our Brisbane-based team on a part-time basis. This role is a unique opportunity to harness your technical expertise to make a tangible difference in communities across Australia and beyond.

The Role

As our Power Apps, Power Automate, and Virtual Agents Developer, you'll play a pivotal role in enhancing our operational capabilities through custom solutions that streamline processes, improve efficiency, and elevate our disaster relief efforts. Your passion for technology and desire to contribute to a meaningful cause will drive innovation within our organisation, directly impacting our ability to respond to crises.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and implement solutions using Microsoft Power Apps, Power Automate, and Virtual Agents (Copilots) to address complex business challenges.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand requirements and deliver high-impact applications that improve disaster response operations.
  • Provide expert guidance on the capabilities and best use of Microsoft Power Platform technologies to maximise organisational effectiveness.
  • Conduct training sessions for staff to ensure optimal adoption and utilisation of developed applications.
  • Stay abreast of the latest advancements in Microsoft Power Platform technologies and explore innovative ways to apply them within the disaster relief context.

About You

  • Profound knowledge and hands-on experience with Microsoft Power Apps, Power Automate, and Virtual Agents (Copilots).
  • A strong portfolio demonstrating previous success in developing and implementing solutions that have driven efficiency and impact.

  • Exceptional communication and collaboration abilities, with a knack for explaining complex technical concepts in an accessible manner.

  • Relevant certifications in Microsoft Power Platform technologies are highly regarded.
  • Prior experience working with Defence will be highly regarded.
