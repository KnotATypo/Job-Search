The Company

Our client is a SaaS leader within their space that builds in automation and streamlines processes within their clients. They currently sit around 40 employees with their head office located in the Brisbane CBD.

The Role

They are seeking an experienced DevOps Engineer to join their growing team. As part of their IT department, you will play a crucial role in enhancing their Azure Cloud environment, optimizing delivery pipelines, and automating operational processes. You’ll collaborate closely with our development team to ensure seamless integration and deployment of product updates.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provisioning and deployment procedures for customer onboarding.
  • Management of existing CI/CD pipeline, transitioning towards Azure DevOps
  • Providing Azure platform support to the development team
  • Automation of procedures and processes
  • Root cause analysis for production errors and performance issues
  • Infrastructure monitoring and management for security and scalability
  • Contribution to the development of next-generation provisioning and deployment platform


About you

  • Proven experience as a DevOps Engineer (3+Years)
  • Team Player with a solution-focused attitude.
  • Proficient across the Azure Cloud Platform (AZ AD Security Admin would be ideal)
  • Strong scripting skills (PowerShell & Azure CLI)
  • Experience with SQL Server DBs & Queries
  • Experience with Windows Server & ISS Administration
  • Sound knowledge across Azure Bicep IaC and/or ARM Templates. (Ideal)
  • Azure Certifications (Ideal)
  • Experience with Octopus Deploy (Ideal)
