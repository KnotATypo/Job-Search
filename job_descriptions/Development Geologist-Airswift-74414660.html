Development Geologist

  • Brisbane, CBD
  • 12 Months Contract
  • Competitive Salary

The main purpose of the role is to act as a Development Geologist in a multi-disciplinary subsurface team within an Operated Coal Seam Gas (CSG) asset.

Key accountabilities include:

  • Apply geoscience expertise and professional judgment to support project delivery and technical studies.
  • Work within a multi-disciplinary team to deliver subsurface reservoir characterization for CSG reservoirs.
  • Appraisal and Development project concept selection and maturation
  • Generation of reliable prognoses in support of safe and efficient well-delivery and well-abandonment operations.
  • Contribute to technical analyses and recommendations at peer reviews, peer assists and functional reviews as part of the value assurance framework.
  • Ensure all own evaluations are documented and related data are handled in accordance with relevant technical standards and recording labelling obligations.

Proven track record with geoscience skills covering:

  • Reservoir characterization and subsurface property distributions, ideally with familiarity of CSG developments
  • Demonstrated experience in static reservoir modelling and subsurface interpretation using Petrel and Python scripting acumen.
  • Knowledge of Well, Reservoir and Facility Management
  • Appraisal and Field Development Planning
  • Stratigraphic well correlations
  • Volumetric estimation skills
  • Experience with volumetric estimation.
  • Well planning and delivery (new and P&A wells).

  • The position requires incumbents to fully appreciate diversity and to have excellent interpersonal skills; the successful candidate will need to rely on drive, personal experience, and judgment, with the support of the larger subsurface community.
  • Familiarity with Surat Basin geology is a benefit for this role.
  • Ability to demonstrate strong written and verbal skills and can explain why this is important to the business.
