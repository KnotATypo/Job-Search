Do you have a passion for data and what good forensics can reveal?

Wotton + Kearney's market-leading eDiscovery and Cyber Forensic (ECF) service is searching for a highly skilled consultant or senior consultant to join our growing team in Sydney, Melbourne, or Brisbane.

About us

Here at Wotton + Kearney (W+K) we have one focus – insurance law. Recognised amongst Australia's best lawyers and ranked Band 1 for Insurance in the legal directories, we are proud to be the largest insurance and dispute resolution firm in Australia.

What you'll do:
  • Become a trusted advisor to our legal teams across Australia, guiding them through all phases of eDiscovery and Cyber Forensics projects.
  • Manage data collection, processing, review, and production, ensuring efficient workflows and utilising industry-leading tools like Relativity and Canopy.
  • Develop and maintain eDiscovery and Cyber Forensic systems, documentation, and procedures.
  • Continuously improve our service delivery by recommending new methods, workflows, and resource models.
  • Collaborate with internal and external service providers to deliver exceptional client service.
Who you are:
  • An experienced consultant with a proven track record in eDiscovery, ideally working within law firms, professional services, or eDiscovery providers.
  • A solutions-oriented thinker with a passion for data forensics and problem-solving.
  • A self-motivated professional with excellent communication, time management, and organisational skills.
  • A team player who thrives in a collaborative environment while also being able to work independently.
  • Technically proficient with at least 2 years' experience in end-to-end eDiscovery, including a strong understanding of Relativity, Nuix platforms, EDRM principles, and Microsoft Office Suite.
Bonus points for:
  • Experience with data breach/PII reviews.
  • Knowledge of SQL databases.
