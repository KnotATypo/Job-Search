About the team

Modernising operations is vital to an organisation's growth and transformation. At Deloitte, the experts in our Core Business Operations (CBO) teams use leading technologies to uncover new, efficient ways to do business.

What will a typical day look like?

We have teams that operate across several areas:
Tech Advisory

Be part of a team advising clients upfront on the tech solutions we offer. Our specialists help organisations bring their strategy to life through efficient, effective, and future-ready operations.

Working on our client's most critical challenges by providing business-led, technology-enabled advisory services to deliver technology and operational solutions for our clients

Contribute to designs, workshops and architecture solutions and engage in vendor and client sessions

Tech Implementation

Now that your client has agreed on the solution, be part of the team that provides the architecture that helps answer the question, ‘How should we do this’? Our experts guide clients along their journey to adopting modern technology and working methods.

We provide guidance to clients on crafting technology strategies, operational models, and contemporary architectures, as well as delivering product/program management and technology-driven business transformation services

Team up with our clients and tech partners to provide cloud (AWS, GCP and Azure), network and security, and digital workplace solutions to help deliver application-focused outcomes

Cloud Managed Services

Deloitte’s Cloud Managed Services helps uplift our clients’ business with end-to-end support for managed cloud infrastructure. We work with all major providers, tailoring our approach to the specific needs of our clients.

Enough about us, let’s talk about you

We know that as a student, you may not necessarily have a tonne of work experience, and that’s okay! At Deloitte, we see past experience and value your ethic and potential to grow more than anything else. You’re someone who:

Has a true passion for all things tech – majored in Computer Science, Information Systems or Engineering (eg. telecommunications, electrical, network)
Lives to solve problems and is comfortable communicating your crazy ideas to non-technical audiences
Has a passion for programming, web APIs, cloud computing, software as a service (SaaS)
Works best collaboratively – you thrive when working with teams
Might have coding skills in .net, C#, Perl, Python and Java (a plus, not a must!)
Might have certifications in AWS, Azure, GCP, Cicso, F5 Networks, HP, Red Hat (highly desirable)

Why Deloitte?

At Deloitte, we focus our energy on interesting and impactful work. We’re always learning, innovating and setting the standard, making a positive difference to our clients and our society. We put coaching at the heart of what we do, helping our people grow their careers in any direction – whether it be up, moving into something new, or even moving across the world.

We embrace diversity, equity and inclusion. We have a diverse collection of people from different backgrounds, with different experiences, gender identities, abilities and thinking styles. What binds us together is a shared commitment to value everyone’s perspective and to cultivate inclusion; so that our work environment is a safe space we can all belong.

We prioritise flexibility and choice. At Deloitte, you get trust on Day 1. We know our people get their best work done when they’re in control of where and how they work, designing their work week around their client, team and personal commitments.

We help you live and work well. To support your personal and professional life, we offer a range of perks and benefits, including retail discounts, wellbeing leave, paid volunteering days, twelve flexible working options, market-leading parental leave and return to work support package.
