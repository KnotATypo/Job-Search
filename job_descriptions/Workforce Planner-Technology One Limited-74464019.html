Our mission is to provide world class software and consulting services to the thousands of people who use our enterprise software every day. Our 99% customer retention rate is proof that what we do is valued by our customers and our Consulting business is no exception as we strive to deliver high calibre ongoing consulting support to our clients.

This opportunity represents an exciting position within our accomplished Consulting Enablement team. As a Workforce Planner, you will work closely with our entire Customer Experience division to ensure that Consulting resources are managed and optimised within assigned regions to ensure that resources are deployed at the right place and time to meet demand.

What you'll be doing…

As a Workforce Planner, you'll be a vital piece in our Customer journey. Duties for this role include:

  • Collaboratively work with Project Managers, Consulting Directors and Project Managers to ensure project plans are in Clarity and resources are scheduled.
  • Build relationships across the business to fulfil TechnologyOne's and customers' requirements.
  • Ensure continuity of resources on all projects and escalate to the Resource Planning Manager if this is not being achieved.
  • Analyse non-billable days and work with Regional Consulting Directors to address utilisation gaps.
  • Understand and put project plans in place to mitigate resource strain
  • Look after the Consultants welfare, through minimising travel, long working hours, etc.





One of our core beliefs at TechnologyOne is "the customer is our true north". It is in everything we do. This role requires someone who has a passion for the customer journey, and who can support and partner with all areas of the business to make sure our implementations are successful and leave our customers happy.

Your Talents…

  • Strong experience in Resource Management, preferably within ERP Software space
  • Ability to translate customer requirements to equivalent product functionality.
  • Previous experience with gathering and analysing Project Capability Requirements.
  • Strong communication skills, including the ability to effectively communicate in writing and verbally with stakeholders across all levels.
