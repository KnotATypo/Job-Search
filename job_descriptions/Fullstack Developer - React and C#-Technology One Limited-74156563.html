This role provides an exciting opportunity to work on the world's best student experience platform that supports and guide students throughout their education journey.

At TechnologyOne, we are obsessed with simplifying life for our customers and helping them adapt to the changing world around us. In response to our continued growth, we're looking for an experienced Full-stack Developer to join our team.

This is a fantastic opportunity for someone looking to join an amazing technology company that seeks to use innovation and simplicity to drive fantastic customer outcomes.

The successful candidate has a passion for building elegant cloud technological solutions in React and C# to simplify people's lives. They will work in a cross-functional agile team that includes UX designers, product owners and other talented developers. In their wider team, they will interact with peers across the globe, including Australia, the United Kingdom, Indonesia, Vietnam and Malaysia.

What you'll be doing ...

Reporting to an R&D Lead, you will play a pivotal role in our Student Experience team by:

  • Working with other Developers, UX Designers and Business Analysts to design elegant, smart and simple technology solutions that exceed expectations.
  • Writing simple, elegant code that is robust, scalable, testable and simple to maintain.
  • Undertake research, tech spikes, and develop innovative technical designs.
  • Building deep and 'hands-on' understanding of TechnologyOne products. You will become an expert in your product's functionality, design, and architecture, adding value to every team discussion.
  • Applying best practice software quality practices, including test automation, to ensure the team meets its quality and performance objectives.

Your Talents ...

  • A passion for technology and agile software development, along with an eagerness to delve into any problem
  • Effective communication skills, including the ability to communicate in writing and verbally with stakeholders
  • Experience with designing systems at scale that work well under load and at a high level of performance
  • Solid software development skills in C#, React, JavaScript, SQL
  • Desirable technical experience in AWS Lambda, API Gateway, CloudFront, and S3.
  • Desirable experience in continuous integration, Bitbucket, GIT, Jira, and Confluence.
