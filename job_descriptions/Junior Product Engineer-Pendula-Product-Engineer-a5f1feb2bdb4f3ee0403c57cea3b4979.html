Purpose of Role

Due to growth, the Pendula Engineering team are looking for a junior engineer to build, maintain and help support the Pendula platform. This role can be based in Sydney, Brisbane or fully remote if in neither of those locations.

The Role

As part of a small engineering team (a dozen of us) you'll have the opportunity to make contribute to the development of our product. You’ll be working in a small, cross-functional squad with 3 to 4 other engineers on our core platform - a distributed system built with an event-driven, microservice architecture. Your squad will also be supported by Product, Design, DevOps and QA.

You’ll spend time on the Following:

  • Build, maintain and help support the Pendula platform
  • Participate in our on-call rotation
  • Write tests
  • Work with our product managers and designers to make sure what we build adds value for our users
  • Actively engage in our Agile process and its rituals

About Us

Pendula is a Proactive Retention Delivery Platform empowering brands to shift their customer communications from broadcast to conversation. We allow everyday users to create two-way conversations with their customers using drag & drop functionality, without the need for any developer experience.

We’re here to be at the heart of every customer conversation and create value for businesses all over the world who struggle to manage their customer journey consistently at scale.

Culture

Our Values are we are Innovators for Our Customers, We are Team Builders & We are Direct with Kindness

We’re fast growing and on the hunt for like-minded people to join our hard-working crew. We’re passionate about our product and customers and everything we do is underpinned with integrity: we don’t rip people off, take shortcuts and we only make good products that people need.

Which also sums up how we treat each other. On top of all of that, we’re a bunch of lovely humans who listen and support each other and our customers. We treat everyone as equals and communicate our shared vision regularly.
