About Us

The Power to Respond and Be Heard

For 40 years, Zetron (a Codan company) has delivered interoperable command & control systems.

Across multiple industries and international markets. Zetron is headquartered in Redmond, WA, USA, with centres of engineering excellence in Victoria, BC, Canada, and Brisbane, QLD, Australia & United Kingdom. Zetron is an established global leader in connecting communications centres with field operations, personnel, and constituents. Our products make a real difference, they get police, fire and ambulance services to the scene of an incident, and they assist with keeping the power on, the trains running, and airports operating. Our customers rely on our systems to be to be available every minute of every day to help keep their staff and the public safe.

What Will You Be Doing?

The role of DevOps Engineer will be responsible for contributing to the success of our cutting-edge projects through collaboration and team engagement with all internal and external stakeholders to design, implement, and maintain infrastructure solutions.

As a key member of the team, you will:

  • Automate deployment, monitoring, and management processes to ensure efficient and reliable systems operation.
  • Implement and manage CI/CD pipelines for software delivery.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve infrastructure and pipeline issues, ensuring high availability and
  • performance.
  • Collaborate with developers to optimise applications for performance and scalability.
  • Stay up to date with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Core essential Skills will include, but not be limited to:

  • Strong knowledge of Vertulization platforms
  • Solid understanding of networking concepts and security best practices (SecOps).
  • Proven expertise in CI/CD tools
  • Hands-on experience with configuration management tools.
  • Experience with monitoring and logging tools
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms
  • Familiarity with version control systems
