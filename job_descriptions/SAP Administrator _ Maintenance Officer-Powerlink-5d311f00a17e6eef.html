Job Summary


We believe life’s about possibilities. And so is the future. That’s why we need you.


We’re working at the heart of Queensland’s energy transformation. Now more than ever, we need people who want to help us deliver a world-class energy future and ensure we continue to navigate through this once in a generation energy challenge. Join us and make it happen.


At Powerlink, every possibility starts with you


As the SAP Administrator / Maintenance Officer, you’ll help us connect a more sustainable energy future for more than five million Queenslanders and 253,000 businesses. Your work with us is about every light turned on, every community connected, and every future made possible.


You’ll enjoy a range of benefits including:


  • Hybrid, family-friendly and flexible working arrangements including substitution of public holidays.
  • Available to you will be working a 9-day fortnight with an RDO every second week.
  • Competitive remuneration package + 12.75% superannuation.
  • Free undercover secure parking, with access to onsite charging stations for electric vehicles
  • Onsite cafeteria offering quality food at subsidised prices and access to the onsite gym facility
  • Additional leave purchasing and 14 weeks paid parental leave – at full or half pay

Find a career that matters and lasts


As the SAP Administrator / Maintenance Officer , you’ll help create clever solutions to interesting problems as you grow your skills every day. And you’ll do it with down-to-earth people who you’re proud to work with and get to know.

You will support a team of maintenance specialists by performing non-technical tasks that support state-wide maintenance services, enabling proactive, routine maintenance to be completed efficiently and effectively.


Here’s how:


  • Ensure all routine asset maintenance plans are entered into SAP in accordance with requests received from your Asset Performance Team members.
  • Ensure that Maintenance Plans are staged logically, enabling the required tasks to be completed safely, effectively and efficiently.
  • Perform analysis and provide reports to enable work orders and maintenance plans to be audited by the maintenance specialists.
  • Provide reports to ensure timely completion of critical maintenance by monitoring and reporting on tasks to confirm that works are completed.
  • As directed, create, maintain and develop reports on data in the SAP Plant Maintenance Module to achieve desired technical outcomes.
  • Support the accurate and efficient use of the SAP Plant Maintenance Module and associated data, tools.
  • Support team members with the creation and maintenance of documents, organisation of meetings, forums, audits and other administrative tasks as requested.

Your expertise


  • Demonstrated ability to use Microsoft Excel, including the ability to manipulate and manage the integrity of large volumes of data.
  • Experience and knowledge of, or a desire to learn about Information Management, Asset Management, Database management.
  • Ability to understand technical concepts and problem solving.
  • High level of verbal and written communication skills.
  • A thirst to learn more.


We’d also love you to have:


  • Awareness or experience with a SAP Plant Maintenance module and its application.
  • Awareness of maintenance management and scheduling principles.


What matters most is your drive to make an impact for the millions of people we serve. Through our core values of accountability, customer service, teamwork and safety, you’ll strive to question and
challenge the ordinary every day.
