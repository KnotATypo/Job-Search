About the company
Palladium is a global leader in the design, development, and delivery of Positive Impact - the intentional creation of enduring social and economic value. With a team of more than 2,500 employees operating in 90-plus countries and a global network of more than 35,000 technical experts, Palladium has improved - and is committed to continuing to improve - economies, societies, and most importantly, people's lives.

About the Project
The Humanitarian Logistics Capability (HLC) contract is a partnership between The Palladium Group and the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade (DFAT). The HLC program itself supports the Australian Government's international disaster relief efforts through the provision of logistical support, disaster relief procurement, and timely technical advice.

About the role
As a short-term contractual role, the HLC is looking to recruit a Platform Developer who is highly experienced in either Oracle APEX or Microsoft Power Apps. The role will drive forward a time-sensitive project for the design, development, and implementation of a new Management Information System. This role is based in Pinkenba, Brisbane.

The primary responsibilities:
Develop a Management Information System on Oracle APEX or Microsoft Power Apps. Ensure platform meets project requirements.
Work closely with Business Analyst and HLC and DFAT stakeholders to gather and understand project-specific requirements.
Design and implement scalable solutions using Oracle APEX or Microsoft Power Apps, SQL, and PL/SQL.
Troubleshoot and resolve issues promptly to meet project deadlines.
Collaborate with the Business Analyst and project manager to ensure project success.
Provide regular updates on project progress and raise any potential risks or roadblocks.
