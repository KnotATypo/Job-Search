Technology has revolutionised the business space and is now more powerful than ever. But with great power comes great responsibility. That’s why we’re here. Part risk, part tech, we help clients protect their intellectual property and data!

Now, more than ever, our world needs a new perspective. Tomorrow won’t be powered by yesterday’s ideas. It requires bright minds, bold ambition and boundless passion. Someone like you! Join Deloitte and help us shape a better tomorrow.

What will your typical day look like?
We’ll be honest, it’s hard to tell you what an average day will look like at Deloitte because every day is just so different! Working on projects with an impressive portfolio of top-tier clients, you’ll get enviable experience as you:

Get exposure to tracing data breaches, intellectual property theft, misuse of computer systems and fraudulent activity (such as corruption and money laundering)
Roll up your sleeves to investigate electronic evidence collected from mobile phones, computers, networks, storage devices and backup systems
Design solutions for engagements relating to electronic discovery, forensic investigations, legal support and digital forensics
Explore and identify patterns in data to underpin decisions and outcomes for clients
Track evidence and prepare detailed reports on forensic analysis findings

Who will you be working with?

Call us biased, but we’re probably the people with the coolest jobs at Deloitte. We help clients navigate tech risks through internal investigations and forensically analysing electronic evidence collected from phones, computers, networks, storage devices and backup systems. Then, we design solutions that help our clients risk-align and future-proof their systems. Pretty cool, right?

Enough about us, let’s talk about you.
We know that as a student, you may not necessarily have a ton of work experience, and that’s okay! At Deloitte, we value your potential to grow more than anything else.

You’re someone who:
Is in their penultimate year of a degree in Computer Science, IT, Software Engineering, Digital Forensics, Data Science or InfoSys
Is comfortable with turning ambiguous problems and open-ended tasks into creative solutions
Is passionate about investigating data to uncover hidden insights
Has a keen eye for detail and possesses strong communication and writing skills
Works best collaboratively – you thrive when working in a team!
Demonstrates a high level of integrity and maintains confidentiality at all times (this is integral considering the nature of work we do!)
Has Australian permanent residency or Australian/New Zealand Citizenship

Why Deloitte?

At Deloitte, we focus our energy on interesting and impactful work. We’re always learning, innovating and setting the standard; making a positive difference to our clients and our society. We put coaching at the heart of what we do, helping our people grow their careers in any direction – whether it be up, moving into something new, or even moving across the world.

We embrace diversity, equity and inclusion. We have a diverse collection of people from different backgrounds, with different experiences, gender identities, abilities and thinking styles. What binds us together is a shared commitment to value everyone’s perspective and to cultivate inclusion; so that our work environment is a safe space we can all belong.

We prioritise flexibility and choice. At Deloitte, you get trust on Day 1. We know our people get their best work done when they’re in control of where and how they work, designing their work week around their client, team and personal commitments.

We help you live and work well. To support your personal and professional life, we offer a range of perks and benefits, including retail discounts, wellbeing leave, paid volunteering days, twelve flexible working options, market-leading parental leave and return to work support package.
