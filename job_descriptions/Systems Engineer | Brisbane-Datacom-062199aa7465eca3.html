About the role
The Systems Engineer role is responsible for supporting Datacom's technical infrastructure including corporate hardware and software as well as providing corporate systems for the Datacom Business. The Systems Engineer primarily performs support activities in our corporate Production Environment, including responsibility for infrastructure changes following formal change control process. This role will also assist with Priority incidents and internal projects as opportunities present themselves.
The Systems Engineer position would suit someone keen to take the next step in their career. We're after people who exude common sense and professionalism, are team players, can work under pressure with minimum supervision and who can demonstrate exceptional problem-solving abilities and a passion for learning.
What you'll bring
As an experienced professional, you'll be a success-driven and naturally passionate about technology, using it to deliver great customer outcomes. You will be comfortable engaging with and supporting all levels of customers from technical staff through to executives and your attention to detail will ensure your deliverables are of the highest standards.
Ideally, your Technical Skills will encompass some or all of the following:
  • Advanced Windows 10/11 troubleshooting experience
  • Basic Windows server experience
  • All-rounder in Microsoft Products
  • Microsoft O365 Admin
  • Microsoft Intune
  • Microsoft Security/ Defender, MFA
  • Azure / Azure AD
  • Active Directory, DNS, DHCP, Group Policy
  • Exchange Online and on-premises
  • Remote access, VPN, LAN, Wireless
  • Experience with SCCM/ BigFix
  • Meeting Room technologies e.g. AV equipment
  • Ability to work on problems of diverse scope
  • Min of 3+ years experience in a similar role and environment
