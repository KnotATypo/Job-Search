About Our Company

Saros provides high quality consulting, monitoring, instrumentation and data management services to our clients in the mining, quarrying, construction and government sectors. To help us provide these services to our clients, we are looking for a new team member to start in our Environmental Services crew.

About the Position

The role is full time and Brisbane based with the majority of work conducted throughout SE Queensland, although travel outside of this region may be required. The Field Technician will provide the following services to our clients:

  • Onsite field monitoring for a range of environmental impacts including, blasting, vibration, noise, air and water
  • Data collation and management from monitoring and remote data acquisition systems
  • Support for environmental monitoring instrumentation
  • Assistance with data and results reporting to clients
  • On-site customer relationship management
  • Operational administration

Skills and Experience

  • Although it’s desirable that you have a suitable qualification or experience in Environmental, Earth Sciences, or Field Sampling and Monitoring, e.g. VET Certificate or Diploma, it is not essential.  We can and will train you to use and operate the equipment required.
  • It is desirable that you have some work experience, ideally up to 1-2 years, and preferably in a role where you have had customer interaction and worked as part of a team.
  • It’s important that you are keen to learn, are able to work independently in the field, and have an eye for detail, data capture and management.
  • We’d like you to be a good communicator, and have the ability to get along with other people.
  • We’re a small business, so you must be willing to work in that type of environment.  That means we’d like you to get involved and learn about the other aspects of our business, so you can grow your career with us.
  • The role requires you to travel to sites to undertake the monitoring.  We’ll need you to have a current full "C class" driver’s license so that you can operate our vehicles.
