About the company

Care Fertility is a boutique fertility unit, committed to excellence, offering assisted reproductive technology services in a warm and friendly environment with a personalised approach to patient care. The unit is located at Greenslopes Private Hospital.  We offer a wide range of fertility services including artificial insemination, IVF and ICSI, IMSI, time lapse embryology, embryo biopsy, semen analyses, cryopreservation of gametes and embryos, known donor egg and sperm cycles.

About the role

Working in a small team, you will be trained to perform a range of standard and advanced clinical andrology and embryology procedures.  Hours will be negotiated with the successful candidate.
