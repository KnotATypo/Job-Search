Position Summary

As our Business Development Representative, you'll reach out to clients via calls, emails, and LinkedIn to pursue profitable partnerships with Pax8.

Every day you will make phone calls, send emails, and utilise tools like LinkedIn Sales Navigator for cold outreach to prospects to set 40 qualified introduction calls per month. You can plan on making 30-40 calls per day.

You do not need experience in this role. We will teach you everything you need to know about tech sales. Here are some attributes you need for this role:

  • You're interested in a career in technology sales, enjoy talking to people on the phone about technical solutions to internal problems, and appreciate ongoing learning.
  • You are comfortable cold outreaching partners regularly.
  • You have an ability to keep going even when facing rejections or obstacles; resiliency is a must.
  • You have a positive attitude and are willing to throw yourself into unfamiliar situations in order to grow and progress.
  • You are proactive about building your knowledge base and take on feedback well.

This position is based in Brisbane, Australia.
