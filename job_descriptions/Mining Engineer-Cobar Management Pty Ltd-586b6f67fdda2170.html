Company Description


CSA Mine is one of Australia’s longest operating copper mines with a history stretching back almost 150 years. At 1.9 kilometers deep, CSA is the second deepest copper mine in Australia and the third highest copper grade in the world. Located near the NSW town of Cobar, CSA Mine employs around 550 people and produces approximately 40,000 tonnes of copper concentrate per year. The ore is mined and processed on site and then transported by rail 700km to the Port of Newcastle for export. CSA Mine was acquired by Metals Acquisition Limited in June 2023.


Job Description


CSA Mine is looking for a Mining Engineer to join the team. You will work in a team that is focused on safety, while driving to meet the plan. You will work in one of the world’s most iconic mining districts, which has the second highest grade copper mine in the country.

In this role you will be responsible for:

  • Primarily working on operational improvements within the technical and operational departments
  • Preparation of weekly and monthly development and production schedules
  • Effectively communicating the weekly plan to the mine foreman
  • Producing stope notes and drill and blast designs
  • Ensure work carried out meets current legislative requirements
  • Undertake stope reconciliations to establish an optimal design process
  • Conduct risk assessments as required

Qualifications


About you:

  • Current Australian Driver’s Licence (minimum P2 or equivalent)
  • Degree in Mining Engineering
  • Minimum 2 years experience in an underground metalliferous environment
  • Experience in mine planning software including Deswik
  • Have completed your underground time required for a production managers certificate (desired)
  • Experience in short term scheduling
  • Experience in drill and blast
  • Experience in mine ventilation and cemented mine backfill (desired)

Location

Located an eight-hour drive to Sydney, nine-hour drive to Melbourne, and a quick three-hour drive to Dubbo, Cobar is a fascinating town rich in mining heritage and natural attractions. Entertainment options include restaurants, pubs, and clubs for dining out. You will be kept busy with a bowling and golf club, rugby and rugby league teams, race club, pony club, Olympic size swimming pool, gym – and more! The town boasts beautiful parks, two reservoirs, picnic areas and playgrounds. The youth club 'Youthie' is a vibrant children’s hub with a games room, gym and weights room, squash courts, sauna, indoor stadium, outdoor basketball court and a skate park.
