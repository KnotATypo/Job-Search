OUR STORY

Fujifilm CodeBlue Australia is a Managed Services Provider, servicing a wide range of Australia-based clients, each with diverse IT environments and topologies. Fujifilm CodeBlue has its roots stemming back to 2003, starting off as a Brisbane-based MSP which now supports customers remotely and locally in all major capitals.

THE OPPORTUNITY

We have an exciting opportunity for a Cyber Security Practice Lead to join our CodeBlue business.

This role will involve leading the security team to deliver best in class cyber security products, services and consulting to CodeBlue’s Managed Service customers. You will also be responsible for the growth of the security practice to new and existing CodeBlue customers.

The Cyber Security Practice Lead is also responsible for the implementation and management of internal security systems, practices, and controls to protect company data as well as the achievement and maintenance of compliance certifications and standards. You will also be responsible for developing and implementing cyber security policies and strategies, educating staff on cyber security best practice, and overseeing cyber security incidents.
