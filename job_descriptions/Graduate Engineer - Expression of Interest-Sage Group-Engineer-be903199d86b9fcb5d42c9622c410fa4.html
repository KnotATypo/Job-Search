SAGE Automation is Australia's leading independent industrial automation and control systems integrator. A recognised leader in the design, delivery and support of industrial and electrical control solutions, SAGE Automation brings the best thinking to clients across transport, manufacturing, resources, Defence, utilities and infrastructure sectors.

We currently seeking expression of interest from talented and enthusiastic Graduate Engineers to join our Brisbane team. Graduate Engineers at SAGE are able to combine professional and technical learning, on-the job learning alongside ongoing mentoring whilst working on real-world, innovative projects. There will be opportunity to work on industrial automation projects from kick-off to hand over, including design documentation, PLC RTU and SCADA programming, factory acceptance testing, on-site commissioning and hand-over documentation.

To be successful in this position you will:

  • Be highly motivated, enthusiastic, proactive and resourceful with a great attitude.
  • Have knowledge of PLC, SCADA and HMI systems (Desirable).
  • Basic understanding of electrical circuit fundamentals.
  • Be highly competent at report and technical writing.
  • Provide assistance with technical delivery and execution of projects.
  • Work within a team environment alongside other junior and senior Engineers.
  • Provide project assistance and support to the Project Managers and Support Technicians.
  • Assist Estimators with the development of proposals and tender responses.
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality, accuracy and prioritise work to meet conflicting deadlines.
  • Possess the confidence and ability to liaise with management and internal staff.


Key requirements:

  • Be an undergraduate or recent graduate of Electrical or Mechatronic Engineering.
  • Have high computer competency, particularly with MS Office.
  • Possess excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Have a strong desire and interest to work within the automation industry.
  • Australian drivers licence.
  • Must have the right to work in Australia.
