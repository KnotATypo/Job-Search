Join Australia’s fastest growing brand!

At Seven, we are here to bring all Australians closer to the moments that move us.

Seven is Australia’s #1 television network and home to iconic news, sports and entertainment brands, plus Australia’s #1 BVOD service, 7plus.

A new and exciting opportunity has opened for a Chief of Staff to join the 7NEWS team in what promises to be an exciting year at Seven.

Based in Brisbane and reporting to the Brisbane News Director, you will hold a critical role in the day-to-day management and organisation of the News Team.

We are looking for someone who has:

  • at least two years’ experience in television newsrooms, preferably reporting or producing
  • outstanding leadership qualities, with first-class people skills
  • an energetic, self-starter attitude with the ability to think clearly and act quickly under pressure.
  • the proven ability to generate, research and develop stories, in conjunction with producers and the team on the ground
  • a sense of urgency and focus on deadlines

In return, we will provide you with:

  • Generous 7Perks and corporate discounts at a range of retail, tech and health & fitness facilities
  • A collaborative, vibrant culture
  • Professional development opportunities through Seven’s Mentoring and Performance & Development programs
  • A reward and recognition program
  • Generous paid parental leave

The Seven Network is part of Seven West Media, the media company of the future , powered by audience, content and technology. Comprising some of Australia’s most renowned media brands, SWM includes not just Seven Network and its affiliate channels 7two, 7mate, 7flix and BVOD offering 7plus, but also 7NEWS.com.au, The West Australian, The Sunday Times and more.

Seven West Media is an equal opportunity employer and we are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace.

Recruitment Agencies : Seven West Media does not accept any unsolicited agency resumes and will not be responsible for any fees related to unsolicited resumes.
