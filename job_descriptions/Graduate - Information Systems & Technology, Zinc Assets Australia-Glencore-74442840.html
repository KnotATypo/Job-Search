About Us

As a key player in Australias mining landscape, Glencore spans across 23 mine sites, responsibly sourcing commodities that advance everyday life. From the copper, cobalt and nickel powering the electric vehicle revolution, and the energy products like coal to help keep the lights on, what Glencore does touches every part of life. Our portfolio enables the transition to a low-carbon economy, while meeting society’s energy needs. Beyond our extensive portfolio, Glencore offers unparalleled career and development opportunities, fostering strong communities. Join Glencore to be part of a team shaping a future where your contributions matter.

About the Role

As a Graduate Information Systems and Technology Professional, you will be exposed to various duties undertaken in the Business Systems, Networks and Infrastructure. Over the course of the program you’ll be shown the ropes by our various teams, then depending on your skillset you’ll be off doing project work – it could be software development, or organisational change management, business analysis or infrastructure support if that’s what your skills are aligned to. No matter which might be, you will assist with experienced IS&T team to create right setup technologically and make a positive difference for optimal business performance across our mine sites.

As a Glencore graduate, you’ll have access to unrivalled opportunities and unlimited potential to grow your career. This is your opportunity to test your skills and creativity in real situations! With over 50 IS&T professionals in your team, you will be working with some of the best in the fields of telecommunication, systems engineering, application development, systems analysts, and IS&T support. Graduates will be provided with an exciting place to grow and develop during the early stage of their careers, often receiving projects very early in their placements. You will have the opportunity to work with leaders in your field in the following locations:

  • Mount Isa, QLD (Residential)
  • Townsville, QLD (Residential)
