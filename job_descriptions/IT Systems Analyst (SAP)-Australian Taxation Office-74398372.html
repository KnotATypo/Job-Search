About the role

As an IT Systems Analyst (SAP), you’ll oversee and manage our SAP, ERP and Corporate Applications ensuring systems and applications are fit for purpose. This may include analysing, designing and implementing solutions to resolve issues. You’ll assess the suitability of IT systems or applications to enhance their intended functionality in collaboration with business areas, end users and vendors.

Your duties

• Contribute to cost analysis, design considerations, development and testing

• Liaise with stakeholders to analyse and understand system requirements

• Resolve complex system issues and data errors

Skills and experience

• Experience using at least one SAP module such as HR, Payroll, FI, SRM, MM, VIM and Solution Manager

• Technical knowledge and skills to contribute to continuous improvement

• Experience preparing and executing complex test scripts, reporting test issues and managing defects

• Communicates with influence to various stakeholders and adapts to their needs

Benefits

• Flexibility to provide work/life balance

• Award-winning and highly professional organisation

• Contemporary workspaces and technology

These roles will be offered in Brisbane CBD, Canberra or Melbourne CBD.

You must be an Australian citizen to work with the ATO.

People with disability and Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples are encouraged to apply.
