Your role:
You will be responsible for overseeing the design, execution, and maintenance of Azure applications and data integration pipelines connecting various systems utilised by our client. Your contribution will be:
  • Working within the Microsoft Azure technology stack to deliver effective integration between external and internal data sources and enterprise systems.
  • Assisting in identifying business opportunities and improvements for stakeholders using industry best practices, latest product innovations, and technical solutions delivered by the Applications team.
  • Building, maintaining, and consolidating a suite of custom Azure Power Platform applications , ensuring that they meet business requirements, are well-documented, and well-received by users.
  • Building strong working relationships with stakeholders to gain a deep understanding of their application needs, and promoting practices and solutions that enable self-service and ease of use.
  • Assisting in the design, support, and development of ETL and ELT data pipelines , Dataverse, data lake, warehousing, and unification assets.
  • Delivering training and guidance to users to enable optimal system acceptance and utilisation.
  • Working within, and in alignment with, defined IT strategic priorities and goals.
  • Working in accordance with all data governance and data security policies, ensuring that users are also aware of such policies.

  • Extensive expertise in database management, SQL querying, and programming.
  • Experience in building data integrations within Azure, utilising tools like Azure Data Factory, Logic Apps, and Azure Functions.
  • Experience working with multiple REST APIs and semi-structured data sources.
  • Familiarity with Dynamics 365/Power Platform, including Dataverse.
  • Proficiency in programming .NET solutions, encompassing C# and database solutions.
  • Knowledge in various data integration techniques
  • Great working knowledge of Azure DevOps .
