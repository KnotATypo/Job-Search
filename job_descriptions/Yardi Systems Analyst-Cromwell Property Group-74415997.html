We currently have a Permanent Full-Time opportunity for a Yardi Systems Analyst to support our finance team in the operation of our Yardi ERP management platform.

Our ideal candidate will be interested in learning new systems and have a desire to apply themselves and solve issues. You will also be looking to utilise knowledge beyond the system, to how the platform can best be utilised within the business architecture and provide benefits to the users.

About the role

As the Yardi Systems Analyst, you will be responsible for supporting our Yardi Specialist to ensure all business-critical requirements are met in relation to the Yardi Platform. You'll work across a range of teams within the business to ensure the system is being used consistently and confidently across our AU portfolio.

This role will see you both maintaining our system while also developing your knowledge to identify issues and potential improvements. You'll be attending user group and professional development meetings to actively contribute to both your development and your understanding of the platform.

You will need to have an eye for detail and be adaptable to the varying situations you'll come across. It is essential that you have experience in a Systems Administration role, ideally in the property industry. Any experience with financial systems such as SAP, MRI and JD Edwards will be beneficial.
