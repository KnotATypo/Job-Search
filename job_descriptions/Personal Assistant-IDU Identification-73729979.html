About the Job

Following the success of our nightlife product and continued growth, we are seeking an experienced and dynamic candidate who has strong administration skills to join the team as a Personal Assistant to the CEO and Senior Managers. You will be able to create and maintain good internal and external relationships, have strong organisational skills, whilst being professional and self-motivated.

About us:

iDU Identification is a Brisbane-based scale-up that specialises in providing innovative identification solutions for businesses. We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality products and exceptional customer service. After just three years iDU is now used by 350+ venues across Australia and we’re rapidly expanding, not just in the licensed venue scene but across other markets as well. We are seeking candidates who are enthusiastic, with a willingness to learn and who are committed to a long-term position.

Key Responsibilities

  • Managing calendars, scheduling appointments, coordinating meetings
  • Screen and manage incoming calls, emails, and correspondence.
  • Draft, edit, and proofread documents, emails, presentations and other communications on behalf of the senior managers.
  • Prepare meeting agendas, documents, and materials.
  • Attend meetings, take notes, and prepare minutes as required.
  • Plan and coordinate travel arrangements, including flights, accommodations, and itineraries.
  • Ensure that travel plans align with the senior managers’ schedules and preferences.
  • Assisting with document preparation, filing and organisation
  • Providing administrative support to ensure efficient office operations
  • Conduct research and gather information as needed for various tasks.
  • Coordinate and follow up on tasks and projects, ensuring deadlines are met.
  • Collaborate with other team members or departments to facilitate the completion of projects.
  • Event planning and management
  • Payroll processing support
  • Accounts Payable/ Receivable management and Bank Reconciliations
  • Weekly / Monthly reporting
  • Budgeting / Expense Management
  • Supplier management
  • Flexibility to handle evolving priorities and responsibilities.

About you:

  • You have a minimum of 2-3 years in a fast-paced personal assistant or administration role.
  • You have excellent interpersonal and communication skills, with the ability to build rapport.
  • You have strong problem-solving and conflict resolution abilities.
  • You are proactive and self-motivated.
  • Tech-Savvy: Proficient in using office software (e.g., Microsoft Office, Google Workspace) and familiarity with relevant technology tools.
  • You have excellent time management, organisational skills and attention to detail, with the ability to multitask and manage multiple competing priorities.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • You are able to commute to our Brisbane office 5 days per week
