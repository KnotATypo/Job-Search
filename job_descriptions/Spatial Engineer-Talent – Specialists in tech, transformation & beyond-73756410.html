Talent International is searching for experienced Spatial Engineer to join our client, a large government body providing services to the QLD community. This is a contract position until the end of June, 2024 with possible extension.

About the Role:
Our client is seeking a suitably experienced Spatial Engineer, to join the team to contribute to the successful delivery of the various projects allocated to the IST Directorate, via a Program Management function that has been established to ensure the subordinate projects are managed in a consistent and coordinated manner.

Responsibilities:

  • Technical leadership and expert advice on the complexities of Spatial Services, to provide support to the Technology Transition Implementation Lead as an integral member of the team responsible for planning and implementing the technical transition of production spatial services; that being the migration of users and their associated accounts, applications, data layers, underlying data and related integrations, from one agency to another, without production degradation.
  • Provide extensive knowledge transfer and training to permanent staff to ensure F&DD spatial officers can support the rapidly changing and critical technology associated with Spatial Services, post the project's go live.
  • Work collaboratively and communicate effectively with operational and project teams, vendors, other business units and management to develop successful working relationships and deliver approved business requirements;
  • Lead the analysis and timely resolution of issues within a complex ICT environment to ensure alignment of project outcomes.


Skills and Experience Required:
Essential:

  • Experience in the management of ArcGIS Online and ArcGIS Enterprise platforms in an large-scale environment
  • Experience with FME
  • Creation/manipulation of geospatial data using scripting (Python) and SQL
  • Experience in building, deploying and supporting spatial applications

Desirable

  • Experience with management and support of GeoCortex
  • Experience in managing and supporting other ESRI geospatial products, including Survey 123
  • Exposure to database platforms (particularly Microsoft SQL Server and Oracle RDBMS)
