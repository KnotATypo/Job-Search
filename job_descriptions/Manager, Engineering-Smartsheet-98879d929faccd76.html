Full job description

Smartsheet, a leader in collaborative project management and productivity software, is seeking a Manager, Engineering to spearhead one of our Content Automation teams in Australia. This role is pivotal in our mission of integrating and transforming content between structured and unstructured data. The Content Automation pillar is critical to our strategy of revolutionizing content handling and presentation of business intelligence data in the AI-assisted future. Smartsheet is committed to driving growth in Australia, positioning it as a hub of AI, innovation and excellence.

As a leader within our award-winning SaaS platform, you will be integral to the ongoing evolution of our product. At Smartsheet, you'll guide a supportive, agile, and results-oriented team, committed to delivering high-quality software efficiently and contributing to Australia's emergence as a leader in AI technology.

This position is remote eligible and reports to the Senior Manager, Engineering.


Role Overview:
As a Manager, Engineering in our Content Automation pillar, you will lead a team of engineers focused on innovative content transformation solutions. You will be responsible for driving results, enabling efficient team delivery, and leveraging differences to create a supportive environment that fosters growth, mentorship, and high performance.


You Will:

  • Manage a team of software engineers to deliver great products to our customers
  • Maintain a high level of operational excellence focusing on application and infrastructure scalability, reliability, and performance
  • Lead, coach, and inspire your team to achieve quality software
  • Collaborate with partners across the company, including product management, UX, and customer teams
  • Establish lean engineering processes that use collaboration and rapid iteration to build the right product for the right problems
  • Hire and grow your team by promoting engineering excellence and career development
  • Collaborate with partner teams across the company including product management, development, UX design, and customer support teams
  • Establish lean engineering processes that promote collaboration and rapid iterations to build the right product to solve the right problems

You Have:

  • 8+ years of software engineering experience and 2+ years managing software engineering teams.
  • A deep software development skills in at least one of the following, Ruby, Go, Java, or JavaScript.
  • Experience with modern technology stacks for building scalable, high availability customer-facing applications.
  • Experience in distributed systems, systems automation, and cloud technologies.
  • The drive to dive in, constantly learn new things, and build awesome products
  • A degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field or equivalent practical experience
  • Legally eligible to work in Australia on an ongoing basis
