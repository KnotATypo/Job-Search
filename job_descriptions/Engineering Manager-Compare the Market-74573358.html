About the role

As an Engineering Manager at Compare The Market (CTM), your primary focus will be on providing leadership and management support to a team of Software Engineers & Testers - guiding, inspiring, and empowering them to perform at their best.

In this role, you'll spearhead efforts in recruitment, retention, and capability improvements to ensure we maintain a top-tier team capable of surpassing expectations. Collaborating with other Engineering leaders, you will help establish and enforce development and testing standards, implement best practices to ensure consistency and excellence in all our endeavors.

Your expertise will play a vital role in streamlining software engineering workflows, driving efficiency, and enhancing productivity. Through your leadership in innovation and continuous improvement initiatives, you'll propel our software engineering capabilities to unprecedented levels, pushing boundaries and establishing new benchmarks for excellence.

What you’ll do

  • Provide leadership and management of 10+ Engineers & Testers.
  • Drive recruitment, retention, and talent development efforts.
  • Define and enforce development and testing standards and best practices.
  • Optimize & track engineering workflows for efficiency and productivity.
  • Lead technical components of various initiatives and projects.
  • Collaborate with IT leaders to streamline technology maintenance, enhancements, and various delivery processes.
  • Identify and mitigate risks associated with development and testing activities.
  • Maintain operational excellence focusing on systems reliability and performance.

What you’ll bring

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science, IT, or related discipline.
  • Minimum 8 years of experience leading and managing software engineering and testing functions.
  • Demonstrated track record of hiring, retaining, and developing high-performance teams.
  • Proficiency in modern software engineering and testing standards, methodologies, and tooling.
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively.
  • Proficiency in identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks associated with IT software engineering and testing.
