Full job description

Opportunities available for Registered Nurses in our Day Oncology/Haematology unit Have a say in your work roster and apply now.


About the Role

In this role you will be responsible for providing a high level of clinical care and exceptional customer service to our Oncology/Haematology care patients and families. Duties will include assessing, planning, coordinating and evaluating the care delivery needs of each individual patient within your care.

We have permanent full-time and part-time roles available, working across Monday - Friday.


Your Skills and Experience

  • Current registration with AHPRA as a Registered Nurse
  • Minimum two (2) years’ experience in an acute hospital setting
  • Desirable chemotherapy and immunotherapy competent, CVAD competent and cannulation competent

Remuneration

On offer is an hourly rate ranging from $40.47 - $52.19 + superannuation based on experience level and qualifications, exclusive of penalties or allowances. (QLD RN/Midwife gr2-gr8) [FTE $79,968.72 – $103,127.44]


What’s in it for you?

Professional Advancement: Recognition, Rewards, Professional Development, and Employee referral programs. Free access to LinkedIn Learning, education scholarships and the Ramsay Leadership Academy to support career progression.

Discounts: Hospital, Allied Health & Pharmacy discounts - you’ll pay less for hospital cover and receive higher extras limits for things like General and Major Dental.

Health and Wellbeing: Flexible work opportunities with the ability to have a say in your roster, Flexible Leave Program, and a free Employee Assistance Program offering access to confidential counselling, coaching and support.

Leisure/Social/Financial: Discounts for leisure travel with selected Hotels, salary sacrifice opportunities e.g., airport lounge membership, self-education expenses, novated leasing and more.


About Our Hospital

Greenslopes Private Hospital is proud to be one of Australia’s leading private teaching hospitals, owned and operated by Ramsay Health Care. Located on the southside of Brisbane - approximately 5km from the CBD, Greenslopes Private is renowned as a world-class tertiary medical facility offering the latest treatments and first-class service to our patients.

We are dedicated to providing the best possible care around the clock across all specialty areas - from our 24/7 Greenslopes Emergency Centre to our dedicated medical and surgical services. We have some of Queensland’s finest specialists - many of whom are leaders in their field and work across both the public and private sectors.


Requirements
