The role :
The Lead Data Analytics Consultant plays a crucial role in harnessing data analytics to propel business outcomes, with a specific emphasis on optimising claim validation processes. The successful candidate will take charge of designing, developing, deploying, and integrating comprehensive analytics solutions that strategically utilise data to enhance commercial outcomes.

This role is instrumental in fostering a culture of data-driven decision-making across Auto & General. This is an excellent full-time opportunity to advance your career in an analytical and data-driven capacity with Australia's foremost insurer!


Accountabilities:

  • Developing and implementing data analytics solutions using the Google Cloud Platform (GCP) utilising various solutions including BigQuery, Cloud Functions, Vertex AI and Composer (Apache Airflow).
  • Utilising SQL and Python to extract, transform, and analyse large datasets.
  • Collaborating closely with business stakeholders to identify data requirements and translating them into actionable insights that drive informed decision-making.
  • Building and maintaining data-driven solutions across the entire spectrum of analytics and data science, including DataMart’s, reporting systems, statistical analyses, and machine learning models.
  • Developing and delivering data visualisations to effectively communicate findings and support decision-making processes.



Knowledge and experience:

  • Degree in Data Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science or a related field/commercial experience.
  • 4+ years of experience in a data science/analytics-centric role.
  • Strong proficiency in SQL and Python.
  • Expertise in working in Cloud Environments -AWS/Azure/Google Cloud Platform (GCP Preferred).
  • Experience deploying Machine Learning solutions into production commercial environments.
  • Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills..
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Passion for data-driven decision-making.
