Full job description

Brunel have partnered with a Tier 2 builder with a strong presence in Queensland to bring on board Structures / Finishes Foreman for a $200m project in Brisbane City.They specialise in high-rise residential, industrial and commercial projects with values ranging from $5m - $200m+.


  • Opportunities to get exposure to a variety of high value projects
  • Stability from a strong pipeline of works - a respected builder with a reputation for quality builds
  • Clear career development paths with proven examples within the company.



About this role
  • Lead, guide, and mentor the on-site team and trade partners.
  • Work closely with the CA and Project Manager on projects.
  • Strategically manage the project program.
  • Engage with clients and stakeholders to ensure high-quality service.
  • Play a hands-on role, both with and without tools, to meet project demands.
  • Implement a solution-based approach to mitigate risks.
  • Oversee Health & Safety, quality, cost control, and budgeting.
  • Ensure the project meets its Key Performance Objectives.
  • Adhere to and ensure the team follows relevant policies, codes, and acts.
  • Manage subcontractors effectively.
  • Direct and lead site meetings and toolbox sessions.



About you
  • Demonstrated experience as a Foreman overseeing high-rise projects from start to finish.
  • Ability to cultivate strong relationships with all on-site personnel and promote a productive culture.
  • Possess leadership skills to drive the project schedule and optimize team and subcontractor performance.
  • Forecast and plan for programming needs, swiftly address emerging issues with effective solutions.
  • Maintain a high level of safety and quality awareness, ensuring compliance with rigorous standards on-site.
  • Maintain a well-organized and clean work site, recognizing its importance for overall success.
  • Understand cost implications and project requirements, capable of making informed decisions with a commercial perspective.
  • Willing to actively participate in project work, demonstrating a proactive approach to achieving goals.



Our offer

  • Opportunity to work on high value, quality projects.
  • Secure role within a growing company providing career development opportunities.
