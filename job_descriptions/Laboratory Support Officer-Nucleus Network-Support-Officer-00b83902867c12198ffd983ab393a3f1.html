We have an exciting opportunity for a Laboratory Support Officer to join our growing team!

Nucleus Network is the only global Phase 1 clinical trial specialist with facilities in the USA and Australia, fulfilling our purpose of “advancing medicine and improving lives”, by attracting the best talent.

Since our establishment in 2004, Nucleus Network has conducted well over 1000 Phase 1 clinical trials for biotechnology and pharmaceutical companies from the USA, Europe, Australia, Japan, South Korea, Taiwan and China.

Come and join us through this exciting journey of expansion and gain world-class experience servicing some of the most innovative biotechnology and pharmaceutical companies globally.

This unique opportunity will provide laboratory support to Nucleus Network staff, Sponsors, associated organizations and prospective clients.

Key responsibilities

  • Assist Laboratory Project team in a range of clinical trial start up activities and management of administrative tasks
  • Day to day administrative and clerical duties to support laboratory by preparing documentation for the timely receipt, processing, storage, and shipping of study samples
  • Developing and maintain working relationships across departments
  • Participate in the daily activities in the laboratory and clinical unit

Requirements

  • Completed relevant tertiary qualification in science (Health or Life Science)
  • Experience in a clinical or research laboratory
  • Outstanding time management and prioritization skills
  • Excellent communication skills with exceptional attention to detail
  • Computer literate with sound knowledge of MS Office package
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Able to work independently and effectively in a team
  • Open to flexible hours

Benefits

  • Meaningful Impact - 'advancing medicine, improving lives' of people around the world
  • Professional Growth Opportunities - We believe in investing in our employees' development
  • Employee Recognition - Your hard work and achievements will be recognised and rewarded
  • Employee Wellness Initiatives - We prioritize the health and wellbeing of our employee
