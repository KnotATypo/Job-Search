Full job description

Headquartered in Switzerland, Adfinis is a leading systems integrator that drives digitalization with open standards and technologies, shaping a world of innovative, sustainable, and resilient IT solutions based on trusted open-source technology.

At our core, we offer consulting, design, and implementation services. By leveraging open-source technologies with an emphasis on automation, we help our customers to enhance their processes and expedite their delivery capabilities. We collaborate closely with partners such as HashiCorp, Red Hat, SUSE, and GitLab to deliver innovative solutions that are carefully tailored to meet the individual needs and requirements of customers. In addition, we provide comprehensive 24/7/365 support and monitoring services.

For our growing Australian team of five people, we are looking for a determined and autonomous System Engineer who supports our customers with expertise in Linux cluster solutions, container platforms, automation tools, databases, and collaboration solutions.

Tasks

As a System Engineer, you work on customer projects, solve engineering challenges, and support our customers in Europe and ASIA Pacific as part of our managed services team.

Your task and duties will include:
  • provisioning, maintaining, configuring, and upgrading Linux server systems and/or cloud-managed services and applications
  • debugging and troubleshooting Linux server systems and/or cloud systems, providing in-depth technical support and assistance
  • solving specialized issues and eliminating the causes of malfunctions, if necessary in cooperation with software suppliers
  • analyzing monitoring events, identifying performance bottlenecks, and taking measures to optimize operations
  • creating main components of technical documentation from scratch
  • coordinating the collaboration with the customer and participating in meetings
Requirements

Must-Have Hard Skills

  • You have at least two years of work experience as a system engineer in a Linux-centric environment.
  • You have good knowledge in current open-source technologies as well as Cloud and DevOps customer journeys and/or enterprise IT requirements (e.g., IAM, Web/App Servers, Collaboration, Groupware, Database, SAP/HANA, Virtualization, Containers, Linux Clustering, Systems Management).
  • You have a good working knowledge of computer science fundamentals (e.g., compilers, linkers, and interpreters; lower 4 OSI layers / TCP/IP stack)
  • You can write code that includes basic error handling routines such as catching and displaying error messages or logging errors for further analysis.
  • You can fix existing automations and write and implement new ones that execute simple changes (e.g., account deletion) and are triggered by the system.
Must Have Soft Skills

  • You work effectively with others toward a common goal, participate constructively in joint efforts, and foster a supportive and trusting work environment.
  • You work thoroughly and diligently, are dependable, take responsibility for your actions and decisions, and strive for error-free, high-quality results.
  • You analyze complex situations, detect unwanted deviations, and develop viable solutions based on critical thinking, data analysis, and rational judgment.
  • You utilize your resources and consciously regulate your efforts to achieve and maintain high levels of productivity.
  • You anticipate, prepare, and establish a systematic course of action to achieve goals and objectives while ensuring the effective and efficient use of available resources.
  • You deliver high-quality services and solutions to customers that meet their needs and expectations while aligning with the company's goals and interests.
Nice-to-Haves

  • You have experience with GitLab, HashiCorp, RedHat and/or SUSE products
  • You have GitLab, HashiCorp, Red Hat and/or SUSE certifications.
  • You have AWS, Azure and/or GCP certifications.
