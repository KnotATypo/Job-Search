The Role:

Talent Street is looking for an MS Power Platform Developer for a large Federal government department. D365 platform is strategic business applications delivery platform for better collaboration, resource optimization and to deliver integrated solutions. The client's delivery teams are structured into Agile Solutions Train with three operational Agile Release Train (ARTs) with multiple scrum teams operating in DevOps model within Scaled Agile framework (SAFe). In DevOps model Delivery and Operational responsibility are assigned within scrum teams. As a Microsoft Power Platform Developer and member of the Agile project team, and under direction, you will help design, develop, implement and support the required systems and business solutions.
