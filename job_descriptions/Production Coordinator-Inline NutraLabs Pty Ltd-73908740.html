About Us

Inline NutraLabs is a privately owned food manufacturing company based in Salisbury, Brisbane. We are well known in the food industry for formulating, blending, and re-packing the best quality functional dry powder blends, predominantly for the complimentary health care market. Our company aim is to create innovative products that improve health through nutrition.

About You

Coming from a solid food manufacturing background, you may already have proven experience as a production leader, or you may be seeking that next step in your career and you will be able to bring to our business;

  • Strong knowledge of production planning / scheduling / quality control and lean manufacturing (Ostendo Operational Software knowledge highly regarded).
  • The ability to effectively manage high pressured situations regarding troubleshooting and decision making.
  • Exceptional interpersonal behaviours to ensure effective collaboration across all areas of the business.
  • Natural leadership qualities that can promote, motivate, and inspire a diverse workplace.
  • Strong computer skills, with experience in spreadsheets.

About the Role

As a key support to the Operations Manager and the OPS Team, the responsibilities of the Production Coordinator will include (but not limited to);

  • Day to Day production administration including development and implementation production plans / schedules.
  • Support the Production Supervisor in leading the production team, providing guidance and training.
  • Monitor production processes, identifying areas for improvement and implementing lean manufacturing principles as per company practices.
  • Work with cross-functional teams, including, Quality Assurance and Supply Chain, to support operations processes and ensuring a quality product and delivery expectation are meet.
  • Ensure the production process meets the company requirements to compliance for safety, company policies, and quality standards.
  • Identify equipment maintenance needs and coordinate maintenance activities with internal maintenance team to minimize downtime and optimize machine performance.
  • Drive continuous improvement by maintaining accurate production records.

We don't work weekend! Hours of work are 38 hours per week Monday to Friday, start and finish times 9.00am to 17.00pm.
