The Opportunity
Join HBF as the Head of Business Applications in our Enabling Technology Department on a 12 month fixed term contract. Reporting directly to the General Manager of Technology and Cyber, this pivotal leadership role combines managerial expertise with hands-on technical proficiency. As the Head of Business Applications, you will lead a dynamic team, driving operational efficiencies, and contributing strategically to the continuous improvement of our business application landscape.

About You
If you are a seasoned leader with a passion for innovation and a track record in IT business applications, we want to hear from you!

We are seeking a dynamic leader with experience in strategic projects and streamlining initiatives.

As the ideal candidate, you possess:

Leadership Experience: Demonstrated ability to lead and drive a high-performance team, providing support and guidance in strategic outcomes within your team and across the organisation.
Technical Expertise: Strong experience and knowledge of IT business applications and delivery of key strategic programs of work.
Collaboration Skills: A collaborative mindset with the ability to build value-adding relationships with internal and external stakeholders.
Strategic Vision: Proven experience in assessing and recommending upgrades or new installations of business applications to align with strategic goals.

Essential Experience:

To be successful in this role, you should have:

• Leadership experience, managing performance, resourcing, and workloads for multi-scale teams.
• Refined analytical, conceptual, and problem-solving skills.
• Exemplary communication skills, both written and verbal, with excellent technical specification writing/documentation skills.
• Strong customer service focus and stakeholder management skills.

Additional Experience and Qualifications:

• Experience in Application Security and working in organisations using scalable Agile frameworks.
• Knowledge of the health insurance industry, including business and information systems.

Preferred Experience and Qualifications:

• Tertiary qualification in Computer Science, Information Management Systems, or a related discipline.

Are you ready to take on a leadership role where your expertise in IT business applications can make a real impact? Seize the opportunity to join HBF as the Head of Business Applications and lead a dynamic team towards achieving strategic objectives.

About HBF
As one of WA’s longest established private health providers, (we’ve been around for 80 years!), we continue to innovate and challenge the status quo; adopting a strategy that focuses on business growth and sustainability, diversification, national expansion and significant enhancements to how we service our members through technology and relevant related businesses.

As a not-for-profit fund with no shareholders, we are dedicated to doing the right thing for our members. That’s why our iconic brand’s reputation is built on personalised service, community support and a motivation to deliver for our members in the moments that matter.

When you work for HBF, you work for a company with a clear vision and a team of people that are passionate about providing the best service, experience and products for our members across Australia.
