As a Hogan Systems Developer, you will support the Core Hogan System and be reporting to the Core Banking Systems technology manager.

The position includes the design, development and implementation of custom application changes required to support technology and business projects as well as the identification and resolution of software problems, interfacing with other application teams and custom coding.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Design the component of a solution covering a technical or application domain as a member of a team of developers for a project involving multiple domains
  • Design, build and test integration solutions for specific business problems or project requirements
  • Document program code in accordance with user requirements, and system & technical specifications
  • Provide input to, or develop business systems solutions estimations and specifications
  • Partner with the support organisation to ensure resolution of incidents and appropriate scheduling of reverse demand activities
  • Engage with clients, other software developers, business analysts, support analysts, quality analyst, integration, and senior management
  • Closely monitoring workload and progress
  • Instilling a sense of urgency on tasks and defect resolutions
  • Be Customer focused: ability to interpret and analyse customer requirements; influence design solutions in-line with Technology strategy
  • Produces requirements documentation and system plans, and encode, test, debug, maintain and documents programs and applications.

Skills required:

  • Appropriate qualification in Information Technology and/or Business or equivalent industry experience
  • Technical experience involving the Hogan System conversion process
  • Technically experienced in applying the Hogan programming standards
  • At least 5+ years appropriate Hogan Core Banking industry experience within:
  • IDS   –  Integrated Deposit Systems
  • UMB –  Umbrella Programming Interface
  • Other Hogan application experience (e.g., ILP, ODS, CIS, SPS,FSS) will be an advantage and will be taken into consideration
  • Mainframe z/OS COBOL, VSAM, DB2, JCL, TSO, CICS and Assembler

Desirable:

  • Experience developing or supporting high availability production systems
  • Experience in IT in a large organization within the Financial Services industry

Essential requirements:

  • Excellent English verbal and written communication skills
  • Demonstrated ability to organise, facilitate, and recap meetings
  • Able to prioritise and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment
  • Experience working in a client-facing role with the ability to build strong relationships
  • Utilize established internal and external networks to promote innovative solutions for customers
  • Australian Citizen or PR with Full working rights in Australia
