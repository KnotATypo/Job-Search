About Your Next Opportunity

Sullivan Nicolaides Pathology now has a full-time opportunity for an administrative professional to work as a pathology customer service support team member in our Doctor Service Contact Centre based at our main laboratory in Bowen Hills, Brisbane.

In this role, you will be primarily responsible for providing customer service assistance to our referring practitioners.

It will require a friendly, customer-focused individual with a highly professional nature, strong phone etiquette, and the ability to work effectively and multi-task in a fast-paced environment.

Your experience and organisational skills will make you an integral part of a team committed to delivering outstanding customer service and improving the experience and outcomes for our clients.

Additionally, your strong written and communication skills and commitment to service excellence will ensure that you proactively and willingly deliver a personalised customer service experience.

Sullivan Nicolaides Pathology’s multi-skilled customer service team is unique in that it is a varied role and will suit someone who thrives in a multi-skilled environment.

Working at Sullivan Nicolaides Pathology’s main office at Bowen Hills and part of the Marketing team, the Doctor Service Contact Centre is responsible for several essential operation functions, including:

  • Providing exceptional service with strong customer focus to medical practitioners and their patients and SNP team members, ensuring effective communication between various sections within the laboratory;
  • Answering a high volume of incoming phone calls from medical centres, hospitals and nursing homes
  • Issuing of patient results to referring doctors and hospitals, including critical results that may require more extensive follow-up;
  • Actioning the urgent/critical phone queue
  • Performing daily administrative tasks
  • Providing assistance with pathology tests and reports, maintaining and updating medical centre and hospital details, and general enquiries

The successful candidate will be hard-working, enthusiastic and have the ability to show initiative. This is an excellent opportunity to join the team and make a positive contribution to the overall level of service delivered by Sullivan Nicolaides Pathology.

Your Positive attitude and willingness to exceed customer expectations, combined with effective problem-solving skills, are key attributes that will see you succeed in this role.

Extensive training will be provided over a six-month period.
