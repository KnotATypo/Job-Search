ITSM Specialist | Brisbane, QLD | 2-3 days in the office

Our client is seeking a highly skilled ITSM Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in ITIL processes and a proven track record in Incident Management, Change Management, Problem Management, and Request Fulfillment, with specific experience working with ServiceNow. As an ITSM Specialist, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency and effectiveness of their IT services, driving continuous improvement, and enhancing the overall customer experience.

This role involves no people management.

Responsibilities:

  • Implement and maintain ITIL best practices across the organization, ensuring alignment with business objectives and IT service delivery standards.
  • Lead Incident Management efforts, including identification, prioritization, resolution, and escalation of incidents to minimize impact on business operations.
  • Oversee Change Management processes, including assessing change requests, evaluating potential impacts, and coordinating implementation to minimize disruptions.
  • Drive Problem Management initiatives to identify root causes of recurring incidents, implement corrective actions, and prevent future occurrences.
  • Manage Request Fulfillment processes, ensuring timely and accurate delivery of services to meet user needs and expectations.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement service improvement plans, leveraging data-driven insights and metrics to drive operational excellence.
  • Provide guidance and support to team members, promoting a culture of accountability, collaboration, and continuous learning.
  • Utilize ServiceNow platform to streamline ITSM processes, configure workflows, and generate reports for performance analysis and decision-making.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field/experience.
  • ITIL Foundation certification desired.
  • 3+ years of experience in IT Service Management roles, with a focus on Incident Management, Change Management, Problem Management, and Request Fulfillment.
  • Strong understanding of ITIL processes and best practices, with experience implementing and optimizing ITSM frameworks.
  • Experience working with ServiceNow platform, including configuration, administration, and reporting capabilities.
  • Proven ability to effectively prioritize and manage multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment, while maintaining attention to detail and quality.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively with stakeholders at all levels of the organization.
  • Analytical mindset with a focus on data-driven decision-making and continuous improvement.
  • ITIL Intermediate or Expert certifications in relevant disciplines (e.g., Service Operation, Continual Service Improvement) a plus.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and benefits package
  • Opportunities for professional development and career advancement
  • Dynamic and collaborative work environment
  • Flexible work arrangements
  • Health and wellness programs
  • Company-sponsored events and activities
