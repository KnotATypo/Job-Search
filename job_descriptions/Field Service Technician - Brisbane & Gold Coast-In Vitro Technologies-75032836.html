In Vitro Technologies is a Healthcare and Scientific distribution organisation who specialise in the sales, marketing, and support of medical products throughout Australia and New Zealand.

In Vitro Technologies are the premier supplier for cell biology and translational research. Our range of cells, consumables, reagents, and laboratory equipment provide support for our life science customers’ workflow in application areas of Cancer, Neuroscience, Immunology, Cell & Gene therapy, amongst others.

ABOUT THE ROLE:

The role services Medical facilities across the Brisbane & Gold Coast region.

This position will suit a Technical minded person that has a good mechanical and electrical aptitude with an eye for detail.

The primary role is the Installation, maintenance and service of industrial grade infection control equipment including Autoclave sterilizers, Washer disinfectors & Ultrasonic cleaners throughout the Sydney / Newcastle / Central Coast region.

The successful applicant will come from a service plant maintenance background and possess good technical skills.

Field service technicians working on industrial equipment and electrical or mechanical fitters encouraged to apply.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Experience diagnosing and repairing mechanical, electromechanical, and/or electronic equipment.
  • Experience troubleshooting and responding to customer concerns.
  • Proven record of being reliable and accountable for all aspects of their job.
  • Proficient level of computer skills including MS Word, Excel and Outlook.
  • Excellent analytical, interpersonal and communication skills with the ability to communicate complex technical issues in an easy to understand manner.
  • Resourceful, with the ability to work independently.
  • Strong time management skills.
  • Ability to adapt to changing circumstances.
  • Decision-making, problem resolution and creative thinking skills
  • Ability to multi-task activities with shifting priorities. Able to work productively in a pressurized environment.

  • Electrical, Instrumentation, Electronics trade or Mechanical trades with a Disconnect/Reconnect Licence (or willing to obtain)
