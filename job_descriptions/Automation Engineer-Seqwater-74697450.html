About Seqwater

Seqwater is the Queensland Government Bulk Water Supply Authority, responsible for delivering safe, secure and cost-effective bulk water supply for over 3 million people across Southeast Queensland.

Seqwater is one of the largest water businesses with the most geographically spread and diverse asset base of any capital city water authority. Our operations extend from the New South Wales border to the base of the Toowoomba ranges.

We commit to delivering a high standard of customer service aligned to our strategic vision. We value working collaboratively with our internal & external customers to improve service delivery.

About Your Opportunity

Reporting to our Lead Engineer – Automation & Process Control, you will provide expert technical client facing automation engineering leadership for Seqwater along with the

development and implementation of software defined automation solutions involving industrial computerized environments. With a focus on PLC & SCADA and future technology areas you will lead the planning, design, build, test, and implementation of software assets managed by the team.

Key responsibilities:

  • Lead software development cycle including engineering, programming, testing, defect corrections, governance or specialist support for release management, deployment, and implementation.
  • Manage various software information system(s), including standards, workflows, software change management, testing records, code repositories, configuration files, etc.
  • Provide specialist advice, to projects and operations to ensure integration into consistent environment and conventions.
  • Lead software-based business initiatives in line with Seqwater’s strategy whilst guiding, consulting, setting outcomes and providing assurance to projects where solutions are supplied by others.
  • Identify and design software engineering solutions for complex business problems, including recommendations to substitute infrastructure for code, improve processes with the use of automation, and strategic planning.
