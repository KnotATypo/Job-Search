Your next exciting job opportunity:

We are seeking highly skilled and motivated Marine Test & Evaluation Engineer in Brisbane . The T&E Engineer will provide expertise, engineering management and governance as related to RAN processes for BMT’s defence clients.


In this role you will be able to

  • Provide expertise with Defence Engineering process, specifically related to the Royal Australian Navy.
  • Provide strong Electrical Engineer, Mechatronics or, Mechanical/Marine engineering advice to clients and partners for the project.
  • Manage internal and external project stakeholders.
  • Providing technical advice, leadership and mentoring within the Defence project environment.

Relevant Education, Experience, and Skills:

Essential:

  • Bachelor's or higher degree in Engineering (preferably in a relevant field such as Electrical, Mechatronics or Marine Engineering.
  • Minimum of 6 years of experience in a similar role, with a Defence/Maritime background.
  • Demonstrated technical leadership skills and experience in managing and mentoring teams.
  • Understanding of the Engineering structure of Navy and associated policies and procedures
  • Understanding of Seaworthiness requirements
  • Experience working within the Australian Defence Sector in an Acquisition program
  • Strong technical knowledge and understanding of engineering principles, standards, and regulations.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to liaise effectively with stakeholders at all levels.
  • Current NV1 clearance or ability to obtain.
  • Australian Citizenship is essential.
