Why us?
We’re an innovative technology company that has achieved remarkable growth over the past 25 years, starting from humble beginnings with a single computer to becoming a thriving global technology business. We work with over 13,000 causes globally and have helped raise over $250 million last FY.

Here, innovation isn't a buzzword—it's our way of life. We thrive on pushing boundaries, from brainstorming sessions to code reviews, every voice matters, and every idea has the potential to be impactful.

A Peek At Engineering: At Jumbo, software engineering isn't always about writing code—it's about weaving together innovation, collaboration, and purpose to craft experiences that leave a lasting impression. We empower our engineers to work closely with our product department, translating business objectives into tangible outcomes and taking ownership of their work from conception to deployment.

Who we are looking for
  • Passionate about developing high-quality mobile applications
  • Strong in CS fundamentals, data structures, and algorithms
  • 3+ years of Android Mobile Development (Kotlin & Java) or can demonstrate a strong portfolio of apps
  • A good knowledge of mobile user interface design, from phones to tablets and other large-screen devices
  • Someone with an interest in keeping up with and curiosity to learn the latest technologies
  • Enjoys working in a team environment and has strong written communication skills for efficient remote work
Familiarity with Git, code review, REST API design, automated testing using Espresso, Jetpack Compose and other Jetpack libraries, Kotlin coroutines, design patterns, and dependency injection will be highly regarded. Experience with other mobile frameworks such as Flutter and React Native is also an asset.

What you will be doing
You’ll be working within a cross-functional team (Heatwave) alongside developers, testers, designers, and product managers to conceptualise, design, build, test, and release high-quality native Android apps to the Google Play Store. This will add the capability for the growth initiatives that Heatwave is working towards and the roadmap objective. As well as:
  • Write high-quality, maintainable, and testable code
  • Take ownership of and technically lead the development of new features and products
  • Keep up to date with the latest tech and explore emerging technologies to keep the code aligned with modern standards
  • Be involved in planning sprint goals, understanding product needs and motivations, and transferring mocks and prototypes into compelling interfaces and experiences
