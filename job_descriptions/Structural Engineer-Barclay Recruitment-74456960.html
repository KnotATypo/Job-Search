The Role
As a Structural Engineer, you will be expected to deliver quality engineering outcomes for clients and complement the mechanical engineering function of the business.
  • Prepare design calculations and reports
  • Produce technical specifications
  • Develop concepts, pre-feasibility, feasibility, and detailed design tasks
  • Support the drafting function, as required
  • Prepare tender submissions
  • Use and maintain the internal job management and reporting systems
  • Budget and schedule management of your team
  • Liaise with clients, as required
While this role is predominantly office based, you will be required to travel from time to time to attend site visits, inspections and/or audits as well as attend external client meetings.

You
  • Tertiary qualification in Structural Engineering
  • Experience working in a local consultancy ( applicants must have Australian or New Zealand experience)
  • Experience working on industrial projects – mining, ports, heavy industry etc
  • High level structural design abilities
  • Competency in industry standard design software e.g. SPACE GASS, STRAND7
  • Attention to detail
  • Excellent communication skills – both verbal and written
