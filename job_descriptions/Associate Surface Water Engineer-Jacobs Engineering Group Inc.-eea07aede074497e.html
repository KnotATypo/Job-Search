About the role

We are looking for an Associate Surface Water Engineer to help us deliver flooding elements of a strong pipeline of work on high profile and challenging projects that span natural resource management to major infrastructure design.

The responsibilities of the role include:

  • Manage projects, ensuring on-time, cost-effective delivery and client satisfaction.
  • Communicate technical information effectively to diverse audiences.
  • Win work, including cost estimation, proposal writing, and client relationship management.
  • Provide technical leadership and mentorship to project and discipline team members.
  • Support innovation within your technical field.

Here’s What You’ll Need:

  • Approximately 10 years’ experience in the water resources industry
  • Strong technical knowledge and proven experience in flooding water resources assessments including infrastructure design.
  • Relevant Engineering degree (or equivalent) and relevant industry Chartership/Accreditation, ideally RPEQ.
  • Competency in industry standard flood modelling and GIS packages including TUFLOW, RORB, URBS, Infoworks ICM, MIKE products and QGIS.
  • In-depth knowledge of Australian guidelines, including working experience with ARR2019
  • Sound market knowledge, the ability to build strong client relationships, and the acumen to identify, bid, win, and deliver water sector projects.
  • Ability and willingness to guide and mentor team members.
  • Successful candidates will have an inclusive mindset and enjoy collaborating across geographies as part of diverse teams.
  • Be open-minded and willing to adapt with change and transformation.
  • Demonstrate a passion for waterways.
