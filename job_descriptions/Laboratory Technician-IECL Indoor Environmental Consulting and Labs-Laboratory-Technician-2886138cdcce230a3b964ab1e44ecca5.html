ABOUT US

At Indoor Environmental and Consulting Labs (IECL) we test mould to determine the source and spread, ensuring high quality air supply in your building.  To find out more about us please visit www.iecl.com.au

A vacancy exists for a Laboratory Technician (and a Lab Manager – see related Advert), part-time, any days Monday to Friday.

ABOUT THE ROLE

As our Lab Tech we need you to:

  • Work independently, in a small team environment
  • Have a demonstrated ability to plan and prioritise your workload and work methodically and efficiently to complete tasks on schedule
  • Maintain and calibrate laboratory equipment
  • Possess a related degree in science (or be studying towards it)
  • Have demonstrated experience in a laboratory setting
  • Be able to follow documented procedures and communicate effectively
  • Possess good computer literacy, including skills in Microsoft Word and Excel
  • Possess good verbal and written communication skills
  • Have sound knowledge of quality assurance and quality control management practises
  • Participate in all laboratory activities including safety training, meetings and audit
  • Comply with all relevant standards, company policies, procedures and work instructions
  • Operate and perform maintenance on laboratory equipment as required
