About us

Flavour Creations is 100% Australian owned and operated. Founded in Queensland, by Bernadette Eriksen, for over 26 years, Flavour Creations has developed science-backed nutrition products to make nutrition easy, enjoyable, and accessible.

A leading provider of nutrition products for people with a variety of health conditions, including dysphagia (swallowing difficulties), today, Flavour Creations is expanding globally with trusted names in healthy-living and performance nutritional products.

About the role

We are on the hunt to find a motivated and experienced Warehouse Operator to join our team located in Tennyson. This full-time role, is running our busy warehouse, the work is varied and you will be supported by the wider team.  On the job training is provided.

This is an autonomous role and central to the success of our production.  You will be responsible for the following:

  • All incoming goods, checking they meet specifications.
  • Documenting these deliveries into our accounting software.
  • General warehouse duties including loading and unloading trucks.
  • Picking stock for production shifts.
  • GMP of your warehouse.
  • General management of the raw materials, FIFO.
  • Some manual handling mainly forklift operation.

What you need to succeed

  • Minimum 12 months experience in a similar role, with excellent all-round warehouse knowledge.
  • Current forklift licence.
  • Physically able to perform the duties, and capable of lifting up to 25kg bags.
  • Good time management with the ability to meet deadlines.
  • Self-motivated and possess the ability to analyse and problem solve.
  • Active team player who can communicate well with everyone in the business.
  • Computer literate, with experience in Microsoft Suite.
  • Available to work - Monday to Thursday 7am – 3:30pm and Friday 7am – 1:30pm
