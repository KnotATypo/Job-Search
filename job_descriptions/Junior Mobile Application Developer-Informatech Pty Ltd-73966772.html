Responsibilities

Mobile Application Developers will be a part of agile scrum teams to design and code for mobile devices.

A software developer will:

  • design and develop and maintain system changes and have experience with Xamarin
  • test, debug, update and improve software to ensure quality, security, and value. - provide technical advice to solve problems,
  • collaborate with others to identify and progress improvements in software delivery and support a culture of continuous improvement.
  • guide less experienced staff by reviewing their work and providing feedback to build team capability.
