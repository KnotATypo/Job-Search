About Careview

Careview is at the forefront of disability software solutions in Australia, revolutionizing care with our comprehensive web and mobile platforms. Trusted by over 700 organizations nationwide to support more than 150,000 NDIS Participants, we are deeply committed to enhancing service delivery for Australians living with disabilities through innovative technology.

The Role

As a Senior Software Engineer at Careview, you will play a pivotal role in advancing our B2B SaaS platforms. With a focus on .NET Core, this full-time position offers a dynamic blend of development, from APIs to microservices, all within an Azure-centric environment. Whether you're in Brisbane or the Gold Coast, we provide the flexibility to suit your lifestyle, coupled with the opportunity to occasionally collaborate in person at our Gold Coast office.

What You'll Do

  • Focus on enhancing our platform's backend services using .NET Core. Your work will directly impact our service's efficiency and scalability.
  • Build robust APIs for effective communication with front-end applications and integration with distributed microservices architecture.
  • Use Azure SDKs for developing backend features that interact with Azure services, emphasizing secure, scalable, and maintainable solutions.
  • Write clean, maintainable code supported by comprehensive unit and integration tests to maintain high-quality standards.
  • Work closely with the frontend team and other backend developers to ensure seamless integration and optimal performance of our applications.
  • Use Entity Framework Core for data access and tools like ReSharper to enhance code quality and development efficiency.
