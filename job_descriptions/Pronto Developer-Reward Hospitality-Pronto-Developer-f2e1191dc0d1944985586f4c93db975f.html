About the Role

The key scope of this role is to undertake system process analysis and consultation with key stakeholders to identify and implement improvements to the Pronto Enterprise Resource Plan (ERP) and other critical business applications including Cognos reporting and Mobile Sales Web Application. This role is based at our Yatala Corporate Office however flexible working arrangements including a virtual role may be considered for the right candidate.

Key Areas of Responsibility

Systems Analysis and Support

  • Create and change 4GL programs in Pronto based on functional specifications to achieve business requirements
  • Use SQL, Cognos and other tools to write reports required to provide key information to stakeholders
  • Administration of Pronto server operating system
  • Strict planning and reporting of progress of projects and other tasks to ensure deadlines are met

Testing and Training

  • Manage all testing phases of Pronto changes/upgrade to ensure business process improvements are achieved according to plan
  • Documentation and creation of training material for users
  • Assist the development and implementation of company approved end user training for Pronto and other applications

Skills and Experience

Essential Requirements

  • Extensive experience in a similar position, using knowledge and skills to write programs to change /improve business processes using Pronto
  • Experienced and confident in writing and changing programs with Pronto 4GL
  • Strong knowledge of Pronto objects and data fields
  • Previous experience with data extraction and manipulation
  • Experience with Unix. Linux, Windows, SQL, RDBMS
  • Competent using Microsoft suite of products
  • Excellent attention to detail including a high quality of code writing
  • Strong communication / interpersonal skills and able to engage confidently with management and external providers
  • Well-developed time management and organizational skills required to manage multiple requests by prioritizing work and making sound decisions to meet strict deadlines
  • Sound analytical and able to utilise initiative to problem solve
  • Must be a strong team player, and be highly self-motivated
  • Ability to take accountability for your performance, contribute as a team player and be able to act with a sense of urgency.
  • Demonstrate resilience during busy / complex situations.

Desirable Requirements

  • Experience gained within an international multi-site wholesale distribution company
  • Previous experience writing and publishing Cognos reports

About Reward Hospitality

Reward Hospitality is Australia's largest distributor of non-food consumables to a range of industries such as hospitality, catering, health & aged care and education.  We supply a wide range of products including tabletop, commercial catering equipment, janitorial and washroom supplies, disposables and packaging solutions from around the globe. Reward Hospitality has sites throughout Australia and New Zealand and is able to offer the full solution to our customers with an unmatched presence.

Reward Hospitality, the consolidation of Reward Distribution and The Hospitality Store, is proudly owned by the E.CF Group - the European leader in hospitality, catering and general food service supply with key markets in Europe, the Middle East and North Africa.  Paired with market leaders in Europe, Reward offers customers access to products and trends from around the globe.
