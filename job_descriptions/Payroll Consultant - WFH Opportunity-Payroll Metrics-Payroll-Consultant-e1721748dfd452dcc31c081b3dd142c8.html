Payroll Metrics is seeking to fill the position of Payroll Consultant with emphasis on implementing our software.

Payroll Metrics is a software developer specialising in payroll and workforce management (rosters, timesheet management time recording) software for Australian employers, delivered as a SaaS solution in Microsoft’s Azure cloud.

The Payroll Consultant will be responsible for implementing payroll solutions for our clients, ensuring a smooth transition from their existing systems to our payroll platform. The ideal candidate will have experience in payroll implementation and be able to manage multiple client projects simultaneously.

The Customer Services team reports to the Customer Relationship Manager.

The role of Customer Services is pivotal:

  • Work with clients to understand their payroll requirements and identify opportunities for process improvement.
  • Design and configure the payroll system based on client requirements.
  • Implement and test payroll system to ensure accuracy and compliance with regulatory requirements.
  • Collaborate with partners and cross-functional teams to ensure successful implementation of the payroll system.
  • Provide training and support to clients on the use of our payroll platform.
  • Stay up to date with industry trends and regulatory changes related to payroll.

Payroll Metrics will provide you with knowledge of its software through training and collaboration activities with Customer Services team members.

We are looking for a candidate with proven experience in implementing/delivering payroll solutions in medium to large sized organisation and who will have:

  • superior payroll and IR knowledge,
  • strong project management skills,
  • the proven ability to quickly learn new payroll software,
  • excellent written and verbal skills
  • strong human relations skills.

The salary for this position will be $90,000+ depending on proven skills and expertise plus SGC at the legislated rate.

Payroll Metrics is located in Oakleigh, Melbourne. Working from home arrangements in all States are accepted for suitable candidates.

Payroll Metrics was formed in 2013 with the objective of providing a payroll solution that is easy to use and which can readily integrate with related applications such as HR, Finance, LMS, banks and gateway suppliers. Today more than 400 organisations are using our software and we are advanced in developing a New Zealand payroll solution.

We care about security and quality. And we work in a blameless environment where everyone is respectful of each other. We are intent on providing high levels of customer satisfaction.

Payroll Metrics is accredited with ISO 27001 and ISO 9001 standards.
