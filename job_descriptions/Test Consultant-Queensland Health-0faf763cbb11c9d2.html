The role: The Test Consultant is responsible for leading systems testing design and improvement methodologies and reviewing technical and business design requirements and solutions. You will design and improve methodologies to ensure systems can be migrated from a development environment through to implementation within quality assurance guidelines.
Is this opportunity for you?To be successful in this role, you will be experienced in the planning of system and acceptance tests and possess a thorough knowledge of testing methodologies, tools, and techniques (including automated testing) applicable to a large scale, complex IM/ICT project environment.
Your proven experience in the implementation of quality testing methods and frameworks to ensure the integrity of testing is essential. Demonstrated analytical, problem solving and decision-making abilities are essential to assess and evaluate testing requirements. within a complex Information Management/ Information and Communication Technology environment. A flexible and adaptable approach is required and the ability to manage competing work priorities to meet deliverables without compromising outcomes is required to secure this role. Demonstrated experience in administration, test design and automation using industry standard tools like TOSCA, Selenium, Cucumber, eggplant.
eHealth Queensland is one of the largest Information Communication Technology operations in the state. It is responsible for ensuring the smooth operation of information systems and technologies so that Queensland Health employees and health providers have access to information that supports the delivery of health care.
The Clinical Program is a commercial business unit within Delivery Services Branch that operates on a fee for service basis. Consultancy services offered to Queensland Health customers include Analysis, Design, Project Management, Development and Testing.
The Clinical Program operates similar to commercial ICT consultancies where consultants move between assignments (and clients) and may be expected to work simultaneously on multiple assignments/projects as they transition between assignments or if an assignment is small in size and complexity. The Clinical Program encourages consultants to develop close working relationships with clients and frequently physically co-locate with clients across Brisbane in order to develop close working relationships.
Benefits of working with eHealth Queensland: By joining eHealth Queensland, you will work within a fast-paced, challenging, and supportive environment where every employee plays a critical role in our ongoing success.

You will enjoy a variety of benefits, including:
  • Competitive salary + generous superannuation and leave loading.
  • Flexible working arrangements
  • Diverse work culture
  • Career training and development
