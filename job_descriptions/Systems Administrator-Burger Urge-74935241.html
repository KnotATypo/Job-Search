BURGER URGE - Systems Administrator

As the Systems Administrator at Burger Urge, you will play a pivotal role in developing and managing our business intelligence functions and operational capabilities. Reporting directly to the General Manager of Corporate Services, you will be responsible for the successful implementation and maintenance of our technical tools and software, including but not limited to POS systems, inventory and labor management software, and file management software.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and manage Burger Urge's business intelligence functions and operational capabilities.
  • Assist with new technology projects, including data warehouse management.
  • Maintain and develop the BU website.
  • Manage store internet connections and the PBX phone system (SD-WAN).
  • Ensure network security and cyber resilience.
  • Maintain the technology infrastructure asset register.
  • Support the IT Junior System Support Analyst in resolving service issues and requests.
  • Setup new accounts for users and staff members.
  • Collaborate with the General Manager and team to resolve IT issues within the store network.

Essential Requirements:

  • Technical proficiency in business intelligence tools and systems, including POS systems.
  • Expertise in website maintenance and development.
  • Understanding of store internet connections and PBX phone systems.
  • Strong problem-solving and troubleshooting skills.
  • Ability to collaborate diplomatically with stakeholders.
  • Attention to detail and organizational skills.
  • Strong communication skills with a customer-focused approach.
  • Eagerness to learn and adapt to new technologies, including familiarity with ticketing and RMM protocols.
