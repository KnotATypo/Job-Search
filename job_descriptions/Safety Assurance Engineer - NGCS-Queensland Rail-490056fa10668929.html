Safety Assurance Engineer - NGCS

Req. Id: 7148
Date: 22 Mar 2024
Location: Brisbane, QLD, AU, 4000
Company: Queensland Rail

Our team of over 7,500 and our customers are central to everything we do, we have a proud history as a safe and reliable railway operator, with a commitment to ensuring that our operating systems reflect the highest standards. Queensland Rail inspires innovative thought, passionate motivation, teamwork, and forward thinking, where our people can achieve their own career aspirations.



Why Queensland Rail?


We will provide you with an exciting and diverse career opportunity to join a company that continues to strive in the delivery of a modern and world-class railway provider, you will be able to lead and apply your innovative thoughts to enhance our rail network. For this role we also offer:


  • Highly competitive salary plus a generous superannuation contribution
  • Opportunity to be part of the planning and preparation for the 2032 Olympics
  • Collaborative and engaged work place and team environment
  • Genuine work life balance
  • Free Citytrain travel to and from work and access to other free or discounted SEQ rail travel
  • Employee Assistance Program (EAP) providing employees with holistic and contemporary health and wellbeing programs.
  • Professional development and leadership opportunities

About this role:



Working within Queensland Rail’s MPI team, the Systems & Safety Assurance Engineer role will see you providing and coordinating expert advice, analysis and assurance to ensure compliant delivery of major projects, such as The Logan to Gold Coast Faster Rail Project. In an ever-growing and exciting area of the business, now is a great time to join QLD rail and really make your mark!


You can expect some of the responsibilities of the role to include:


  • Contribute to the development of requirements and assurance for the acceptance of new assets
  • Review and recommend changes to integrity and assurance process
  • Provide strategic guidance and interpretation of requirements and standards
  • Guide and review systems assurance and integration deliverables
  • Maintain strong stakeholder relationships with internal and external agencies
  • Establish and maintain knowledge of critical aspects within contract packages
  • Ensure successful and compliant Project assurance delivery into Queensland Rail

Some of the skills you will bring to the role:


  • Extensive knowledge of asset acceptance, risk and assurance principles
  • Extensive knowledge of engineering construction and maintenance practices
  • Sound knowledge of the fields of design, asset management and inspection/assessment/auditing
  • High level of skill in performing and documenting risk assessments
