The Opportunity:

Due to continual growth across our business, there are multiple job opportunities for surveyors to work across a variety of commercial, industrial, and residential subdivision projects.

Any given day may see you… Undertake surveying responsibilities with the opportunity to use leading edge equipment and technologies.

  • Conduct set outs as per Area Manager / Foreman instructions.
  • Communicating how survey set out is to be used by construction crews.
  • Liaising with Foreman regarding site activities and ensure required survey information is in place for the next activity.
  • Liaise with the Project Managers and/or Consultants to resolve design conflicts.
  • Maintain digital data and document control as per company standards.
  • Liaising with Area Manager and Project Manager to ensure current revisions are utilised.
  • Conduct site visits to ensure the client is supported by correct technologies.
  • Overseeing equipment maintenance.
  • Conduct Quality Assurance and Quality Control audits.
  • Undertake computations and required checks to create set out data and 3D models.
  • Maintain rework and variation registers and generate monthly progress claims.
  • Training Junior Staff on survey computations and digital data management.
  • Conduct day to day survey resourcing.
  • Create and submit quotes to client.

Here’s what we are looking for… A person with survey experience that is wanting to become part of a new and developing team in emerging markets.

They must:

  • Hold relevant qualifications in either Survey, Spatial Science and/or Geometrics.
  • Demonstrate experience gained across subdivision and land development projects.
  • Demonstrate exposure to using Total Stations is advantageous coupled with experience using GPS equipment and machine guidance technology.
  • Demonstrated ability in proactively building relationships with key stakeholders and team members.
  • Be outcomes focused and have the ability plan and keep organised.
  • Have a customer focused mindset.
  • An attitude that engages all individuals by way of clear communication and effective interaction.

About Survey Management Solutions:

Survey Management Solutions (SMS), a professional survey company, has provided solutions for medium/large infrastructure and land development projects throughout the east coast of Australia for over 15 years.
