The Role:
  • Oversee the operational integrity of our production platform, managing cloud workloads and platform components.
  • Vigilantly monitor the availability of cloud resources and infrastructure.
  • Enhance the quality, reliability, and security posture of all platform-level applications.
  • Continuously measure and optimize performance to meet service objectives.
  • Take proactive measures to minimize incidents and ensure system stability.
  • Thoroughly document manual actions for potential automation and assist teams in establishing Service-Level Objectives (SLOs).
  • Ensure software engineering teams have adequate monitoring and alerting configurations.
  • Implement Chaos Engineering techniques to fortify production systems’ resilience.
  • Champion modern solutions for agile software development and deployment in cloud environments.
  • Provide support to software engineering teams in configuring and maintaining CI/CD and Infrastructure as Code (IaC) processes.
  • Offer a Site Reliability Engineer perspective to inform platform requirements and priorities.
  • Maintain comprehensive documentation for self-service consumption by software engineers.
Experience:
  • Hold a formal degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related discipline.
  • Possess certification in cloud computing platforms, with a preference for Azure.
  • Showcase substantial experience in Site Reliability Engineering roles, particularly focusing on cloud-native software engineering.
  • Demonstrate proficiency with various cloud computing platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, along with their associated services.
  • Exhibit comprehensive knowledge of cloud-native architectures, including microservices, containerization (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes), and serverless computing.
  • Showcase expertise in utilizing third-party monitoring tools such as New Relic, Datadog, and Splunk.
  • Possess strong communication and interpersonal skills, enabling effective collaboration and empathy with software engineering teams.
  • Demonstrate adeptness in analytical thinking and problem-solving, prioritizing scalability, reliability, and high performance.
  • Display familiarity with security and compliance best practices within cloud-based environments.
  • Have proficiency in Linux CLI tools, including Git and Docker.
  • Possess proficiency in scripting languages such as Python, Bash, Ruby, and others.
