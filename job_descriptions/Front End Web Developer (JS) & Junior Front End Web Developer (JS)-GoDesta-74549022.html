About Godesta:

GoDesta helps transport operators in the road freight industry optimise their delivery of services, achieving greater efficiency from their fleets.  An essential part of GoDesta’s mission is to provide a reliable and user-friendly experience allowing staff and subcontractors at transport operators to:

  • experience a shorter learning curve in using our solution;
  • obtain greater insight into their fleet operations; and
  • achieve greater efficiency whilst meeting their commitment to delivering safely.

Your consideration of users’ needs and the application of your skill and effort to provide an excellent user experience and contribute reliable, maintainable code are a core part of GoDesta’s vision.

About you:

Due to aggressive growth, GoDesta is now looking for an experienced front ent web developer and a junior front end developer to join our growing product development team, constructing an industry leading SAAS product servicing the road freight industry.

To be considered for this exciting opportunity, you will be a developer working across all aspects of the development lifecycle, enjoy building quality code and being included in the teams’ decision making process.

Highly regarded / desirable:

  • Experience with mobile app development, including Flutter (helpful but not required).
