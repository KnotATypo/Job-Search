Start your game…
As a Mobile Engineer at Entain, you'll have a pivotal role in defining the technical direction of the new Ladbrokes and Neds apps. Your focus will be on ensuring that the apps meet the highest technical standards and effectively cater to our customer's needs.

In this position, you'll actively contribute to the development of mobile applications by working on core functionality and seamlessly transitioning from react-native to native. Your expertise will be invaluable in writing efficient and maintainable code as we enhance the apps' performance and improve the user experience.

This opportunity is exceptional as it allows you to join us during a significant transformation phase. By helping to shape the future of our mobile apps, you'll have the power to make a genuine impact on the customer experience.


Where and how you can work
This position can be based in various locations including Brisbane, Australia (HQ), Darwin, Sydney, and Melbourne, or can be remote within Australia. At Entain AUS & NZ, we value a flexible work environment and support a hybrid working model, allowing employees to spend two days a week at the office, ensuring work-life balance and individual preferences.
