Full job description

The Company:

Skyaus Infrastructure is one of Australia’s leading telecommunications contracting services companies. We design, build, supply and maintain wireless, and copper networks and products for the region’s leading operators and provide infrastructure solutions for the telecommunications sector working on major 5G and IBC rollouts.

The Role:
If you are a Telecommunications Linesworker (Rigger) with experience in the telecommunications industry, this will be a great challenging opportunity.

Some of your responsibilities will include:

  • specifically identifying and reporting to the Project Managers/Engineers.
  • tower pole and mast installations, maintaining, repairing and analyzing faults of telecommunication systems such as microwave, radio, in built coverage networks, telemetry, multiplexing, satellite, wireless and other communication systems.
  • are team player focused with a positive attitude to quality and HSE;
  • Carry out rigging works in the installation of steelwork on structures, including the requirement to climb poles, rooftops, towers and masts.
  • Arranging and integrating network and telecommunications technology with computer software, hardware, desktops, peripherals, databases and operating systems
  • Carry out microwave installations and commissioning including a comprehensive understanding of the Antenna systems involved, including installation of feeders, antennas, hyperflex cables, RRU’s and ancillary equipment.

The Successful Candidate will have :

Experience in the Telecommunication field and have a solid background as a Telecommunications Linesworker (Rigger) predominately across Mobile Wireless Networks or will be starting out in the telecommunications industry.

  • Possess sound technical knowledge of the relevant aspects of the various networks and the work practices required by the client.
  • Be committed to excellent performance and workmanship.
  • Must be able to work under pressure and meet deadlines, while maintaining a positive attitude and providing exemplary customer service.
  • Ability to work independently and to carry out assignments to completion within parameters of instructions given, prescribed routines, and standard accepted practice.
  • Hold a relevant AQF associate degree, Advanced Diploma or Diploma in a relevant field plus 2 years’ experience or 5 years relevant work experience in the relevant field.

Benefits we offer:

  • Exciting career development opportunities
  • Flexible working hours
  • Great collaborative team
  • Competitive salary

You will be strong and able, be willing to travel and be physically fit to climb and work on telecommunications structures.
