Job Description:

We are currently seeking a talented Multi-Lingual Systems Administrator to join our team and play a pivotal role in the implementation and support of our clients' technical infrastructure. As a Systems Administrator, you will be responsible for ensuring the smooth operation of our clients' systems, from initial setup through ongoing maintenance and support.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Engage in all phases of assigned projects, including architecture, design, and functionality decisions, to ensure alignment with client needs and project objectives.
  • Conduct site assessments and collaborate closely with clients to develop customized strategies tailored to their organization's requirements.
  • Provide recommendations on workflow, applications, interfaces, reports, testing, and uploads, guiding clients through the implementation process.
  • Ensure readiness for system go-live and provide ongoing support post-implementation, working collaboratively with internal teams and clients to ensure customer satisfaction.
  • Deliver on-site technical support to clients, including planning, implementing, and monitoring automated backup systems.
  • Regularly verify backups to maintain data integrity and assess strategies to meet evolving client needs.
  • Administer and maintain user accounts on network servers, implementing preventative measures to mitigate hardware or software failures that could disrupt client operations.
  • Perform security and backup routines to safeguard client data and systems.
  • Administer, maintain, and support customer production, test, training, and pilot/demo servers.
  • Troubleshoot, analyse, and resolve technical infrastructure incidents and complex problems, providing guidance to support teams as needed and escalating issues to third-party vendors.
  • Apply patches for maintenance and security updates on customer servers.
  • Develop and review technical and administrative documentation, including procedures and policies.

Additional Responsibilities:

Customer Service:

  • Ability to establish and maintain strong relationships with a range of multicultural clients.
  • Provide technical support via various channels including face-to-face interactions, phone calls, emails, and chat support.
  • Fluent in verbal and written English, Italian, and Spanish.

Work Location/Availability:

  • Brisbane office-based.
  • Able to work evenings on a shift roster to support after-hours and bilingual customers.

Desired Technical Skills:

  • Proficient in AWS and Azure Cloud Platforms
  • Experience with Microsoft Windows Server, Applications and Hypervisor
  • Experience with Linux server.
  • Experience with VMware server products.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or Australian Computer Society Certification in your field.

  • How many years of experience as a Systems Administrator?
