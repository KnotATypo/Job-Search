The core capability requirements for this role are:

• Conduct analysis of business requirements and evolve those requirements for technical design and implementation of solution components.
• Apply governance processes to ensure the quality assurance of technical products delivered by suppliers.
• Translate business requirements into functional and technical specifications incorporating analysis of maintainability, scalability, security, supportability, usability and other non-functional requirements.
• Define key test scenarios and carry out verification testing to ensure the solution is complete and meets agreed business requirements.
• Provide support to builders and testers to ensure the required solution outcomes are achieved, including assistance with documentation and testing of defect fixes and changes.
• Provide advice on database, desktop, infrastructure (server and operating system), infrastructure (virtualisation and storage), mobility, software and telecommunication solutions, products and services to internal and external stakeholders.
