As Atlassian experts, they understand the power of Atlassian products but don't stop at out of the box services. The Opportunity

This is an opportunity to leverage your technical background in an environment where the focus is on contribution above responding to tickets.

Working closely alongside an experienced and motivating Senior Consultant, you will have the opportunity to develop your career. You will be supported and empowered to experiment and explore while growing your skills and experience. Pathways forward from this role include working on technical aspects of client projects or in consulting and account management.

Collaboration and customer centric teamwork are at the heart of Togetha Groups' culture. Their collaborative culture alongside strong systems and support means this is an opportunity with flexible and remote working options.  With a central office in Canberra, team members also are located remotely with a number of people currently working from NSW and VIC.

The Role

In this role, you will play a key role in managing and configuring Togetha Group's  IT systems, ensuring they operate at peak efficiency.

From patching applications and operating systems to performing scheduled activities based on operating procedures, this is not a hardware application and infrastructure role, it's all about coding and integrations.

Initially with a primarily internal focus, as you get up to speed, this role will see you take on key project work and responsibility for internal automation processes, configuration and server management.
  • Supporting configuration and management of Linux and Windows environments, enhancing system security and efficiency
  • Managing the integration of Atlassian applications with third-party products, creating workflows and innovative solutions
  • Play a key role in the mentorship and development of junior team members, sharing knowledge and fostering a culture of continuous learning and improvement.
  • In time, contribute to the strategic direction of digital offerings, collaborating closely with senior consultants to refine and expand service portfolio.
The Ideal Person
  • You have at least 3 years experience within government, MSPs, banks, telecommunication, consulting or enterprise sector
  • You have a strong background in system administration with a specific focus on managing Linux and windows system
  • Your core strengths include configuring and integrating complex IT systems. Competency in programming or scripting is crucial, especially in languages such as Java, Groovy script or Bash
  • Your expertise is not just technical; you have strong communications and writing skills, with the ability to translate technical documentation in an easily understandable way
  • You're looking for a culture that values innovation, where your ideas and contributions are not only welcomed but essential to the team's success
  • You are curious, love learning and thrive in a collaborative team environment
  • Knowledge of Atlassian products or a security clearance is desirable
You must be an Australian citizen willing to undergo a police check to be successful for this role
