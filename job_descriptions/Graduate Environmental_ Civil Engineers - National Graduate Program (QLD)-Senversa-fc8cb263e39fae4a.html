Full job description

Commit to people, change, and the environment by beginning your career with Senversa!


Senversa Pty Ltd is an Australian employee-owned environmental consulting firm with offices in Melbourne, Geelong, Sydney, Newcastle, Adelaide, Perth, and Brisbane . Our team is committed to delivering high quality environmental advice to our clients and while providing a supportive environment for our staff. Our highly skilled professionals range from civil, environmental and geotechnical engineers, geologists, hydrogeologists, chemists, environmental managers, project managers, remediation technicians, and business support staff.


We have several exciting opportunities for a passionate Graduate and/or young professionals with up to two (2) years’ experience in the environment field, to work across our Brisbane based Contaminated Land and Engineering team. In this full-time role, you will work with, and be supported by a highly qualified team, providing you with exposure to a broad spectrum of interesting projects across a range of industry sectors. The role presents a great opportunity to grow and shape your future with the support of our industry leading staff, who have a proven track record of delivering high quality, strategic and commercially astute professional services to federal, state, and local government entities, together with large multi-national private and public companies.


If you want to unlock your potential in the environmental and sustainability industry, Senversa is the place for you.


Does this sound like you?


  • In your final year of university, or have less than two (2) years’ experience working in an environmental field.
  • Completed a degree in Environmental or Civil Engineering, Environmental Science, Geology, Hydrogeology, Environmental Geochemistry, Climate Change & Sustainability, or a related field.
  • Excellent analytical, research, and problem-solving skills.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Bring a wealth of energy, enthusiasm, and a thirst for knowledge in the workplace.
  • Hold a valid Australian driver’s licence.

At Senversa you will have the opportunity to develop your skills as a consultant, learning from one of the most-experienced and well-revered teams in Brisbane, and gaining valuable knowledge in the environmental and sustainability field. We offer extensive opportunities for Graduate training and development nation-wide, and pride ourselves on promoting a collaborative working environment that truly puts our people and culture first.


YOU are the future leaders of our business and communities. We are dedicated to investing in your development and providing you with the tools and resources necessary for your career to thrive.


If you are interested in these exciting opportunities and meet the above criteria, please submit your CV and cover letter. We look forward to hearing from you.
