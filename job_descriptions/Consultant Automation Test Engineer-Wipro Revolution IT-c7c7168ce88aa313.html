THE ROLE REQUIREMENTS:

We are recruiting for experienced Automation Test Engineers to focus on the delivery of delegated quality engineering solution components. You'll have sound knowledge in one of the following languages: JavaScript, Java, C#, Python or VBScript aswell as being a strong team player! .

You'll have proven software development and testing experience, be able to contribute to automated and manual testing delivery across all test phases and be a testing all-rounder who is conversant with all test phases and test types.

KEY REQUIREMENTS

  • 4+ years working as an Automation Tester / Quality Engineer / 7+ for Senior Automation Engineers
  • Proficiency with a number of different technical quality engineering tools such as: Selenium, Cucumber, Appium, RestAssured,SOAP UI or Postman, SpecFlow, MicroFocus UFT, Tricentis TOSCA etc
  • Experience working with modern DevOPs tools and techniques including Jenkins, AzureDevOps, Docker, Kubernettes, CI/CD. Able to demonstrate proven experience with continuous testing.
  • Decomposing user stories and acceptance criteria into test conditions and cases.
  • Conversant with experience with test case design including coverage analysis techniques such as pair wise analysis.
  • Build an understanding of the product on a technical and functional level, understanding the context of our clients.
  • Develop automation scripts in JavaScript, Java, C#, Python, VBScript or VB.net
  • A consultative approach and be someone who can make a positive client impression
  • Good experience working in Agile environments
  • Hold a bachelor’s degree in Information Technology/Computer Science/Software Engineering or similar discipline is highly regarded
