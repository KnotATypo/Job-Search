Let’s talk about the roles and responsibilities:
Capgemini Engineering is seeking a talented Asset Management Specialist to join our team in Brisbane. As an Asset Management Specialist, you will play a key role in optimizing the lifecycle management of assets for our clients.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Utilize your expertise in asset management to develop and implement strategic asset management plans.
  • Ensure compliance with IS0 55000 Asset Management Series Standards and other relevant regulations.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify opportunities for improvement in asset management processes.
  • Provide technical support and guidance in asset lifecycle management.
  • Deliver insightful reports and presentations on asset performance and optimization strategies.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in a related field.
  • Proven experience in asset management, with a focus on lifecycle management.
  • Familiarity with IS0 55000 Asset Management Series Standards is preferred.
  • Strong analytical skills and attention to detail.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Suite.
