About the Role

Based in our National Service Centre located in South Brisbane, we have an exciting opportunity for a Pronto Xi Financial Consultant to join our team.

The Synergy IT Department is the central point of contact for all IT related incidents, service requests and data management across all subsidiaries of Synergy. The role of the Pronto Xi Financial Consultant is to be the Subject Matter Expert (SME) regarding the AP, AR, Fixed Assets and GL modules in Pronto Xi.

Responsibilities

As a Pronto Xi Financial Consultant, you will be the central point of contact for the finance and migration team regarding the functional of Pronto Xi financial modules such as AP, AR, Fixed Assets and GL. You will work alongside the finance and migration team to implement new processes and procedures along with playing a key role play in Pronto Xi migrations. This role requires a combination of technical Pronto Xi expertise, understanding/development of the business processes and undertaking end user training.
