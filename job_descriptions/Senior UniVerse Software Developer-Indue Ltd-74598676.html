About the Role

We’re seeking a passionate and skilled Senior UniVerse Developer to join our dynamic team and help us shape the future of banking technology. As a key contributor, you’ll play a pivotal role in enhancing and maintaining our core banking system.

Responsibilities for the role include:

  • Create, extend and support business-specific UniVerse software programs that facilitate delivering Indue’s transactional payments processing and settlement services.
  • Understand and implement software enhancements to solve business problems in a highly secure Payments focused environment.
  • Design, implement, and test software designs using the UniVerse platform in an agile scrum team and maintain appropriate documentation.
  • Participate in an after-hours on-call roster to support business critical functions.

About You

You will have all or majority of the following skills and attributes:

  • At least 5 years’ experience with UniVerse coding in the UniBASIC language.
