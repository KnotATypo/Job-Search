Full Stack Developer

The Role

Be part of a small diverse team of developers. Assist with the further expansion and improvement of a newly developed complex web application in the industrial IoT space. This role has many exciting challenges such as the transformation of existing rich desktop applications into Azure backed web applications as well as API development for internal and external applications.

As a full stack developer you will have an opportunity for your ideas to influence the future front & back-end application design.

You'll join a flexible team with a flat hierarchy. The role is open to applicants looking for a balance of remote work and office-based work from our offices in Canningvale, WA or Riverview, QLD.

Key Responsibilities:

  • This role has a strong focus on front-end development. In addition to developing UIs with the ‘Wow’ effect, you will also develop analytical charts using available React charting libraries.
  • Interface and development of RESTful APIs for seamless communication between back- and front-end
  • Interactions with your product owners and the applications team for understanding the needs and requirements of users and tailoring systems to these specifications.

The key things about you:

  • You drive to write high quality code.
  • You take pride in your work.
  • You are a team player.
  • You take security of applications seriously.
  • You document your work.
  • You value feedback from code reviews.

The skills we’re looking for:

  • Extensive experience in React JS, TypeScript and the concepts of state management.
  • Knowledge of Figma and UX.
  • Minimum of 3 years’ experience in commercial projects developing web applications in C#, ASP.NET, MVC & .NET Core, at least .Net 6.
  • Experience in Azure SQL, MSSQL, EFCore/ Dapper Framework.
  • Knowledge of SOLID principles and its usage and implementation.
  • Familiar with Azure DevOps and Git.
  • Experience with and closely follow OWASP-10.

The extras to stand out:

  • Commercial experience with JavaScript chart libraries for visualisations.
  • Familiar with TailwindCSS, Remix or NextJS.
  • Practical knowledge in Azure services such as Azure API Management, Azure B2C, Azure Front Door.
  • Experience working with IoT infrastructure and applications.
