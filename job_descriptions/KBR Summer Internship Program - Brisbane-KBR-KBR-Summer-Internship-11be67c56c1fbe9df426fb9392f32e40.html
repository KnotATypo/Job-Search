Join KBR's Summer Internship Program and embark on a journey to shape the future!

Are you ready to tackle the world's most pressing challenges? Dive into the realm of possibilities with our Summer Internship Program, where you'll take the reins of your career.

At KBR, we're dedicated to delivering sustainable infrastructure projects that enrich the future for all Australians. With over 29,000 employees worldwide, we're pioneers in technological innovation, constantly pushing boundaries to advance science, technology, and engineering solutions.

We work with customers across the nation to deliver sustainable infrastructure projects that enhance the future for all Australians through providing innovative solutions across the transport, rail, water and environment, buildings, defence infrastructure, program management and construction consultancy markets.

Your opportunity to make an impact

As an Intern, you'll gain hands-on experience across a spectrum of projects and disciplines, guided by seasoned professionals who will nurture your growth into a future leader. Our self-guided program empowers you to chart your own course and pursue your passions, complemented by comprehensive training to hone your technical skills.

But it's not all work and no play! Our IMPACT Program offers a blend of social activities and industry events, connecting you with like-minded professionals who share your enthusiasm for the future.

The 12-week Summer Intern program could even pave the way to a Graduate role upon completion of your degree.

Are you the curious and driven individual we're looking for?

To be eligible for our intern program you must be in your penultimate year of study of a bachelor’s degree in the following disciplines:

• Civil Engineering

• Structural Engineering

• Digital Engineering

• Computer Science

• Environmental Engineering

• Electrical Engineering

• Mechanical Engineering

• Process Engineering

• Sustainability

• Project Management

In addition, you will hold either Australian citizenship or permanent residency.
