Laboratory Technician
SUGAR LABORATORY

Gateway Laboratories Pty Ltd is the leading provider of NATA (National Association of Testing Authorities) accredited laboratory and testing services for the Queensland and Australian sugar industries.

The contract positions will be located at our Sugar Laboratory in East Brisbane, during the sugar-crushing season (approximately 6 months from the start of June 2024). The work involves the chemical analysis of sugar and sugar products using instrumental techniques.

Previous laboratory experience is preferred; however, a science/laboratory background is not essential for the right applicant as full training is provided.

We are seeking applicants who possess the following characteristics:

• Chemistry study at a tertiary or TAFE level (including Sugar School)

• A Certificate/Diploma in Laboratory Technology (or equivalent work experience)

• Computer literacy

• Capable of working independently after appropriate training

• Previous experience in the sugar industry would be a distinct advantage

• An appropriate work ethic for a commercial analytical environment
