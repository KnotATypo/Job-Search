About Us

Knight Piésold is a specialised international consulting firm offering engineering and environmental services in Mining, Environment, Hydropower, Water Resources, and Roads & Construction Services. We are proud of the longstanding relationships we have with our clients and delivery of exceptional services on a global scale.

The Role

We are currently seeking an experienced Project Engineer with general civil or geotechnical experience to join our friendly team and be responsible for:

  • planning, scheduling, conducting and delegating engineering and geotechnical investigation work;
  • civil/geotechnical design for bulk earthworks, water dams, tailings dams, heap leach pads, foundations and civil structures;
  • monitoring work for compliance to relevant codes, practices and standards;
  • ensuring effective communication between clients and internal project stakeholders;
  • confirming earthworks are constructed in accordance with the design intent.

Please note this role is based in our Brisbane Office therefore relocation assistance will be offered to the successful candidate.

Our Ideal Candidate

Our ideal candidate will be degree qualified in Engineering with either Geotechnical or Civil specialisations, have worked within the mining, civil or geotechnical industries, and will possess:

  • 5 - 8 years’ experience within a similar role.
  • Demonstrated ability to maintain strong relationships with stakeholders.
  • A high level of attention to detail.
  • A strong ability to prioritise tasks and manage projects effectively.
  • Demonstrated ability to maintain strong relationships with stakeholders.
  • Experience in bulk earthworks construction management.
  • Excellent communication and reporting skills.

What we offer

Knight Piésold offer excellent remuneration packages and a competitive range of benefits including:

  • Unique opportunities to travel that take you on a career enhancing experience and adventure.
  • Generous performance bonus incentive programs.
  • Subsidised gym memberships / fitness classes.
  • Training and development opportunities to develop and strengthen your skills.
  • Referral bonuses; and
  • Early access to long service leave.

If you believe you have most of the skills and experience outlined above, we would love to hear from you! Please submit your resume and cover letter to recruitment.au@knightpiesold.com .
