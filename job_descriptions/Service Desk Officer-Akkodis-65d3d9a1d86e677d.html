About the role

Are you prepared to step into an engaging role that recognizes your expertise and provides opportunities for advancement? We're looking for a skilled and motivated individual to join our Customer Service team as a Service Desk Officer.

Responsibilities

  • Demonstrated track record of delivering exceptional customer service to a diverse clientele.
  • Proficiency with Service Desk software (or equivalent platforms).
  • Familiarity with call management software (or similar systems).
  • Background in offering Level 1 support to customers for Outlook email, laptops, and mobile devices.
  • Advanced communication abilities for effectively addressing customer inquiries.
  • Proficient in problem-solving and troubleshooting methodologies.
  • Strong technical aptitude and ability to thrive within a dynamic team setting.
  • Experience working with Microsoft products.

Desirable Qualifications and Skills

  • Deliver high-quality initial support for customers regarding ICT incidents and requests.
  • Follow defined processes, including escalation procedures, when responding to requests and incidents.
  • Offer constructive feedback on Service Desk processes, procedures, and documentation to enhance service provision.

Personal Qualities

  • Proficient at swiftly adjusting to shifts in technologies and procedures.
  • Dedicated to delivering exceptional customer service.
  • Capable of assimilating and structuring vast amounts of information.
  • Thrives in collaborative team settings.
  • Quick learner with a penchant for productivity.
  • Initiates action and stays self-driven.
  • Possesses analytical and systematic thinking.
  • Embraces constructive criticism for personal growth.
  • Maintains a keen eye for detail.
