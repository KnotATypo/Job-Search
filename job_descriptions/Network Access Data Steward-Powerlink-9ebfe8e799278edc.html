Find a career that matters and lasts


The purpose of the Network Access Data Steward role is to provide timely technical support in the provision of access to Powerlink Networks, supporting real-time network operations. This includes creating and maintaining data within Powerlink’s Operational Systems, Drawings & databases ensuring accurate allocation of nomenclature for high voltage plant and entering customer outage requests.


The role may also include data audits, reporting, project coordination activities, training, cross-training to support other Data Roles when required and procedure/documentation development.


Here's how:


  • Provide Network Access technical support updating policies and procedures, coordinating projects and maintaining relevant data to minimise network down time
  • Monitor and maintain the HV Index and Outage Management System to ensure compliance with relevant Australian Standards
  • Liaise with other Powerlink groups and relevant stakeholders, such as operators, generators, customers and maintenance service providers


Your expertise


  • AQF6 qualifications in Electrical Engineering or equivalent formal qualifications from a recognised tertiary institution
  • Demonstrated ability in the application of specific and critical procedures for Safe Access to High Voltage Electrical Apparatus for the purpose of preparing, writing and authorising of works packages.
  • Demonstrated knowledge of transmission networks, its elements and protection schemes.
  • Extensive experience providing technical support; or working in a Transmission or Distribution associated power system operations services field


We'd also love you to have:


  • Demonstrated ability to plan, prioritise and schedule tasks during periods of high workload to meet customer needs
  • Considerable experience in working with customers to deliver project outcomes.
  • Database Skills MS-SQL, MicroStation CAD Skills, AutoCAD Skills, SAP, Outage management System experience.
