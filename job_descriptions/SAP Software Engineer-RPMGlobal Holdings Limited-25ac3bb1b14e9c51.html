About the Role

A vacancy exists within our software development integration team. We require a software developer with SAP experience combined with enterprise application integration to contribute to our ongoing development of world class software.


This is a permanent full-time role based out of our headquarters in Brisbane CBD.


Key Responsibilities

  • Support RPM’s SAP Integration solutions.
  • Design and create objects / integration flows in SAP CPI and SAP PO.
  • Design and produce high quality XSL Transformation software code for customer specific requirements.
  • Undertake analysis, testing, debugging, configuration and troubleshooting ABAP code and integration framework.
  • Work within the agile project development framework.
  • Document and support RPM's SAP Integration.
  • Meet software quality standards and corporate objectives within a timeline and budget.
    • Contribute and support the implementation and production use of RPM products integrated with SAP.


About You

  • 5+ years working with SAP ECC systems.
  • Demonstrated experience with Enterprise Application Integration combined with SAP.
  • ABAP / PI / CPI integration experience.
  • Appropriately skilled with knowledge and experience in various SAP tools and technologies; ideally exposure to HANA.
  • Extensive design and application development experience in programming environments using SAP technology, including producing accurate and comprehensive technical and design documentation.
  • SAP specific or ERP functional knowledge in the areas of Logistics, Accounting, PM and PS an advantage.
  • Knowledge or strong understanding of object orientated design principles.
  • Relevant tertiary qualification, industry certification or equivalent work experience.
    • Good written and spoken English.


Other valued experience

  • Unit Testing Frameworks
  • Application Lifecycle Management
    • Participation in large scale, complex software projects
