Supervisor (Retaining Walls)


Job no: 493628
Work type: Full time
Location: Brisbane, Gold Coast
Categories: Foreperson / Supervisor

ABOUT US:
Winslow Queensland is part of the Winslow Group and is a renowned Civil Construction business specialising in the design and installation new TMR solutions and subdivisions in Queensland.

Winslow Queensland take pride in the work we do and care about our people. As an expanding business, with an increasing workbook and plenty of exciting projects on the horizon Winslow Queensland is a progressive business that allows its people to flourish and develop. This is a great opportunity in an exciting dynamic workplace.


THE ROLE:
Due to continued growth we are actively recruiting for experienced Supervisor with strong retaining walls experience to join the team on a full-time basis.

This role is responsible for the successful delivery of sub-division & infrastructure projects by ensuring labour, sub-contractors, equipment, and materials are effectively managed.

ABOUT YOU:

  • Experience as a Foreman or Supervisor within the subdivision, roads & Infrastructure space with a 'Wall' specialty'.
  • Experience in working with Government based clients
  • Excellent client relationship management
  • Building rapport with sub-contractors
  • Solid people management skills

Duties will include but are not limited to:

  • Forecasting and programming
  • Procurement and scheduling of labour, subcontractors, equipment, and materials
  • Efficient and economic use of resources
  • Administration including timesheets, delivery dockets and site diary
  • Startups, toolboxes, site inductions and checking tickets
  • Mitigating and reporting incidences
  • Audits and reporting
  • Referring and communicating requirements of DBYD, PMPs, SWMs, and MSDs

For more information please contact: recruitment@winslow.com.au
