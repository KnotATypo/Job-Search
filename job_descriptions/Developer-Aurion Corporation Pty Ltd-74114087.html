Software Developer

At Aurion, we’ve been the trusted people and payroll partner of Australian businesses for over 35 years. We are committed to delivering exceptional people and payroll solutions to make life work better for our customers.

Our powerful, industry-leading people and payroll software supports hundreds of Australian businesses to have simple, effective solutions to meet their unique and complex requirements.

As an Aurion Software Developer, you will be passionate about leveraging technology to deliver tangible business outcomes for our customers. We are looking for our next innovative thinker who is looking to bring the latest development trends and design patterns to deliver elegant people and payroll solutions.

Working as a part of our T50 Development team, you will be working with our Software Operations department to deliver the next version of our industry-leading Aurion people and payroll software.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate as a part of a multi-skilled team to deliver software solutions to the business
  • Deliver high quality working code (frontend and server side) using modern design patterns, techniques and processes in an Agile Development environment
  • Facilitate a Test-Driven development approach across the team
  • Work on all aspects of the software delivery process, from requirements through to design and delivery to implementation and support
  • Coordinate and work closely with all development teams to deliver all stages within the project, from the initial prototype through to production releases
  • Mentor and teach other developers within the team, through code reviews, pair programming and other more formal formats

Skills and experience

  • Preferable development skills in TypeScript, Node.js, Javascript or Python
  • Web technologies and frameworks such as React (preferred), Vue or Svelte
  • Server-side business transaction experience
  • Web Services, including RESTful APIs
  • Experience with Cloud Platforms (AWS preferred)
  • Previous experience of building large, custom-built micro-services based applications
  • Understanding of Agile Development practices, specifically Scrum or Kanban
  • Practical experience with unit testing frameworks and automated testing tools
