Full job description

Planit are world leaders in application quality engineering. We provide solutions that support organisations to deliver high quality systems, applications, and IT architecture. Specialising in digital quality, Planit have a geographic footprint across many continents, with 1700+ permanent employees and have been recognised in Gartner's Magic Quadrant for Application Testing Services.

Planit is now a proud NRI company and part of a global movement to deliver a sustainable and secure future through better Information Technology exchanges. Our team offer expert consultancy, bespoke services, tailored training and unique solutions to complex projects.

About the Role:

As an Automation Quality Engineer, you will consult to our top tier client base, covering all areas of the quality engineering lifecycle in Automation. Knowledge and expertise on all software delivery methodologies and test processes, you will use your experience and capability to support large scale projects on schedule and to the highest quality.

This permanent full-time role is based in Brisbane, Queensland. As an Engineer, you will be expected to be involved in the following activities:

Contribute to the analysis, creation and validation of functional and non-functional requirements for smaller pieces of work within a project phase by identifying technology limitations, deficiencies and root causes of issues in existing systems, processes, procedures and methods. Assists with identifying, reporting and assists mitigating product quality risks.

  • Write, review, configure and maintain software to meet requirements, design and technical specifications, for smaller pieces of work, with oversight and support from seniors.
  • Be familiar with container-based deployments, serverless cloud infrastructure, CI/CD frameworks and infrastructure as code. Can deploy code by utilising existing frameworks by following relevant procedures.
  • Write and maintain documentation for end-user manuals, technical programs and operational procedures for smaller pieces of work, and contribute to release notes and installation plans.
  • Collaborate with developers, BAs and users to understand quality and test requirements, including what to automate and how best to automate.
  • Follow an automation strategy and provide feedback to ensure requirements are measurable and tracked within the automation process.
  • Work under guidance to create, maintain, and execute automation code to meet software release development and project testing requirements. Provide technical support for test automation tools and frameworks. Follow sound coding standards and has good understanding of delivery processes such as version control, code review, and continuous integration (CI).
  • Provide accurate reporting of problems identified during test execution. Can triage if the problem is related to the product under test or an issue with the automation code. Follow up with team members and ensure appropriate resolution to issues.
  • Identify and troubleshoot defects in non-prod and production systems
  • Document and report progress, risks, issues, ROI and defects

EssentialSkills & Experience :

  • Bachelor degree or above in software engineering or a relevant field
  • At least two years of experience in the industry
  • In-depth experience delivering, developing, and executing quality test automation solutions with tools such as Selenium WebDriver, Ranorex, SoapUI, TOSCA, QTP etc.
  • A solid understanding of OOP development practices and demonstrable experience with Java, C# or similar
  • A good understanding of web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript and AJAX
  • proficient in coding in at least one key programming language (C#, Java, JavaScript, Python, Ruby)
  • Demonstrated ability to solve technical problems and implement innovative solutions
  • Excellent relationship building and communication skills

Desirable Skills:

  • Familiar with Web Service, API, Service Virtualisation and Mobile frameworks
  • Practical SQL and database experience
  • Experience with version control systems such as GIT or SVN
  • Exposure to Continuous Integration tools
