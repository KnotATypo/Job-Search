We are looking for a special kind of person - a St Vincent's kind of person. Someone who puts compassion, connection and caring for other people at the heart of everything they do.

As an Allied Health Assistant you will be part of an experienced, engaged and dedicated team that is committed to creating a welcoming, professional and caring environment for the residents of St Vincent's Care Corinda. Our commitment is to listen to each resident, understand who each person is - a unique individual who we respect and treat with kindness and compassion.

Located just 14 km from the CBD in the leafy western suburbs, St Vincent's Care Corinda is a beautiful newly built home. Opened in early 2022, the home delivers outstanding levels of wellbeing and comfort for our residents and a positive uplifting work/life balance for our staff.

Things that work for us:

  • Qualification or Certification IV in Allied Health Assistance or similar
  • Experience in Aged Care
  • Experience in Aged Care Gym supervision
  • Registration with Exercise and Sports Science Australia (ESSA)
  • Basic computer skills
  • Strong attention to detail and excellent communication and customer service skills
  • Ability to follow directives as per care plan
  • Understanding of Aged Care and resident rights and responsibilities
  • Current flu and COVID-19 vaccination and National Police Certificate (within the last 6 months)
  • NDIS Worker Screening Check may be a requirement of this role
