The Opportunity

The Digital Architect & Design team at Ramsay Health Care are seeking enthusiastic and dynamic Appian Developers!

Appian Developers are responsible for the development of high-quality software that brings value to Ramsay Health Care (RHC).

Two full-time equivalent roles available!

Key Role Accountabilities

  • Work closely with other departments to develop and implement solutions and applications on the Appian platform
  • Provide key technical expertise across multiple highly collaborative agile delivery teams who work closely with our business stakeholders
  • Adhere to best practices and standard operating procedures for development on the Appian platform
  • Facilitate the capture and documentation of business requirements to technical requirements
  • Analyse and fix defects
  • Be inquisitive, and not afraid to challenge the status Quo

Key Skills and Experience

  • Minimum of two (2) years’ experience developing Appian
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and talent for identifying creative solutions
  • Exposure to database development (e.g. MySQL, SQL Server, MariaDB)
  • Experience in requirements gathering, requirements analysis, business process analysis, and data modelling
  • Mindset and proven ability to continuously learn (and rapidly apply) new technology methods and techniques
  • Experience within an agile business and project delivery environment
  • Strong communication and stakeholder management skills
  • A focus on human centered design

Highly regarded, though not essential

  • Experience in healthcare related environment
  • Proficiency with industry standard tools, such as Jira and Confluence
  • Knowledge of Robotic Process Automation systems (UI Path, Blue Prism etc)
  • Experience with automated testing systems and automated CI/CD deployment processes
