Full job description

Are you a highly skilled and experienced Senior Civil Engineer, Civil Engineer or Site Engineer looking for a new challenge? On the back of recent successes and a strong pipeline of work, we have multiple Civil Engineering roles currently available.

Inertia is an engineering consultancy specialising in delivering civil, structural, and water engineering solutions for clients within the built environment. We have developed an enviable reputation for delivering high-quality, innovative solutions and are known for our multidisciplinary approach that delivers exceptional outcomes for our clients.

On Offer

  • A competitive salary package offered to reflect experience and skill.
  • Diverse projects across Urban Development, Land subdivisions, Roads, and Infrastructure markets.
  • Opportunity to foster existing and develop new relationships in a client-focused orientated business.
  • An excellent values-driven company culture based on mutual respect and enjoyment.
  • Central, modern Brisbane location with river views close to public transport options.

Requirements

  • Relevant tertiary qualifications or equivalent
  • Industry accreditation/membership (RPEQ/CPENG status) desirable.
  • Demonstrated experience in civil engineering or construction environment including site experience, preferably in Urban development, Land subdivisions and Infrastructure.
  • Previous contract administration experience desirable.
  • Knowledge of and ability to apply engineering principles to comply with scope of works and technical criteria.
  • Demonstrated ability of reviewing project technical work, providing direction and guidance to project personnel and delivering results.
  • Experience in interacting with clients and project teams, agreeing on deliverables, and managing resources to deliver projects.
  • Well-developed oral and written communication skills.

You:

  • Like working in an energetic and fun culture, while being committed to excellent project outcomes.
  • Want to work on interesting projects that will challenge your skills and creativity.
  • Have technical and mentoring skills to assist and develop Civil Engineering personnel.
  • Have the ability to delegate tasks and ensure projects are delivered on time and on budget.
  • Have a result-orientated approach and can effectively communicate within a team and with our partners.
  • Have full working rights to live and work in Australia.

Us:

We will:
  • Recognise your efforts and celebrate your achievements.
  • Provide you with opportunities to use your creativity, experience and skills to deliver outstanding projects and succeed as a professional.
  • Support your desire to innovate, challenge the norm and push boundaries by enabling access to resources to enhance your knowledge and support your growth.
  • Foster a culture where teamwork is championed, collaboration is valued and balance is achieved by enjoying what we do and who we are.

For any questions relating to this role or other career opportunities with Inertia please contact recruitment@inertiaeng.com.au or click apply. Your application will be treated as strictly confidential.

Only Candidates demonstrating all of the above skills & attributes will be considered and only short-listed candidates will be contacted.
