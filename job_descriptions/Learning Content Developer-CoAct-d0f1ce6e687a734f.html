Full job description

Introduction:
Embark on this exhilarating 12-month contract and become the powerhouse behind our e-learning, online training, and virtual sessions. If you're seeking a role where you can harness technology as your greatest ally, unleash your creativity, and let your imagination guide you, then this opportunity is tailor-made for you!

Experience autonomy and the thrill of working from home. Join us on this exhilarating journey!

CoAct is a non-profit organisation dedicated to empowering everyday Australians through essential employment services and community activation programs. Our team shares a common passion for making a positive impact and giving back to society.

Description:
What Awaits You…

The Learning Content Developer will craft captivating and dynamic learning materials that adhere to our Learning & Development framework. Collaborating closely with the Learning Experience Designer and subject matter experts, they'll ensure the quality and efficacy of the learning content.

Here's what you'll be diving into every day, but limited to:
  • Develop a variety of learning materials, from presentations to interactive modules
  • Collaborate with subject matter experts to validate content accuracy
  • Ensure learning materials are accessible and inclusive for all learners
  • Update existing materials as needed, ensuring version control
  • Utilize e-learning authoring tools and platforms effectively
  • Adapt content to suit different delivery methods and formats
  • Stay updated on new tools and technologies in the learning industry
  • Ensure learning materials align with the specific needs of the employment services sector
Skills and Experiences:
Key Competencies for Excellence

  • Innovative thinker with a knack for solving problems and a fervor for learning
  • Exceptional organizational and time management abilities
  • Proficient at working both independently and collaboratively in a dynamic setting Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal
  • Dedicated to ongoing improvement and personal development
Expertise & Qualifications

  • Proven track record in content development and instructional writing
  • Background in operational employment services or leadership roles is preferred
  • Proficient in multimedia authoring tools and e-learning platforms
  • Exceptional attention to detail and quality assurance skills
  • Strong collaborative abilities with cross-functional teams
  • Bachelor's degree in Education, Instructional Design, or a related field
