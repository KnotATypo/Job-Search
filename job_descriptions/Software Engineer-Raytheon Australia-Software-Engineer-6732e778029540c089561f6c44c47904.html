The role:

  • Developing, problem solving, testing and supporting software capabilities and services within the Mobile Threat Training Emitter System (MTTES) program. This includes collaborating with OEM software developers.
  • MTTES provides Electronic Warfare (EW) Training Range services to the ADF in the Western Training Area (QLD), Delamere Air Weapons Range (NT), and other deployed locations.
  • You will also provide technical expertise, and complete assigned tasks to support operations and help ensure capability is maintained and delivered in line with program objectives.
  • This role requires regular travel to Amberley, Toowoomba, Delamere NT and various other locations to support operations and maintenance activities.
  • Working from the Raytheon Australia office in Springfield QLD

  • Strong knowledge of modern programming languages (Python/C#/Powershell scripts).
  • Client side GUI development on Windows.
  • Experience using distributed version control (Git) in team environments.
  • Experience with black-box testing, analysis and documentation of 3rd party software applications.
  • Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines.
