Hardware Engineer

L3Harris Space and Airborne Systems Australia

Eight Mile Plains


About this opportunity and L3Harris

Are you looking for an opportunity to grow and enhance your skills as a Hardware Engineer in a dynamic industry that is constantly developing and delivering innovative, next-generation capabilities?


What the role will involve

The responsibilities of your role will include the following:

  • Develop and prototyping of new power and control circuits, including programmable logic devices, or design modification of existing products
  • Programming and simulation of logic circuitry in VHDL using Xilinx
  • Develop software for microprocessor-based circuits, including serial communications interfaces
  • Generate DC schematic diagrams and lay out DC PCBs using Altium
  • Creation of DC power budgets to track power consumption
  • Documentation of product design either directly or through supervision of contractors or drawing office staff as appropriate
  • Perform stress analysis and reliability predictions
  • Qualification testing of new products, including EMI/EMC, etc
  • Ability to deal with customers and present design details during customer reviews
  • Review project schedules and meet financial and time constraints
  • Participate in R&D meetings to evaluate new technical opportunities and contribute to company strategic direction

About Us

L3Harris Space and Airborne Systems Australia is a Brisbane based subsidiary of L3Harris and part of our ICI Division. We provide design, manufacture, integration and sustainment of advanced electronic warfare components and subsystems using cutting edge wideband radio frequency and photonic technology. We are a trusted, sovereign design and production partner to the Australian Department of Defence and an established exporter to global defence primes.
