About the role

As a Product Owner for Global Payments, focus ing on our API product stream , you will be the voice of the customer . Reporting to the Head of Product y ou will be responsible for ensuring that the products and associated features being created by the development teams align to customer and business requirements.

Working within the existing Agile framework of Large Scale Scrum ( LeSS ), you will promote and enable continuous improvement in high quality products and services while collaborating with key stakeholders to ensure the technical teams have all the knowledge and support required .

Other key areas of responsibility will include:

  • Manag ing the product backlog, defin e user stories and work with the development team and key stakeholders to build the best solutions to deliver customer aligned outcomes .

  • Breakdown problems and needs into Epics and user stories with clearly defined acceptance criteria;

  • Understanding the problem and needs of the customers and stakeholders, and communicating this to the development teams

  • Gather ing feature requests and coordinate work throughout sprints;

  • Work ing with the Product Management team to develop the product roadmap;

  • Work ing closely with P roduct M anagement and senior management in order to create a product that fits he company portfolio;

  • Understand ing and be up-to-date with industry trends and market dynamics.
