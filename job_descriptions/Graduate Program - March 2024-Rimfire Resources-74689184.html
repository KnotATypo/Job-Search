The Company

The Queensland Rural and Industry Development Authority (QRIDA) is a specialist provider of government grants, loans, advisory support and farm debt services to rural and regional Queensland. They have been helping primary producers make a start in agriculture, improve sustainability and profitability, recover from natural disaster and other challenges for more than 25 years.

As the Queensland Government’s experts in rural finance, they manage a host of programs to support the agricultural sector, but increasingly are also doing more in many other sectors. QRIDA’s vision is to foster growth, sustainability and economic development in rural and regional communities by providing proactive, responsible and reliable financial services and assistance. There are five values and behaviours that underpin their culture:
  • Customer first
  • Ideas into action
  • Unleash potential
  • Be courageous
  • Empower people
The Program

As a graduate on QRIDA’s Graduate Connect Program, you will work on projects that will help develop your skills and capabilities, including rotations to other areas of the department. The 2-year program will support your transition from study to full-time employment. You will be supported by a mentor and a supervisor to ensure your time in the program is a success and participate as a member of a team that is making a difference for the people of rural and regional Queensland.

The Program is designed to:
  • Develop leadership behaviours
  • Gain insight into issues by analysing available information in a critical manner
  • Share and seek creative ideas
  • Take initiative to collaborate with people from a diverse range of backgrounds, experiences, and expertise
  • Contribute to the development of team objectives
  • Take accountability for learning and pursue new knowledge and experiences
  • Actively seek feedback and modifies approach to enhance own effectiveness
The Person

We are keen to hear from you if you possess the following skills, experience and key attributes:
  • Qualifications from a recognised tertiary institution in Policy, Business, Commerce, Economics, Data Science, Science (Agriculture/Horticulture/Marine/Environment/Ecology/Animal/Chemical Engineering/Botany)
  • Graduated between 2022 and before February 2024
  • Hold Australian citizenship or reside in Australia and have permission to work in Australia under Commonwealth law
  • Excellent time management skills with the ability to prioritise
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Work effectively both autonomously and within a team
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office
