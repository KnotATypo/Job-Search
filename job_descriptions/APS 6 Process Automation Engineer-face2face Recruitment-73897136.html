About the role:

As a Process Automation Engineer, your essential role involves designing, implementing, and maintaining Process Automation solutions across the IT Engineering Sys and Ops Section and the wider organisation.

This encompasses the identification of repetitive tasks, the creation of scripts or workflows for their automation, the integration of different systems and tools to improve efficiency, and the ongoing enhancement of automation processes to minimize manual intervention, reduce errors, and enhance overall productivity. Additionally, the IT Process Automation Engineer will be pivotal in identifying opportunities for process improvement, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and ensuring that automated solutions align with business objectives and adhere to IT best practices.


Duties:

  • Work closely with the teams within the IT Engineering Sys and Ops Section to understand their business requirements and design customised solutions.
  • Develop and implement solutions that extend and enhance the functionality of Azure and ServiceNow.
  • Provide technical guidance on best practices and recommend optimal solutions.
  • Prepare and maintain comprehensive technical documentation for solutions implemented.
  • Contribute to knowledge-sharing initiatives within the team.
