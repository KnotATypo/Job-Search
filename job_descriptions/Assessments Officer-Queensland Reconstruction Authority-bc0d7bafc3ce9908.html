Full job description

Your contribution to this role will be:
  • Liaise with local government applicants to facilitate submissions under the Disaster Recovery Funding (DRFA) arrangements and other forms of funding, including registration of proposal/funding information through QRA's online portal.
  • Facilitate, collate, and support direct submission and registration of proposal information provided on behalf of local government and other applicants.
  • Support local government and other applicants seeking further information on DRFA funding program, including liaising with Regional Liaison Officers or Operations' assessments teams as appropriate.
  • Develop and maintain collaborative and productive relationships with internal and external stakeholders particularly local government applicants and delivery agencies, and other business areas of QRA.
  • Work with and across divisions of QRA to ensure the timely and successful assessment of submissions.
  • Format, coordinate and finalise briefing papers, correspondence, reports and other information in compliance with the QRA templates and requirements.
  • Provide operational support with travel bookings, correspondence, onboarding and report preparation to support administrative functions across the division, including mail merge.
  • Provide feedback and updates to Managers, Team Leaders, Senior Assessors and Assessors on the collation of assessment materials.
  • Ensure that the evidence, research and administrative documentation required for assessment, program performance or inspections proposals is accurate, complete and ready to enter the assessment process.
  • Liaise with applicants or delivery agents to obtain additional information for assessment where administrative documentation is missing, inconsistent or incomplete.
  • Provide feedback and updates on the use of the registration portal, including identifying emerging trends and escalating more complex issues.
To find out more about QRA visit our website, or reach out to the contact officer for more information about this role.
