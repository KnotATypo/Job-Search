Full job description

We’re inventing the future, right here, right now, at Thales. We design the critical security solutions of tomorrow by combining the curiosity to explore, the intelligence to question and the vision to create. Together we solve complicated problems by combining our experience in the market with our leading research and development capabilities.

Thales Graduate Program

Our program focuses on developing talented graduates to help us deliver tomorrows high-tech solutions. Over the 18 months you will engage in on-the-job training, mentoring, a graduate project and structured learning modules.

There will be opportunities not only to become an expert in your discipline but also learn from the wider Thales business, gaining valuable exposure to a wide range of Thales leading solutions.

Our Team

Our Protected Vehicles team works closely with clients to design, manufacture, maintain and upgrade protected vehicles that keep people safe, all around the world. Our state-of-the-art workshops are set up to help us deliver Hawkei and Bushmaster military vehicles to strict defence standards – and set our people on rewarding career paths.

Your Role

Based in Eaglefarm, Brisbane on Jagera and Turrbul land, you can expect to rotate between multiple departments. On any one day you might

  • Help to design, develop and validate components and systems for our defence vehicles

  • Interpret our customer requirements and provide design solutions including engineering analysis and investigation

  • Undertake learning and training activities to develop skills and experience as a Graduate Engineer

After experiencing multiple departments, we aim to find you a long-term role that suits your interests and experience.

Your Experience

What we value most from our graduates is a willingness to learn and the confidence to offer solutions, however there are a few other things you need to consider:

  • Ideally degree qualified in Mechatronics or Electrical Engineering, however we would consider Automotive or Aerospace

  • A true desire to work in a diverse team towards a common goal

  • Leadership experience demonstrated through previous employment or extra-curricular activities whilst studying university
