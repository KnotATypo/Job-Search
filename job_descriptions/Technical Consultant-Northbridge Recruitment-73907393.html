Exciting Opportunity for a Technical Consultant

About the Role: As a Technical Consultant, you'll play a crucial role in the analysis and oversight of various program scope items, including the integration of a Finance sub-ledger function, meeting security requirements, implementing a Reporting solution, and providing general technical and architectural assistance across multiple activities.
