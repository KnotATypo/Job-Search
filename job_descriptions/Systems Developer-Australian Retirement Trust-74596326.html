About the role

Every one of our team contributes to helping make our members’ world better.

As a Systems Developer, you will be responsible for the development, configuration and support of Australian Retirement Trust’s application solutions.

Key accountabilities will include:

  • Development and delivery of solutions in support of Australian Retirement Trust’s strategy.
  • Maintain application support processes, and check that all requests for support are dealt with according to agreed procedures.
  • Develop, document and implement changes based on requests for change.

For a full list of key accountabilities, please refer to the position description.

This role is available as a full-time, permanent opportunity.

About you

If this is the right role for you, your skills and qualifications may include:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering or equivalent.
  • Working knowledge of Relational Databases and query languages (SQL).
  • Experience developing within Azure Cloud environment.
  • Demonstrated knowledge and experience in version control tools/concepts and automated builds with (GIT) being the preferred tool (supplemented with SourceTree, Azure Repos andAzure Pipelines).
  • Knowledge of Solution Delivery Lifecycle methodologies (Waterfall & Agile), Release Coordination and quality assurance processes.
  • Experience developing in the .NET framework using C# and Web API’s.
