About CPM
CPM Advisory & Project Management (CPM) is a reputable consultancy dedicated to delivering innovative solutions and exceptional results to client organisations across Australia.

We achieve success in delivering value across the entire project lifecycle to governments, asset owners and project owners in the infrastructure and building sectors. We provide strategic advisory, transaction management, design management, construction management and project management services of the highest calibre.

Our culture is our number one asset and attracts the industry’s best to join our team. We are an experienced group of down-to-earth professionals achieving amazing results in our professional and personal endeavours.

Our culture is underpinned by our values and these are the DNA of the company guiding us daily; People First , Adaptability & Technical Excellence , Collaboration and Positive Legacy .

About the Role
You will be joining a close-knit talent acquisition team and reporting to the Business Leader (Talent). Your key function will be to represent the CPM brand to the talent market with the intent of recruiting the industry’s finest.

This will involve the following key tasks:
  • Writing and posting advertisements
  • Database management & candidate communications (JobAdder)
  • Developing and maintaining talent pipelines & talent pools.
  • Managing & coordinating the screening, interviews, shortlisting, referencing and offer process.
  • Utilising systems to enhance the candidate experience.
  • Ensuring hiring managers are kept informed through regular updates, reports and attendance at resourcing meetings.
This role will be focused on sourcing quality candidates for specific roles varying from senior level engineering/construction/project management professionals to project administration & support staff to health/safety/environmental specialists.

Key to your success will be your ability to demonstrate your credibility and to build lasting relationships with all stakeholders across the business and project.

Skills | Experience | Qualifications
  • Ideally possess a mix of internal and external agency recruitment experience specialising in within engineering, construction, project management or similar industries.
  • Exceptional written and verbal communication skills.
  • Be process driven and take pride in following the recruitment process from start to finish.
  • Strong desire to achieve beneficial outcomes for all stakeholders.
  • You are a proven performer with a nous for recruitment.
  • Be a people person that enjoys networking with internal stakeholders and candidates.
  • Experience in delivering recruitment results ideally, in both a reactive and project environment.
