Overview

The 3D Generalist will contribute to the artistic vision by building and texturing 3D film & TV assets, such as environments, objects and characters. Candidates must possess the ability to create and finish appealing 3D models, across a broad range of needs.

You are a professional artist who works collaboratively as a part of a team and across departments. You are a forward thinker with excellent time-management, who delivers their work on schedule and to brief. Your portfolio should demonstrate an ability to model, texture, light and finish CG elements for film & tv; both of original design and based on a real-world object or reference.

We invite both generalists and specialists to apply who have a strong combination of creative and technical skills across a variety of disciplines.

These include but are not limited to:

  • Houdini FX (incl. pyro, water, destruction)
  • Modelling (incl. both hard & soft surfaces)
  • Animation (incl. creature, character and object)
  • Lighting & Rendering
  • Pipeline & Technical Direction

Applicants presenting photo realistic work in their portfolio, will have their application reviewed with priority.

Responsibilities

  • Alongside VFX Supervisor, evaluates creative and technical approach for assigned shots
  • An understanding of and experience with 3D pipeline for film & television.
  • The ability to adapt artistic style to match concept art or vision.
  • Working with and supporting other members of the 3D department towards a common creative vision
  • Conversion and interpretation of 2D concepts into textured 3d models
  • Review, QC & tech check your work for internal and external delivery, ensuring it addresses creative and technical requirements.
  • Ability to work collaboratively with artists from other disciplines.
  • Contribute to creative and technical conversations, with a view to raising the quality level of the final image.
  • Manage your workload. Advise your Supervisor and other key stakeholders of any hurdles which may impact delivery quality, timeliness or budget.
  • Coach and mentor other less experienced 3D Artists using the latest techniques to grow their craft and the quality of work in the studio as a whole
  • Promote a collaborative and enjoyable workplace.

Required Skills + Experience

  • Minimum 2+ years creating CG assets for live-action film & television projects.
  • Create CG lighting and assets for the integration of CG elements into live action
  • Create CG lighting and assets for full CG shots
  • Study real world colour and light direction and replicate that look in CG
  • Technical and creative problem solving with a can-do mindset.
  • Critical eye and solid understanding of photo-realism in terms camera, lighting, composition, perspective, motion and colour
  • A proficiency in 3D multi-pass rendering techniques
  • A team oriented mentality with a 'get it done' attitude
  • An ability to thrive in a high-pressure environment
  • The ability to be adaptable, flexible and proactive when it comes to troubleshooting
  • Working knowledge of the VFX pipeline and entire post-production process, including interdepartmental dependencies and hand over processes.

Preferred Skills + Competencies

  • Demonstrated animation experience is highly preferred.
  • Familiarity with rigging and skinning is looked favourably upon but not required.
  • Additional art skills such as drawing is preferred, but non-essential.
  • Utilitarian knowledge of other discipline software such as After Effects or Nuke is preferred but not required.
  • Knowledge and experience in industry-standard tracking software is looked favourably upon but not required.
  • Python experience is preferred but not required.
  • Ideal candidate has strong fine arts foundation skills, such as photography and photographic lighting. A Bachelor degree in fine arts, film or computer science is looked favourably upon but not required.
