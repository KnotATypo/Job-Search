About the Company

Our client is a Queensland based company who support some of the state's largest industries and employers. With deep roots in regional Queensland, they contribute to local communities through annual sponsorship and donation programs, as well as supporting local suppliers.


About the Role

The UC Network Engineer is responsible for the design and governance of architecture relating to Network & Unified Communications infrastructure platforms including virtualised, physical, and cloud Networks, and Unified Comms for IT and OT where needed.


Key Responsibilities

  • Design, implement, and maintain network environments for cloud and datacentre solutions, ensuring optimal performance and reliability.
  • Provide expert support for Cisco networks, Call Manager, Aruba Wireless, and Palo Alto Firewalls, ensuring seamless operation and security.
  • Demonstrate a proven track record of adding value to ICT environments through innovative solutions and efficient problem-solving.
  • Support Microsoft Teams voice and video functionalities, including Logitech Team devices, to facilitate effective communication and collaboration.
  • Manage telephony systems for 4G/5G networks and Call Recording Platforms to ensure reliable communication services.
  • Oversee Video Conferencing Platforms, CCTV, Door Security Platforms, and implement necessary upgrades or improvements to enhance security and efficiency.
  • Implement and maintain Alerting & Monitoring Systems to proactively identify and resolve network issues, ensuring uninterrupted operations.
