About the role
McConnell Dowell has an exciting opportunity for a Site Engineer to join our project team at our Aura & Harmony trunk infrastructure project on the Sunshine Coast. This role plays a crucial part in the successful operation of a project that is delivering critical new water and wastewater infrastructure to support the growth of Queensland's Sunshine Coast.

If you’re seeking a Site Engineering role on a project that values work/life balance and operates on a Monday – Friday work cycle this role would be ideal for you.

As the Site Engineer, your responsibilities will include:
  • Coordinating with Managers and Superintendents on Engineering, temporary works, scheduling and status of the project;
  • Providing overall progress reports and other reports related to schedule and cost as required by the Project Manager;
  • Raising eMPR’s and AFE’s for all responsible expenditure prior to committing. Raise internal variations for approval for all contracted works prior to works commencing. Review subcontractor progress claims to verify works completed;
  • Provide general technical assistance to supervisory personnel;
  • Prepare reports on progress, manpower and costs and coordinate the work scheduling and procurement.
About you
  • Tertiary qualification in Engineering;
  • Previous experience on pipelines or civil works highly regarded.
  • Competent in planning and organising work schedules and a demonstrated ability to deliver to timelines;
  • Sound working knowledge of HS&E requirements and implementation of same.
  • Strong and clear communication skills with proven ability to engage others.
  • Have strong commitment to the safety promise of “Home Without Harm” .
