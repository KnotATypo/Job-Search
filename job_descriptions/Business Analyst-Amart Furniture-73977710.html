An opportunity is available for an up and coming Business Analyst ready to take on the next challenge in their career and be part of a company-wide technical transformation.

This role works across the entire Amart business working collaboratively to understand the technology needs of Amart’s growing and complex business. Your skills in listening, documenting and developing requirements for large projects, software development initiatives and solving day to day problems will be a key requirement for success in this role. This role will support small works projects and larger projects along with supporting BAU as required from time to time.

Responsibilities include:

  • Mapping of current and future state business processes (As-Is and To-Be)
  • Evaluate business process requirements gathered from multiple sources
  • Decompose high-level requirements into detailed user journeys /requirements
  • Work with developers and Solution Architects to ensure a common understanding on business requirements and scope of the work
  • Providing testing services
  • Assisting end users with handover of technical changes or new systems

About you:

  • Degree in IT, Computer Science, Data Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
  • Experience as a BA with a technical focus across BAU and projects
  • Understanding of project management methodologies (certification advantageous)
  • Impressive interpersonal skills to work with and contribute to a team environment
  • Excellent engagement, listening and communication (written and verbal, formal documentation skills
  • Excellent stakeholder management skills and ability to determine requirements of non-technical business stakeholders
  • Knowledge or experience working with software development, retail systems, ERPs and integration platforms would be highly regarded
