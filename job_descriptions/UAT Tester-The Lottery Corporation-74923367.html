Two UAT Testers are required to manually test the new retail terminals that will be rolled out across our retailer network. This is a Brisbane based, 3+ month day rate contract role on assignment with The Lottery Corporation via Hudson RPO.

What you’ll do

  • Review existing process documentation and training to create testing scenarios
  • Test functionality of the terminal
  • Document outcomes of testing
  • Liaise with selected retailers to provide testing scenarios, collate feedback and provide support
  • Work with technology testers and Channel business owner to raise defects
  • Retest defects
  • Engage with Channel team members to support business testing as requested by the business owner

What you’ll bring

  • Retail experience
  • Lotteries sales experience
  • Experience using Jira and Confluence
  • Attention to detail
  • Ability to work in a team
  • Time management skills
  • High level of computer literacy
  • Good written communication
