About the role and you:

As a Project Engineer at Sequana Partners, you will play a crucial role in our organisation's mission to deliver cutting-edge solutions in the water industry. Your experience and expertise will contribute to the successful execution of large-scale water treatment and desalination projects, from initial design and construction through to commissioning. This position offers an opportunity to contribute to the development of innovative water infrastructure solutions, making a tangible impact on communities and the environment .

What you'll do:

Project Planning and Coordination:

  • Develop project plans, including scope, objectives, deliverables, timelines, and resources required.
  • Coordinate with stakeholders to define project requirements and expectations.

Design and Development:

  • Work closely with design teams to create detailed engineering drawings, specifications, and technical documentation.
  • Evaluate and select appropriate materials, components, and technologies for project implementation.
  • Ensure designs adhere to industry standards, regulatory requirements, and client specifications

Risk Management and Quality Control:

  • Contribute to Risk Management by identifying potential risks, developing mitigation strategies, monitoring project risks and taking proactive measures to address them.
  • Implement quality control procedures to ensure that project deliverables meet the highest standards of technical excellence, safety, and sustainability.

Relationship Management and Team Focus:

  • Collaborate with clients, contractors, regulatory bodies, and other relevant stakeholders to ensure effective communication, address concerns, and maintain positive relationships throughout the project lifecycle.
  • Fostering a collaborative, innovative and enjoyable work environment while helping motivate fellow team members to achieve project objectives.

Technical Delivery:

  • Ensure comprehensive documentation of technical deliverables, feasibility studies, and engineering designs and ensure the quality and accuracy of this documentation.

Industry Knowledge:

  • Stay abreast of industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices, and contribute to the continuous improvement of project methodologies, tools, and processes.

Experience you'll need:

  • A Bachelor's degree or similar relevant qualification in Engineering or a related field is preferred.
  • Minimum of 3 years of relevant water industry experience, with a focus on water treatment including desalination.
  • Proven project delivery skills, with a demonstrated track record of successfully contributing to complex infrastructure projects on time and within budget.
  • Knowledge and understanding of water treatment processes including desalination technologies, design principles, construction methodologies, and commissioning processes.
  • Experience in all phases of project delivery, from initial concept development to operational handover, with a strong appreciation for regulatory requirements and compliance.
  • Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively engage with diverse stakeholders, both internal and external.
