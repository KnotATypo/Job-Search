Auto – Field Service Officer

Bowen Hills, Brisbane Qld

SITECH Construction Systems, is the Trimble Heavy Construction distributors for Eastern and Central Australia. Trimble offers a range of machine control technology systems to suit graders, excavators, dozers, scrapers, compactors and more.

We are now seeking to recruit a career minded person for a position based in our Brisbane office with the opportunity of some remote and local travel.

Exhibiting a strong commitment to safety, and after undergoing an extensive training program, you will take responsibility for the entire installation process of Trimble Machine Control technology on customer machinery including quality assurance of products, calibration, and commissioning.

This position will be a great fit for candidates currently with an interest or experience in auto related trades looking for a long-term career where they can build on their existing skills in a role working with cutting edge machine control technology.

To be considered for this role you will be able to demonstrate the following:

  • Current unrestricted drivers’ license – essential
  • Problem solving skills
  • Customer service focus
  • Trade qualification or on the job experience an advantage but not essential

The successful candidate will be offered an attractive salary package including generous incentives, a company vehicle and mobile phone. As a SITECH Construction Systems team member you will enjoy a focused and supportive work environment where your input will be valued and rewarded.

Thanks for taking the time to read our ad and we look forward to hearing from you.
