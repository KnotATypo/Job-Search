Who are we?

Wabtec Corporation is a leading global provider of equipment, systems, digital solutions, and value-added services for freight and transit rail as well as the mining, marine, and industrial markets. Drawing on nearly four centuries of collective experience across Wabtec, GE Transportation, and Faiveley Transport, the company has grown to become One Wabtec, with unmatched digital expertise, technological innovation, and world-class manufacturing and services, enabling the digital-rail-and-transit ecosystems. Who will you be working with? Reporting to the Director Product & Design; you will lead interface design for a major industrial multimillion dollar enterprise product. In this role, you will create useful and desirable software products and service experiences for the industrial internet of things.

You’ll be closely partnered with a Product Manager and an Engineering Tech Lead to scope, plan, and execute on the team’s roadmap. In addition to working with assigned Product Team, you’ll be part of the larger Design Team where you’ll have the opportunity to collaborate, solicit critique, and grow alongside other designers and researchers.

Where is the position located? This is full time position at our office in Eagle farm, Brisbane

Job Responsibilities

  • Shape and influence enterprise applications that impact the world. Our applications are used by the rail and mining industry around the globe to efficiently move goods and people.
  • You will simplify complex functionality into easy to digest, fluid experiences.
  • Develop a keen understanding of our potential users and their needs, in partnership with your cross-functional team.
  • Partner with our Research and insights team to use qualitative and quantitative data to inform the experience across the customer’s journey.

Required Experience & Qualifications

  • An updated and polished portfolio that demonstrates strong user experience and interaction design informed by empathy for the user. This is key for us.
  • 8+ years of product design experience in product or service design, under your belt, ideally for supply chain and/or data visibility services in a B2B setting.
  • Strong facilitation and cross-functional collaboration abilities, a flexible and friendly approach to working with teams, and the ability to collaborate with, influence, and impact different cross functional partners and teams across the organization.
  • Ability to apply your design skills in research, interaction design, and visual design to help craft and build new features.
  • Experienced in modern tools such as Figma, FigJam, and Teams.
