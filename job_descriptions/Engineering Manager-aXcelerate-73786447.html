About us

aXcelerate is a leading SaaS provider that powers millions of learners' course and competency records throughout Australia, with a mission to transform people's lives by enabling skills development. aXcelerate has achieved some massive growth, and is leading the market with a world class Student Management and Learning Management solution based on cutting edge technology. Recognized as a Great Place to Work® 2023, our values are central to our culture and we all come to work knowing we are making a difference.

About the role

We’re looking for a passionate leader to roll up their sleeves and work alongside our highly talented group of engineers, solving tomorrow's education challenges through scalable technical solutions that leverage the latest technology trends, automations and AI. In this high paced and energetic environment, you’ll build and nurture a high performing team, drive product delivery and ensure the technical quality and performance of our platform. As part of the leadership team, you will also play a key role in shaping the technical vision and direction of the company.

Responsibilities:

Lead and mentor a team of talented engineers, fostering a culture of ownership, accountability, and continuous improvement.

Define and execute strategies to drive change and innovation within the engineering department, aligning with the company's goals and objectives.

Collaborate cross-functionally with product management, design, and other teams to prioritize and execute initiatives that enhance our SaaS offerings.

Provide hands-on technical guidance and support to the engineering team, leveraging your expertise to overcome technical challenges and drive excellence.

Cultivate a growth-oriented environment, identifying opportunities for skill development, talent acquisition, and onboarding best practices.

Foster effective communication and collaboration within the engineering team and across the organization, ensuring alignment and transparency.

Lead by example, demonstrating strong emotional intelligence, resilience, and a commitment to fostering a positive and inclusive work environment.
