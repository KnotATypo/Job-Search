An exciting opportunity now exists for Experienced Secondary Education Tutors with experience in English or Mathematics to join the team at the Kip McGrath Education Centre at Coorparoo QLD as they enter a new growth phase.


ABOUT US


Since opening their doors more than 40 years ago, Kip McGrath has become a well-known and respected brand in the education sector and has experienced significant transformational change as they have grown into a global business. Through the development of proven methodologies, individually customised learning programmes, world-class technology, and motivated and skilled educators; their aim is to create a brighter future for children across the globe.


Programs are individually tailored to the needs of each student, offering remediation, consolidation and extension learning in numeracy and literacy from kindergarten through to college.


ABOUT THE ROLE


At Kip McGrath , we focus on what's important... TEACHING! In a small group setting, you will be able to realise your passion for teaching literacy and numeracy. You will help children to grow their confidence and meet their goals every day.


In this role you will be responsible for:

  • Teaching students using the Kip McGrath methodology
  • Tutoring students in a positive, caring manner to promote self esteem and self worth
  • Quickly learning how to use our state of the art software to plan and conduct face to face lessons - saving you on prep time!
  • Being available to commit to every class you are scheduled to teach
  • Using your strong rapport building skills to develop and maintain a positive relationship with students and parents
  • Keeping an accurate record online of your students progress and goals on a weekly basis
  • Providing verbal and written feedback to parents
  • Work up to 2 - 3 sessions a day (Monday to Saturday)
