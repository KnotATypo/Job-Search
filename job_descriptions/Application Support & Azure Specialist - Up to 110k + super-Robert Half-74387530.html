Application Support & Azure Specialist

Are you looking for a diverse role in a company that fosters growth and autonomy, rewards achievement, and puts culture first? A leader in the Australian Medical Software market is seeking an Application Support Specialist with strong Azure experience.

The Role

Working with a high level of autonomy and shared team accountability, the key responsibilities include:

  • Resolve ServiceDesk incidents, adhering to SLAs and conducting root cause analyses.
  • Build and deploy Azure Cloud environments within a Cloud Security Framework
  • Conduct software training sessions, both online and in classrooms.
  • Oversee customer User Acceptance Testing for software installations and upgrades.
  • Share responsibility for after-hours support during critical incidents.
  • Extend application support to Azure Hosted Clients and On-Premise installations.
  • Execute SQL scripts, understand system downtime, and perform basic SQL queries.

Your Expertise:

  • Strong foundational skills in Azure - building, configuring, deploying Azure Cloud environments within a secure framework.
  • Azure or AWS Certifications or equivalent.
  • 3-5+ years of L2 Helpdesk or Support experience.
  • Strong self-management, excellent written and oral communication.
  • Intermediate-level MS SQL/Oracle scripting.
  • Australian Full Driver's License and full working rights.

The Business

  • Unwavering commitment to work life balance.
  • A culture of recognition, celebration of achievements, and collaborative spirit.
  • Established relationships with major health departments.
  • 25-year reputation built on goodwill and word of mouth.
