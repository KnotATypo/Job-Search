The Opportunity:

We are currently seeking Expression of Interests from highly motivated Mission Planning Technical Specialist with Aviation Mission Planning experience who will join our team to provide technical expertise and advice to support the Aviation Mission Planning Team.

The Mission Planning Technical SMEs would provide technical expertise and advice to support with implementation of mission planning system requirements by providing design and implementation guidance.

The Mission Planning Technical SME would also provide expertise and guidance relating to future requirements for Aviation Mission Planning and integration into a broader implementation strategy.

All candidates will be required to hold and maintain an active NV1 clearance. Only candidates holding an NV1 clearance or above should apply.

The Mission Planning Technical SME would be responsible to the Aviation Mission Planning Systems Team Lead to provide technical support for deliverables, and represent Aviation Mission Planning Team as follows:

  • Provide specialist advice for mission planning design and integration for fixed and deployed computer networks.

  • Provide technical advice in relation to the system hardware and software.

  • Provide technical expertise to support the development of mission planning elements and the integration of the Aviation Mission Planning with other Defence planning, co-ordination and reporting systems.

  • Provide specialist advice to acquisitions projects for Aviation Mission Planning interfaces.

  • Maintaining knowledge of allied forces development and employment of Aviation Mission Planning System, through attendance at technical forums such as Interface Control Working Group (ICWG) meetings and through review of documentation supplied by allied forces.
