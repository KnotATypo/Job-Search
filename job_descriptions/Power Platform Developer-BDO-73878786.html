About the team

The Innovation and Digital Transformation team are responsible for advancing and discovering new ideas and ways of working which will improve how BDO in Australia delivers products, services and experiences to our people and clients.

As a team, we support service lines nationally with:

  • developing streamlined ways of working through the implementation of digital technologies.
  • piloting new products and services to improve service delivery and operational efficiencies.
  • cultivating value-adding ideas by empowering teams to leverage innovative thinking and design concepts.

Current opportunity

As a Power Apps Developer, reporting to the Technical Lead in our Innovation team you will work as part of a development team supporting, maintaining, and enhancing Power Apps to support BDO innovation strategy.

This role would be suitable for a developer or IT specialist who has honed their skills in power platform and is passionate about automation and process improvement.

You will be resourceful, detail-oriented, and self-motivated, with a continuing passion and eager to learn new technologies.

What you’ll be doing

  • Assist citizen developers/business owners to identify opportunities for process improvement using the power platform as well as the larger Microsoft ecosystem, including D365, SharePoint, Dataverse, Power apps, Power Automate/Flow and Power BI
  • Develop prototypes for proof of concepts
  • Research, analysis, design, development, testing and implementation of Power Platform solutions
  • Assist in troubleshooting and/or enhancing existing developed Power Platform solutions

What you’ll need to succeed

Work as part of a team to contribute, design and develop next generation power app applications to solve complex problems, automate business processes and improve the customer experience.

  • An understanding of Power Apps, D365, Power Automate, Power BI and SharePoint from an administration, configuration, customization, integration and testing perspective
  • A good understanding of the software development lifecycle
  • Some experience with PowerFx, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Information Technology or a related field
  • Desirable Microsoft certified Power Platform developer Associate (PL-400), Microsoft Certified Power Platform Functional Consultant Associate (PL-200)
