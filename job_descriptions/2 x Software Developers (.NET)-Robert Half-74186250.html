The Company

Robert Half Technology are working alongside a growing healthcare software business in Brisbane who are looking to expand their engineering team with two Software Engineers. With a range of different products, our client has an impressive portfolio of their own clients and are expanding.

The Role

These are newly created positions and are off the back of increased volume of work and the desire to migrate a lot of different applications into the latest technology. Currently in the development team there are 5 other full stack developers and 2 testers which will now grow to 7 developers.
The goal is to bring on 2 developers who are comfortable with the full Microsoft Stack and have commercial knowledge of mobile applications and APIs that are also at times ok with working with a slightly older stack for initial maintenance reasons.

Responsibilities:

  • Work in a close knit team and be a crucial part to the development practice and bring ideas to the group
  • Write robust code using modern frameworks including .NET Core
  • Help guide other team members when needed to complete projects in a timely manner.
  • Participate and contribute to the design, architecture, development, and testing of the projects and write documentation.

Your Profile:

  • Extensive experience working .NET, C# and SQL Server
  • Experience working with APIs
  • Working knowledge with Xamarin, .NET Maui or an alternative mobile framework would be an advantage
  • Be confident in presenting your work to business stakeholders.
  • Be happy to work with more legacy code at times when needed to help support their existing products
