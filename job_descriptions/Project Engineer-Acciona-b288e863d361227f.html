Job Description
About the Project:
Acciona Construction Australia has partnered with Georgiou Group to deliver the first stage of the Gold Coast's largest road infrastructure project. The project will involve construction of six new bridge structures in an environment with unique geotechnical conditions and challenging interfacing on a gazetted green-field site. You will have the opportunity to take on a varied and exciting role delivering the first stage of an important region-shaping project.
About the opportunity:
As part of the Bridges team you will be reporting to the Senior Project Engineer - Structures. You will assist with the planning, execution and closing of the project by providing technical, administrative, HSEQS support to the project team. A key requirement is to accurately report on how your project is running (cost, time, scope & quality) at all times requiring you to know and understand your daily costs and scope of works.
Key Responsibilities:
  • Ensure the project is delivered to overall program, cost, and time with necessary resources leading to high client satisfaction
  • Maintain documentation and systems for quality, safety, and environmental standards
  • Participate in client/contractor meetings as required
  • Financial assistance with cost tracking and invoicing of subcontractors on different packages of work
  • Report to senior project engineers but gain leadership exposure with Site Engineers/ Grads/ Undergrads
Required Skills and Competencies
  • Relevant tertiary qualification in Engineering
  • 5+ years experience working in concrete structures and or bridges
  • Working knowledge of relevant legislation/standards
  • Strong planning ability, knowledge of cost reporting and excellent communication skills are vital for this role
If you feel like you meet some or all of the above criteria and are interested in joining a global company making a positive impact, Apply now!
