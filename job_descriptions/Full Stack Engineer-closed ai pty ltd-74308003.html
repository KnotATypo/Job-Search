About us

Closed Ai is a small, growing Ai enterprise.  We have several products active and under development that span multiple verticals. Our flagship product is Voice. Voice provides automated services for Contact Centres and help desks.  As well as making enterprise knowledge more easily accessible for workers. As a Full Stack Engineer at Closed.AI, you will play a key role in designing and implementing end-to-end solutions for our web-based applications. You will work collaboratively with cross-functional teams, including front-end developers, back-end developers, AI and machine learning specialists and UI/UX designers, to deliver high-quality and scalable software solutions.

Qualifications & experience

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
  • Minimum three years experience as a Full Stack Engineer.
  • Proficient with front-end technologies, React, typescript is essential
  • Strong server-side development skills and languages, Python and NodeJS is essential
  • Experience with database systems such as MQSQL or PostgreSQL and MongoDB or DynamoDB
  • Familiarity with AWS cloud platform.
  • Experience with DevOps principles and practices will be highly regarded
  • Experience with open source AI technologies will be highly regarded
  • Strong problem-solving and attention to detail
  • Experience with CI/CD

Tasks & responsibilities

  • Design and develop scalable and maintainable full-stack applications.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
  • Build and maintain efficient, reusable, and reliable code.
  • Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs and implement them.
  • Integrate user-facing elements with server-side logic.
  • Utilise your knowledge, experience and creativity to provide valuable insights
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices.
