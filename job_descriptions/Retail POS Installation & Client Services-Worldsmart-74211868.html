Position overview

We are looking for an individual looking to build a career with a market leading technology company. We take pride in our proprietary software – which has recently been re-engineered – to set Worldsmart up for future growth. More importantly this allows our retailers the opportunity to drive further efficiencies through new technologies, to help grow their business.

As a member of our team, you will report to the QLD State Manager and work closely with our WorldSmart team to deliver high quality, value-add solutions to our retailers. The primary focus is on providing the highest quality Installation, Implementation, Training and Support services for our Point-of-Sale(POS), E-Commerce and Loyalty products to our client base.

We are searching for a motivated individual who can handle responsibilities both in a team environment and autonomously. Core responsibilities include;

  • Installation of POS hardware & software products to clients
  • System trouble shooting and problem solving
  • Customer training and support
  • Pre sales support
