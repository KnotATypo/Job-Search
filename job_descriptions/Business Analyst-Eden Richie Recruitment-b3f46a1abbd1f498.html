Principal Business Analyst - Human Resources Business Intelligence (HRBI)

Digital Passport Project

  • 3 month contract
  • Brisbane CBD location

Position Overview:

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Principal Business Analyst to join the HRBI team. In this role, you will play a pivotal role in leading, managing, and executing the identification, analysis, and documentation of customer requirements within a dynamic and complex Information Management (IM)/Information Communication Technology (ICT) environment. You will be responsible for driving the success of key program/project(s) by delivering valued, integrated, and effective business solutions.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead and manage the identification, analysis, and documentation of customer requirements for HRBI initiatives.
  • Undertake project scoping and define work packages to ensure successful project delivery.
  • Facilitate requirements gathering sessions with stakeholders from various business units.
  • Utilise business process modeling techniques to capture and visualise business processes.
  • Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to ensure alignment between business requirements and technical solutions.
  • Provide guidance and mentorship to junior business analysts within the team.
  • Continuously assess and improve business analysis processes and methodologies.
  • Act as a liaison between business stakeholders and technical teams to ensure successful implementation of solutions.

The successful applicant will have:

  • Proven experience working as a Business Analyst in a complex Information Management (IM)/Information Communication Technology (ICT) environment.
  • Strong expertise in requirements gathering, analysis, and documentation.
  • Proficiency in business process modeling techniques such as BPMN or UML.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively interact with stakeholders at all levels of the organisation.
  • Strong leadership and project management skills, with the ability to lead and motivate cross-functional teams.
  • Experience working with HRBI solutions or within the Human Resources domain is preferred but not required.
  • Relevant certifications such as CBAP (Certified Business Analysis Professional) or PMI-PBA (PMI Professional in Business Analysis) are a plus.
  • Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field is highly desirable.

If you are a passionate and driven individual with a strong background in business analysis and a desire to make a difference in the HRBI space, we encourage you to apply!
