The Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS) is looking to fill the role of HCL Notes and Domino Administrator in our Security and Infrastructure Branch. This is a permanent position at the APS 6 classification level. Successful applicants can work either full-time or part-time hours. Flexible working arrangements, including a hybrid model of working from home and the office applies.

The HCL Notes and Domino Administrator role may include the following duties:

  • configure, integrate, manage HCL Software Infrastructure including Notes client, Domino, MarvelClient, HCL Connection, HCL Volt MX Go
  • providing technical support of Notes Client and Domino application (level 2/3 type support)
  • understanding HCL future software directions (HCL Volt MX Go and Domino REST API)
  • providing expert advice with regards to the adoption and appropriate use of HCL Software (Notes / Domino, HCL Volt MX Go, Domino REST API)
  • consulting with business and technical stakeholders to perform requirements gathering and implement solutions to satisfy those requirements
  • developing repeatable processes through scripting and automation.

Common responsibilities across all APS 6 level roles in the Security and Infrastructure Branch include:

  • ensuring the delivery of ABS business outcomes through participation in multi-disciplinary agile teams
  • liaising with internal and external stakeholders (including vendors), leveraging their expertise to resolve critical issues and inform decisions
  • adhering to the ABS Contract, Change Management and Financial management processes
  • utilising your technical leadership experience to mentor staff and contribute to organisational capability uplift and support Executive Level staff to lead and manage the section
  • preparing and maintaining appropriate documentation
  • an expectation to participate in a 24 x 7 on-call roster.

What we are looking for

To be considered for a HCL Notes and Domino Administrator position (APS 6), you will need most, or all of the following skills, qualities and experience:

  • demonstrated experience in deploying, managing and troubleshooting Domino Services
  • demonstrated experience in configuration and support of the Notes Client
  • knowledge or experience with interoperability between HCL Notes / Domino and Microsoft 365
  • knowledge or experience with the following desirable:

o HCL Connections, HCL Volt MX Go, HCL Domino REST API

o HCL Notes/Domino Development

o Panagenda MarvelClient, Greenlight, iDNA.

o HCL Domino Containerisation.

To apply, complete an online application form on the ABS e-recruitment system. You will need to upload your current resume, provide referee details and respond to the following:

In no more than 750 words, provide an example of how you have applied your technical skills and/or leadership skills to deliver an IT solution for a business need. Clearly articulate what your role and contribution was and the business benefits achieved, in the context of the selection criteria.

To be eligible to apply for this role you must be a current Australian citizen.

Applications close at 11:30 p.m. (AEST), Sunday 7th April 2024.
