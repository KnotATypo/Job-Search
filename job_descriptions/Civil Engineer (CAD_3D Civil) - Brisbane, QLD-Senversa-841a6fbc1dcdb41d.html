Full job description

Be a part of a fantastic community connected and dedicated to improving our future!


Senversa is a wholly Australian employee-owned civil and environmental engineering, geospatial and digital, and broader environmental & sustainability professional services firm operating across seven locations. We are committed to and are recognised for delivering high quality advice and solutions to our clients across Australia.


Through ongoing organic growth in the Queensland region, we have a new opportunity for a Civil Engineer skilled in CAD and 3D Civil design software packages to join our fantastic team. This is your opportunity to enhance our growing and high-performing Engineering team and develop your general drafting and 3D modelling technical skills through on the job learning on a broad range of civil-environmental design projects for engineered waste containment facilities and other civil works.


Senversa values collaboration and demonstrates this through our solutions focused approach to working with our clients. You will enjoy being a key role within integrated multi-disciplinary teams of highly skilled civil engineers, hydrogeologists, and contaminated land professionals to provide our clients with effective solutions.


Our commitment to you

Senversa is 100% employee owned, is a certified B Corporation, carbon neutral and a DCA Inclusive Workplace, recognising that we constantly strive to make a positive impact across our staff, clients, suppliers, community, and the environment.


In addition to a diverse and inclusive, fast-paced working environment, you will be part of a team that prides itself on technical excellence and our senior staff are committed to coaching and mentoring staff in a dynamic, positive, respectful, and constructive professional environment. You will benefit from sustainable professional growth whilst maintaining flexibility to achieve a work-life balance.


We pride ourselves on a culture of sustainability, corporate social responsibility, workplace diversity, flexible working conditions and equal opportunity. Feel free to have a chat to us about the numerous ways in which we are promoting this culture. Senversa is a company that lives and breathes our values and is truly committed to our people. You, along with your colleagues, will benefit from a company that is committed to a great working culture.


So, what will you do?

You will assist on a range of projects for government and private sector clients which include waste infrastructure projects. You will work as part of an excellent & supportive team delivering projects from concept design to finished construction for contaminated land remedial earthworks, containment facilities for contaminated soil and solid waste, together with other waste management infrastructure. As we strive not to work in ‘silos’ at Senversa, you will have regular opportunities to work closely with all project’s teams within Senversa achieving a variety in project knowledge and skills.


Highly desirable professional skills you will bring to the team.

  • At least three years’ experience with AutoCAD and AutoCAD Civil 3D packages working independently to prepare 3D earthworks models and volume estimates. Experience with other similar CAD packages such as 12d Model will also be considered favourable.
  • Experience working as part of a design team preparing civil earthworks and geosynthetic liner design drawing sets.
    • A strong desire to be successful in a rapidly evolving environmental and civil consultancy.



We take pride in our vision as “the most satisfying workplace for quality environmental, geosciences and waste management professionals”, meaning you will be welcomed into a supportive organisation that embraces diversity and inclusion, encourages collaboration across teams and offices, and values input from all team members. We will provide you with the flexibility you need to perform at your best.


Don’t worry if you don’t have all of skills outlined above. If you have proven experience in some or most of these technical areas of practice, you will be working within a team that promotes professional development so we can assist you in acquiring skills and experience, where required.
