About the Role:

As a ServiceNow Specialist, you'll be at the forefront of configuring and administering our ServiceNow platform, ensuring it aligns perfectly with the diverse needs of our users. With a focus on enhancing user navigation and developing seamless procedures, you'll play a key role in matching our customers with the exceptional products and services they deserve.

  • Certified as a ServiceNow – System Administrator (CSA).

  • Strong communication and organizational abilities.
  • Willingness to adapt to a flexible schedule.
