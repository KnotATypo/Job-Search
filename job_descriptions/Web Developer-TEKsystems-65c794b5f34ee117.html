My client is looking for a Web Developer to join their organisation on a 6-month assignment where they will be to be part of a team engaged in the development and maintenance of websites that will support their current and emerging work.

Role Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, implement, maintain and edit high standard websites, online forms, multimedia, web applications and collaboration sites that comply with our clients publication, branding and security guidelines.
  • Support the maintenance and development content management systems (Squiz Matrix, WordPress, Drupal) by updating content, developing templates, taking responsibility for the management of CMS configuration, assets, user roles, permissions and workflows and by training and supporting content contributors, editors and approvers.
  • Implement web-based projects, providing advice and recommendations on the technical and usability aspects of online communication and technologies while ensuring that the integrity of information architecture and a high-level ease-of-use for customers is maintained.
  • Manage allocated projects with minimal supervision, including the analysis of requirements, research, development and implementation of solutions, negotiation of outcomes and by meeting agreed deadlines.
  • Design, develop, implement and document all aspects of front-end design including user experience and user interface design.
  • Produce and analyse web metrics reports, including but not limited to the production of Google Analytics reports.
  • Optimise resource discovery by developing and managing metadata, creating and editing search forms and search result templates, and by managing and configuring search engine performance.
  • Maintain awareness of changes in web standards, trends and technical developments and support the Webmaster in the documentation, maintenance and development of standards, policies, registers and technical guides

Skills and Experience:


To be considered for this position you must have the following experience:

  • Ideally Experience with designing and managing web content on Squiz Matrix, WordPress, Drupal
  • Experience and knowledge of developing content strategy, information architecture and navigation
  • Experience in user centred design, web standards and accessibility, and stakeholder market testing
  • Experience with the above in a Government setting (local, state or Federal Government) is preferred.
