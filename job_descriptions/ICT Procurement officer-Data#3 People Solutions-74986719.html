Enterprise Software licencing Officer/ ICT Procurement officer

  • The term of contract engagement our client is currently seeking up to 4 months, plus potential extension option.
  • Flexible working hours
  • George Street, Brisbane

As a Enterprise Software licencing Officer/ ICT procurement Officer you will be working within Information and Technology Partners, the successful candidate will be part of the Software Licensing Team that holds responsibility for the delivery of the software asset management function across our partner agencies.

Role Responsibilities:

You will be required to perform the following-

  • Undertake software auditing, licence reconciliation and quality assurance with keen attention to detail and data accuracy.
  • Enter data into the supporting registers and/or related licencing systems and complete other tasks to ensure the data is kept up-to-date and accurate.
  • Query the database that you are looking after and undertake the data analysis and reporting as requested.
  • Undertake Service Desk tool queue management, including workload triage and prioritisation, escalation and job resolution.
  • Have or acquire a sound level of knowledge of ICT licencing products and services.
  • Have or acquire an understanding of governmental policies, procedures and standards that impact on the duties of the position.
  • Develop and maintain constructive relationships with clients.
  • Works well within a team.
  • Able to work autonomously.
  1. Essential Qualifications/Certifications/Skills:
  • Strong aptitude and direct experience in working with databases, systems, tools and interfaces.
  • Strong attention-to-detail so that data and documentation is accurate and high quality and that advice is clear.
  • Direct experience in undertaking data analysis and data cleansing.
  1. Highly Desirable to this role are:
  • Prior experience/knowledge in working within Software Asset Management function .
  • Demonstrated Information Technology/ICT Procurement knowledge and experience
  • Self-motivated, enthusiastic, and results-oriented approach
  • Well-developed communication and interpersonal skills and the ability to work with multiple styles of clients and stakeholders.
  • Demonstrated ability to work effectively and independently and as a member of a multi-disciplinary team - including ability to organise own work, planning, prioritising work, meeting deadlines, maintaining confidentiality, and exercising discretion in the absence of decision makers.
  1. Term of Engagement:

The term of contract engagement the department is currently seeking up to 4 months, plus potential extension option.

Suitable for: Project coordinator, IT Project Officer, ICT Project support officer
