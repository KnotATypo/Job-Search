Job Description:

We're looking for a Data Engineer with 5-15 years of experience in Data Warehousing and Business Intelligence, specifically skilled in Data bricks and DBT.

Responsibilities:

  • Hands-on work in Data Warehousing/Business Intelligence.
  • Quick learning in Data bricks and DBT, with a bonus for Data Migration experience.
  • 8+ years of experience in Data Modeling and Development, including AWS Redshift and Netezza.
  • Leadership experience with teams of at least 5 people.
  • Strong collaboration, communication, and code review skills.
  • Exposure to reporting tools like Cognos, Tableau, ThoughtSpot is a plus.
  • Design experience with HLD and LLD.
