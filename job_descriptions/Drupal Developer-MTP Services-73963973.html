About the Project
The Drupal 9 developer will be responsible for contributing to the redevelopment of the web presence to ensure a fit-for-purpose, user-focused and best practice solution. This role will focus on the planning, technical design and development of the Drupal 9 website and will work collaboratively with the ICT web technical team and website project team.

Duties for this role are
  • Work collaboratively with the website project team to develop the Drupal 9 website, ensuring the new website meets user needs, provides an optimal user experience, and meets technical standards.
  • Provide expert technical advice to ensure the successful delivery of the website, and contribute to planning development sprints to ensure goals and deadlines are met, and milestones are delivered.
  • Migrate, redevelop or reconfigure content types from the existing Drupal 7 websites.
  • Develop scripts for content migration from Drupal 7 websites.
  • Make sound technical recommendations and provide advice to the project team.
  • Knowledge transfer to Web Team.
