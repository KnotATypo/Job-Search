The Opportunity

We are seeking talented systems engineers to support the Battlespace Communication System (BCS) Program. BCS is a next generation networking product that is the centerpiece for multiple projects and campaigns. The BCS combines both developmental and off the shelf hardware and software to provide world leading communications capability for ADF personnel. It can be rapidly deployed from anywhere, and its modular design provides a flexible, scalable solution that can be customized for various mission needs.

We have a range of opportunities available as we plan for future work. This role will see you applying your skills to either our core product for Army, upgrading the capability to integrate with related maritime communications projects, or modernization of the joint air battlespace management system.

Responsibilities

As a BCS Systems Engineer you will work as a member of a multi-disciplinary team charged with owning and delivering products to our customers across the full product lifecycle.  As a part of this team you will have an evolving set of responsibilities are the project progresses, including:

  • Championing understanding and analyzing customer requirements alongside the development of innovative solutions that ensure the full product lifecycle is optimized.
  • Coordinating with other design and specialty disciplines (e.g. software, hardware, safety, security and support system engineering) to ensure that your team's products are properly integrated into the solution and delivered in a way that delights the customer.
  • Working with the broader project team to ensure the engineering scope is delivered within the quality, schedule and budgetary constraints of the project
  • Development of engineering documentation and configuration management of system hardware infrastructure.
  • The ability to communicate and collaborate with a diverse team, in a conscientious and inclusive way.
  • Verifying and validating designs and products to ensure they meet operational and functional requirements

Experience/Qualifications

Essential:

  • Bachelor's Degree in an Engineering discipline, Information Technology related fields or equivalent industry experience.
  • Applicants must be Australian Citizens to meet defence security requirements.
  • We invite a diverse range of people to apply, including people from outside of the defence industry.

Desirable:

  • Experience within Defence or complex engineering project environments is desirable.
  • Understanding of Systems Engineering principles and lifecycle management.
  • Experience developing and executing against Systems Engineering artefacts including Systems Engineering Management Plan and Verification and Validation Plan
  • Experience in technical problem solving
  • Experience with complex communications systems deployed in challenging environments
  • Experience in utilizing digital systems engineering tools including DOORS and CAMEO/Magic System of Systems Architect
