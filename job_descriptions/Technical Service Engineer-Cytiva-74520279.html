Working at Cytiva means being at the forefront of providing new solutions to transform human health. Our incredible customers undertake life-saving activities ranging from fundamental biological research to developing innovative vaccines, new medicines, and cell and gene therapies.

At Cytiva you will be able to continuously improve yourself and us – working on challenges that truly matter with people that care for each other, our customers, and their patients. With associates across 40+ countries, Cytiva is a place where every day is a learning opportunity – so you can grow your career and expand your skills in the long term.

Forming part of the Biotechnology segment at Danaher, we bring together dedicated technical expertise and talent to develop the next generation of life-changing therapeutics.

This highly dynamic role’s main focus will be to provide service support to Cytiva’s customers in Australia. You will be working on Cytiva’s Single Use Bioprocessing equipment like AKTA ready and AKTA readyflux as well as Xcellerex (Bioreactor and Mixer) systems located in GMP facilities across ANZ. In addition, you will be working on our flagship lab-scale AKTA systems like the AKTA Pure, Go and Avant!

The essential requirements of the job include:

The initial focus of the role will be to become an expert on working on our equipment in regulated environments. This will include performing preventative maintenance and level 1 troubleshooting and repair jobs on Bioprocess scale systems.

You will become an expert on performing qualification (IQOQ) of new systems during the first 12months in the job.

Become familiar with our digital and automation offerings (UNICORN, Figurate, Chronicle, OptiRun Connect) to eventually deliver, install and maintain these on site.

  • Communicate and partner with teammates and colleagues to learn, advance your skill set and become an expert yourself!
  • Keep up to date on administrative responsibilities such as maintaining customer service logs and internal service records in a timely manner, ordering repair parts, and managing cycle times, manage calibration records for tools, manage trunk stock.
  • Participating in sales opportunities such as contract renewals and assist with promoting and implementing revenue generating programs.

You will be responsible for delivering on-time & best in class scheduled maintenance services and installs of new equipment. You will learn from & work alongside experienced Field Engineers. You will be supporting Cytiva’s customers throughout ANZ, so you will get to travel lots & experience the various GMP environments that our instruments are used in! You will be part of a very successful team & work in an exciting & booming industry! If you are looking for a challenge and a successful career path in the BioPharma services industry, this is the role for you!

Who you are

  • We are looking for someone with >5yrs of biomedical or life science field experience.
  • As this role will be focusing on customers producing critical therapeutic drugs you will require a high attention to detail and have a desire to become an expert in working in GMP environments.
  • You are willing to support some key customers after-hours by being on-call during certain periods.
  • You have strong hands-on technical experience.
  • You are good with computers and can easily setup and troubleshoot networks.
  • It is essential that you have amazing communication and customer-oriented skills.
  • You are a team player but can also work independently and remotely and are someone who takes initiative!
  • You have a passion to work in the Biotechnology research industry and want to make a difference.
  • Ability to travel away from your home state for about 50% of the time is essential. This travel will be to do maintenance and installation jobs across all states, training at various training centres and do on the job training with other engineers.
