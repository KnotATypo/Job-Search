The purpose of Queensland Fire and Emergency Services (QFES) is to empower communities to prevent, prepare for, respond to, and recover from the impact of fire and emergency events. As the primary provider of fire, rescue, and emergency management programs and services throughout Queensland, they are committed to ensuring the safety and wellbeing of all Queenslanders. QFES encompasses various services including the Fire and Rescue Service, disaster management, Rural Fire Service, State Emergency Service, and supports other volunteer groups providing emergency response.
