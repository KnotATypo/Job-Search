What can you expect from our Graduate Program?

This is a 2 year program as a graduate in our National Industrial team focused solely in the Energy Improvement space . The role is based at our South Brisbane office however please note it will be expected of you to travel domestically to our sites. You’ll spend time on the factory floor understanding the various roles, processes and day to day operations of production. We’re with you all the way and provide training that includes leading and coaching, project management, an introductory course on the dairy industry run by an expert training organisation, a development week working on your communication and key transversal skills and more…

About you:

You are a recent Engineering Graduate keen to get exposure to a global company and relevant work experience in a manufacturing environment, then we have the pathway for you. Our graduate program is underpinned by a strong learning and development framework with structured programs and support.

You have completed a Chemical or Mechanical Engineering degree within the last 24 months, we are looking for someone who has studied Thermodynamics , Fluid Mechanics as well an interest in Computer Science.

You are an Australian Citizen, Permanent Resident, or on a 485 VISA and you have a relevant degree that aligns.

As you're a recent graduate, you do not need experience, however the following would be highly beneficial:

  • A passion for wanting to work in FMCG / dairy
  • A desire to grow and succeed with Lactalis Australia
  • Strong academic results
  • Awareness of health and safety within a work environment
