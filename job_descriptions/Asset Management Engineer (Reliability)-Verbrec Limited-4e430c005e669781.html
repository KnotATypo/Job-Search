About Verbrec

With over 500 dedicated team members operating out of locations across Australia and New Zealand, Verbrec is a leading mid-tier ASX listed company (ASX: VBC) providing exceptional engineering, operations and training services across the entire asset lifecycle, with a significant focus on growth into new and existing industries.

About the role

This role provides crucial Asset Reliability & Maintenance advice to clients from both strategic and front-line perspectives. You will be an integral part of a high performing team of reliability and maintenance professionals executing jobs of varying sizes and complexity. To ensure your success, you will be mentored by professionals with a wealth of experience with mineral processing, mining and oil and gas assets including conveyors, crushers, pressure vessels, rotating equipment and process machinery.

The type of assignments may include:

  • Maintenance Strategy Development and Optimisation
  • Critical Spares Assessments
  • Failure Investigations / Root Cause Analyses
  • Maintenance System Builds and Updates
  • FMECA Studies
  • RAM Studies and RAM Modelling
  • Developing maintenance programmes and asset class plans

About you

This role would suit a person with a background in mining, hydrocarbons or chemicals environments. The person will require excellent communication skills and proven capability to perform under pressure, individually and as part of a team.

Key Skills & Experience:

  • A degree in Mechanical, Reliability, Material or Corrosion Engineering
  • Between 3 and 5 years work experience preferably within a reliability/maintenance function in a mining, hydrocarbons or chemical environment
  • Understanding of local and international standards
  • Highly developed computer skills including Word, Excel, PowerPoint & Visio
  • An understanding of computerised maintenance management software and/or reliability management software / databases

Behaviours:

  • Proactive safety focus for physical and process safety situations
  • The energy and passion to challenge themselves, innovate and step outside their “comfort zone”
  • Ability to communicate effectively to multiple levels within internal and external teams
