About STELaRLab

STELaRLab are partnering with Australia’s finest minds and academic institutions to solve enduring and emerging strategic challenges.

If you are interested in solving complex Defence and National Security problems, in collaboration with high performing teams across Australia and the world, then the STELaRLab Operations Analysis Team is the right fit for you.

Our analysts develop and use cutting edge models, simulation tools, algorithms, and analytic techniques to ensure that Australia and our allies maintain the capability edge in a complex and uncertain world.

About the Opportunity
