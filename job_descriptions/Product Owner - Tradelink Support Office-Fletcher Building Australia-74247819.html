Why this Role

We know that you're passionate about helping people and businesses succeed. In this role, you’ll get to do both. You will:

  • Have a passion for helping a business grow through your direct efforts
  • Have the ability to drive adoption through deep engagement and understanding of our customers’ needs, branch and key sales staff, systems, and business processes
  • Work closely with internal and external stakeholders to ensure the correct items and information displays, help implement promotions and brand messaging
  • Participate in customer visits, feature launch events, in-store activities, training webinars, and retrospectives to constantly track our ROI and future focus
  • Have the skills to be able to troubleshoot issues that both customers and staff
You’ll love this role for the opportunity to see the results of your efforts in action! There is also never a shortage of opportunities to build your skills, and this role will give you great exposure. Another drawcard is our team – you couldn’t find a more authentic and supportive bunch. Finally, we have a long-term vision that we believe in, and love to try new things so your ideas will always be heard.

At the same time, it’s true that not every job is for everyone. You will find that this role will need you to pivot quickly and adapt to changing needs. You'll be working in a super-fast environment with competing demands on your time. Everyday you’ll need to bring high levels of energy and positivity for your team members and stakeholders. Finally, you'll need to be comfortable working hands-on, be self-directed, and resourceful.

Most importantly, we’re looking for:

  • Someone experienced with Jira, Confluence and key analytical tools such as Lucky Orange, UXCAM, Google Analytics (GA4)
