The Role

Get ready to flex your coding muscles one line of code at a time!

Your prowess will be automating processes for maximum efficiency and ensuring the delivery of high-quality data that will enable the development of solutions that meet client and business requirements while aligning with the company's strategic goals.

Key responsibilities include:

  • Ensuring accurate and efficient data delivery from external sources to internal systems, enhancing the company's data pool, and proactively integrating new data sources to optimize data processing and analysis.
  • Troubleshooting and debugging data feeds and process issues to minimize disruption and maximize efficiency.
  • Maintaining compliance with company standards and policies by implementing best practices and continuously improving processes in collaboration with the Tech Lead to enhance the company's IT environment and increase efficiency.
  • Ensuring secure and effective use and management of data across systems and processes and accurately documenting Data Engineering processes to facilitate continuous improvement.

  • Extensive experience with MySQL and at least one other database (pgSQL, MS SQL, Oracle, DynamoDB, etc.)

  • Experience automating processes, especially data-related ones
  • Experience with CQLs like Neo4j or GraphQL
  • Ability to navigate and operate in a Linux CLI environment
  • Experience integrating with APIs and other data infrastructure
