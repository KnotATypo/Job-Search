Enviropacific is Australia’s leading specialist environmentally focused services business. We deliver sustainable engineering and applied science solutions to make our environments and communities safer and more sustainable.

We are seeking a Projects Administrator to join our Queensland Water projects team.

About the Role:

As a Projects Administrator you will professionally engage with Project Managers, project staff, clients and other stakeholders, providing crucial administrative support in the delivery of our projects.

Initially based in our Pinkenba office and working across multiple projects key responsibilities for this position will include the following:

  • Participating in the development and submission of project tenders.
  • Coordinating site activities in conjunction with managers and engineers.
  • Checking and approving supplier and subcontractor invoices prior to payment.
  • Supporting the administration of project contracts.
  • Assisting staff with expense claims and end of month requirements.
  • Deliver Quality reviews and feedback of key documents across the projects.
  • QHSE support and update of online systems following events.
  • Coordinate the safe work of subcontractors within the Pinkenba yard.

About You:

We are looking for an individual who is able to manage a range of responsibilities to support the successful delivery of multiple diverse and exciting projects.

To be considered for this position you will need the following:

  • Previous project-based experience and a strong understanding of the project life-cycle.
  • A high level of computer literacy and proficiency using Microsoft Office and Adobe Acrobat.
  • Strong attention to detail and an ability to manage multiple responsibilities.
  • Exceptional written and verbal communication skills.
  • A friendly, organised and professional demeanour.
  • Experience using Jobpac or Gensuite would be an advantage, but not crucial.
