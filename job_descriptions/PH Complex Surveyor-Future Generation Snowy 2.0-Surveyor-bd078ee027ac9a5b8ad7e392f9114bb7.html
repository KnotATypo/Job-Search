Company description:

Future Generation Joint Venture (FGJV) is the principal contractor for Snowy Hydro's Snowy 2.0, Australia's largest committed renewable energy project. We bring the combined engineering expertise of three companies - Italy's Webuild (formerly Salini Impregilo), Australian-based Clough and US-based Lane Construction.

Job description:

The Future Generation Joint Venture brings together the combined engineering experience and expertise of WeBuild, Clough and Lane and has been selected to design construct Snowy 2.0 . This project is based within the Snowy Mountains in NSW and will deliver one of the largest pumped hydro schemes in the world.

Purpose of the Role:

The Surveyor will report to the Senior Surveyor and will assist with the survey functions on the Snowy 2.0 project.

Qualifications:

  • Tertiary Qualifications or related discipline such as, advanced diploma or higher in surveying and spatial sciences
  • White Card

Experience:

  • Minimum 3 years' experience working in the Construction Industry in a similar role - preferred
  • Experience working on large scale construction projects or other suitable projects works

Experience in surveying software's including but not limited to: AutoDesk, 12D model, Leica Infinity, Mircrosurvey Starnet,

Required Competencies:

  • Sound knowledge of Australian standards, regulations and policies
  • Competent in use of surveying computer based software
  • Ground Settlement Monitoring (automatic & manual systems) including reporting of data
  • Good understanding of Australian and International Standards relating to concrete and earthworks
  • Exceptional verbal and written communication skills
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Ability to collaborate across technical functions, site construction engineers and external stakeholders Sound interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work collaboratively
  • Ability to work to deadlines and targets and can prioritise tasks under pressure
  • Able to multitask, prioritize, and manage time efficiently
  • Good interpersonal skills and communication
  • Strong computer skills; Microsoft Office Suite or similar software
  • Strong organizational skills and strong attention to detail
  • Excellent safety awareness with good housekeeping habits.
  • Ability to carry out work instructions and directives to provide satisfactory outcomes.
  • Ability to work within a team environment to achieve desired results

Core Accountabilities and Responsibilities:

  • The surveyor is responsible for marking all lines and levels required to guide accurate and consistent construction
  • They are primarily responsible for collecting all as-built data and saving the data into the correct format.
  • Ensure the accuracy of survey data including measurements and calculations.
  • Provide survey services to ensure the accurate location and layout of work within the lines, grades and elevations indicated in the design drawings.
  • Provide survey activities to meet project work schedules.
  • Work in conjunction with the tunnelling and construction teams
  • Support the Senior Surveyor in liaising with Design management as required to confirm consistency of any proposed changes to reference design with relevant environmental / contractual requirements

The above listed authorities & responsibilities are only a guide and may change as directed by your Manager/Supervisor or as per the requirements of FGJV Management.

These positions are in remote challenging areas with extreme cold temperatures which need to be considered before applying for these roles.
