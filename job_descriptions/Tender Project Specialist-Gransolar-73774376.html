At Gransolar Group we strongly believe that all our actions must be guided by our values and ethical principles. In this way, we can contribute positively to the development of the communities that we work in and to provide our employees an opportunity to develop themselves professionally and personally in each project.

We believe in the talent and strength of our team, so that, in aim to keep growing together, we are looking for a Tender Project Specialist for our Tendering Team in Brisbane.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • To manage engineering, procurement and construction (EPC) proposal submissions and contract negotiations for large scale photovoltaic projects (PV), battery energy storage solutions (BESS) projects and hybrid projects (PV + BESS).
  • To work closely with the technical, commercial and procurement departments within Gransolar Group in Australia and have a good understanding of the company's global capabilities and focus areas.Assessment of tender documentation received from the client, ensuring a full and complete understanding of the technical and commercial requirements.
  • Planning of the preparation of the tender, to ensure the proposal is compliant, is submitted on time, and is within budget.
  • Coordination of preliminary engineering to be performed as part of the tender on the basis of the requirements from the end Client. Selection of main equipment, technology to be used, site specific particularities to be accounted for, etc.
  • Coordination with the legal and construction teams to complete the mark-up of the contract schedules.
  • Engagement with clients to communicate GRS expertise and experience in the sector, to understand the tenders they are running, and to seek potential collaboration schemes.
  • Engagement with subcontractors and suppliers to get specific quotes for the different projects, clarification of their queries, and assessment of their offers once received.
  • Preparation of the tender including: budget preparation, execution strategy, site visit and project conditions review, identification of main risks and mitigation measures, preparation of the construction schedule.
  • When a project is awarded, interface with the client and coordination of the contract negotiations and lenders due diligence during the EPC and O&M contracts closing.

Experience required:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering or Science required.
  • Experience in bidding and business development.
  • Min 2 year’s relevant work experience within solar and/or battery storage industry.
  • Excellent oral and written communication and presentation skills.
  • Proficient skills in computers: MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Adobe PDF, email.
  • Advanced skills in MS Project.
  • Advanced skills in AutoCAD.
  • Master’s degree in Engineering, Science or Business Administration preferred (but not essential).
  • Knowledge of large scale solar and Battery Energy Storage Systems (but not essential).
