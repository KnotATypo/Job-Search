MiTek is a construction technology leader on a mission to advance the adoption of off-site construction. For the Brisbane office, we are seeking an energetic and dynamic Structural Design Engineer.

Client Details

As a subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway, MiTek Australia is a globally diversified provider of engineered products, automation systems, and software with a presence in more than 100 countries.

Focused primarily on the residential and commercial construction sectors, the company offers advanced technological solutions to transform the way we design, make build.

Reporting to the Design Engineering Manager, the Structural Design Engineer will be responsible for providing engineering and technical support both to external and internal stakeholders in construction projects, involving timber.

Description

As the Structural Design Engineer, you will be responsible for:

  • Services and technical advice for architects, engineers, builders, and others in timber engineering, mainly trusses/floor trusses and frames and their application.
  • Providing engineering and technical support including designs, software support, rectifications, and site inspections for fabricator clients.
  • Participate in the development of new products and solutions.
  • Liaise with MiTek clients and provide the highest quality customer service in terms of engineering support tailored to client's requirements.
  • Develop structural designs for fabricators, architects, and engineers.
  • Stay updated on advancements in timber and structural engineering.
  • Collaborate closely with other engineers to ensure the delivery of top-tier service.
  • Contribute to training courses for client staff.
  • Conduct on-site inspections for roof trusses, wall frames, and floor trusses for certification or forensic investigations. Prepare reports containing professional advice and recommendations to ensure smooth communication.
  • Engage in relevant professional development activities to maintain or pursue accreditation by Engineers Australia.

Profile

The ideal Structural Design Engineer meets the following requirements:

  • Degree in Structural Engineering, Civil Engineering, or similar.
  • 3-5 years of experience in the role, desirable knowledge of timber structures
  • Proficient in Structural Engineering
  • Passionate about timber
  • Excellent communication skills required to liaise with different levels of stakeholders
  • Knowledge of AU standards applicable to residential/light commercial construction
  • Willingness to deliver site inspections in not confined spaces
  • Familiarity with software design solutions (AutoCAD)
  • willingness to comply with Engineers Australia Code of Ethics
  • Eligibility or commitment to work toward recognition with NER or CPEng programs.
