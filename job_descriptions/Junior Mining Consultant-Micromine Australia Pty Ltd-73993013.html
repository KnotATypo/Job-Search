About The Opportunity

Our Precision Consulting Team leverage their deep industry experience to enable clients to optimise their operations and reduce costs. As the company continues to thrive, an opening has become available for a Junior Mining Consultant at our Brisbane, Fortitude Valley office.

In this role you will work very closely with Senior Consultants to create customised solutions for our mining clients. Other responsibilities will include:

  • Work closely with our mining clients to gather requirements and consult on projects.
  • Use those requirements to script customised solutions within the Micromine Spry software using C#.
  • Provide general technical input as part of the Precision Consulting business, including training and product development.
  • Building client relationships and identifying further opportunities.
  • Contribute to the ongoing enhancement of the Micromine software.

About You

In this Junior Mining Consultant position you will have a tertiary degree in engineering with experience and/or interest in the mining industry.

Other key skills, experience, and qualifications:

  • Very good communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Understanding or experience within mining will be highly desirable.
  • Comfortable with periodic travel to client mine site.
  • Scripting experience in C# is a bonus
  • Consulting experience is a bonus
