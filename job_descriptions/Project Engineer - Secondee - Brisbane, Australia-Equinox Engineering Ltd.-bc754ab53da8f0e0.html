Description


Equinox is looking for an experienced and talented Project Engineer - for a secondment role (initially 12 months) with one of Equinox’s key clients. The Client is one of Australia’s leading Energy producers – based in Brisbane.
The Project Engineer role will support a large gas processing project and be based in the client’s office in the Brisbane CBD. The project involves the multi-year design and construction of a large gas gathering & processing / field compression program in the Surat Basin of Queensland. This would also lead into construction field and commissioning support.
The role is largely a Project Engineering role and would support the Owners Team project and stakeholder management including overseeing contract packages, project controls, stakeholder support management, technical meetings etc. The role will not be overly technical however Oil & Gas experience would be an asset.


Key Skills & Abilities


  • Must possess strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Coal Seam Gas project experience an asset.
  • EPCM experience valued.
  • Experience in overseeing Contract Packages for oil & gas facilities.
  • Experience in Project Controls in an engineering firm environment.


Key Responsibilities


  • This Owners Team’s team role would be as a support to the lead Facility Project Engineer/Manager and would be a full- time in office role at the Clients office, in Brisbane.
  • Managing/coordinating the interface between Vendors / Contractor’s (both Designer and Constructions) / owner’s team.
  • Interfacing with internal functional team (FEED, Procurement, HSE, ICT etc.); Understand, establish the requirement of various aspects and execute in accordance with agreed procedure and process.
  • Managing various packages / Vendors that applicable to the scope (both technical scope and non-technical scope).
  • Coordinating pre-mobilisation activities for Contractors (preferably in QLD CSG industry).
  • Coordinating the establishment of the construction / commissioning completion system.
  • Strong in risk identification and mitigation to support over all project planning, monitoring and reporting.
  • Attend Technical meetings as required.


Education and Qualifications


  • 3 to 7 years of experience in an EPCM Oil & Gas engineering environment an asset


About Equinox Engineering Ltd.

Established in 1997, Equinox is a distinguished EPCM service provider globally. Our wide-ranging portfolio includes Sweet and Sour Gas Processing Facilities, Heavy and Conventional Oil Production, Steam Pipeline Systems, and an increasing focus on sustainable energy solutions like Carbon Capture, Utilization, and Storage (CCUS) facilities and pipelines, Renewable Natural Gas (RNG) projects, and Landfill Gas (LFG) initiatives.

As a market leader in natural gas projects, we specialize in sour gas ventures, having executed thousands of projects from remote wellsite tie-ins to comprehensive gas processing facilities.

In the realm of heavy oil, our team excels in thermal in-situ executions for bitumen production and processing, extending our proficiency to steam generation and delivery systems, as well as water de-oiling and treatment.

Equinox is committed to sustainable energy practices, playing a pivotal role in technological advancements in energy production. We excel in the design and implementation of CCUS facilities, RNG, and LFG projects, and their associated pipelines.

Our work in conventional oil projects is comprehensive, spanning from wellsites and gathering systems to central batteries, pipeline, and pump station projects.

In every endeavor, whether it's an EPCM grass roots project or a complex facility modification, our unwavering dedication to quality and sustainability ensures we deliver the most effective and environmentally responsible solutions in a fast-paced energy sector.
