Front End Software Engineer

Are you a motivated Front End Engineer, looking to kick start your career and ready to join a dynamic development team with a fun and inclusive culture? We want to hear from you!

About iVolve

As an Australian industry leader, iVolve offers cutting-edge technology solutions to small and mid-tier mining operations, quarries, and heavy industries both domestically and across the globe. Our real-time data capabilities empower clients to execute informed decisions, elevating their operational efficiency and strategic initiatives.

Our client base includes three of the largest entities in the sector, and our burgeoning presence in the Americas and Africa is a testament to our global impact and industry prowess.

Responsibilities:

- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design and develop robust, scalable, and high-performance frontend applications for our mining and quarry fleet management software.

- Leverage your expertise in ASP.Net web development, .Net Core, and .Net 6 to enhance and maintain the functionality of our cutting-edge software solutions.

- Implement responsive and user-friendly interfaces using frontend frameworks, ensuring optimal user experience for our clients in the mining and quarry industry.

- Customize use of frontend UI libraries Bootstrap and DevExtreme to provide feature-rich and efficient solutions tailored to the unique needs of fleet management.

- Work closely with backend developers to seamlessly integrate frontend components with our state-of-the-art fleet management system.

- Prioritize and implement security measures for web APIs, ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of sensitive data in compliance with industry standards.

- Optimize applications for speed and scalability to meet the demanding requirements of fleet management operations.

Requirements

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.

- Proven experience in ASP.Net web development, .Net Core, and .Net 6.

- Experience in JavaScript and Node.js, with a focus on creating frontend solutions for complex software systems.

- In-depth knowledge of Vue 3 and Vite, demonstrating the ability to deliver exceptional user interfaces.

- Solid understanding of CSS and its best practices for designing responsive and visually appealing interfaces.

- Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills.

- Strong communication and collaboration skills to work effectively within a team.

Advantageous

- Experience with DevExpress and DevExpress UI library to design and implement feature-rich UIs for fleet management applications.

- Experience with fleet management systems or related industries.

- Experience with Machine learning and AI, and programming languages like Python/R

- Familiarity with authorization and security practices for web APIs, with a focus on protecting sensitive fleet data.

- Contributions to open-source projects or a strong online presence showcasing your work.

- Certifications related to software development and/or cloud development
