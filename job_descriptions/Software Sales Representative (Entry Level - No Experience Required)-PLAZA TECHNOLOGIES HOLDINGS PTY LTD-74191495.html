The role & the team

We are seeking a motivated and enthusiastic Sales Development Representative to join our dynamic team. As an entry-level role, you will be responsible for generating new business opportunities and establishing relationships with potential clients. You will also play a crucial role in the company's growth by proactively reaching out to prospects, qualifying leads, and setting up meetings for the founding team.

This is an excellent opportunity for individuals who are eager to kick-start their career in sales and gain valuable experience in a fast-paced environment.

Day to day

  • Conduct outbound prospecting activities, including cold calling, email campaigns, and social media outreach, to generate and qualify leads.
  • Identify key decision-makers and influencers within target accounts and engage them in meaningful conversations to understand their needs and pain points.
  • Articulate and effectively communicate our value proposition, product features, and benefits to prospects, highlighting how our solution can address their specific challenges.
  • Collaborate with the founding team to strategically develop and execute sales strategies, ensuring a seamless handoff of qualified leads.
  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date records of all activities, conversations, and opportunities in the CRM system.
  • Collaborate with the marketing team to provide feedback on lead quality and contribute to the development of sales collateral and campaigns.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends, market dynamics, and competitive landscape to effectively position our solutions.
