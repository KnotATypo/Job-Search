Are you looking for a rewarding, well-paid career where you can use your customer service or recruitment experience to help people realise their career potential?

About us .

Exclaim IT’s mission is to connect tech and digital professionals to opportunities that help them grow throughout their careers. We have over 15 years of industry experience and a reputation for quality service delivery.


With offices in Brisbane and Canberra and a high-spirited team who support each other to get stuff done, we’ve achieved significant year on year growth. Now, we look to further build on our enviable success by investing in our future, our people!

About the role .

Join our growing Brisbane branch as an Engagement Specialist and help job seekers and contractors find their next role.


The Engagement Specialist position is dynamic and fast-paced, often emphasised by short deadlines. So, you’ll never have a dull moment. But you’ll share these moments with a tight-knit and inclusive team in Brisbane. In addition, your passionate and highly invested Team Lead will ensure you have all the support you need to meet your career potential.


As an Engagement Specialist, you’ll undertake an industry-leading induction training program. You’ll also have direct and ongoing access to coaching and development following this initial training. So, within the first year, you’ll confidently manage multiple roles at once.


Of course, the best part of your job will be the satisfaction that comes from connecting candidates with career-changing opportunities. The extra benefit will be the uncapped earnings as you become more and more proficient!

About you .

We’re looking for someone who embraces opportunity, is hungry for success and keen to bring their best. You’ll also need to remain humble, customer-focused and be a real team player.

Our Engagement Specialists have .

  • Solid relationship development skills
  • Excellent problem-solving ability
  • A passion for connecting with people
  • A competitive spirit and strong work ethic
  • A demonstrable track record in sales or customer service
  • Resilience and determination
  • While IT recruitment or agency recruitment experience is desirable, it’s not essential. We hire for potential!

  • A solid foundation to kick off your recruitment career
