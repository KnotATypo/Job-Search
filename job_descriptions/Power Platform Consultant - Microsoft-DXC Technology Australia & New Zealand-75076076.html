  • Good understanding of the Microsoft Dataverse, Microsoft SharePoint Online and build integrations with other data platforms.
  • Business / Systems Analysis

  • Bespoke collaboration portal development
  • Office 365 Platform Deployment
  • Office 365 Platform Support

  • Ability to work and engage with business users and key stakeholders to propose, design, develop and automate application solutions as required.

  • Contributes to applying Microsoft best practice, in role played, to determine the methodological steps that are appropriate for an engagement.

  • Establishes credibility at all levels with customers and builds problem-solving partnerships with clients and colleagues.
  • Understands sales process, information and support requirements, and assists sales staff in identifying leads.
