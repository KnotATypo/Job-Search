Overview

Are you passionate about developing top-notch solutions for Azure Kubernetes Service customers? If so, we have an exciting opportunity for you! Azure Kubernetes Service is at the forefront of innovation, leveraging cutting-edge technologies to deliver exceptional solutions to Azure customers.

As a Software Engineer II, on the Azure Kubernetes Service team, you will have the chance to work across various layers of the technology stack. From enhancing the user experience for our customers to building scalable distributed cloud services that handle traffic from customers to Azure Kubernetes Service, your responsibilities will span the entire spectrum. This opportunity will allow you to, be involved in designing and implementing solutions that cater to global services operating at a massive scale. Collaboration is at the heart of our team, and you will work alongside a diverse group of individuals who embrace challenges, value continuous learning, and thrive in a collaborative environment. Additionally, this role offers mentorship opportunities, career growth prospects, and involvement in high-impact areas of the business.
