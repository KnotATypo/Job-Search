Who is M2P Engineering?

M2P Engineering are a market leading construction, engineering and fabrication service provider specialising in heavy industrial Structural, Mechanical, and Piping (SMP) projects across Australia.

But put simply, we are a business based on values and ethics: we want to treat people well, work bloody hard, have skin in the game and do the job properly.

It’s fabrication and construction – the way it should be.

A few quick notes about what we do:

  • To date, our key achievements include major SMP construction projects at remote mine sites, both surface and underground, and industrial facilities in our key operating centres.
  • Scopes we have successfully completed include crushing and conveying systems, mineral processing facilities, ventilation, refrigeration and associated infrastructure.
  • We have recently expanded our service offering to include Energy, Oil and Gas projects.
  • M2P have a number of strategically placed workshops across the country where we fabricate heavy industrial pipework, platework and structural steel.
  • We also design & manufacture our own patented specialised inflatable products, which are used in a number of innovative industrial safety, ventilation & production applications.

We've built all of this with determination, plenty of hard work and a little bit of luck, in the short space of time, since M2P was established in 2017 by our MD, Adam (although, there is still much more ahead of us that we'd like you to be a part of).

Want to know more about what could be your new role?

While we continue to grow, we are now a much larger, forward-thinking business. We’d like to give back and present opportunities for a Graduate Mechanical Engineer to join our team at Salisbury, Brisbane.

Why choose M2P?

We’re not for everyone, but if you’re keen to get stuck in and get the job done, you’ll be offered a variety of opportunities you may not be exposed to anywhere else, including but not limited to:

  • getting out to site and getting your hands dirty
  • learning scheduling, project controls and procurement functions
  • gaining invaluable experience and knowledge from some of the best engineers and project managers
  • Undertake estimation and detailing work in our workshops.

You’ll also have some ownership of your own destiny – there’s no strict or limiting program here, we’ll support you to find what you love and how to get there.

Your career - the way it should be. Get it done with M2P.

The work we do, isn’t easy, our people are resilient, and they have each other’s backs. It’s a real sense of mates looking after mates and rising to the challenge together.

Some of the things you can expect to be involved in when you join the team include:

  • Production planning and coordination of fabrication packages across our workshops.
  • Working closely with production to maximise the utilisation of our automated robot welding and CNC beamline equipment.
  • Supporting the Engineering team with the preparation of project documentation. e.g: tender documents, scope of works documents and design drawing packages.
  • Liaise with clients, internal teams, suppliers and sub-contractors as required.
  • Developing work packs, including the creation, compilation and review of technical and quality documents required.
  • Providing input into daily, weekly and monthly reporting as well as project claims.
  • Assist with technical planning input into the development of project schedules and ensuring the respective milestones are met by engaging with all key stakeholders.

There are some important skills and experience we believe will support you in succeeding in this role, including the most important - a bachelor’s degree in mechanical or structural engineering.

What’s it like working at M2P?

We work bloody hard, and we expect plenty of each other. Our people get that because that’s how we win. And it’s how you will, too. Here, you’ll work shoulder to shoulder with an honest, reliable and respectful team. They’ll step up for you and do what it takes to get the job done right.

We’re winning but we’re not sitting back. We’ve got our eyes on the future, and we’re growing and evolving all the time. So, if you’re ready to get stuck into good, honest work… to build a secure career for the long run… and to help make a great business even better, get it done at M2P.

We believe someone who has the following qualities, will enjoy this role and the working environment:

  • People who are intrinsically motivated.
  • Those who thrive on meeting deadlines and exceeding expectations.
  • Someone who is comfortable with changing priorities and capable of "flexing" to differing requirements.

The above gives you a taste of who we are, what you will be doing, the way we do things and the environment in which you'll be doing it. If this aligns with your values and how you like to operate, we'd love to hear from you and have you join us in writing the next chapter of M2P history.
