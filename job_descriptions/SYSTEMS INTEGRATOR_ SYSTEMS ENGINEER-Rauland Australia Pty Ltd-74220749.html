About the Role:

Reporting to the ICT Delivery Manager, work closely with a broad internal team and external customers. You will be supported to perform duties including:

  • Designing deployments and implementing our Concentric Care solution.
  • Integrating our Concentric Care Platform with 3rd party ICT system.
  • Providing technical escalation to the ICT service desk and field service team.
  • Supporting and delivering ICT projects and ICT-managed services.
  • Creating technical and user documentation including design architecture documents, systems diagrams, testing plans and service documentation.
  • Collaborating closely with ICT Consultants, Hospital and/or Aged Care IT Departments, clinicians, and carers, as well as internal stakeholders across various disciplines
  • Providing sales support pertaining to the technical implementation of Concentric Care solutions

About You:

You are self-motivated, energetic, enthusiastic and enjoy building strong relationships with all stakeholders internally and externally, as well as your team. You thrive on challenges (and challenging assumptions), creative problem-solving, providing recommendations and being accountable. Managing time, priorities and expectations in a dynamic environment, while demonstrating leadership as required, are what keep you interested.

Skills & Experience:

  • Administration and implementation of Linux-based (CentOS, Red Hat Linux, Oracle Linux) Operating Systems. (Essential)
  • Fundamental knowledge of ITSM Principles of change management, incident management and problem management as per ITIL framework.
  • Working experience in complex ICT systems integration, as well as the deployment of ICT systems and applications.
  • Proven expertise in executing technical project delivery with a focus on quality.
  • Well-developed interpersonal, written, and oral communication skills.

Exposure to below technologies is highly desirable:

  • Programming and Scripting Language (PowerShell, Python, Bash, PHP)
  • SQL Database Systems (MSSQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, MySQL)
  • Cloud Computing Technologies (AWS, Microsoft Azure)
  • Containerisation Technologies (Kubernetes, Docker)
