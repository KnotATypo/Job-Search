NOJA Power designs, manufactures and supplies medium voltage switchgear products to over 100 countries globally.  The Group operates from our manufacturing campus and global headquarters in Murarrie, Brisbane with Factory and Offices in Brazil, Canada, UK, USA, China and Vietnam.

We are committed to an inclusive environment where people feel comfortable to be themselves.  We want our people to feel that their voices are heard, all cultures respected and that a variety of perspectives are not only welcome, but they are also essential to our success.

About the Role:

We are seeking applications for the position of System Test Engineer for our global headquarters and manufacturing campus in Murarrie, Brisbane.  You’ll be part of a supportive team, backed by your Leader to grow and achieve your own personal goals in the team.  This role is a full-time position Monday to Friday.

Reporting to the Testing Manager, your key responsibilities will include but not limited to:

  • Develop, execute test plans, and test cases for software application (mobile and desktop), firmware.
  • Document and monitor testing outcomes weekly and communicate testing updates to relevant project stakeholders. Collaborate with developers to resolve issues.
  • Perform manual and automated testing of NOJA Power products.
  • Contribute to building test automation framework.
  • Assist in product release documentation, product handover to all relevant teams (QA, Service, Engineering and Production)
  • Assist development team or service team on customer support cases when required.
  • Test interim builds weekly, generate weekly build test reports, perform regression testing frequently.
  • Perform Benchmark testing (Performance) and Stability testing frequently.

What You’ll bring:

  • A team player with keen learning and can-do attitude, motivated and energetic self-starter with willingness to contribute to wider organization.
  • Good verbal and written communication are a must.
  • Software application (mobile and desktop) and/or firmware test engineering experience.
  • Experience in Testing life cycle, System testing, Software Quality Assurance, strong knowledge of QA methodologies, tools, processes, and other software issue tracking/documentation tools (Jira, Confluence).
  • Experience in scripting languages preferred for test automation (Python), previous use of any test framework and Linux experience is beneficial but not required.
  • An Engineering background (Computer science, Software, Electrical or Electronics) preferred but not required.
  • Experience in relevant industry such as Automotive, Aeronautics, Hi-Tech, Mining, Electrical, Energy or Manufacturing is beneficial but not required.
  • Good understanding of networking concepts, ports or protocols or previous test experience in networking preferred but not required.
  • Any experience or working knowledge of IEEE 1686 Cyber Security will be highly regarded.

What we offer:

  • A work environment where safety is always number one priority.
  • A permanent position working for NOJA Power.
  • A competitive salary reflective of your skills and experience.
  • Career development and opportunities to grow your knowledge.
  • Job security, knowing you’re working for an award-winning successful manufacturer which is Australian owned, operated and based in Brisbane.

This role will provide you the challenges and rewards you have been searching for.
