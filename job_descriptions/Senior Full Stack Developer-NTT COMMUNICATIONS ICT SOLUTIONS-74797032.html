Want to be a part of our team?

We are seeking an experienced and highly skilled Senior Full Stack Developer with a strong background in cross-platform mobile app development. As a part of the team, this specialized role would contribute to planning, designing, and executing the migration of Xamarin apps to .NET MAUI. This role is pivotal in ensuring a smooth transition of mobile apps to a supported platform.

Working at NTT

Key Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Consult with clients in the planning and design phase of application development projects to determine client needs and solution design. Initially this will involve:
    • Collaborating with clients and other team members to assess and plan the migration of clients' Xamarin apps
    • Designing and architecting migrated mobile app solutions ensuring that the new app maintains or enhances the user experience and functionality of the existing Xamarin app
  • Provide technical leadership to project application development teams
  • Provide technical guidance and mentorship to junior developers
  • Develop applications that effectively accomplish client objectives and user needs
  • Address technical challenges and troubleshoot issues that may arise during projects
  • Conduct code reviews to ensure code quality and adherence to best practices
  • Create comprehensive technical documentation.

Knowledge, Skills, and Attributes

  • Proficiency in C# and .NET framework
  • Proven experience in cross-platform mobile development (Xamarin app development: Xamarin.Forms, Xamarin.Native and XAML)
  • Familiarity with .NET MAUI
  • Experience with migration projects, refactoring, and code optimization
  • Experience in technical lead role
  • Strong problem-solving and debugging skills
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
  • Knowledge of mobile app design principles and UI/UX concepts
  • Familiarity with version control systems.

Academic Qualifications and Certifications

Bachelor's degree in computer science or software engineering
