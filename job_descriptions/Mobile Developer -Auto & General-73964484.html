About us

At Auto & General (A&G), we provide great products and services to safeguard our customers in their time of need. Our range of general insurance products protect customers on the road, at home and on holiday with various Car, Motorcycle, Home, Contents, Pet products as well as Roadside Assistance.

Our culture of ‘high performance with high integrity’ underpins our values and the way we interact with our customers, the community and each other. We’re excited about the future and we’re always on the lookout for talented, passionate individuals who can help us achieve our goal of being Australia’s best insurer! If this sounds like you, apply today.

Purpose of the Role

Be part of a brand new team focused on developing a new market-leading mobile “Super App” for A&G customers using Flutter/Dart.

You will be a member of a small, dedicated team with the end-to-end responsibility for the successful execution of this new mobile strategy.

Supported by a Senior Mobile Developer, product owner, and business analyst you will contribute to the development of a new mobile service with features and functionality designed to provide our customers with an engaging and feature-rich market-leading experience. You will bring your development experience and technical skill and be responsible for the delivery of features from an extensive development pipeline.

You will be responsible for coding the mobile frontend, in flutter and coordinating with the other developers in the Mobile App team, to achieve the technical delivery to the agreed strategy and roadmap.

You’ll be a part of a significant strategy to create and maintain Australia’s best mobile insurance experience.

Position Accountabilities

  • Work with the product owner and business analyst on the product feature roadmap to specify development to deliver the feature set/functionality catalog in line with the agreed strategic approach.
  • Work closely with CX/UX and UI Specialists to ensure consistency of customer experience.
  • Code and software design, including UI/UX components, data structures, API integrations (to mobile backend, Authentication APIs), creation of re-usable interfaces/modules etc.
  • Production of high-quality code and structures to build features from the backlog to deliver against the roadmap - ensuring that features are designed & built to specification without bottlenecks.
  • Performance of key Agile duties; assistance with backlog and sprint management, estimates, troubleshooting, and problem-solving.
  • Stable execution and deployment of the resulting App & features, including Google Play and Apple App Store approval and deployment.
  • Ensure that the resulting product adheres to IT Sec best practices.
  • Conduct code peer reviews, and ensure that code is well documented and conforms to good practice. i.e. clean efficient, reusable code 2
  • Unit testing, and regression testing, both manual and automated. To ensure the resulting code is of high quality.
  • Comfortable using a distributed source control environment (e.g. Git) and common methods of operation (e.g. feature branches, push/pull request workflow etc)
  • Responsible for the build and deployment of features alongside the testing team and the broader business before release to our customers
  • Actively participate, contribute, and execute against a Mobile Community of Practice to ensure alignment across the wide A&G businesses.
  • Review and interpret log, crash, and monitoring data from mobile app users and consolidate information into actionable items, defects, and tracking.

Knowledge and Experience:

  • 5+ years of development/Strong coding skills with other OO-based languages (e.g. Java, C# etc)
  • 3+ years of mobile development experience with Flutter, possessing a strong understanding of state management and cross-platform development for Android and iOS mobile applications.
  • Proficiency with testing frameworks and writing automated tests
  • Proven record of delivering Mobile App solutions to market including signing/packaging apps for GooglePlay/AppStore/Testflight
  • Experience with both Apple (iOS) and Android (AOS) apps, including Apple HIG and Google Material Design principles.
  • Strong agile/scrum methodology knowledge.
  • Experience integrating RESTful / other standardized APIs
