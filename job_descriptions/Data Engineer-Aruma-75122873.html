About the role…

The Data Engineer plays a crucial role in offering business support through the effective management and transformation of data. This entails possessing a comprehensive understanding of various aspects including data governance, collection, pipelines, mapping, cleansing, analysis, and business intelligence. The role requires prior experience in implementing data engineering solutions to support data and analytics business platforms. An essential aspect of the role involves translating business requirements into structured data formats that facilitate and enhance business systems, processes, and analytics.

A day in the life of a Data Engineer ….

  • Utilise Azure data storage and processing solutions, including Microsoft Fabric.
  • Design and implement a consolidated data management platform with efficient ingestion of data from vendors/partners and source systems, automated collation and exception identification, transformation to canonical dataset(s) and/or data mart schemas and maintenance of data governance standards.
  • Deliver quality data to the Enterprise Insights environment for analysis and reporting.
  • Support data literacy and increase platform adoption and efficiency.
  • Provide day-to-day support and resolution of data-related queries and issues.
  • Support BAU tasks within the landscape of legacy data solutions.
  • Participate in data governance initiatives.
  • Facilitate transition to the new platform where applicable.
  • Understand business and technical rules in legacy solutions.
  • Support teams throughout transformation/business project lifecycle.
  • Understand scope, design, and assess impacts to current data and reporting.
  • Design and undertake data migration activities to support systems upgrades and evolution.

What you need to be successful in this role…

  • Sound understanding of conceptual, architectural, and technical principles in data management, data lake/warehouse, and visualization solutions.
  • Experience with ETL/ELT tools and solutions.
  • Ability to comprehend business goals, rules, and requirements, addressing stakeholder pain-points for efficient and accessible solutions.
  • Efficient collaboration with management and front-line employees, transitioning between detailed data and high-level insights.
  • Strong organisational planning and implementation skills, adaptable in rapidly changing business environments.
  • Adoption of lifecycle management of data solutions, including source code management and technical documentation.
  • Proficient in issue investigation and resolution.
  • Demonstrated accuracy, attention to detail, effective time management, and self-motivation.
  • Results-oriented with an agile mindset, able to work effectively under pressure to meet deadlines.

Skills and Experience to Stand Out:

  • Minimum 5 years' experience in business intelligence and data engineering.
  • Conceptual understanding of Data Warehouse/Data Lake principles.
  • Experience in data modeling and ETL design.
  • Solid software development experience, including full systems development life cycle and formal testing approaches.
  • Technical writing skills for documenting requirements and designs.
  • Understanding of Azure fundamentals.
