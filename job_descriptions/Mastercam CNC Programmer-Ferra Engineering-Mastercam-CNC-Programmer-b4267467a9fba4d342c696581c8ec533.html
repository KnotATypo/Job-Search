Ferra Engineering is currently seeking a highly skilled and motivated Mastercam CNC Programmer to join our dynamic team. As a Mastercam CNC Programmer, you will play a crucial role in programming 3, 4 and 5 axis CNC machines to produce high precision parts according to engineering specifications.

Responsibilities

  • Generate accurate, high quality, and timely programs using Mastercam for 3, 4 and 5 axis machining centres.
  • Generate shopfloor documentation, setup sheets, and process instructions.
  • Optimize CNC programs for cycle time reduction and continuous improvement while maintaining quality standards.
  • Design, model, and program fixturing requirements.
  • Stay current with industry trends, best practices, and advancements in CNC programming technologies.

. To be considered for this role you must have:

  • Minimum 3 years proven experience as a CNC Programmer, with expertise in Mastercam software.
  • Hands on experience with 3-5 axis CNC machines is preferable.
  • Proficient in reading and interpreting engineering drawings and GD&T.
  • Trade or Engineering qualifications.
  • Excellent problem-solving and troubleshooting skills.
  • Detail-oriented with a focus on producing high-quality work.
  • Effective communication skills and the ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
