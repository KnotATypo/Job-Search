Become part of a professional and dynamic team as a Data and Digital Officer within a NFP, member-based organisation with numerous branches across Queensland. Reporting directly to the Head of Digital & Data, this role presents a fantastic opportunity to contribute significantly to this high-profile organisation by ensuring the reliability and accuracy of data during their busiest periods!

About the role:
The key responsibilities of this role will include:
  • Conducting regular data integrity checks to uphold the accuracy and reliability of information.
  • Generating and issuing invoices and receipts
  • Payment checking to ensure legislation compliance
  • Uploading and editing digital content on websites
  • Creating and drafting marketing emails (copy provided)
  • Managing incoming calls to assist with inquiries regarding financial transactions e.g., processing payment over phone
  • Processing financial transactions via the iMIS database system in line with legal requirements

About you!
To be successful in this role, you will have the following skills/ attributes:
  • 1-2 years’ experience in a similar role with a NFP or member-based organisation
  • Experience with IMIS or similar CRM
  • Experience with Kentico or similar CMS
  • Absolute attention to detail
  • Maturity, reliability, and commitment to confidentiality.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft products (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively interact with people on all levels.
