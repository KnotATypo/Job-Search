About the role

Our government client is seeking an Application Technical Specialist to provide technical support and administration for a large range of diverse and complex Applications.

Key responsibilities

  • Provide expert technical support for Applications ensuring timely incident and problem identification and resolution to the agreed-stated levels of service.
  • Provide expert technical knowledge in the ongoing administration and optimisation of the organisation's Applications.
  • Participate in technical projects in relation to application design, evaluation, implementation, installation, and upgrades.
  • Assist the gathering, development, and maintenance of business requirement documentation.
  • Coordinate unit and business testing of various business system enhancements and upgrades.
  • Develop and maintain the operational procedures, policy and standards pertaining to the systems administration of Applications; ensuring all relevant information is exchanged and shared throughout the team where appropriate.
  • Maintain a high-level technical understanding of Seqwater's Applications
  • Ensure working relationships between ICT and customer business units is maintained to identify, agree, and meet customer requirements for core services provided.
  • Monitor and report on vendor support and consultancies in relation to Application enhancements and incident resolution.
  • Perform tasks/projects as directed by the Supervisor, Technical Specialists (Applications), on time and within budget allocation.
  • Observe and comply with all Seqwater Occupational Health and Safety standards and practices, Quality Management System Procedures and Environmental Management system procedures.

Skills and experience

  • Tertiary qualifications in an information technology discipline or associated business discipline and/or substantial experience commensurate with the duties and accountabilities of the position
  • ITIL certification is desirable.
  • Minimum three years' experience supporting and administering a large range of diverse and complex applications with a technical background and knowledge of hardware.
  • Knowledge of the Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) framework and how it applies to the management of ICT services.
  • Demonstrated ability to be proactive, innovative and deliver flexible approaches to solve problems, improve procedures and in the application of new technology.
  • Experience supporting SQL server.
  • Experience in the development and maintenance of high-quality standards and documentation supporting application systems administration functions.
  • Proven ability to work with minimal supervision, manage a heavy workload, and achieving results under pressure.
  • Strong communication and customer service skills with demonstrated ability to prioritise and manage competing demands, establish, and maintain working relationships with clients and peers and deliver to agreed service levels.
  • Knowledge and familiarisation with legislation and standards applying to information management.
