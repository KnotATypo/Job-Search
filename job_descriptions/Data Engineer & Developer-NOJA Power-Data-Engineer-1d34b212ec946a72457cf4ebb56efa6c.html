NOJA Power designs, manufactures and supplies medium voltage switchgear products to over 100 countries globally. The Group operates from our manufacturing campus and global headquarters in Murarrie, Brisbane with Factory and Offices in Brazil, Canada, UK & USA.

We are committed to an inclusive environment where people feel comfortable to be themselves. We want our people to feel that their voices are heard, all cultures respected and that a variety of perspectives are not only welcome, but they are also essential to our success.

About the Role:

We are seeking applications for the position of Data Engineer & Developer for our Head office in Murarrie, Brisbane. You’ll be part of a supportive team, backed by your Leader to grow and achieve your own personal goals in the team. This role is a full time position Monday to Friday.

Your key responsibilities will include but not limited to:

  • Take a lead role in NOJA Powers drive to improve data systems within our manufacturing setting.
  • Implement systems and processes that assist our transition to an Industry 4.0 environment.
  • This may include:
    • Digital data capture of full product build and test information
    • Automated data capture of production process information
    • Analytics on product assembly, product test and production process information
    • Support the addition of automation and robotics to production systems
    • Automation of warehouse data (physical warehouse), and its integration with ERP (SAP)
    • Set up and maintenance of data flows between systems
  • Guide and oversee the work of cadets (university students) who may assist in data systems.

What You’ll bring:

  • A keen interest and willingness to be involved in all aspects of database design, data capture, storage, processing, and analysis in a manufacturing environment.
  • Skills and experience in both front end software and back end systems
  • Understand the challenges of integrating system within a business environment.
  • Relevant tertiary qualifications with a couple years’ experience, or proven industry capabilities
  • A team player, comfortable working with staff at all levels of the business; engineers (mechanical, mechatronics, electrical), production assembly operators, managers, etc

What we offer:

  • A work environment where safety is always number one priority.
  • A permanent position working for NOJA Power.
  • A competitive salary reflective of your skills and experience.
  • Career development and opportunities to grow your knowledge.
  • Job security, knowing you’re working for an award-winning successful manufacturer which is Australian owned, operated and based in Brisbane.

This role will provide you the challenges and rewards you have been searching for.
