The role:

This successful candidate will be required to manage the design of highly complex and multi-faceted infrastructure based systems including application solutions; server and network configuration and layout; environment planning and design; firewall planning; and user privileges, ensuring compliance with project requirements and safety engineering standards.

In addition to this you will be responsible for:

  • Influence and provide expert technical advice and leadership to Queensland Health Program/Project teams and key decision makers relating to ICT project infrastructure testing, development and on-going integration.
  • Oversee and lead ICT technical implementations within a project management framework across multiple complex and diverse Queensland Health change programs and initiatives based on clients' business and technical requirements.
  • Provide high level input and advice to the Manager Design Team on infrastructure development planning to ensure that service delivery meets future client demands.
  • Work closely with other Design, Project, Operations and Architecture Office staff to manage collaborative relationships and consultations with senior stakeholders across Queensland Health and external technology vendors to develop, implement and integrate required changes to architecture, design and management of ICT infrastructure.
  • Lead the continual development and improvement of technical designs and solution architectures and ensure strategic alignment to enterprise-wide business and ICT architectures within the whole of Queensland Health.
  • Liaise and negotiate with internal and external stakeholders with a customer service focus, to resolve issues relating to technical concepts and ICT techniques and processes.
  • All staff are responsible for identifying, managing and reporting risks relating to their work.

Are you right for this role?

  • Demonstrated ability in designing, configuring and documenting enterprise systems with experience in some or all of the following hardware and software: Health Level Seven (HL7) based clinical system Interfacing, including messaging., Microsoft server and SQL, Citrix, VMware, Netware, Solaris, Oracle, various desktop applications and utilities. (SFIA: DESN 5)
  • Extensive experience, including exposure to industry certifications, such as Health Level Seven (HL7) Certification / Implementation, Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE), Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP), Certified Network Engineer (CNE) and Certified Citrix Administrator (CCA) (SFIA: TECH 6)
