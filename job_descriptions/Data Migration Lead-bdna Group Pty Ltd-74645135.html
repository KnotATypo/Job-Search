About the job

At bdna, we exist to improve society. We choose to only work with clients that have a direct impact on community wellbeing, safety and improvement - which means that we get to make a difference every day!

We are seeking a skilled and experienced Data Migration Lead to join our dynamic team for a long-term project. The Data Migration Lead will play a critical role in overseeing and executing data migration, ensuring the successful transfer of data from legacy systems to the new platform. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in data migration methodologies, data analysis, and project management, with a proven track record of delivering complex data migration initiatives on time and within budget. Some travel will be required.

What you'll do:

  • Lead end-to-end data migration for the project, including planning, analysis, design, execution, and validation phases.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to define data migration requirements, scope, and objectives, ensuring alignment with business goals and objectives.
  • Develop data migration strategies, methodologies, and best practices to ensure the efficient and accurate transfer of data.
  • Conduct data profiling and analysis to assess data quality, completeness, and integrity, identifying and resolving data issues as needed.
  • Design and implement data migration workflows, scripts, and tools to automate migration processes and minimize manual effort.
  • Manage data mapping, transformation, and validation activities, ensuring that data is migrated accurately and consistently across systems.
  • Coordinate with cross-functional teams, including IT, business users, and external vendors, to facilitate smooth data migration activities.
  • Monitor and report on data migration progress, risks, and issues, proactively addressing any challenges to ensure project success.
  • Lead the data migration team by providing guidance and support to team members, fostering their professional development and growth.
  • Document data migration processes, procedures, and outcomes, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards.

What you’ve got:

  • Ideally a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Systems or related field.
  • Proven experience leading data migration projects in complex, enterprise-level environments, within Public Safety and Policing a plus.
  • Strong technical skills in data analysis, data modelling, and SQL programming.
  • Proficiency with data migration tools and technologies, such as ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) tools, data integration platforms, and scripting languages.
  • Excellent project management skills, with the ability to prioritise tasks, manage resources, and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities, with keen attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively with diverse stakeholders.
  • Experience working with cloud-based platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure) and knowledge of data governance and compliance principles are desirable.
  • Relevant certifications in data management, project management, or related fields are a plus.
