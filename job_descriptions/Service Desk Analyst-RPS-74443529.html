Service Desk Analyst

We are a dynamic and innovative company seeking a talented individual to join our global Service Desk team. As a leading provider of technical support services, you will have the opportunity to work with cutting-edge technologies and provide exceptional service to our colleagues worldwide.

Responsibilities:

  • Provide frontline technical support for hardware, software, networking, and other PC-related technologies across multiple offices, locally and globally.
  • Utilise RPS’ ITSM and support toolsets to evaluate, prioritise, record, and manage incidents and service requests.
  • Ensure timely resolution of technical incidents and requests within defined SLAs.
  • Create user accounts and configure hardware for new hires and replacements.
  • Provide audio and video equipment support for offices and conference rooms.
  • Maintain a high 1st level fix rate by developing and maintaining comprehensive knowledge of supported technologies.
  • Create and update technical hardware and application knowledge articles.
  • Ensure continuous improvement by sharing and receiving knowledge on services and solutions.
  • Maintain accurate records for all computer and software assets.
  • Provide technical and administrative assistance to other Technology teams as required.
  • Flexibly work to ensure coverage of core business hours.

Education and Experience:

Essential

  • Diploma or degree in Information Technology or equivalent professional experience.
  • The ideal candidate will have an eagerness to learn, strong communication skills and knows how to build a computer from scratch and install software,
  • Proficient in deploying and maintaining Windows and Apple PC hardware and software.
  • Experience supporting mobile and printing peripheral devices.

Desirable

  • Work experience in a fast-paced service role.
