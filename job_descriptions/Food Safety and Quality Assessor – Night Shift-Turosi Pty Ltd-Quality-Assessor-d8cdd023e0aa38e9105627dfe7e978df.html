The Opportunity:

A vacancy currently exists for a for a Food Safety and Quality Assessor on our night shift at our Eagle Farm Site in Queensland.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Organise daily micro samples.
  • Pre-operational checks to clear equipment and area prior to production
  • Coordination of in-process and finished product samples for analytical analysis
  • Calibration of quality critical equipment as per schedules
  • Identify and partake in process improvement initiatives
  • Participate in relevant project work as required
  • Ensure that all personnel completing CCP checks are signed off as competent

Experience in the following areas are highly desirable:

  • A degree or diploma in Food Science or related discipline or relevant industry experience
  • Have strong attention to detail
  • Excellent word processing, filing and organizational skills
  • Excellent computer skills – in particular the Microsoft Office Suite
  • Capable analytical skills
  • Excellent and professional verbal and written communication skills
  • Good time management skills
  • Understanding of HACCP (certification)
  • Internal audit experience
  • Documentation maintenance of a quality assurance system manual
