Embedded Software Engineer

Are you passionate about working on products that genuinely make a difference in people's lives? Do you thrive when given the opportunity to take ownership of challenging projects? Do you get a kick out of developing innovative solutions on a global scale? We’d love to talk to you.

At Audeara, we’re building the next generation of audio products designed to deliver the highest quality personalised experience to all listeners. In 2021 Audeara won the coveted Hearing Technology Innovator Award and was listed on the ASX. Audeara’s headphones not only sound amazing, but are unique in that their simple app-based hearing test which tunes them perfectly to the user’s hearing profile. This means that regardless of hearing ability, the user experiences crystal clear sound quality customised for their ears.

Our dynamic team is focused on delivering powerful, market-changing products by engaging with and deeply understanding the needs of our customers. We’re looking to evolve our current product lines and develop new technology to serve different needs.

We are seeking a talented and experienced Embedded Software Engineer to join our team. In this role, you will be responsible for designing, developing and maintaining the embedded software in our audio products. You will work closely with stakeholders to gather requirements, design test scenarios, and deliver innovative solutions to meet project objectives.

Key responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, test and maintain high-quality embedded software for audio products on ARM platforms.
  • Gather requirements from stakeholders and translate them into actionable software specifications.
  • Design and execute test scenarios to ensure the quality and reliability of solutions.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including hardware engineers and software developers, to integrate firmware with hardware components.
  • Adhere to industry standards, coding guidelines, and best practices in embedded firmware development.
  • Preparation and maintenance of design documentation including product requirements, specifications, detailed hardware/software architecture designs, test plans, and test reports.

Need to have:

  • 4+ years of equivalent experience in embedded software/firmware development
  • Proficiency in C, C++, or assembly language
  • Strong understanding of embedded systems architecture and hardware interfaces
  • Experience with Agile development methodologies
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Experience with USB interface communications
  • Experience with Bluetooth technology and Bluetooth-enabled products
  • Ability to communicate effectively with stakeholders and team members
  • Legal rights to work in Australia (i.e., citizenship, permanent residency)

Nice to have:

  • Passion for music and audio technology
  • Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or related field
  • Experience in the audio technology industry or consumer-facing products
  • Knowledge of digital signal processing (DSP) techniques
  • Familiarity with audio codecs and audio processing algorithms
  • Exposure to healthtech, medical devices and/or audiology industry
  • Located in Brisbane, but we will happily consider strong applicants based anywhere in Australia as our team are spread across multiple cities
