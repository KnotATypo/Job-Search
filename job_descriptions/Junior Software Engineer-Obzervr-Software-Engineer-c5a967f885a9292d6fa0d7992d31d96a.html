About Us

Trusted by frontline teams every day across the globe, Obzervr transforms paper forms into a cloud-based, field mobility solution.

Our core competency is mobile productivity. We are passionate about technology & innovation, we love our product and we are constantly looking at ways to optimise our service offering within the Mining, Oil & Gas Sectors.

Role

We are growing, nationally and internationally, and we have an exciting opportunity for a software engineer to join our friendly team.

You will be responsible for building, testing, and improving our SaaS platform. You will work alongside a team of engineering, service delivery and support.

Responsibilities

  • Write understandable, testable, and secure code
  • Write unit and integration tests
  • Design features and estimate the effort to complete short-term coding tasks
  • Collaborate with the team, assisting over messaging tools, and attending stand-ups, workshops, and other meeting
  • Work with support personnel on a rotation basis to review issues that require developer clarification and confirmation

Qualifications

  • 1+ years of experience in software engineering for the web, mobile, cloud, or SaaS
  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, or equivalent

Technologies we use

  • Xamarin / MAUI
  • ASP.NET Core / C#
  • Angular / TypeScript
  • MS SQL / Azure SQL
  • MS Azure / Azure DevOps

Why join us?

You will enjoy our flexible working arrangements. You will also be working in a supportive team environment exposing you to all areas of the business. There are ample opportunities for skill development and growth, allowing you to maximise your potential.
