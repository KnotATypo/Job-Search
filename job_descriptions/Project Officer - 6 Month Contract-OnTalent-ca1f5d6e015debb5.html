Full job description

OnTalent is excited to partner with a leading regulatory body dedicated to upholding integrity and excellence in Queensland's racing industry in the search for a dynamic Project Officer.

Our client is committed to ensuring the highest standards of animal care and integrity in the racing industry. Join our diverse and inclusive workplace, where every employee's contribution is valued.

What's On Offer: As a Queensland Public Sector Statutory Authority, we offer:
  • Flexible working options to support work-life balance
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Health and wellbeing programs
  • Pet-friendly workplaces
  • A supportive and inclusive culture
The Role: As Project Officer, you will play a pivotal role in supporting the Change Program, ensuring the smooth delivery of key recommendations outlined in a Best Practice Review. Reporting to the Program Manager, Culture Change, your responsibilities will include:
  • Managing the development, implementation, and monitoring of project plans and progress.
  • Documenting policies and procedures to support employee understanding and compliance.
  • Coordinating project initiatives, including planning and performance evaluation processes.
  • Identifying and evaluating risks and implementing appropriate mitigation strategies.
  • Resolving operational problems and assisting in the development and implementation of initiatives.
  • Coordinating the implementation of policies, practices, and procedures related to the project.
  • Preparing reports, submissions, and briefings on program matters.
  • Participating in designated project and change activities.
  • Supporting required training and tracking implementation progress.
Key Competencies: We are seeking candidates who demonstrate the following competencies and personal qualities:
  • Excellent interpersonal skills to work effectively across a wide range of stakeholders.
  • Strong attention to detail, analytical, and problem-solving skills.
  • Demonstrated experience in project management and the application of supporting processes and tools.
  • Ability to manage conflicting priorities and escalate issues in a timely manner.
  • Proficiency in managing program information using various system tools, including MS Office Suite and Teams.
  • Ability to make good decisions based on analysis and sound judgment.
  • Supports a culture of achievement, strives for quality outcomes, and ensures compliance with regulatory requirements.
Special Requirements and Working Conditions: The successful candidate will:
  • Undergo pre-employment checks, including criminal history and probity checks.
  • Be subject to mandatory gambling restrictions policy.
How to Apply: Submit your current resume and a statement addressing key competencies (no more than 2 pages).
Join Our Team: If you're ready to make a positive impact and contribute to the success of Queensland's racing industry, apply now and become part of our dedicated team.
We are an equal opportunity employer and welcome applications from candidates of all backgrounds.
