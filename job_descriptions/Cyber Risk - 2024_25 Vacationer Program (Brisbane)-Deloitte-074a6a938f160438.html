Be part of Australia’s largest cohort of Vacationers – you’ll have a community of other students to lean on and learn from
Gain real experience at our clients as part of projects and engagements
Build transferable professional skills and networks as you work with our people and our clients

At Deloitte, we're not just looking for future cyber professionals; we're in search of the next generation of leaders who are inspired to tackle the evolving challenges of our technology world.

What will your typical day look like?
Our commitment is to match your exceptional skills and interests with a role that fits you perfectly, whether that's in advisory, transformation, engineering, or operations.
Embark on a journey with us to gain hands-on experience, mentorship from industry-leading experts, and the opportunity to contribute to crucial cyber security projects that are central to strengthening Australia's digital security framework. Join us in our mission to build a safer digital world for the future.

Who will you be working with?
Deloitte is proud to boast a network of over 12,000 cyber professionals globally and a robust portfolio of pivotal cyber projects. This expansive reach offers a diverse and dynamic range of opportunities for Vacationers like you.
Here, you'll find an unparalleled platform to launch and accelerate your career. We provide varied roles, from Security Architect and Engineering to managing emerging threats with innovative technologies, and developing robust frameworks designed to withstand advanced cyber threats.

Enough about us, let’s talk about you.
Your skills in offensive, defensive, security testing, cyber strategies, identity and access management, threat intelligence, digital forensics, and protecting data privacy in the midst of digital transformations are highly valued and can be honed with us. We know that as a student, you may not necessarily have significant experience in cyber security, and that’s okay! We are seeking candidates who:

Have a true passion for helping organisations address the many dimensions of cyber risk
Has likely majored in IT, Information Systems, Computer Science, Software Engineering, Cybersecurity or Business/ Commerce/Law with an interest in technology
Are quick to learn, naturally inquisitive and always up for a challenge
Have excellent communication and interpersonal skills
Works best collaboratively – you thrive when working in a team!
Is proactive in driving your career forward – you might have entered a few hackathons, capture the flag competitions, maybe you have a side hustle or even taught yourself how to code!
Have Australian permanent residency or Australian/New Zealand Citizenship

Why Deloitte?

At Deloitte, we focus our energy on interesting and impactful work. We’re always learning, innovating and setting the standard; making a positive difference to our clients and our society. We put coaching at the heart of what we do, helping our people grow their careers in any direction – whether it be up, moving into something new, or even moving across the world.

We embrace diversity, equity and inclusion. We have a diverse collection of people from different backgrounds, with different experiences, gender identities, abilities and thinking styles. What binds us together is a shared commitment to value everyone’s perspective and to cultivate inclusion; so that our work environment is a safe space we can all belong.

We prioritise flexibility and choice. At Deloitte, you get trust on Day 1. We know our people get their best work done when they’re in control of where and how they work, designing their work week around their client, team and personal commitments.

We help you live and work well. To support your personal and professional life, we offer a range of perks and benefits, including retail discounts, wellbeing leave, paid volunteering days, twelve flexible working options, market-leading parental leave and return to work support package.
