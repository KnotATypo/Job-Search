About the roles…

We're on the lookout for Trainee Soil and Concrete Technicians eager to kickstart a fulfilling journey with Construction Sciences. These positions are initially based south of Brisbane / Logan and are perfect for those at the outset of their careers. Our Trainee permanent wage rates typically start at $24.59 to $25.17 per hour, encompassing overtime, holiday, and sick pay, ensuring a comprehensive compensation package. We encourage applicants from all backgrounds to apply and join our team.

In these roles, you'll engage in various tasks, including testing soil and concrete samples, utilizing on-site testing equipment, handling manual tasks, and employing computer tools to analyze and validate results. Depending on your expertise, you may have the chance to work on customer construction sites, undertaking concrete & soil investigations, drilling, and related duties. Your career path is yours to shape - whether you prefer the lab environment or thrive on-site.

These roles welcome individuals of all genders who value safety, enjoy active work, and aspire to a long-term career with us.

  • Company benefits such as paid parental leave and access to a company ute for site-based roles
  • Negotiable pay for those with prior experience in soil or concrete work
