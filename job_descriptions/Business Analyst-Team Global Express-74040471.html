About the Role:

Are you passionate about leveraging data-driven insights to foster continuous improvement and drive business success? We are seeking an enthusiastic and analytical-minded Business Analyst to join our dynamic team.

As a Business Analyst, you will play a pivotal role in extracting, analyzing, and transforming data to provide actionable insights. Collaborating with various stakeholders, you will drive improvements through data-driven decision-making processes and contribute to our continuous improvement and customer management initiatives.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Extract, interrogate, and cleanse data from various sources using automated tools.
  • Perform in-depth analysis to derive actionable insights, presenting them through visualisation tools like PowerBi.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to identify and qualify improvement opportunities based on data analytics.
  • Develop and maintain databases and data systems to optimize statistical efficiency and quality.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify process improvement opportunities and devise data governance strategies.
  • Prepare comprehensive reports highlighting trends, patterns, and predictions derived from data analysis.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

  • Proficiency in SQL databases, reporting packages (Business Objects), PowerBi, R, Python, and MS Office.
  • 3+ years of Business Analyst experience in a transport, supply chain logistics industry
  • Demonstrated capability to work with large data volumes from diverse sources.
  • Strong communication skills, including the ability to develop presentations based on data analysis.
  • Qualifications in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Data Science, or related disciplines.
  • Experience working effectively in a team environment and managing competing priorities.
  • High attention to detail and the ability to meet critical deadlines in a fast-paced environment.

Desired Personal Attributes:

  • Proven problem-solving abilities and high interpersonal skills.
  • Self-motivated, results-driven, and adaptable to changing environments.
  • Strategic thinker with an innovative mindset and a focus on continuous improvement.
  • Integrity, accountability, and a customer-centric approach to work.
