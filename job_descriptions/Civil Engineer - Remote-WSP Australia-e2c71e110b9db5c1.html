Join our team of intelligent and collaborative Changemakers and make your mark on tomorrow!

WSP currently have an exciting role for a Civil Engineer to join our growing team in Brisbane.

Our Property and Buildings team is a world-leading management and technical consultancy operating across all sectors. We are passionate about tackling the complex and sophisticated challenges of the built environment and delivering innovative and sustainable solutions to create accessible, resilient and liveable communities.

Our recent projects span across a range of different sizes and sectors covering major Airports, Health, Education, Commercial, Retail and Defence. You won't just be working on QLD specific projects, but national projects across Australia where you will be proud to say, "I worked on that".

Our Changemakers enjoy:

  • Flexible working hours and a hybrid work environment
  • A social, collaborative, and inclusive culture with a calendar full of social and networking events
  • Access to our Employee Assistance Program and to corporate health and travel discounts
  • 12 weeks parental leave
  • Options for global mobility and purchased additional leave
  • And much more.

What you’ll do

As a Civil Engineer you will:

  • Have the opportunity to work on iconic projects within a multi-disciplined engineering team to deliver a range of civil engineering design projects
  • Manage ongoing communication and effective professional relationships with clients
  • Deliver our current civil engineering projects from inception through to construction in support of technical and commercial standards, and within time constraints and cost budgets
  • Support the wider team and other teams within the company nationally when the opportunity presents
  • Communicate effectively with internal and external stakeholders and be committed to team approach and outcomes
  • Provide ongoing continuous improvement and development inputs to create more efficient workflows within the team

About you

The successful candidate we are looking for will have:

  • Proficiency with 12D would be highly desirable
  • Typically 5+ years experience as a Civil Engineer in the Property and Building space within consulting
  • Degree in Bachelor of Civil Engineering or equivalent
  • Relevant industry experience on Australian civil engineering projects from a selection of sectors, e.g. commercial, residential, industrial, land development including stormwater design and management
  • Excellent client relationship management skills
  • Proven track record of concurrently project managing multiple, medium scale projects.

About WSP

WSP is one of the world's leading engineering professional services consulting firms, bringing together approximately 6,000 talented people across 14 offices in Australia. We have committed to halving the carbon footprint of our designs and advice by 2030. Our sustainable solutions and Future Ready™ projects will help societies grow for lifetimes to come.

We are committed to providing an inclusive, diverse and equal opportunity workplace. We promote Indigenous voices and are actively delivering a ‘Stretch’ level Reconciliation Action Plan. Accredited by the Workplace Gender Equality Agency as an Employer of Choice, we also support the LQBTQI+ community, and encourage all employees to bring their whole selves to work.
