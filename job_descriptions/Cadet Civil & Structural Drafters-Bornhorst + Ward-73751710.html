About us:

Bornhorst + Ward is one of Queensland's leading independent engineering consultants, with almost 60 years of world class engineering experience. Based in Brisbane, we are a multi-disciplinary practice, led personally by seven experienced directors who are actively involved in engineering and market development.

Our experience covers a wide range of projects for both government agencies and in the private sector.

We are again seeking applications for entry level Cadet Drafters to join our team.

About the B+W Cadet Program opportunity:

Our dedicated Cadet Program has been operating for over 40 years. During this time, we have earned a reputation for producing industry leaders due to our dedication to developing and mentoring junior employees.

Successful applicants will be trained in the use of the latest BIM software and develop the technical skills to produce engineering drawings for a range of Civil and Structural projects.

Cadet Drafters are encouraged to undertake part-time study in the Associate Degree in Civil Engineering to further their personal development.
