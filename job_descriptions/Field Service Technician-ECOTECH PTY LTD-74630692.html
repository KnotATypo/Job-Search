At Acoem Australasia (Ecotech Pty Ltd), we believe together, we create environments of possibility. We do this by working with our customers to provide bespoke solutions by designing, manufacturing, operating and supplying sophisticated monitoring equipment.  www.acoem.com

Working as a Field Service Technician at Acoem Brisbane is your opportunity to use your scientific instrumentation, field service or electronics experience while being part of a global leader in air quality monitoring solutions. In the Field Service department, our focus is to provide clients with operations and maintenance services of their Air Quality Monitoring Systems to the highest level.

To be successful you would be comfortable working on various electronic instruments, calibration equipment and will also have experience with data logging and telemetry. There will be travel throughout Queensland and occasional interstate and overseas travel.

Typically, you will need to demonstrate a high degree of self-sufficiency and the ability to independently troubleshoot a variety of measurement systems.  As much of the work is NATA accredited, diligent and thorough record-keeping is an essential part of this role. Comprehensive training will be provided.

Key Responsibilities will include:

  • Coordinating and planning of scheduled and unscheduled service visits to existing customers.
  • Providing high-level technical support to customers and internal staff as required.
  • Performing maintenance procedures on environmental monitoring systems.
  • Performing installation and commissioning on new monitoring systems.
  • Technical troubleshooting as required.
  • Maintaining and enhancing our safety culture.

What qualifications, skills & experience would you need to be successful?

  • Relevant tertiary qualifications (TAFE certificate/diploma or equivalent).
  • Previous related experience such as ambient air quality, particulate / dust, CEMS monitoring and environmental instrumentation would be highly regarded.
  • Instrumentation background and/or suitable mechanical and electrical skills.
  • Experience with industrial communications protocols.
  • Sound level of computer literacy.
  • Demonstrated ability to follow instructions and work with minimal supervision.
  • Excellent customer service skills.
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate effectively and work collaboratively in a team.
  • Accurate and diligent record keeping skills.

What are we offering you?

  • Competitive remuneration package.
  • Participation in company bonus scheme.
  • Work with a dynamic and highly skilled team.
  • Fully maintained company dual cab utility vehicle.
  • Flexible working arrangements.
  • Training and development opportunities.
