About Cox Automotive

Cox Automotive is the world’s largest automotive service organisation, transforming the way the world buys, sells, owns and uses vehicles. As part of the Cox family of businesses, we have been making our mark since 1898 by building and evolving world-class businesses, helping over 40,000 clients across five continents and bringing together the best solutions and teams.

The role

Our high-performing and collaborative Customer Experience (CX) team is the heartbeat behind ensuring we provide a friendly, efficient and effective customer service to all of our clients. Playing a crucial role as a Customer Success Consultant , you will be the first port of call to handle technical questions and provide expert support to enable our clients to better connect with their customers.

Based at our Eagle Farm site, our CX team operates Monday to Friday between 8 am to 6 pm on a roster basis with on call support.

Skills & Experience:

  • A passion for technology and customer service
  • Customer Service experience via different mediums (phone, email, online, etc.)
  • Previous experience with data aggregation, file transfers and word press
  • Ability to learn new systems and processes quickly
  • Exemplary written and oral communication skills

Desirable but not essential:

  • Automotive dealership registration clerical experience
  • Previous experience within a Tech Support/SME role
  • Working knowledge of DAIS/Rego Experience
  • Website CMS administration – application of WordPress preferable

What’s in it for you?

  • A fantastic entry-level position to kick-start your career within our customer experience team
  • Initial and ongoing training on our systems and processes across multiple platforms that allow you deliver superior customer service
  • Configuration, maintenance & ongoing support of all Dealer Solution Products to all customers
  • Internal software and systems testing
  • Monitor in-house systems to ensure all tasks are processed accurately in within allocated timeframes
  • The opportunity to use of your analytical ability to analyse and resolve any problems reported by customers
  • Ongoing development and an opportunity to cross-train and continually support and learn other areas within the team
  • Being a part of progress by identifying smarter ways to serve the customer

Why join us?

  • Our team members have spoken, and we are officially certified as a Great Place to Work
  • We offer on the job training to ensure you are successful in the role and other training and development opportunities such as LinkedIn Learning, Pluralsight, Employee Resource Groups and many more
  • Our Benefits offerings include Birthday Leave, Volunteering Leave, Service Awards, access to Reward and Recognition platform just to name a few
  • Employee Assistance Program (EAP) – EAP is a professional service that is voluntary, confidential and free of charge to all team members and their immediate family members and dependents 24/7
  • Find more benefits at careers.coxautoinc.com.au/pages/benefits


We offer a collaborative team environment where you can thrive, grow and make a meaningful impact. If you are looking for a fun, challenging and rewarding opportunity to build your career, apply now and make your mark!
