Contal is a young, fun company with projects spanning a range of industries including aged care, travel, training, and hospitality. We are seeking a creative Web Designer to work with us to support the growth of new and existing projects.

This is a permanent, full-time in-house position located in Spring Hill, for which you will need to reside in Brisbane and be qualified to work permanently in Australia.

Responsibilities:

  • Produce mockups for websites, web-based software and mobile apps that are visually appealing as well as functional
  • Develop responsive web interfaces in HTML and CSS
  • Develop and ensure consistency in all creative elements
  • Create wireframes and screen flows
  • Work with other designers and project manager to establish design systems, rules and developer guidelines for consistency across all screens
  • Communicate effectively with our in-house team of programming, design and other stakeholders throughout development
  • Other graphic design tasks as required

Factors needed to be successful in this role are:

  • A degree or diploma in a relevant field, or progress towards a degree or diploma in a relevant field combined with relevant experience
  • Strong knowledge of HTML and CSS
  • Familiarity with/appreciation for UI/UX design principles
  • Familiarity with the kinds of tools used to produce web and mobile interfaces
  • Ability to plan, prioritise and work to deadlines
  • A good eye for detail
  • Good communication skills
  • Excellent spelling and grammar
