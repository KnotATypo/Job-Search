Job description:

We are now seeking an Operational Technology Systems Specialist to join our Brisbane head office to maintain and provide advanced technical support for OT platforms such as endpoint devices, applications and supporting systems, servers, and network infrastructure. The role would suit someone with a strong technical skillset and a comprehensive understanding of mining technology systems.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Provide day-to-day technical advice, problem solving and support for operational technology systems.
  • Monitor, troubleshoot and resolve advanced technical issues related to OT hardware, software and network connectivity.
  • Install, configure, and upgrade OT endpoint devices, applications, servers, and network infrastructure.
  • Develop detailed designs, technical documents, work instructions, procedures, and other documentation for BUMA knowledge bases.
  • Manage the deployment of new applications and systems, ensuring that they are properly tested and integrated in coordination with the business.
  • Visit sites on a routine basis to perform onsite maintenance and support activities and liaise with site stakeholders to understand technical challenges.
  • Identify and explore technologies and innovations that could add value to the business.

It is expected that this position works from the BUMA South Brisbane office five days a week. Occasional travel to site is required.

Profile description:

About You:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering or a related field.
  • Experience in supporting operational technologies, preferably in the mining or heavy industries, and working with end-users.
  • Broad level of IT proficiency, including competence in IT networks and cloud infrastructure, cybersecurity, data warehousing and wireless networking and mobility platforms. Recognised IT professional certifications are desirable.
  • Strong attention to detail and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to work well in a team-oriented environment.
