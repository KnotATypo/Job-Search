Full job description

Ausenco is a fast-growing company with big ideas. We redefine what’s possible in some of the world’s most complex projects and toughest environments. Delivering innovative, value-add consulting, project delivery, asset operations and maintenance solutions is what we do. From 26 offices in 15 countries, with projects in over 80 locations worldwide, we create sustainable outcomes for our people, clients and communities.

Our team is growing, we are currently recruiting for an Experienced Mechanical Engineer to join our team in our Brisbane office.

About the role

  • Contribute to the delivery of multi-disciplinary engineering designs and feasibility studies.
  • Prepare technical reports related to specific design issues.
  • Deliver best practice technical solutions in accordance with project scope, timeframes and standards.
  • Prepare preliminary and detailed designs, specifications and documentation for all Mechanical engineering project work, ensuring compliance with conditions and parameters set out in project scope of works
  • Preparation of package documentation to support equipment procurement including Scope of Works, Specifications, Data sheets and Technical Bid Evaluation of vendor submissions.
  • Prepare plans, designs, calculations and cost estimates for conventional engineering, research and development assignments.
  • Familiar with standard discipline deliverables and work process on projects.
  • Demonstrate initiative and basic leadership skills to give direction within a small work group.
  • Deliver superior quality designs and deliverables that meet the technical schedule and budget requirements.
  • Develop networks and relationships within teams, and to effectively communicate with colleagues and clients.
  • Assist with the preparation of proposals and tenders (such as estimating design hours).
  • Monitor and remain within assigned budget and alert supervisor if the budget estimate will be exceeded.
  • Opportunities to work on international projects with potential for site based construction support and commissioning secondments.

About you

  • Degree qualification in Mechanical Engineering with eligibility and desire to pursue RPEQ accreditation
  • 3 – 5 years post grad experience.
  • Strong experience in engineering consulting (Minerals Processing) and EPC/EPCM projects in the mining and resources sectors.
  • Experience with copper, gold, nickel or base metals is highly advantageous
  • Design experience using S3D, Helix, Sidewinder, Rocky, Fluidflow etc. highly regarded
  • Sound understanding of current codes and standards including Australian and ASME
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong problem solving and organisation skills and ability to meet tight deadlines
  • Project and / or site experience highly regarded
  • Willingness to travel domestically and internationally highly desirable
