About Us

Established in 1988, Rest is one of Australia’s largest profit-to-member superannuation funds.

We support nearly two million members, with around $75 billion of funds under management and are recognised as a responsible investment leader*. We believe when members understand and engage with their super, they’re more likely to get a better retirement outcome.

Everything we do at Rest is underpinned by our values and behaviours, we want to Be Daring, Keep it Simple, Take Action and Have Grit. To put it simply we want our people to thrive and love the work they do.

About the Job

Your role will be responsible for assisting the Portfolio Governance team with the day-to-day activities related to the governance of the transformation portfolio. Collaborating with the Portfolio Delivery team, you will be collating key information and performing initial analysis of portfolio performance data and assisting with monthly and quarterly Portfolio Governance reporting.

This role will also provide secretariat support to the Portfolio Governance Committee and provide advice and guidance to stakeholders on the governance framework, portfolio governance cycles and including supporting staff with how to complete business case templates, benefits realisation plans and capital allocation plans.

  • Assist stakeholders to embed Portfolio Governance framework and artifacts across the transformation portfolio,
  • Assist in the preparation and review of all new and ongoing funding requests
  • Responsible for the preparation of Portfolio Governance Committee pack, documenting minutes for Portfolio Governance Committee and maintain decisions log and storing key artifacts for future reference.
  • Work with program, project, and stable team benefit owners to ensure benefit realisation plans are captured and tracked
  • Collate and maintain key information regarding program and project milestones
  • Collating, validating, Program Workbooks
  • Work with Portfolio Governance Analyst to develop new or updated reports and maintain monthly dashboards.
  • Develop and maintain Portfolio Governance Information repository/site
  • Develop and maintain forward view of PGC forward calendar
  • Coordinating our continuous improvement surveys and backlogs
  • Assisting with the development of Portfolio Governance training material, coordinating training groups, training registers and the creation of Portfolio Governance LMS modules.
  • Contribution to the Portfolio Governance Refresh sessions, preparing health check reports, identifying themes and presenting insights to the Change Delivery community.
