Hello, Hej, 你好, Salut!

At Flight Centre Travel Group (FCTG) our purpose is to 'open up the world for those who want to see' . Every day, we give people all around the world the opportunity to experience something really amazing – travel!

Are you a driven individual who is obsessed with creating a difference. Whilst we reimagine our business, automation has become a pivotal part of our journey. We are currently responsible for a portfolio of automation tools however the focal point is Service Now as the ITIL platform of choice and platform adoption in the form of automation.

If you enjoy executing technical solutions in capacity as a ServiceNow lead and being a subject matter expert in this area, please apply!

Day to day:

  • Communicating technical problems and solutions to both technical and non-technical audiences
  • Consulting with business analyst/project manager to develop appropriate technical solutions. · Documenting and gain acceptance / approvals of selected final solutions
  • Executing technical solutions in capacity as a ServiceNow lead, subject matter expert
  • Coaching and advising other Developers/ support engineers
  • Develop, test and implement Client Scripts, Business Rules, UI Policies, UI Actions, and ACLs including advanced scripting of each, forms workflows, and custom Service Portal widgets
  • Ensure proper operation and configuration of the platform through testing and validation
  • Develop, test, and implement data integrations via API calls to various other technologies within our Platform environment
  • Administration of the application, including but not limited to, installation/upgrading, configuration, operation, and maintenance
  • Advance the maturity of the environment by researching and implementing new tools and technologies

You'll be perfect for the role if you have:

  • 3+ years of experience as a developer on the ServiceNow Platform
  • ServiceNow Certification System Administrator and Application Developer
  • Working knowledge of ServiceNow components such as Change Management, Service Catalog, Service Portal, Problem Management, Incident Management, CMDB, Knowledge Management, etc
  • Knowledge of HTTP, REST, and API design best practices. Understanding of event-driven architectures. Understanding of asynchronous programming concepts. Knowledge of modern web design (CSS, HTML, etc.), JavaScript, and Angular
  • The ability to provide ServiceNow customization and configuration including but not limited to: scripting, workflow administration, report setup, module configuration, data imports and third-part software integration
  • Experience defining problems, collecting data, establishing facts, and drawing valid conclusions
  • Experience working in an Agile or Scrum software development environment

You'll earn brownie points with:

  • CMDB/IRM/CSM Implementation Experience
  • Integration and API Experience
  • ITIL certification (at least ITIL V3 foundation)
  • Demonstrated understanding of ITIL practices in particular Incident management, problem management and change management.
  • Experience in automation tools, such as Blue Prism, UI Automation, UiPath, Power Platform
  • Proficiency in programming languages, such as C, C++, Java, Python or .NET
  • Basic knowledge of Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial
