Join Aruma's IT Team as an Application Support Analyst, contributing to the seamless management and support of Microsoft Dynamics 365 across the organisation. Reporting into the Application Support Team Lead, in this role, you'll play a pivotal part in advancing our technological capabilities and systems, driving sustainability, and enhancing user experiences for both our valued employees and customers.

A day in the life of an Application Support Analyst …

  • Interpret end-user needs and challenges, proposing solutions that fostered engagement across all stakeholders and were in line with the organisation's goals.
  • Demonstrate proficiency in configuring, importing, and exporting data, facilitating seamless integration with Microsoft Dynamics CRM.
  • Efficiently maintain and update Dynamic CRM template forms to accommodate local customisations.
  • Collaborate with end-users to address and resolve Microsoft Dynamics CRM support inquiries, escalating unresolved issues when necessary to ensure prompt resolutions.
  • Support the smooth and accurate migration of application data to Microsoft Dynamics CRM, adhering to established timelines.
  • Cultivate strong relationships with essential business stakeholders to oversee and manage reports and dashboards, ensuring ongoing alignment of solutions with evolving business requirements.
  • Produce comprehensive progress and delivery reports for transformative technologies and systems, aligning with Aruma's strategic priorities. Qualifications and Skills:

Skills and Experience which will make you stand out…

  • Bachelor's degree in computer science, Information Technology, or related field (or equivalent experience).
  • At least 2 years of hands-on experience in supporting Microsoft Dynamics CRM 365.
  • Minimum of 5 years' experience providing support to business users.
  • Experience with Power BI, PowerShell scripting and Microsoft Azure.
  • Knowledge of SQL and relational databases, worked with MS SQL Server.
  • Experience with systems administration and security management.
  • Strong problem-solving abilities with a focus on end-user satisfaction.
  • Exceptional customer service skills and a collaborative mindset.
  • Detail-oriented with a proactive attitude toward identifying and resolving issues.
