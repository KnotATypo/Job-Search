About us

Are you looking to join a people-centred organisation that prioritises your skills development, work-life balance and career progression? The search is over; welcome to Sigma Aero Resources Australia Pty Ltd.

Sigma Aero Resources Australia Pty Ltd is the leader in managed aircraft maintenance, known for our award-winning team, supportive culture, and excellent results. We have proudly recruited and trained talented aircraft maintenance engineers in Australia for over 15 years. As a leader in aircraft maintenance, repair and operations (MRO), our company is built upon a passion for aviation and core values of integrity , quality and continuous innovation . We are driven by our mission to develop talented aircraft maintenance engineers and connect them to promising careers with our top-notch contractors and airline partners.

So, why join an agency when you can join Sigma Aero Resources Australia Pty Ltd, an industry leader in aircraft maintenance?

Who are we looking for?

We are currently looking for a highly experienced and disciplined Aircraft Maintenance Engineer (Mechanical or Avionics) to join our team in Brisbane, QLD . If you’re a proactive, flexible and experienced professional with a drive to succeed, you’re the type of person we would like to welcome onboard.

Your role

In this role, your key responsibilities will include:
  • Dismantling, inspecting, testing, repairing and reassembling aircraft engines, ancillary components, accessories, and electrical systems;
  • Conducting routine pre–flight checks and inspections of engines and equipment;
  • Maintaining thorough records of actions taken;
  • Assembling components, parts and subassemblies of aircraft engines and equipment, and;
  • Testing, maintaining, replacing, and upgrading aircraft safety equipment.
The essential requirements
  • 2+ years experience on Dash 8 Q200, Q300 and Q400 aircraft (or similar type, e.g. turboprop aircraft);
  • Trade Certificate IV (Mechanical) or (Avionics);
  • Highly skilled and competent in aircraft mechanical system troubleshooting, repair and installation of mechanical components.
  • Strong team player;
  • Ability to follow supervisory direction;
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills;
  • Consistent positive and proactive attitude;
  • Passion for safe work practices;
  • Red ASIC card (or ability to obtain); and,
  • Current Australian Driver's licence.
  • Valid work visa ;
Shifts will be primarily a rotating 4 on 4 off permanent night shift roster.

Additional qualifications

We are specifically looking for candidates capable of committing to flexible working arrangements; further, please ensure that you are eligible and available to work in Brisbane QLD before applying.
