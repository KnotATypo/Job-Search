About the role and you:

Due to organizational growth, a new position has arisen for a Project Engagement Advisor to play a critical role in ensuring effective communication between Sequana and its stakeholders, both internal and external. This role involves developing and executing strategic communication plans, building, and maintaining positive relationships with stakeholders, and providing counsel on stakeholder engagement strategies. The advisor will collaborate with various teams within the organization to convey clear and consistent messages that align with the organization's goals and values.

What you'll do:

  • Develop and implement complex stakeholder engagement plans.
  • Conduct in-depth stakeholder analysis to inform engagement plans and identify social impacts.
  • Develop and implement stakeholder engagement strategies to foster positive relationships and collaboration.
  • Create comprehensive communication plans that outline targeted messages, channels, and timing for reaching different stakeholder groups.
  • Coordinate engagement events including sourcing quotes, booking venues and promotional activities.
  • Develop content for communication channels, including traditional media, social media, newsletters, briefing notes and others, to effectively convey messages.
  • Facilitate small to large stakeholder sessions and meetings.
  • Liaise with project staff to solve complex stakeholder related issues.
  • Respond to enquiries and complaints in a timely manner while maintaining relevant up-to-date stakeholder databases.
  • Establish relevant metrics to assess the success of communication strategies and stakeholder engagement efforts.

Experience you'll need:

  • Bachelor's degree or similar relevant qualification in Communications, Public Relations, Marketing, Business, or a related field
  • Min 5 years’ experience in stakeholder engagement, ideally within infrastructure, transport, or logistics sectors. Demonstrated understanding of stakeholder engagement principles and communication best practices.
  • Highly developed written and oral communication skills including the writing and delivery of strategies, plans and communications materials.
  • Demonstrated ability to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams and leadership in a project environment.
  • Proven ability to work with diverse stakeholders in politically sensitive environments.
