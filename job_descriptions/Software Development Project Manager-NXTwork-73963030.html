About us

NXTwork (recently rebranded to ‘Code Squirrel’ is a software development company focusing on delivering innovative and creative solutions to our clients.  We take a very modern approach to software development, with teams of mixed specialities sharing the work across multiple projects. We work together as a team - giving everyone the opportunity to thrive and work on projects that interest and challenge them while leaning on each other whenever we need a hand.

Qualifications & experience

  • Project management or team leadership experience essential
  • Software development experience preferred
  • Basic server/network experience preferred, but not required
  • Outstanding organisation and time management skills
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Proactive approach to daily work
  • Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously, with competing priorities
  • Strong communication skills, including empathy, negotiation and listening
  • Ability to collaborate effectively via video conferencing as well as in person
  • Ability to assess importance of tasks to align with the overall goals of the customer
  • Effective technical and proposal writing skills

Tasks & responsibilities

  • Coordinating software development projects with our software development team, ensuring projects are on schedule, and built in line with their scope & design documentation
  • Liaising with customers to share project progress and gather their feedback
  • QA testing of mobile and web applications - making sure new features and fixes in apps are working correctly
  • Managing timelines of projects
  • Hosting customer update meetings to update them on the status of the project and any potential roadblocks, take minutes of meeting and demo any recent changes
  • Hosting internal meetings to take minutes, assign work, discuss priorities, receive updates and listen to the team
  • Ensure all customer requests are logged and prioritised in accordance with the project goals
  • Communicate with your development team to keep them updated on any changes from the stakeholders
  • Communicate with the UX Designer to convey requirements and provide feedback on designs, in line with client requirements
  • Managing projects in Jira including identifying priority tickets, drafting the next sprint worth of work, and liaising with the Senior Developer for allocations based on availability and skills
  • Lead customer engagement to test the product when ready for feedback
  • Conducting training with the stakeholders
  • Creating and managing project documentation so all user-facing functions are recorded in a way that the customer’s team and other developers can read through to understand how the product works
  • Working closely with other Project Managers and Senior Developers to manage competing priorities and allocation of developers
  • Reporting project progress in regular team meetings so everyone is aware of high level changes or requests which differ from the original scope
  • Ensure all phases of projects, including customer sign off, review and feedback are part of standard project practice
