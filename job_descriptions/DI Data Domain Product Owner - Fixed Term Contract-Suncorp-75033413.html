DI Data Domain Product Owner - Digital Insurer Program

  • Number of fixed term 12 month+ contracts available
  • Locations are flexible across Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane

About the roles:

The DI Data Domain PO enables realisation of the data vision and requirements for design & build within the Digital Insurer program. The role ensures DET squads within the Digital Insurer program are set up for success by ensuring clear requirements are elicited, documented, and provided to DET squads, and acts as a representative for business stakeholders with knowledge of their respective areas.

This role will work closely with supporting resources, such as Business Analytics and technical subject matter experts (SME’s), ensuring strong alignment between stakeholder requirements and program imperatives is achieved.

The intent of the Digital Insurer program is to enable the business to deliver new products (including modular products), seamless customer experiences, and support delivery of a simpler, digital business. Radical simplification and significant improvement in the speed & cost of change are a critical measure of success of the program, and this role must ensure this lens is applied to both requirements given to, and deliverables produced by delivery teams.

Candidates with a passion for data and an interest to further develop emerging skillsets as a product owner are encouraged to apply.

Key Accountabilities:

  • Works with the business SMEs to analyze and understand business requirements, and review/produce technical and business specifications of Data focusing to assigned business area.
  • Understands the importance of strategic alignment between business and technology goals and seeks to achieve this in requirements gathering/squad delivery.
  • Uplifts the data quality and integrity by ensuring that standards and frameworks for management and collection of data are adhered to including appropriate use of reference data tools.
  • Prepares materials to ensure effective communication of data requirements and/or to explain future changes to the landscape.
  • Works with architects and DET teams to ensure that reference and master data roadmaps are consistently updated in line with changing business needs.
  • Drive data decisions aligned to the North Star principles which require radical simplification.
  • Understands Suncorp future state data architecture and tooling and drives data transformation, simplification (tooling & Environments) and value for the business.
  • Drive the right outcomes and trade-offs between simplification and business regulatory & business value requirements.
  • Supports Data Stewards and Governance roles in ensuring that data maturity standards are upheld.
  • Models team behaviours to deliver results and support others.
  • Contributes to a team that prospers from diversity of ideas and approach and celebrate inclusivity.
  • Contributes to an environment where safety and wellbeing is prioritised and actioned and people are motivated towards meaningful work and common goals.
