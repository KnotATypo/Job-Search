About the Role

Step into the thrilling role of Cyber GRC Analyst at BOQ Group!

You will be required to identify, assess, and report on cyber risks using the powerful ServiceNow platform. Dive into the realm of data mastery as you craft and collect metrics using Power BI and Excel, providing a clear measure of the effectiveness of BOQ's cyber risk management program. Your expertise extends to supporting the development and implementation of innovative risk mitigation strategies, ensuring that our defences are fortified against evolving threats.

Stay at the forefront of industry trends and best practices and become the torchbearer of innovation in cyber risk management. Furthermore, collaborate with various teams to ensure timely and effective closure of risk events. Join us on this journey where every action you take contributes to the resilience of our digital landscape.

About you

We are looking for an enthusiastic Cyber GRC Analyst with hands-on experience in cyber risk management, reporting, and the art of metrics creation and collection. Ideally, you will have a great understanding of ServiceNow, PowerBI and Excel.

Your analytical and problem-solving skills are the secret weapons we're looking for – the sharper, the better! But it's not just about technical skills; your excellent communication and collaboration skills will be key towards our cyber defence efforts. Knowledge of industry standards and frameworks like CIS18, NIST, ISO, and COBIT, would be very beneficial in this role.

Whether working independently or as part of a dynamic team, your ability to navigate the digital landscape makes you the perfect fit for our Cyber GRC adventure. Join us and let's shape the future of cybersecurity together!
