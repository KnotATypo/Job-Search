About us:

Aqseptence Group is a leading global supplier of specialised products, equipment and system solutions for filtration, separation and water technology for a wide range of different applications and markets. Johnson Screens, with its trade mark Vee-Wire®, is the leading brand of well screens for the water well, O&G, Geothermal & Mining markets. We are looking to appoint a Business Unit Manager for our Water Well (WW) team in Australia, based at our Eagle Farm QLD location.

About the role:

The primary objective of the role is to manage the WW team and build on a long-standing successful product line in an established market. We seek a candidate that can leverage existing successes, and grow the business by identifying new applications and market opportunities and building key business relationships across Australia.

The position is responsible for:

  • Overseeing the performance of the Australian WW business unit
  • Developing new business opportunities and exceeding sales targets
  • Managing and coaching the WW sales team
  • Managing key end-user accounts and distributors and reporting market activities to the WW APAC manager
  • Work internally with engineering, production, procurement & other teams to ensure effective management of customer orders & quotes

Success in this position requires some understanding of our products, services, and customer needs. It also requires the identification of opportunities for introducing additional products and services based on an accurate assessment of customer needs, while developing and implementing sales and marketing plans.

Ideal Core Competencies and Skill Sets:

  • Some experience in environmental, O&G, irrigation, water treatment or other WW markets
  • Proven track record in sales in a managerial position
  • Passionate about sales and growth
  • Good computer skills and some experience with CRM (Salesforce) and ERP (SAP) platforms
  • Excellent communication and people skills
  • Have the right attitude and be a team player
  • Ability to work independently and make decisions
  • Tertiary qualifications or experience in Earth science will be highly regarded

Frequent travel within Australian states will be required.

To the successful applicant, we will offer flexible working conditions, competitive annual salary, salary continuance insurance, service awards, a supportive team environment, clean modern facilities, and training and development opportunities.  We are an equal opportunity employer.
