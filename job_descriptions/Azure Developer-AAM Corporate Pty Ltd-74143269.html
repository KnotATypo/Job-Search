About us

AAMC are Australia’s leading motor accident management and assessing specialist. We have been delivering services to meet the individual needs of our clients for over 20 years.

Our people are our greatest asset. We are committed to recruiting and retaining a talented and diverse pool of extraordinary people. We value their experience and encourage the informal sharing of knowledge day-to-day. Formally, we provide a range of development opportunities to take the skills of our team from good to great.

The Role

This role is an excellent opportunity for a tech-savvy individual with a passion for solution design and development in the Azure environment to use their skills to deliver value for the business.

As an Azure Developer, you will play an important role implementing new and supporting existing solutions. You will ensure secure-by-design principles are followed, technical specifications are documented for approval and solutions are robust and maintainable. You will also be responsible for working with our various suppliers’ if the solution requires external expertise.

In this role you will receive guidance to develop your Enterprise Architecture skills and to learn the technologies required of the role.

You will be operating within a small team who predominantly follow the Agile methodology. You will have direct access to stakeholders at all levels to ensure solutions are produced that meet the business need.

Experience with RESTful API development is advantageous.

Primary Purpose

The core functions of your role will be to assist in the solution design and implementation processes. You will also assist in parts of the requirement gathering process where it will add value from a technical perspective. The duties of the role include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Use requirements and stakeholder engagement to help design solutions that are fit for purpose,
  • Where ideal for internal development, build the solution in an automated and repeatable way, with an appropriate level of developer, unit and user testing,
  • Reduce rework by challenging ideas to ensure that solution designs meet the business need or address a root cause,
  • Clearly document solutions, including technical details and security requirements,
  • Implement robust solutions with adequate monitoring and alerting,
  • Ensure reporting can be derived from the solution to support business decisions,
  • Where an existing solution fails, fix the issue immediately, but also identify the root cause and propose a solution to prevent future incidents,
  • Supply data and insights to support the analysis of ROI post-implementation to validate our decision-making processes,
  • Support the business at all levels to help address problems or improve efficiencies using your technical skillset.

You will use the following tools to build technical solutions:

  • Azure resources such as:
  • Functions (with Python)
  • Service Bus
  • Logic Apps
  • Key Vault
  • Storage accounts (Blob and SFTP)
  • Cosmos Db
  • Azure Data Explorer
  • API Management
  • Programming tools used:
  • Docker
  • GitHub
  • GitHub Actions (with Terraform)
  • VS Code
  • JIRA and Confluence
