YOUR FUTURE ROLE

We are looking for career starters on graduate level (f/m/d) to strengthen our delivery capabilities in the Analytics & Insight Services organization. Our team's focus is to provide consulting and professional services in the field of Planning & Analytics and Artificial Intelligence with our main mandate to drive customer adoption and innovative use cases. Our consultants and architects work with some of SAP’s largest customers on their digital transformation journey. As such, we help to define and review a customer’s strategic direction, drive the adoption of our software products and ensure that projects will successfully go live and operate according to SAP’s best practice no matter if deployed on premise, hybrid or cloud.

Within the Analytics & Insight Services team you will work on topics such as:

  • Work on digital transformation and cloud transition projects using SAP’s latest solutions offered as part of the SAP Business Technology Platform (SAP Analytics Cloud, SAP Data Warehouse Cloud and SAP AI Core, BTP full-stack development)
  • Create and support our customers end-to-end processes from data inception to data mart creation, visualisation and application/AI model design
  • Collaborate with senior AI developers to design, develop, and implement AI solutions.
  • Assist in the collection, pre-processing, and analysis of data for AI models.
  • Contribute to the development and optimization of machine learning algorithms.
  • Support in building and maintaining AI systems, including testing and debugging.
  • Stay updated with the latest advancements in AI technologies and methodologies.

YOUR PROFILE

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, or a related field with a focus on Artificial Intelligence.
  • Basic understanding of machine learning algorithms, deep learning frameworks, and statistical analysis.
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++, with the ability to write clean, efficient code.
  • Familiarity with data manipulation tools and libraries like NumPy, pandas, and scikit-learn.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with a meticulous attention to detail.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities, with a desire to learn and grow in the field of AI.
  • Prior experience with AI projects, internships, or coursework is a plus.
  • Theoretical and practical experience with SAP applications in the field of Data & Analytics is not expected but a benefit.

MEET YOUR TEAM

The Analytics & Insight Services team is part of a global organization located within SAP’s Customer Services & Delivery board area. A&I delivers Planning & Analytics cloud solutions from project positioning to full implementation on a global level. As thought-leaders, the organization drives strategy by leveraging its rich experience in the field to create business content and automation assets. Working with SAP Partners is their ambition to support awareness and adoption of SAP’s cloud solutions.
