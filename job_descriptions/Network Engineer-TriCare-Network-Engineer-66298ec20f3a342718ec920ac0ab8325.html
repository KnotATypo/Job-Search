Established for over 50 years, TriCare is one of Queensland's leading privately owned and managed retirement communities and aged care residences.

TriCare head office at Mount Gravatt is looking for a Network Engineer to join the IT team for a permanent full-time position.

Reporting to the Chief Information Officer, the Network Engineer’s role is to select, design, implement, and support telecommunication and security solutions to meet the business requirements across all 28 TriCare sites.

Responsibilities include SDWAN, firewalls, advanced threat detection, VOIP telephony, LAN, Wi-Fi and cloud services. Key stakeholders include the internal business, the Information Services team and suppliers.

The ideal Network Engineer will demonstrate the below key requirements, skills and experience :
  • Minimum experience with switch configuration and troubleshooting (Cisco, Aruba preferred)
  • IT security, including firewall, multi-factor authentication, device security, advanced threat management (Sophos advantageous), Fortinet.
  • Deep understanding of networking technologies such as VLAN, LAN and WAN, including SD-WAN (VeloCloud advantageous)
  • Knowledge of Wireless infrastructure setup and design in both public and private scenarios (Aruba advantageous)
  • Experience with Consumer broadband network technology for Guest Wi-Fi services is an advantage.
  • VPN configuration and design
  • VOIP telephony technology using hosted services
  • Cloud computing (Azure and Express Route advantageous)
You will possess the following skills, experience, and attributes:
  • Tertiary Qualifications in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Systems Engineering desirable.
  • At least 2 years of network experience.
  • Relevant certifications desirable: CCNA, Microsoft Azure Security & Networking
  • Well-developed communication skills, both written and verbal, including the ability to engage with multiple stakeholders
  • Be an effective contributor in a team environment
  • Self-motivated with keen attention to detail
  • Flexible attitude to working hours
  • Ability to work under pressure to meet deadlines
  • Ability to analyse system data/logs and solve complex problems
  • Willingness to increase skills and keep abreast with industry developments.
