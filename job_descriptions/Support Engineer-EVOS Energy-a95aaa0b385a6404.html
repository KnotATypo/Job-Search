Support Engineer

A fantastic opportunity has arisen for a hands on, customer focused Support Engineer to join the EVOS team. This is a newly created role, reporting to the CX Technical Manager.

As a Software and Hardware Support Engineer, you will play a crucial role in providing comprehensive technical support to our customers, partners, and internal teams. Your primary responsibility will be to troubleshoot, diagnose, and resolve software and hardware issues while providing an exceptional level of customer service. You'll work closely with various cross-functional teams to improve our products and services based on your insights from customer interactions.

Responsibilities:

  • Provide technical support for both software and hardware products, diagnosing and resolving complex customer issues.
  • Work closely with engineering teams to troubleshoot and resolve hardware and software problems.
  • Assist customers in the installation, operation, and maintenance of our software and hardware products.
  • Identify and report product issues, ensuring they are fully documented for product management and engineering teams.
  • Create and maintain knowledge base articles, FAQs, and user guides to help customers better understand and utilize our products.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Information Technology, or a related field, or equivalent work experience.
  • Minimum of 1 years of experience in a technical support role, preferably supporting both software and hardware products.
  • Strong knowledge of software troubleshooting techniques and tools, as well as understanding of hardware systems.
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills, with the ability to diagnose and resolve technical issues.
  • Strong understanding of network systems, databases, and operating systems.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with a customer-focused mindset.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and priorities in a fast-paced environment.
