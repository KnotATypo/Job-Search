About Us

Odyssey Gaming (a Federal Group company) is an innovative and growing business that delivers cutting edge technology to the hospitality electronic gaming sector. We pride ourselves on having a flexible, collaborative and people-focused culture.  We play the long game, making sound strategic investments in technology and ensuring we build long term meaningful relationships with our customers and our people.

In this role, you will be:

  • Working with the Technology Engineering team to deliver analysis, design, implementation, maintenance and support of technology infrastructure (network, compute and storage) deployed to Odyssey office, datacentre, AWS/Azure cloud and customer tenancies.
  • Working closely with product delivery squads to deliver innovative management and automation solutions across the Odyssey IT systems landscape.
  • Using your analytical and problem-solving skills to help identify, communicate and resolve issues to maximise the benefit of IT systems and product platform investments.
  • Creating system design models, specifications, diagrams and charts to provide direction to operational teams
  • Designing server and security audits in accordance with the Business Continuity Disaster Recovery (BCDR) strategies
  • Championing, and acting as a custodian of, the Odyssey’s cybersecurity framework to ensure appropriate security measures for the protection of Federal Groups information assets.
