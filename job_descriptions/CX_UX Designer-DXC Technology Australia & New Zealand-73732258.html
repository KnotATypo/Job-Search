Job Description:
We are seeking a talented CX / UX Designer with a unique skillset combining strong customer experience design expertise, front-end development skills, and experience working with Power Pages and Portals. You will play a crucial role in creating exceptional customer experiences for our web applications, Power Apps, and portals, ensuring seamless interactions and visually appealing interfaces for our customers.

Responsibilities:
Customer and User-Centric Design: Apply customer-centered design principles to create intuitive, user-friendly, and visually engaging interfaces for web applications, Power Apps, and portals.
Front End Development: Implement user and customer designs using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure pixel-perfect rendering and responsive layouts across various devices and browsers.
Power Pages and Portals: Leverage your expertise in Power Pages and Portals to design and customize compelling user experiences within the Microsoft Power Platform environment.
Collaborative Design: Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including developers, product managers, and stakeholders, to understand project requirements and translate them into effective and elegant UX/UI solutions.
Prototyping: Create interactive prototypes to demonstrate and validate design concepts, iterating on feedback to achieve optimal user experiences.
Usability Testing: Conduct usability testing to gather user feedback, identify pain points, and make data-driven design decisions.
Accessibility: Ensure that all UX/CX designs and front-end code meet accessibility standards to provide an inclusive experience for all users.
Continuous Learning: Stay updated with the latest trends and best practices in UX design, front-end development, and the Power Platform, and share your knowledge with the team.

Requirements:
  • Proven experience as a UX / CX Designer with expertise in Front End Development, Power Pages, and Portals.
  • Strong portfolio showcasing a range of UX/CX designs and front-end development projects.
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design principles.
  • Hands-on experience with Power Apps, Power Pages, and Portals an advantage.
  • Understanding of customer-centred design methodologies and usability principles as well as customer journey mapping.
  • Ability to create interactive prototypes using tools such as Adobe XD, Sketch, Figma, or similar.
  • Familiarity with accessibility standards (WCAG) and their application in UX/CX design.
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.
  • Problem-solving mindset and a passion for delivering high-quality user experiences.
  • Knowledge of other front-end frameworks (e.g., React, Angular) is a plus.
  • Strong Customer Journey Mapping is essential
