We are in search of an experienced Business Development Manager to join our team in BRISBANE SOUTH . The ideal candidate will play a crucial role in enhancing the professional profile and credibility of our company, ensuring the delivery of our brand portfolio and key selling messages to medical aesthetic practitioners and the cosmetic medicine industry.

Key Responsibilities

  • Achieve National, State, and Territory Sales Targets for the Aesthetic portfolio, covering Restylane (Filler), Dysport (Toxin) & Sculptra (PLLA)

  • Plan and execute daily sales activities, including appointments and meetings with HCP's and cosmetic clinic staff.

  • Exceed call frequency targets, ensuring effective communication with key stakeholders.

  • Record all customer engagements in the CRM system promptly, including discussion points and stock distribution details.

  • Identify growth opportunities within the territory and existing businesses, fostering the expansion of the Galderma aesthetic portfolio.

  • Conduct Business Reviews at least twice a year, showcasing Galderma's value proposition and incorporating Clinical Nurse Specialists and Trainers where applicable.

  • Implement tactical and strategic initiatives to capitalize on growth opportunities.

  • Establish and maintain relationships with key business contacts and opinion leaders within the region.

  • Provide regular written and verbal reports on territory activities and trends.

  • Conduct Clinical Exploration Programs (CEP's) to enhance clinical support to customers.

  • Contribute to national business strategy planning by offering valuable insights on business and competitor information, including customer segmentation data.

  • Execute Galderma's commercial policies with customers and maintain territory average selling price.

  • Represent Galderma at various National and International congresses as required.

Skills & Qualifications

  • Business or Science degree preferred.

  • Minimum of 3 years of relevant sales experience, with a background in the Pharmaceutical, Cosmetic Medicine, or Medical Device industry advantageous.

  • Proven track record of achievement in previous sales roles.

  • Professional resilience and excellent communication skills.

  • Strong computer literacy, with proficiency in spreadsheet management and analysis, CRM databases, Excel, and PowerPoint.

  • Positive and energetic attitude, capable of independent decision-making within company policy.
