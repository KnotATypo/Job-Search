Frequentis is the leading international supplier of communication and information systems for control centres with safety-critical tasks. These 'Control Centre Solutions' are developed and distributed by Frequentis in the business segments Air Traffic Management (civil and military air traffic control, air defence) and Public Safety & Transport (police, fire and rescue services, emergency medical services, maritime organisations, and railways).

As a successful, innovative company, Frequentis offers employees an inspiring working environment: This is achieved with a culture of openness, diversity, and appreciation, as well as with motivating career development opportunities and equal promotion of all genders.

Position Purpose

The Systems Engineer provides technical engineering expertise in the development and implementation of Control Centre communications and information systems.

Main Tasks

  • Perform requirements analysis and engineering design
  • System installation, deployment, configuration and integration
  • Testing and error identification during integration of software into customer environment
  • Develop technical knowledge of Frequentis’ existing products and processes
  • Creation of technical documentation of systems and their interfaces
  • Creation of system/subsystem specification artefacts
  • Acceptance of internal and external deliverables for assigned work packages

The above lists are not exhaustive, and the role may evolve to meet the overall objectives of the company.

Training

Specialist Frequentis system training will be provided for products related to the role from the Frequentis product portfolio.

Experience and Skills

  • At least 5 years of project systems experience, ideally in safety critical, control centre design, integration and verification.
  • Technical education for software solutions, communication solutions, traceability processes and test engineering
  • Hands-on experience with specification, system installation, integration, and testing
  • Experience with Voice Communication Systems in a mission critical environment (e.g. ATC, Rail, Control and Command Centre related industries)
  • Ability to work in the mission-critical environment (with particular focus on safety)

Qualifications

  • A University qualification to the level of an Australian Bachelor Degree or equivalent qualification in Engineering, Software, Information Technology or related discipline.
