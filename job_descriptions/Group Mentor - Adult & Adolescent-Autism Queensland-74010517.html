Our therapy groups help provide a safe and inclusive space for autistic people of all ages to explore special interests and help build their skills.

We are looking for mentors who have a high level of knowledge and experience with digital media, creative industries and/or games development and design to engage our adult and adolescent clients using their preferred technology and their own interests.

This role will see you demonstrating various programs, technologies, games and troubleshooting and hardware or software issues that may arise during group sessions while supporting our coordinators and therapists in helping our clients work towards their chosen goals.
