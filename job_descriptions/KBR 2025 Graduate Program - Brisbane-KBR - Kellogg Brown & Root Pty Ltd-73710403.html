Who is KBR?

We work with customers across the nation to deliver sustainable infrastructure projects that enhance the future for all Australians through providing innovative solutions across the transport, water and environment, buildings, defence infrastructure and program management and construction consultancy markets. Learn more about Infrastructure Solutions here: https://kbr.foleon.com/is-australia/who-we-are/who-we-are

With more than 29,000 employees located across the world, KBR’s global business is at the forefront of technological innovation. We are constantly evolving and moving industries and our customers forward to deliver leading science, technology and engineering solutions to protect, advance and serve life on Earth and in space. Organisations and governments around the world come to us to take on their most complex objectives, for which our experts produce solutions for the most critical challenges of our time.

Your opportunity to make an impact

As a graduate beginning your career at KBR, you will explore what’s possible for your future with hands-on, real-life experience on our full suite of projects and disciplines, with guidance and mentoring from experienced professionals who will help you grow into a leading professional in your sphere of expertise.

Your on-the-job experience will be balanced with regular training that builds a solid foundation, setting you up to become a future leader in your field who’s technical skills shine.

You’ll also experience a healthy dose of fun at social activities and industry events through our IMPACT Program, connecting you with our national network of young professionals who share your excitement for the future. https://vimeo.com/ 715801665

About you

Help shape tomorrow by applying today!

1. Submi t your application
Submit online application, CV, cover letter, transcripts and right to work. Make sure you include details about work experience, education, and extracurricular activities, such as volunteer work, clubs, etc.

2. C omplete the Online Evaluation
Selected candidates will be invited to complete a blended online test where we will be able to get to know you better – we’ll take a look at cognitive ability, personality and situational judgement.

3. Comple te a video interview
Selected candidates will be invited to complete a video interview at a time convenient to you. We will provide you with several questions for you to answer and record via an online video interviewing platform. You will have a chance to read the questions prior to recording your answer. Let us see your personality and really get to know the real you!

4. Attend an in-person interview

Selected candidates will be invited to attend a face-to-face interview which will include an individual interview with the business. This provides you with an opportunity to share with the team a bit more about yourself and the reason why you want to join KBR as a Graduate.

5. Offer to join the Graduate Program If you are successful, you will receive a phone call from our recruitment team to offer you a position within our program. Congratulations!

Don’t miss out on your opportunity to leave an impact on our society with one of Australia’s leading science, engineering and technology businesses. Apply now and follow us on LinkedIn to stay up to date with all our latest news and job opportunities.

KBR values an inclusive and diverse work environment, and we encourage candidates from all backgrounds to apply. Our hiring decisions are based on the key requirements of each role and candidates are selected based on their unique strengths and experiences.
