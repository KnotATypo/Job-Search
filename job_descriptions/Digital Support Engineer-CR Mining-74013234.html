Are you a skilled Digital Support Engineer with a passion for computer networks and a background in mining?

The Opportunity

As a Digital Support Engineer at CR Digital, you'll be at the forefront of innovation, providing crucial support for our cutting-edge products and solutions. You'll have the opportunity to work in a hybrid role (home and office) as well as on-site, ensuring that our customers receive the highest level of service. As part of the global CR group and now the 19000 strong Epiroc group, you'll work with leading products like Titan 3330, GET.Trakka, and Thunderbird, solutions engineered to unlock productivity, enhance safety, and reduce maintenance requirements. From diagnosing and resolving issues to conducting maintenance and commissioning new systems, you'll play a key role in driving the success of our products. Your work will not only involve technical support but also collaboration with various teams to enhance our products and processes. This role offers a dynamic and fast-paced environment where you can make a real impact in shaping the future of global mining technology. This hybrid role is based at our CR Technology Hub in Goonda, Brisbane.

Your strengths:

  • Strong understanding of applied technologies, including IoT, electronics, software, data bases, radio communications and networking
  • Demonstrated experience in the mining industry, preferably on site.
  • Aptitude for mechanical, digital systems and dc voltage systems
  • Strong understanding of IP networks, server architecture, routers, VPNs, and Vlans
  • SQL database familiarity and knowledge of AZURE server setup and configuration
  • Strong analytical skills with a high attention to detail and a methodical, deliberate approach to troubleshooting
  • Strong ability to visualize systems and communicate with remote technicians during troubleshooting.
