About us:

Lutheran Services is a not-for-profit organisation that has been serving the people and communities of Queensland since 1935. We provide quality, contemporary care and support for people of all backgrounds, at more than 20 locations throughout Queensland.

Our dedicated workforce of nearly 1800 makes a difference to the lives of many people every day. They bring our vision to life – helping those we serve to pursue the lives they hope for, and enabling the communities we are part of to thrive.

As a member of the Lutheran Services team, you will enjoy a great work-life balance, generous benefits, flexible arrangements and ongoing opportunities for professional development and career progression.

About the role:

The role of ICT Business Analyst (BA):

  • supports the business by supporting the Discovery activities through business requirements elicitation, business case development, market engagement and
  • assists in successful implementation of ICT projects.
  • contributes to the wider BA practice and Enterprise Architecture (EA) for Lutheran Services.

In addition, you will:

  • Conduct analysis of existing systems, applications and functions to ensure strategic and EA alignment
  • Work with stakeholders to perform business analysis to determine business requirements through extensive consultation
  • Verify successful implementation through implementing traceability for the assigned projects
  • Apply industry standards and frameworks and methodologies to drive increased maturity in ICT investments
  • Work with the ICT & Projects team to enhance and embed the Lutheran Services Business Analysis framework

This role will lead engagement with our stakeholders through workshops and will also drive a wide variety of process and information mapping.

About you:

To see you be successful, in this role, you will have completed a bachelor’s degree in computer science, information systems, business management or equivalent work experience in this field. Furthermore you will have;

  • Experience as a Business Analyst in an enterprise environment
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills

  • Excellent stakeholder relationship skills with the ability to tailor communication to suit various audiences.
  • Demonstrable experience in developing requirements, mapping requirements traceability, creating use cases, acceptance criteria and test cases.
  • Experience with process and information mapping - data analysis and modelling
  • Worked with a variety of Cloud based ICT solutions – preferably TechnologyOne, Humanforce, Telstra Health products and Community Services products
  • Knowledge of the Aged Care and Community Services industry with multiple business functions is desirable

In addition, you will be strong in relationship building and being able to transform technical data into understandable reporting and communication.

If you look forward to the opportunity to see your work result in enhanced delivery of care services to people, then apply now.
