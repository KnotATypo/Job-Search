Bravus is a diversified group of Australian businesses delivering integrated energy and infrastructure services for the future. We walk the talk on the energy transition by helping to provide a sustainable and affordable global energy mix.

We produce high-quality thermal coal for export at the Carmichael mine and renewable energy from the Rugby Run solar farm, both in central Queensland. Our Bowen Rail Company, Abbot Point Operations and North Queensland Export Terminal businesses transport high quality coal from Queensland’s Bowen and Galilee basins to the world.

These operations provide reliable and affordable energy solutions that help reduce poverty in the Asia Pacific region and power growth. We are proud to provide secure jobs and opportunities for businesses in regional Queensland, and to help to support local economies so towns, cities and their residents can thrive.

We are part of the global Adani Group. Together, our operations are progressing the global transition to a lower emissions future – and fast. The Adani Group is the world’s largest multinational solar energy company and a pioneer in emerging renewables technologies via Adani New Industries Limited, in areas such as green hydrogen.

ABOUT THE ROLE

Are you enthusiastic about overseeing the entire lifecycle of applications, from procurement to maintenance?

This position entails crafting application architecture, executing projects, and pinpointing opportunities for enhancement. If you're a strategic-minded individual with exceptional organisational abilities, this opportunity is tailor-made for you.

The position can be based in our Townsville or Brisbane office working a 5/2 Monday to Friday roster. Occasional travel to Principal sites and other financial centres may be necessary as per business needs.

Responsibilities of the role include:

  • Manage an application portfolio and associated projects
  • Serve as the Single Point of Contact for all application-related tasks, including procurement, installation, configuration, and maintenance
  • Develop and maintain application architecture to ensure optimal performance
  • Evaluate, implement, and deliver key projects to meet organisational needs
  • Identify opportunities for application optimisation and process improvement
  • Coordinate periodic training sessions for easy adoption and continuous improvement
  • Act as an escalation point of contact for all application-related issues
  • Liaise with business users to deliver dashboards and monitor system health
  • Manage software inventory and arrange cyber security assessments
  • Provide administrative support across various applications
  • Collaborate with stakeholders and IT team to develop strategic visions and ensure compliance
  • Manage application maintenance outsourcing and software contract agreements
  • Develop tools to track stakeholder satisfaction and maintain relevant documentation.
