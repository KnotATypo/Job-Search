We’re building Queensland’s future

CBGU JV is working on Queensland’s largest infrastructure project and we’re building our team for the future. South East Queensland is about to embark on a period of sustained growth in the lead up to the 2032 Olympics, and we are thrilled to be involved in developing Cross River Rail, which will forever change the way Queenslander’s travel.

CBGU JV brings together CPB Contractors, BAM, Ghella and UGL to deliver the Tunnel and Station Package for Brisbane’s Cross River Rail, the 10.2 kilometre rail line between Dutton Park and Bowen Hills, including 5.9 kilometres of tunnel under the Brisbane River and CBD. The project also includes construction of four new underground stations at Roma Street, Albert Street, Boggo Road and Woolloongabba, where the future upgraded Olympic stadium will stand.

All project sites and offices are based in inner city Brisbane, and what’s not to love. Queensland’s ‘River City’ is booming with activity, while still retaining its laidback lifestyle. Don’t miss out on the chance to be part of working on South-East Queensland’s largest infrastructure project that will leave a lasting legacy for the city of Brisbane for years to come.

One project team, building career opportunities for you.

Reporting to the Project Manager, this role sits within the construction delivery team. You will be responsible for providing on-site engineering support to assist in the delivery of successful project outcomes for a large-scale underground station site. We are particularly interested in your recent experience in coordinating and overseeing civil/structures or building trade packages.

You will be responsible for:

  • Monitoring key construction requirements to ensure they are achieved in line with agreed specifications.
  • Managing work conducted on site by subcontractors and suppliers.
  • Implementing prompt and safe corrective action on site if required.
  • Maintaining all relevant project document control and records.
  • This is not a design role - must have construction site experience as a Project Engineer

Minimum requirements for this role include the following:

  • Recognised degree in Engineering or Construction Management.
  • Demonstrated site experience coordinating subcontractors, preferably in a large-scale building or infrastructure project environment.
  • Ability to apply engineering principles to comply with scope of works and technical criteria.
  • Experienced in overseeing subcontractor safety, quality and progress to achieve desired project outcomes.

At CBGU JV , we are committed to supporting women in construction and Indigenous advancement and participation, and we encourage people of all cultural backgrounds to apply.

Please note: This role is being sourced through CBGU JV partners directly and we will not accept applications via external recruitment agencies.
