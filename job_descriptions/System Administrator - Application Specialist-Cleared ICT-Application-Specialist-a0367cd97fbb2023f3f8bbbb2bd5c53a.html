System Administrator - Application Specialist

Cleared are working with a Global leading defence aerospace enterprise and are looking to speak to a System Administrator Application Specialist with preferably a NV2 clearance based in Brisbane!

The skilled System Administrator Application Specialist will become a vital part of the team in Brisbane, contributing on a Major Defence Program.
The programme stands as a pinnacle in providing Australia with one of the most advanced airspace battle management capabilities globally.

In this crucial role, you will be integral to the client's Infrastructure team, supporting regional activities in Australia & South Pacific. This includes the distribution, training, and professional services, Defence and training and repair businesses, as well as autonomous systems. Your responsibilities will span general corporate support for Infrastructure activities, involving the implementation, maintenance, support, and configuration of Network and Infrastructure equipment.

Key Responsibilities

Install and optimize software applications, ensuring compliance with security standards, and collaborate with vendors and other IT teams during setup.
Regularly update and patch applications, ensuring they are protected against vulnerabilities, and monitor their performance.

Serve as the primary point of contact for application-related inquiries, providing timely resolution and exceptional customer service.
Develop and update documentation related to application installation, configuration, troubleshooting procedures, and user guides.
Collaborate closely with the IT team to seamlessly integrate applications with existing infrastructure and systems.

Identify opportunities to streamline application-related processes, enhance user experience, and optimize system performance, proposing and implementing solutions for improved efficiency and productivity.
Conduct delivery systems installation, configuration, and disposition of servers and workstations, validating end-to-end system functionality through unit and integration testing.

Experience/Qualifications
A bachelor's degree in computer science, Information Technology, or a related field is desirable (or equivalent experience) with a minimum of 5 years.
Proven experience as a System Administrator or Application Specialist, preferably in a corporate environment.
Preferred experience with Windows/Linux/Hyper-V/VMWare/Citrix.
Familiarity with virtualization technologies (e.g., VMware, Hyper-V) is desirable.
Solid understanding of networking principles and protocols (e.g., TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP).
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills to identify and resolve application issues systematically and logically.
Applicants must be Australian Citizens to meet Defence security requirements with the ability to obtain an NV2 Security Clearance.
