Role snapshot

As an important part of our People & Culture team, you'll play a critical role in administering and managing our HRIS, Dayforce system, including:

  • Configuring the system and managing user accounts, access controls, and security.
  • Ensuring data accuracy through regular audits and troubleshooting any issues.
  • Providing technical support to business users and contributing to their training.
  • Developing & maintaining system documentation.
  • Collaborating with stakeholders to identify and implement system enhancements & improvements.
  • Generating and distributing reports and maintaining the organizational hierarchy.
  • Supporting compliance with data privacy regulations and managing test environments and accounts.
  • Contributing to the rollout of new releases.

Our successful candidate

Ideally you have a degree in Information Systems, Computer Science, Business Administration or related, along with proven experience in working with a HRIS platform (preferably Dayforce). You also have a strong understanding of HR processes & procedures.

We're also seeking the following qualities:

  • Strong understanding of Dayforce HRIS system and previous experience setting up a similar function from scratch.
  • Strong attention to detail.
  • Ability to manage conflicting priorities and meet deadlines.
  • Excellent communication skills, and able to adapt to both technical and non-technical users.
  • Strong analytical & problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality with sensitive information.
  • Work both as part of a team & autonomously.
