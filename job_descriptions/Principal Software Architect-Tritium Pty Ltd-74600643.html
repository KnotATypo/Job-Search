About the Role

This position reports directly to the Vice President of Software Engineering at Tritium PTY Ltd and is based at Murarrie, Brisbane.

As the Principal Software Architect (PSA), you will be a critical asset in shaping and guiding the design, development, and security of our software solutions. Your primary responsibility will be to ensure the alignment of software architecture with security standards, compliance regulations, and contemporary best practices. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams, provide technical leadership, and contribute to the creation of secure and innovative software systems.

The PSA’s top priority is to drive emerging development, industry best practices and security by design into Tritium software platforms.

Key responsibilities for this role include, but are not limited to:
  • Security and Compliance:
    • Lead the integration of security best practices, including PCI DSS, GDPR, and cyber security standards, into software design and development processes.
    • Collaborate with legal and compliance teams to ensure software solutions adhere to relevant regulations and data protection requirements.
  • Protocols and Communications
    • Lead the evolution of Tritium products to meet compliance with standards such as OCPP, OSCP, ISO 15118
    • Assist in customer negotiations on remote access technologies, techniques and protocols.
  • Architecture Design:
    • Develop scalable, robust, and secure software architectures that meet business requirements while aligning with industry best practices.
    • Design systems that can withstand security threats, including but not limited to authentication, authorisation, encryption, and data integrity.
    • Focus on system quality attributes, architectural tactics and metrics.
    • Implement feedback loops for architectural decisions.
  • Modern Techniques and Practices:
    • Drive the adoption of modern software techniques, such as microservices architecture, containerisation, and service-oriented design, to enhance agility and scalability.
    • Stay updated on emerging trends and technologies to make informed recommendations for architecture improvements.
    • Provide advice on cloud architectures and patterns
    • Provide advice on IoT architectures and communications
  • Technical Leadership:
    • Provide mentorship and guidance to development teams on security-conscious coding practices, architecture decisions, and implementation strategies.
    • Collaborate with stakeholders to prioritize security-related features and enhancements.
    • Actively engage software architectures and technical leads to drive an integrated vision
    • Engage teams and provide practical advice on managing technical debt.
  • Risk Assessment and Mitigation:
    • Conduct risk assessments and threat modelling to identify potential vulnerabilities and security risks in software solutions.
    • Design and implement strategies to mitigate risks and respond to security incidents effectively.
  • Continuous Improvement:
    • Champion continuous improvement initiatives, promoting a culture of learning and innovation within the development organization.
    • Collaborate with QA and DevOps teams to automate security testing and monitoring processes.
About You

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field.
  • Proven track record of designing software architectures and solutions.
  • Extensive experience with security compliance frameworks, such as PCI DSS and GDPR, exposure to remote access, payment systems and control protocols.
  • In-depth knowledge of cyber security principles, protocols, and technologies.
  • Sound knowledge of modelling languages such as UML and/or SysML
  • Strong expertise in software languages (e.g., C++, Java, C#, Python) and architectural patterns.
  • Proficiency in modern practices like microservices, containerisation (Docker/Kubernetes), and service-oriented architectures, including CI/CD pipelines and DevOps/DevSecOps
  • Strong understanding of data collection, storage and manipulation techniques will be highly valued.
  • Excellent communication skills to convey complex technical concepts to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
  • Relevant certifications or understanding (e.g., CISSP, CSSLP, TOGAF) are a plus.
