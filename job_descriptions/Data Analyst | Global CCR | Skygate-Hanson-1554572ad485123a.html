Data Analyst | Heidelberg Materials

Join our amazing Global team as our new Data Analyst located at our Brisbane Global Skygate office.

The successful candidate will be responsible for supporting global initiatives by analysing data that clearly identify business improvement opportunities and returns on improvement initiatives. Due to the nature of our team, this role will suit a Data Analytics professional with at least 2 years' experience in this field. Successful candidates will be well regarded if they have skills in Microsoft Excel, Tableau, SQL and Microsoft applications, or other similar products.

What you’ll get to do:

  • Create models and professional communication presentations that represent complex ideas in a simple manner.
  • Proactively source and work with data from 40 countries.
  • Investigate and interpret results to deliver consistent and replicable business insights.
  • Analyse large complex data sets and convert them to management information.
  • Problem solve core issues within unstructured or incomplete data sets providing insights currently not visible.
  • Show off your effective time management skills while prioritising tasks and adapting to changing demands.

What you bring to the table:

  • You are looking to develop your skills, knowledge and career within data analytics.
  • Experience in Microsoft Excel, Tableau, Power BI, PowerPoint, SAP/HANA is advantageous.
  • Experience in SQL essential.
  • Exposure in production and logistics processes would be highly regarded however not essential.
  • Have the confidence and assertiveness to present opinions and strategies based on analytically supported findings.
  • Highly self-motivated with a result focused approach to planning and overcoming any obstacles.
  • The ability to develop strong partnerships with colleagues globally through virtual platforms.
  • Flexibility in the hours that you work, may be required to work outside of normal hours to communicate with global peers/stakeholders.

What’s in it for you:

  • You will be rewarded with an attractive remuneration package.
  • Be part of a small dynamic group within HeidelbergMaterials with Global exposure.
  • Exciting ongoing career and development opportunities.
  • Undercover onsite parking at Skygate (Brisbane Airport)
  • Weekly coffee meetings with the team (Coffee and Code!)
  • Quarterly "Potluck" & "Morning tea" gatherings in the office.
  • Regular team events such as steps challenge, social outings etc.


HeidelbergMaterials are an equal opportunity employer, and we employ people from all walks of life. We’re all individuals and we encourage applications from Indigenous Australians, people with disability, those from diverse cultural backgrounds, LGBTQI+ community, as long as you have full Australian work rights and can turn up every day “fit for work”, then we'd love to hear from you.
