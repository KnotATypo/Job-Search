We offer an exciting opportunity to work with our fantastic Brisbane based team to provide support to our clients using the COSTAR Tyre and Automotive software.

ABOUT US

MICROHOUSE has been providing software for the Tyre & Automotive industries for over 40 years. From humble beginnings in 1982 as a family business, operating out of shared office space, Microhouse has grown into a leader in our industry across Australia and New Zealand. We have stayed at the forefront of technology services and continue to deliver on our promise to provide successful, reliable business systems.

In 2001 we formed a partnership with COSTAR Computer systems and have continued to offer our clients class leading software specific to the Tyre and Automotive industry which allows us to offer software systems for tyre retail, tyre wholesale, automotive workshop management, inventory control, purchasing and bookkeeping. Review our website (www.costar.com.au) and Facebook page to learn a little bit more about us.

ABOUT THE ROLE

Based in Brisbane, the Support Specialist role is a permanent full-time position working with a close-knit team providing second and third level software support. We are NOT looking for a networking or hardware guru, but rather a person that understands how (SQL) databases and server/client software operate under the hood. Someone who has an interest in computer languages but doesn't want to be full blown developer would be perfect for the role.

Our support is provided by phone, email and video conferencing and covers point of sale, inventory management, and financial queries to meet the growing needs of our clients and to match the increasing capabilities of our software.

The successful applicant will:

  • Undertake a well-established and comprehensive in-house training program.
  • Interact with clients ranging from small retail clients to large national companies.
  • Provide effective and courteous second and third level software support via both electronic and phone correspondence.
  • Record, track, and document help desk requests through to the final resolution.
  • Identify, escalate, and triage requests to the next level of support in a timely manner.
  • Troubleshoot Windows operating systems.
  • Identify potential changes and system improvements to present to the team for consideration and implementation.
  • Complete testing of software changes including the documentation of results.
  • Update support documentation when required.
