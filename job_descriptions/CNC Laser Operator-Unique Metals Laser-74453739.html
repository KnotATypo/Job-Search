About Unique Metals Laser

Proudly Australian and a privately owned company, Unique Metals Laser’s history stretches back to 1972. Unique Metals Laser have multiple sites in Australian mainland states that provide high precision laser cutting and fabrication services to a variety of industries.

For more information about Unique Metals Laser, and the services we provide please visit our website at www.uniquelaser.com.au
