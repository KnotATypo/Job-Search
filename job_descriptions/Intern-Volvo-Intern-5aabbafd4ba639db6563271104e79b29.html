Transport is at the core of modern society. Imagine using your expertise to shape sustainable transport solutions for the future? If you seek to make a difference on a global scale, working with next-gen technologies and the sharpest collaborative teams, then we could be a perfect match.

The primary objective of the Logistics Engineering Intern role is to work cross functionally with other departments in order to help the team identify the bottlenecks and deliver reports on the relevant improvements to fix the problem. The candidate will participate in Volvo programs that drive efficiencies through data driven KPI’s and process improvements according to Volvo Operations Concepts.

This position needs to primarily understand the current Way of working through on the job training and support Material Controllers with relevant data skills such as Excel, Qlik View/Power Bi in order to support them for capacity planning and KPI’s. The internship will provide opportunity for application development, reporting and KPI development.

  • Support the management of incoming parts and setup the new part locations.
  • Coordinate with relevant stakeholders to remove the obsolescence parts.
  • Work cross functionally to investigate and promote continuous improvement initiatives where applicable to the position
  • Follow up local and globally agreed, or other relevant, KPI’s.
  • Participate in local / regional / global networks to contribute to the process development.
  • Data Analytics, ability to analyse operation system functional requirements to determine required data structures and behaviours, to improve processes.
  • Ability to manipulate data from various sources, to support design recommendations and idea generation for allocated projects. Eg. SQL Databases, Excel & Share point.
  • Create detailed functional specifications and low-level designs describing functionality
  • Participate in full app lifecycle: concept, design, build, deploy, test and release to  plant.
  • Write and maintain documentation for all features in development.
  • Accurately translate user and business needs into functional code

Qualifications

  • Studying in Industrial engineering/ Logistics Engineering/Supply chain management or any Engineering preferred or TAFE/BA Degree qualification in electronic and computer science or equivalent
  • Intermediate or advanced Microsoft Excel and Outlook skills required.
  • Experienced working in QlikView /Power BI/ SQL is considered a plus.
  • Strong analytical capabilities.
  • Experience working in a manufacturing environment is considered a plus.
  • Solid understanding of logistics systems, processes, lean manufacturing and Kaizen.
  • Ability to work within a team environment.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication.
  • Ability to engage and communicate with internal and external stakeholders.
