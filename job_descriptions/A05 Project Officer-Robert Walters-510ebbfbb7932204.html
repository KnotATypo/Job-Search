Full job description

Our client is seeking a Project Officer to join their team. This role offers an exciting opportunity to contribute to a major project in South East Queensland, working closely with primary producers and large landholders. The successful candidate will provide technical advice, organise workshops, and coordinate project activities. This role requires a strong commitment to stakeholder engagement and communication, and the ability to work collaboratively within a multi-disciplinary team.

What you'll do:


You will be responsible for planning activities that foster engagement with primary producers and large landholders. Your expertise will be crucial in providing technical advice on the program management strategies. You will also be involved in organising workshops and meetings to promote these strategies. Your role will extend to coordinating project activities, preparing necessary documentation, and providing technical support to the operational delivery of the program. Collaboration is key as you will work closely with various teams within the National Program.

  • Plan and undertake activities that support communication, engagement and extension with primary producers and large landholders.
  • Provide technical advice on effective implementation of the program, including risk mitigation, surveillance, treatment and reporting.
  • Establish, promote and organise workshops and meetings to promote the program.
  • Participate in workshops with key stakeholders, maintaining effective communication and relationships.
  • Coordinate project activities including tracking of deliverables and risks.
  • Prepare documentation and provide support for ordering and distribution of treatment products and equipment relating to fire ant management.


What you bring:


The ideal candidate for the Project Officer role brings a wealth of experience or qualifications in stakeholder engagement and communication. Your ability to plan and undertake activities that foster communication and engagement will be crucial in this role. You possess technical knowledge on effective implementation of pest management strategies, and have experience in organising workshops or meetings for promotional or educational purposes. Your strong coordination skills will be utilised in tracking project deliverables and risks, while your proficiency in preparing documentation will support procurement processes. Your ability to provide technical support in operational delivery of pest management programs, coupled with your experience working collaboratively within a multi-disciplinary team, makes you an ideal fit for this role.

  • Experience or qualification in stakeholder engagement and communication is highly desirable.
  • Ability to plan and undertake activities that support communication and engagement with diverse stakeholders.
  • Technical knowledge on effective implementation of pest management strategies.
  • Experience in organising workshops or meetings for promotional or educational purposes.
  • Strong coordination skills for tracking project deliverables and risks.
  • Proficiency in preparing documentation for procurement processes.
  • Ability to provide technical support in operational delivery of pest management programs.
  • Experience working collaboratively within a multi-disciplinary team.
