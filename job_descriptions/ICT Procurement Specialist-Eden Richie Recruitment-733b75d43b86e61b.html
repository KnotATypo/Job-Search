Full job description

Eden Ritchie is seeking an ICT Procurement Specialist to undertake activities relating to ICT procurement to meet the needs of the Department. The role will involve a high level of complex ICT procurement activities, including multiple ongoing ICT projects that require contract variations and extensions.

The role will report directly to the Procurement & Contracts Manager, however, will require close liaison with the ICT Director and Senior Program Manager within the Information Communication and Technology Services (ICTS) branch.

  • 3 month contract with possible 2 x 6 months extension
  • Brisbane CBD location

As the ICT Procurement Specialist, your key responsibilities will include:

  • Assist with preparing briefing notes for approval by senior and executive level management.
  • Prepare IT related procurement and evaluation plans.
  • Prepare ICT procurement and evaluation plans.
  • Prepare tender (Request for Quote (RFQ) or Invitation to Offer (ITO)) documentation and associated attachments.
  • Assist business units in understanding the procurement process within Department, including setting expectations and timeframes.
  • Assist stakeholders with writing scope requirements/specifications.
  • Assist the business in undertaking both high value, complex and non-complex procurement activities.
  • Guide and assist evaluation teams/panels throughout the offer assessment and contract negotiation processes in accordance with departmental policies.
  • Finalise evaluation reports and manage contract executions.
  • Records management using SharePoint and Share drive.
  • Liaise with Department’s shared service provider, QSS, to resolve purchase order and vendor discrepancies.
  • Prepare purchase requisitions and amendments for processing by QSS.
  • Liaise with the Department Finance and Budgeting teams for ad-hoc day to day procurement questions that may arise.

Highly Desirable skills and knowledge:

  • High level of communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Knowledge and demonstrated experience in the use of SAP financial system.
  • Knowledge and demonstrated experience in the application of the Queensland Procurement Policy and all related policies and procedures.
  • High level of experience in ICT Procurement.
  • Experience in using Queensland Government QITC suite of templates.
  • Experience in developing Queensland Government contracts and tenders, including associated documentation.
  • Demonstrated experience in the use of SharePoint Online.
  • Ability to prioritise workload to meet deadlines.
  • Experience in undertaking ICT procurement activities.
  • Organisational and time management skills.

The candidate will require a high level of understanding and experience in running end to end complex ICT procurement processes.
