IMMEDIATE – Oracle Developer - 12 months (Extensions) – Flexible WFH - QLD Govt Organisation.

Role Title: Oracle Developer

Contract: 12 Months ( Possible Extension)

WFH: R equired to be in the office 50%, which is 3 days 1 week and 2 the following week.

Location: Brisbane (on-site)

The Oracle Developer will be responsible for the following:

Provide Oracle (Forms and Reports) application development and maintenance skills for Business-As-Usual and Minor Works undertaking analysis, design, construction and system testing tasks within the Oracle environment.
  • Experience in Java application development would be viewed as highly desirable
  • Work closely with stakeholders to analyse requirements and advise on technical constraints or opportunities.
  • Participate in multi-disciplinary teams comprising of project managers, technical leads, business analysts, developers, testers and external providers in the design, development, testing and support of software applications.
  • Develop documentation relating to development and enhancements, including production of user stories, technical design documentation and contribution to end user documentation.
  • Develop and maintain a broad knowledge of the full range of applications in the Oracle Developer suite.
  • Provide technical advice and recommendations to team members and product stakeholders on requirements for development.
  • Provide estimates, progress reporting and notifications of slippage Application Services management team.
  • Embrace Agile delivery methodologies and work with the delivery team to adapt Agile practice to the flavours required by the delivery team.
  • Conduct unit testing and assist in system and integration testing of the code ensuring conformance to specifications, standards and accepted practice.
  • Work closely with testing team members to support technical requirements for systems testing and user acceptance testing as required and support test staff in all aspects of test execution.
  • Undertake quality reviews, peer reviews and production support activities
  • Keep abreast of, and contribute to development standards, ensuring that standards are adhered to in all work undertaken.
  • Keep abreast of developments in the Oracle product and participate in knowledge levelling sessions with team members to ensure best current approach and practice are always maintained.
  • Be available to work outside standard hours and be responsible for aspects of the process to release code into production when required.
  • Provide timesheets and task feedback to management weekly, using the standard software.
  • Provide skills transfer to staff, including documentation of decisions made and the approach taken; and other activities as directed by Application Manager.
