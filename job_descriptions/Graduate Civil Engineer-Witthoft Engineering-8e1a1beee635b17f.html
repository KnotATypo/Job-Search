Full job description

Position Summary

Graduate Civil Engineer responsible for the preparation of detailed design and project deliverables for civil infrastructure, specifically in the water and wastewater industry, including:

  • Preparing engineering design deliverables including calculations, CAD drawings, and specifications.
  • Undertaking hydraulic modelling of water/sewer conveyance systems.
  • Producing 2D & 3D CAD design drawings.
  • Conducting routine site inspections/audits as the 'eyes and ears' of the senior engineers, including preparing inspection/audit documentation and status reports.
  • Preparing project and contract documentation, including bills of quantities and project schedules.
  • Contributing to preparation of proposals to provide professional services to clients.
  • Shadowing Senior Engineers and assisting in the management of projects and sub-consultants’ services.

Essential Qualifications, Experience & Competencies

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Civil or Structural Engineering (or equivalent).
  • Construction Induction (White Card).
  • Current Driver’s License.

Experience:

  • 0-2+ years post graduate.
  • Under-graduate or post-graduate experience in urban & civil engineering including but not limited to:
    • Urban development (sub-divisions)
    • Roads and bulk earthworks
    • Stormwater / drainage
    • Water and sewer reticulation
    • Structural engineering
  • Working knowledge of AutoCAD and 3D design packages.
  • Working knowledge of GIS packages.
  • Knowledge of relevant local design codes & standards (SEQCode, WSAA, Australia Standards) viewed favourably.
  • Advanced level experience with MS Word, MS Excel and related programs.
  • Competency in data analysis and manipulation.

Competencies (knowledge, skills, and behaviours):

  • Effective communication skills, including the ability to effectively communicate requests and instructions to individuals and groups.
  • Strong mathematical and technical skills.
  • An enthusiasm to learn and embrace a rare opportunity to gain a rounded experience in a technical career pathway, under the close supervision and mentoring of senior staff.
  • Commitment to, and enthusiasm for, client service and delivery.
  • Flexibility and adaptability to manage changing work requirements and varying volumes of work.
  • Ability to work cooperatively and effectively within the team and the organisation.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously, solve problems, manage and meet deadlines, and maintain a high quality of work.
  • Personal presentation that reflects a professional image and the values of the organisation.
  • Effective time management, and the ability to plan and manage assigned design tasks and take accountability for delivering outcomes.

Main Activities and Responsibilities

Civil Design

  • Assisting Senior/ Principal staff with various design and project related tasks
  • Development of civil design deliverables & documentation for water and wastewater infrastructure including:
    • Engineering calculations
    • Pipeline design and trenchless technologies
    • Hydraulic/surge modelling
    • Detailed design drawings (AutoCAD)
    • Technical specifications & reports
    • Scopes of work
    • Coordinating design outputs with drafting services

Project Management

  • Liaison with clients and other stakeholders.
  • Organise design inputs from various sub-contractors.
  • Preparation and management of design schedule & cost control for design packages.
  • Contribution to tender submissions.

Site Works

  • Site inspections and audits and works supervision.
  • Manage sub-contractors onsite: survey, geotech, Service location, investigations etc.
  • NOTE – Site works could be local and interstate.

Benefits

  • Competitive renumeration.
  • Accelerated career and technical development.
  • Opportunity to solidify your position as an active and valued member within a growing civil design team, in a boutique water engineering business.
  • Rare opportunity to have close mentorship and direct access to very experienced water industry professionals.
  • Exposure to a variety of work and tasks, including site-based roles.
  • Opportunities for professional development in both technical and project management.
  • As you gain experience and seniority, you will handle more advanced design or analysis work, management of more complex projects, client relationships, and internal technical resources.
