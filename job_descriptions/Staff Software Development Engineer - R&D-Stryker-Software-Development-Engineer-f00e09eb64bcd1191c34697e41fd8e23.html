Who we are

Stryker is a global leader in medical technologies and, together with its customers, is driven to make healthcare better. The company offers innovative products and services in MedSurg, Neurotechnology, Orthopaedics and Spine that help improve patient and healthcare outcomes. Alongside its customers around the world, Stryker impacts more than 130 million patients annually. More information is available at www.stryker.com.

The Role

The Staff Software Engineer is responsible for a wide variety of investigative research and pilot technology development, advancing Stryker capabilities and supporting the long-term strategy of the R&D Lab. The Staff Software Engineer provides technical software development skills, innovative thinking, and collaborative support, while engaging with Stryker divisions and University partners to deliver new technologies and platforms.

Please note that this role is a 2 year Fulltime Contract.

What you will do

  • Collaborate with R&D team, marketing partners and clinicians to progress an iterative design process that executes user research, identifies user needs and generates innovative solutions to meet those needs.
  • Work on and lead cross-functional design teams to develop new software products from concept through to product development, making a real impact on patient outcomes.
  • Interface with clinical advisors and internal stakeholders to gather input for design and evaluation of new products, translating user needs into design requirements.
  • Partner with Research team in reviewing clinical research and literature review. Gather input for design and evaluation of new products.
  • Plan and conduct engineering research, design, development and/or manufacturing engineering projects as assigned, using agile methodology. Perform a wide variety of technical work which will lead to new or improved products/product lines/technology platforms.
  • Understand and utilize design controls within the New Product Development Process (NPDP) to support in the review and authorship of NPDP documentation.
  • Effectively communicate new product ideas to all levels of the organisation.

Critical Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in a Software Engineering/Computer Science field required. Masters or PHD preferred.
  • Experience working on medical related software innovations preferred
  • 4+ years of front and back end development experience.
  • Excellent analytical skills, demonstrated systems thinking ability and experience working across software, mechanical and electrical subsystems.
  • Demonstrated software design problem-solving skills.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills; ability to present issues, plans and objectives.
  • Ability to work within multi-disciplinary teams, including stakeholders from multiple institutions.
  • Experience with prototyping and system troubleshooting, as well as system validation and documentation.
  • Motivated, resourceful, ambitious, enthusiastic and attentive to detail.
  • Demonstrated technical impact on a design project or product platform.
