Responsibilities:

Operate and program OTC/Daihen Robotic Welders to perform high-quality welds according to specifications.

Set up, maintain, and troubleshoot robotic welding equipment.

Ensure proper selection of welding parameters, wire feed speeds, and voltage settings.

Perform multipass welding techniques to achieve strong and reliable weld joints.

Utilize synchromotion with both tilt/turn and headstock positioners for efficient welding processes.

Inspect and test welded joints for accuracy, quality, and adherence to standards.

Collaborate with the production team to meet production goals and ensure timely delivery of finished products.

Maintain a clean and safe work environment, following all safety guidelines and protocols.
