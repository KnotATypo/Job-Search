A career at QinetiQ – Your fortune of opportunity
Fast-track your career in our 18-month program
Work with the industry’s brightest minds

At QinetiQ, our graduates work alongside industry experts in a company that actively helps its people thrive and grow. The richness of QinetiQ's culture and commitment to ongoing development provides our graduates with an abundance of experiences to develop their skills and create a wealth of possibilities for their future. We call it your fortune of opportunity.

Who we are

QinetiQ is a global defence and security company and employs more than 8000 people in more than 50 locations around the world. We offer our customers premier expertise in advice, services and creative technology-based products and help solve some of their most challenging problems.

Our determination to deliver and improve today and anticipate and shape tomorrow, makes QinetiQ a very exciting, rewarding and respected place to work and learn. Globally QinetiQ has four Sectors, one of which is the ‘Australia Sector’’. The Australia Sector has more than 1,200 employees operating across four countries, namely Australia, Canada, Germany and the United Kingdom.

Our Graduate Development Program

You will work on unique, challenging and interesting projects alongside inspiring leaders who are experts in their fields. Don’t rely on luck, embrace and immerse yourself in a career at QinetiQ where your fortune of opportunity awaits!

The opportunity

As part of the commercial and procurement team you will be a part of contributing to and creating positive relationships with our customers.

You will have the opportunity to work as part of integrated project teams, supporting the maintenance of current contracts and developing commercial bids/ proposals working within a fast-paced environment.

You will have an interest in commercial business winning and/or contract management and pricing activities in the Defence Sector.

A degree in Commerce, Business, Law or similar is required to be successful in being considered for the role.

Vacancies available in Brisbane or Melbourne.

How to apply?

You must be due to complete an appropriate university degree qualification by the end of 2024, or have already completed your degree

Whilst applications must be made online, enquiries are welcome via email: EarlyCareers.Au@qinetiq.com.au Applications should be addressed to Sarah Middleton, Talent Acquisition Specialist, Early Careers and must include:

A copy of your resume

A copy of your academic transcript to date

Cover letters are optional but are highly encouraged

Applications close 30th April, 2024.

Due to the nature of our work, all applicants must be eligible for Australian Defence security clearance .
