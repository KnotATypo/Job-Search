Adora Fertility is one of Australia's leading fertility providers and closely aligns with what we are and who we aspire to be; a team of compassionate and specialised healthcare professionals, utilising modern science to improve the chances of conception for our patients.

We fundamentally changed the fertility landscape by making high quality fertility services including IVF available with almost all costs covered by Medicare, saving patients thousands of dollars compared to other fertility clinics.

About the Role

We are looking for our next Fertility Coordinator! This is a full-time opportunity based in our Taringa Clinic.

As first point of contact for our patients, we are seeking that special individual with genuine warmth, attentiveness, and positivity. The primary purpose of this role is to provide a high-quality service and support to the healthcare professionals, nurses and patients in our Fertility unit.

Key Responsibilities

  • Preparing patient packs for upcoming nursing consults
  • Preparing patient packs for repeat cycles and mailing them
  • Creating and filling patient checklists as part of preparation for cycles.
  • Emailing/Mailing pathology forms and scan referrals to patients when required.
  • Ensuring all checklists complete in our Patient Management System and that all consent forms and screening bloods are current as patients are commencing a treatment cycle.
  • Manage patient collection trays and pending consent documents.
  • Ensure all collateral patient material is topped up and order through marketing as required.
  • Ensuring medical records, including results, correspondence and any other related material are up to date and filed correctly and in a timely fashion.
  • Manage outbound calls for general patient enquiries via email/website.
  • Manage patient enquiries as deemed appropriate for skillset as triaged by Nursing team and admin.

About you

  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
  • A patient-centred warm and caring approach

  • Strong computer skills
