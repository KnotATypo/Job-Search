About Corporate Enterprise Solutions:
This exciting job opportunity falls under the Corporate Enterprise Solutions branch within the Department of Health and provides solutions and services that support Queensland Health's enterprise corporate applications while also supporting the payroll transactional processes. This application suite includes the largest integrated rostering and payroll solution in the public sector and the finance, business and logistics solution and management environment.

What we are looking for:
  • Experience in or the ability to rapidly acquire the skills and knowledge to utilise payroll and rostering solutions and maintain rostering and HR master data in an accurate and efficient manner.
  • Well-developed communication skills with demonstrated ability to provide quality customer service to various stakeholders in a large and complex organisation.
  • Proven ability to work within a team environment, follow standard business processes and contribute to process improvements to meet timeframes and ensure team outcomes are achieved.
  • Demonstrated knowledge in or ability to rapidly acquire the knowledge of calculating and reconciling WorkCover reimbursements to ensure correct entitlements are paid to the employee.
