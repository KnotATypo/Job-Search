Digital Convergence is a leading consultancy in strategic business process redesign and automation. We look at clients’ whole-of-business objectives to drive our analysis of systems, efficiencies and resources, process mapping and redesign, selection of management and automation software, project management, implementation, transition, change management, performance support, training management and post implementation audit and review. Independent from solution vendors, we focus on the optimisation of innovative, game-changing outcomes for clients.

The Role

Due to an increase in project work leading to increased business opportunities, we are currently looking for an experienced consultant wanting to expand their skill sets through individual growth and learning opportunities. We are looking for someone who is able to consult on customer ERP implementations and on-going client work. The expectation is that you will have at least 5 years Pronto-Xi or other ERP consulting experience.

The role of the Consultant will work closely with key stakeholders, Project Managers, 3rd party software systems, subject matter experts, and end users across all organizational areas to develop a strong, shared understanding of solutions to business problems.

Responsibilities

The Key Responsibilities include:
Understanding client’s business processes to providing advice on best practise
Host requirement workshops with customers to determine business requirements
Assist with defining customer’s business goals and structuring plans to ensure the customer’s vision is achieved.
Definition and documentation the important metrics that define success for the customer and more specifically the project/s
Analysing and documenting business requirements for an issue / enhancement
Produce business process documents to visualise ‘As-Is’ or ‘To-Be’ processes
Provide input into the project timelines for the modules and areas of expertise that you cover
Consulting on several modules in the Pronto-Xi application
Completing data migration activities
Completing and co-ordinating test plans
Conducting End User training to coaching team members
Develop and/or utilise existing reporting tools that monitor the key metrics of success for the customer and ensure the projects delivered show improvement.
Provide ongoing input and mentoring into the Consulting team to constantly assist with Digital Convergence’s philosophy of continual improvement.
When required, complete support tickets and assist other team members with support tickets
Required Experience & Competencies

PRONTO Consultant within the PRONTO system, together with:
5+ years’ experience implementing, administrating Pronto or other ERP system 2+ Years’ experience with 730 or above.
Experience in implementing Pronto-Xi or other ERP systems.
Experience in upgrading Pronto-Xi or other ERP systems Experience managing Projects with multiple resources.
Experience in Pronto-Xi modules such as WMS, Distribution, Manufacturing, CRM, or Point of Sale
Experience with documenting requirements and providing system design artefacts.
Experience with training, mentoring, and supporting customers for implemented systems.
Need to be able to manage implementation tasks.
Strong written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to work in a team environment.
Exceptional time management and organisational skills, with the ability to prioritise multiple tasks with attention to detail.
Self-starter attitude
Excellent troubleshooting skills
You will always be open to constructive feedback and suggestions regarding approaches to solution options expressed by management and peers. This may also include following guidance and preferences of management in the direction to take on a solution to ensure quality and value.

You will be able to work as a team and strive for personal development for not only yourself but other members of the team. This includes mentorship and knowledge sharing with less senior team members than yourself.
