THE ROLE:

We are currently seeking an additional Smoke Alarm Scheduling Coordinator to take the lead in scheduling our team of technicians.


KEY RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:

  • Organizing schedules for routine tasks in preparation for sending Entry Notices by property management as necessary.
  • Collaborating with various stakeholders to ensure timely scheduling of required tasks.
  • Ensuring consistent service and timely completion of all works by their deadlines.
  • Coordinating Smoke Alarm upgrades and inspections.
  • Maintaining high efficiency and productivity for our team of technicians on a daily basis, ensuring full utilization of their time and accommodating additional tasks as needed.
  • Creating key lists when necessary to provide exceptional customer service to our Real Estate clients.
  • Maintaining open communication and providing high-quality service to our team and stakeholders.


ABOUT YOU:

The successful candidate will have:

  • Possesses exceptional communication skills and thrives in a fast-paced environment, bringing positivity and energy to both stakeholders and the team.
  • Capable of addressing and resolving issues effectively even under pressure.
  • Proficient in computer literacy with a willingness to learn new systems and processes.
  • Experience in the smoke alarm industry is advantageous but not mandatory.
  • Able to work collaboratively as part of a team or independently.
  • Previous experience in customer service is preferred.
  • Demonstrates strong attention to detail.
  • Familiarity with the Greater Brisbane suburbs is desirable.


WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU:

  • Comprehensive on-the-job training and mentorship from our experienced team.
  • Competitive Remuneration
  • Supportive and inclusive workplace culture.
  • Opportunities for training and development as we continue to grow.
