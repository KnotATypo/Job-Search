About Us:
At Auto & General (A&G), we provide great products and services to safeguard our customers in their time of need. Our range of general insurance products protects customers on the road, at home and on holiday with various Car, Motorcycle, Home, Contents, and Pet products as well as Roadside Assistance.

Our culture of ‘high performance with high integrity’ underpins our values and the way we interact with our customers, the community and each other. We’re excited about the future and we’re always on the lookout for talented, passionate individuals who can help us achieve our goal of being Australia’s best insurer! If this sounds like you, apply today.

The Role :
This role is a key continuous improvement and application support role building out the best digital tools to support Auto and General’s customers. You will support and improve systems used by Auto and General’s customer-facing teams as well as work in one of several cross-functional agile teams, supported by roles including tester, business analyst and product owner. All software is delivered via agile teams and deployed via a modern DevOps/CI pipeline to AWS.

This role will be working within a large technology team, consisting of developers, testers and agile practitioners at Auto and General’s fantastic location in Toowong.

Accountabilities

  • Execute, and contribute to, improvement and innovation ideas for the system.
  • Work collaboratively within a cross functional Agile team, contributing to support, deployment, and development activities as required.
  • Leverage your experience to assist with solution designs and development of our modern architecture.
  • Perform other reasonable and relevant activities as requested from time to time to meet operational requirements

Knowledge and Experience:

Essential:

  • At least 2+ years of Software Development or relevant industry experience
  • Knowledge of modern front-end architecture
  • Solid understanding of NodeJS and Typescript
  • Experience in or knowledge of application and system monitoring tools
  • Ability to work in the Atlassian suite of products. JIRA, Confluence, Bitbucket and Bamboo

Advantageous:

  • Familiarity with other modern web frameworks such as React or Vue
  • Experience with APIs and GraphQL
  • Understanding of cloud deployment with Docker and AWS
  • Experience with Kubernetes
  • Understanding of security implications for web applications (OWASP)
  • Experience in or knowledge of GitLab DevOps Platform
  • A background in financial services applications is an advantage
