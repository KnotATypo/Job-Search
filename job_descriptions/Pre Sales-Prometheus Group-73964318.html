About the Role

In Pre-Sales, you can look forward to partnering with key stakeholders both internally and externally. You will have the opportunity to demonstrate your expertise and provide a range of technical support to our sales team to ensure smooth operations for our customers.

Responsibilities

  • Demonstrate Prometheus solutions
  • This will be carried out via online meetings primarily. There may be occasional onsite visits, webinars, and trade show appearances.
  • Help prospects understand the technical aspects of Prometheus products
  • Provide expertise on business process and workflow and indicate how Prometheus solutions can assist with this effort
  • Assist with the technical aspects of sales documents, including RFPs, RFIs, scopes of work, etc.
  • Help with customer trials, which includes process consulting, software implementation, and training
  • Assist with software implementation projects
  • Conduct end-user training for both customers and software trials users
  • Create functional and technical documentation for new products before the handoff to support

→ Product Demonstrations

  • Enthusiastically run through product demo's
  • Tailor demo’s to each prospective customer, and allow each prospect to begin to understand the benefits
  • Strike a balance between sticking to your agenda versus taking questions & addressing customer curiosities

Skills and Experience

  • A University degree
  • Knowledge of ERP systems is desirable - specifically the plant management and materials management modules – SAP PM & SAP MM, IBM Maximo, Oracle Primavera, JD Edwards industrial modules.
  • Intermediate knowledge of SAP configuration (desirable, not essential)
  • Functional understanding of maintenance work and plant safety and why it is necessary
  • Functional understanding of plant maintenance workflow, including both routine and shut down/turnaround maintenance.
  • Strong project management skills
  • Excellent presentation and communication skills
  • Excellent interpersonal and presentation skills
  • Motivated to own the technical pieces of a deal
  • Meticulous and driven in preparation
  • Functional knowledge of Prometheus software is desirable
