Your role

As a Signals Intelligence Analyst in the Air Force, you’ll play an important role in turning electronic codes and radar signals into intelligence that supports exercises, operations, and missions. You’ll use cutting-edge electronic warfare technology and software to draw on highly classified material and reverse engineer complex signals into useful information.

Your duties may include:

  • Manipulating large volumes of data to map new and historical intelligence to a particular platform (e.g. aircraft).
  • Identifying and correlating other forms of intelligence and reporting to enhance confidence levels in existing intelligence.
  • Providing timely briefs and reporting to deployed ADF and allied platforms.
  • Identifying unusual activity.
  • Analysing technical intelligence to identify radars, and their capabilities and functions.

You’ll be paid from day one to gain all the skills needed with extensive on-the-job training, all while living an adventurous lifestyle and making a difference to Australia.
