Purpose of the Role

As a Technical Writer, you will work across the Technology department to develop, edit, organise, maintain, and oversee internal technical documentation. This documentation supports the end-to-end development teams in managing their applications, onboarding new developers, and creating new functionality and features.

You will need to work closely with SMEs and end-to-end teams to improve the technical documentation quality, and relevance. Additionally, you will consolidate existing documentation, produce fresh content, and ensure quality standards are adhered to moving forward.


Position Accountabilities

  • Organize existing documentation
  • Develop internal document templates
  • Review and edit processes
  • Ensure documentation follows correct procedures
  • Create and update technical documents
  • Guide others in content authoring
  • Collaborate with SMEs for information
  • Improve documentation processes
  • Write technically accurate content

Qualifications and Experience

  • Technical writing experience
  • Ability to handle complex information
  • Interviewing and communication skills
  • Training and formatting abilities
  • Experience in document creation
  • Independent worker with time management skills
  • Stakeholder relationship building
  • User-centric mindset
  • Experienced with Atlassian and Google Docs
