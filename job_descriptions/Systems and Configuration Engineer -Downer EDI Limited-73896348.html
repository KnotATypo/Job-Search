We are looking to expand our highly skilled Engineering and Technology team and are seeking a Systems & Configuration Engineer to join the legacy QTMP project! Reporting to the Systems and Configuration Engineering Discipline Manager, you will work collaboratively with a team of qualified systems engineers to develop, implement and maintain the Systems Engineering Management Plan.

You will be accountable for:

  • Preparation of technical submissions such as System Requirements Review (SRR) through to System Verification Review (SVR)
  • Preparation of Risk Assessments / Trade off Studies as required to support engineering decision making.
  • Providing the Safety stream with engineering justifications to support safety arguments
  • Producing updated supplier subsystem specifications as required
  • Generating reports from requirements management database

This is a fulltime, permanent role, based in Brisbane, QLD.


About You:

  • 4+ years relevant experience in design/assurance of rollingstock or equivalent industry
  • Demonstrated experience in Systems Engineering & Configuration Management
  • Hold a relevant engineering or computer science degree, or equivalent
  • Knowledge of requirements management tools (E.g. DOORS)
  • Self-motivated and autonomous, with the ability to seek guidance and work in a team as required.
  • Exceptional communication skills (written and verbal)
