About the Role:

As an experienced Quality Engineer, you will have the opportunity to be considered for a range of upcoming opportunities within our existing and growing client base. You will be responsible for the management and facilitation of all site-specific quality assurance requirements and providing day-to-day support to project team members to ensure that specified project quality requirements are met.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Provide technical advice and assistance to management for quality-related activities
  • Understand compliance and reporting procedures and processes
  • Maintain the highest standard of health and safety performance
  • Ensure our clients requirements are being met at all times by reviewing contractor activities
  • Perform on-site quality management on civil engineering and construction work
  • Ensure construction works are carried out in accordance with the latest drawings, specifications and other relevant documents
  • Ensure that all necessary documentation is being produced and recorded
  • Monitor/prepare the daily/monthly reports on all works in progress at the site
  • Ensure regular physical attendance on site at all active work fronts
  • Review subcontractors' quality documentation and ensure they fulfil their quality system obligations

Key Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in engineering (or similar), Building, Construction, Quality Management or Project Management
  • Experience working on large scale, complex, civil engineering or infrastructure projects
  • Demonstrated quality management, and associated engineering and construction skills
  • Ability to negotiate with, manage and supervise external contractors and consultants
  • Demonstrated success in working collaboratively as part of a team and autonomously
  • Ability to read and understand work scopes, technical specifications and drawings
  • Sound knowledge of Quality, OH&S, EEO and Environmental policies, procedures and standards
  • Strong understanding of relevant Australian and International standards, both technical and those related management systems.
  • Internal Auditor and/or Lead Auditor qualifications highly desirable.
