About Us

APA is a leading Australian energy infrastructure business. We own and/or manage and operate a diverse, $22 billion portfolio of gas, electricity, solar and wind assets. Consistent with our purpose to strengthen communities through responsible energy, we deliver approximately half of the nation’s gas usage and connect Victoria with South Australia, Tasmania with Victoria and New South Wales with Queensland through our investments in electricity transmission assets. We are also one of the largest owners and operators of renewable power generation assets in Australia, with wind and solar projects across the country.

We employ more than 2,200 people across Australia and know that they are the key to our success. We are committed to a diverse and inclusive culture, where all our people feel safe, valued, and trusted to do their best every day.

The Opportunity

We now have an exciting opportunity available for a Test Engineer to join the Assets and Operations team here at apa. You will be responsible for the provision of efficient testing services to optimise delivery.

Key Responsibilities

As a Test Engineer you would be responsible for:

  • Providing input to the design and development of Test Execution plans.
  • Active participation in and contribution to agile ceremonies, including but not limited to sprint planning, prioritisation and estimating tasks.
  • Developing high quality test case suites for feature, regression, integration and performance testing scenarios.
  • Participating in Kick-off Meetings (Requirement analysis) and be able to define scenarios and acceptance tests based on acceptance criteria.
  • Test preparation (with Test Data) and execution activities in line with the test strategy and plans.
  • Performing Exploratory Testing
  • Automation of test cases using the available test automation framework.
  • Timely and effective recording and reporting of test results, including defect identification and reporting, re-testing and regression testing.

Your background

We are seeking an experienced Test Engineer with 5+ years of cumulative experience in manual and automation testing. In addition to this, we are seeking experience with behaviour driven development (BDD) and acceptance test driven development (ATDD), demonstrated experience in developing test automation framework using open-source tools such as selenium using cucumber BDD, experience in development and Devops tools (Java, IntelliJ, bitbucket/git, Teamcity). Experience working with agile and hybrid working methodologies with the supported tools Jira, confluence, Zephyr is also highly preferential, as well as experience within the utilities domain and/or with energy components (EC) more specifically.

In conjunction with the technical skills, we are seeking an individual who has demonstrated success in building relationships with key internal and external stakeholders. Our people are at the core of what we do and we are seeking likeminded people-centric individuals who are passionate about fostering great relationships with our people.
