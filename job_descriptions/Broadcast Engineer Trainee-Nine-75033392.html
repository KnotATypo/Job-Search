Job Description

The Nine Engineering staff provide support for all station broadcast requirements in Brisbane and Gold Coast. Supported Systems include Studio Control rooms, News systems, Broadcast Cameras, Editing Suites, Production Suites, Acquisition and Contribution equipment, Graphic Suites, ENPS.

The Trainee Broadcast Engineer role requires shift work to maintain and support all on station and remote systems with an emphasis on graphics, file transfer, news edit and production edit systems, on a 7-day roster.

Day to day you will:

  • Repair broadcast equipment including Camera’s, Links, lights and audio equipment.
  • Provide initial support to Control Room systems, including MOSART, VIZ Graphics, VSM and Dante
  • Provide support for a range of Editing systems, including Velocity, Premier Pro, AVID and Final Cut Pro
  • Provide initial support for newsroom systems, including Imagine News Force, file and tape based ingest, GPRX and ENPS plugins.
  • Provide initial support for a range of broadcast centric systems such as KVM matrices, Multiviewers, sound and video processing systems and talkback  systems
  • Provide input for resolution of operational technical issues, such as upgrade paths and workflow changes, etc
  • Communicate with engineering staff and manager the status of equipment repairs
  • Provide support for the Master Control environment
  • Continue to keep the workspaces clean and tidy
  • Other duties as reasonably required

Qualifications

What we would like to see on your resume:

  • Certificate II or Certificate III in electronics
  • Ability to learn new technology including IT systems
  • Ability to learn to problem solve and analyze technical aspects of complex systems
  • Ability to learn new systems and their integration into existing systems and workflows
  • Must be a team player
  • Good communication skills
