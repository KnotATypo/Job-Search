About the role

The Junior Developer will possess knowledge in key software development stages, from requirements analysis to coding and testing. In a fast-paced and collaborative environment, you’ll experiment with and roll out proof-of-concept solutions for RACQ projects. Working alongside technology specialists across the business, there will be an opportunity to gain valuable experience. Junior Integration Developer will be an integral part of the Integration team. They will be responsible for -

  • Applying the SDLC where appropriate to ensure Solutions Delivery project deliverables match project requirements.
  • Assisting with solutions planning, liaising with relevant team members and contributing to solution design.
  • Ensuring that best practice processes and procedures are followed for all solutions implemented, including awareness and considerations for OWASP Top 10, and other secure coding principles.
  • Developing the solution adhering to quality assurance standards, secure coding principles and agreed processes within the department.
  • Creating unit and integration tests.
  • Getting the peer review completed with other developers and implementing the review comments as recommended with other team members.

About you

  • Experience in programming language/s relevant to integration such as Visual Studio, WCF, .NET 6 and higher, C#, and knowledge in cloud platforms such as Microsoft Azure.
  • Proven diagnostic and troubleshooting skills.
  • Demonstrated development experience within the information system field.
  • Exposure on system design, development, implementation and testing.
  • Excellent analytical, design and consultative skills. Willingness to learn new technologies.
  • Strong interpersonal, communicative and collaboration skills with the ability to work effectively in a team environment.
  • To be able to work outside of normal business hours for key project & critical support related tasks at short notice. Ability to provide, on a rotation basis, out of hours support.
