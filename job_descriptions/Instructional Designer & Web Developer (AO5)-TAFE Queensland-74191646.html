About TAFE Queensland

TAFE Queensland is proud to be the largest and most experienced Vocational Education and Training provider in the State. For more than 140 years, TAFE Queensland has delivered practical and industry-relevant training to provide students with the skills and experience they need to build lifelong careers. TAFE Queensland been named the Large Training Provider of the Year at the prestigious 2023 Australian Training Awards. Our training is delivered to students and apprentices on-site, online, in the workplace, or on-campus to give people the skills they need to enrich their communities, support their industries, and strengthen their local economies.

By working at TAFE Queensland, you can be part of a highly experienced workforce closely connected with their industries and dedicated to delivering best practices and innovative training.

Your Opportunity

As the Instructional Designer & Web Developer you will be responsible for:

  • incorporate both instructional design and multimedia/web development skills to plan, design and convert existing learning resources into engaging and effective solutions for blended and/or online delivery; and
  • design and develop product which adheres to and complies with relevant web standards, visual style guides, product development processes, usability guidelines, copyright and quality assurance practices.
  • contribute to the design and development of high-quality educational materials, including print, online and multimedia learning solutions, with a view to meeting the diverse needs of both learners and educational delivery teams across TAFE Queensland SkillsTech  and achieving desired learning outcomes.

This position reports to the Learning Services Manager.

This is a Permanent, Full-time opportunity.

The position will be based primarily at Acacia Ridge however you may be required to perform work at other TAFE Queensland campuses.

Key Responsibilities

  • Contribute to the success of the organisation through promoting and modelling SkillsTech’s core values and Agreed Behaviours and the established TAFE Queensland values of Working Together, Focussing on our Customer, Taking Responsibility, Showing Initiative, and Safety First.
  • Develop engaging and effective learning resources using industry standard multimedia software and web development tools and templates (e.g. Bootstrap and Adobe Creative Cloud), using coding languages such as HTML, Javascript, CSS.
  • Apply instructional design principles and practices to:
    • design and develop high quality, flexible learning solutions that meet client specifications and/or project briefs;
    • develop online Master Product in accordance with project management protocols and practices to ensure quality and compliance;
    • assemble online product in the TAFE Queensland Learning Management System (LMS) i.e. D2L Brightspace (known as Connect in TAFE Queensland) following agreed protocols and practices; and
    • develop storyboards and online course content through the application of creative, innovative and contemporary design methods and practices, using industry standard and emerging development technologies.
  • Communicate, consult and negotiate with key stakeholders, including educators and the learning services team, in order to foster and support productive working relationships within the Region and more broadly across TAFE Queensland.
  • Actively participate in a multi-disciplinary team-based environment, working collaboratively with a range of individuals and delivery teams, participate in forums, team activities and relevant working groups to achieve successful outcomes.
  • Undertake quality and compliance checks in line with procedures and design specifications to ensure products created are compliant with relevant legislation, regulations and standards.
  • Proactively manage your own work priorities, tracking and monitoring against project plans and timeframes, escalating issues where necessary.
  • Maintain contemporary knowledge of emerging technology and design principles as a basis for the design and development of creative and learner-focused resources.
  • Model a culture of innovation and continuous improvement related to educational products and processes and provide effective leadership, and direction to support the achievement of business objectives.
  • Proactively participate in local Performance Planning and Development (PPD) processes, seeking opportunities for self-development and contributing to the development of the team through knowledge sharing and the provision of support, guidance and collaboration.

How you will be assessed

Within the context of the role description above, the ideal applicant will be someone who has the following key capabilities:

  1. Proven experience in working to design briefs and specifications, applying contemporary instructional design principles and a high level of comprehension skills, and attention to detail.
  2. Demonstrated ability to design, develop and implement multimedia content and functional web pages for engaging educational delivery in blended and online environments using industry standard web authoring and development tools and templates including, but not limited to:
    • Bootstrap
    • Coding languages such as HTML, CSS and JS (e.g. Adobe Dreamweaver)
    • Adobe Creative Cloud software (i.e. Adobe Dreamweaver), and
    • various other learning object software products.

3. Demonstrated ability to cultivate productive working relationships, and effectively communicate and liaise with a range of key stakeholders and contribute to a high performing team.
4. Demonstrated understanding and application of adult learning theories, digital learning principles and the Vocational Education and Training (VET) sector as it relates to the development of units of competency.
5. Proven organisational skills as evidenced by the ability to manage competing priorities, meet external deadlines and demonstrate innovative problem-solving skills with a view to achieving role objectives, high quality products and efficient practices.

Mandatory Qualifications/Requirements

Mandatory skills

  • Adobe Dreamweaver or equivalent industry standard web (HTML) authoring tool.
  • Experience using Microsoft Office Suite for Desktop publishing.

Highly desirable:

  • Diploma in Instructional Design/Web Development or similar; or equivalent relevant industry experience would be highly regarded.
  • Experience using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and various other learning object software products.
  • Knowledge of Learning Resource Design Principles (or Universal Design Principles).
  • Knowledge of Learning Management Systems (LMS)
