The Company

Holmwood Highgate, a third-generation family business, is a globally recognized leader in designing and fabricating bulk liquid storage and transportation systems. With a strong work ethic and clear vision, we've earned an impressive client base and have exciting projects ahead.

The Role: Laser Cutting Operator / CNC Programmer

We are seeking a full-time Laser Cutting Operator / CNC Programmer. You will play a pivotal role in the programming, setup, and operation of CNC laser cutting machines to produce sheet metal and plate components in a timely and cost-effective manner. Additionally, you will get to work on one of the largest and newest laser-cutting machines in the southern hemisphere (17m x 3.2m bed size) complete with bevel-cutting capabilities.

  • Set up and operate CNC laser cutting machines to produce accurate and high-quality components.
  • Select and install proper tooling and fixtures for each job
  • Perform routine maintenance on machines and troubleshoot issues as they arise.
  • Inspect finished products for quality and adherence to specifications.
  • Interpret engineering drawings and specifications
  • Program CNC machines using appropriate software to maximise material usage
  • Maintain Offcuts
  • Collaborate with other team members to optimize production processes.

You:

  • Proven experience as a laser operator, CNC programmer or similar role.
  • Ability to read & interpret technical drawings and specifications
  • Strong knowledge of materials, tooling, and machine setups.
  • Knowledge of safety requirements and best practices in a manufacturing environment.
  • Good organizational and communication abilities.
  • Proficient in CNC programming, preferably with experience in CADMAN-B (desirable)

In addition to your technical expertise, your success will hinge on your ability to be a team-oriented, down-to-earth individual. Joining a team of loyal, long-tenured staff, you will contribute to maintaining high standards of top-quality work and customer satisfaction.

The Reward:

  • Competitive wages + overtime rates + attendance bonus (commensurate with the candidate's experience and qualifications)
  • 4-day work week + O/T on Fridays
  • On-site Parking

Only those selected for an interview will be contacted. We appreciate your interest in joining our team.
