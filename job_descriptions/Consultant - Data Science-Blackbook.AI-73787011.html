About Us

Blackbook.ai is one of Australia’s most successful and progressive automation and AI companies. Our use of RPA, Intelligent Automation, and AI to deliver on our client’s pursuit of bottom-line benefits; has resulted in a company that can claim a 100% success rate in solution delivery. Our unique approach to problem-solving will see you working directly with our customers, capturing additional requirements and developing intuitive solutions while drawing on the vast knowledge of existing team members.

The Data Scientist role will see you working with multiple clients, projects, and stakeholders. Your responsibilities will likely include the following:

  • Work directly with clients as a Blackbook.ai consultant
  • Work with internal solution analysts and business SMEs to ensure requirements are thorough and complete
  • Design and implement scalable data science applications (both cloud and on-premise deployments)
  • Working independently and within a team on everything from cutting edge proof of concepts, to business-critical data science applications
  • Providing subject matter expertise/leveraging existing knowledge in the business on automation solutions
  • Create solutions utilising best development practices

To be considered for the position, you will have experience or have been exposed to the following:

  • Have a software development background or a bachelor’s degree in Information Technology (or similar) from an Australian University
  • 1+ years’ experience consulting or working across multiple business units
  • 1+ years’ experience working with Python (alternatively R) in your day-to-day role to build widely used solutions (not one-off analyses)
  • In-depth experience with a core Data Science field (Object Detection, NLP, Predictive Analytics etc.)
  • Experience with Docker and (preferably) scalable deployment services (Kubernetes, MLFlow etc.)
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Excellent professional presentation and communication skills (written and verbal, formal documentation, diagramming, and modelling)
  • Solutions focused and critical thinking skills
  • Experience using agile project management software like Jira or Trello

Highly Regarded:

  • Experience with designing highly scalable and reliable solution architectures
  • Experience with DevOps/MLOps
  • AWS/Azure ML based certifications (or considerable related training)
  • Experience with additional languages (C++, Rust, R, PowerShell, SQL, Node.js, Angular, JavaScript)
  • In-depth cloud platform knowledge (AWS/Azure)
