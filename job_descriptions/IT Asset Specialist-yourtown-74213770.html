yourtown is a trusted provider of services for young people, with a focus on mental health and wellbeing, long-term unemployment, prevention of youth suicide, child protection, as well as support for those experiencing domestic and family violence.

yourtown helps children and young people realise they have the ability to tackle whatever life throws at them. We help them to believe in their unique strengths, tenacity and self-determination. We recognise the courage and determination of children and young people when they reach out to yourtown for support.

your opportunity

The IT Asset Specialist will support the IT Operations Team to review, manage, and maintain the technology assets that enable the delivery of technology services to yourtown .

In this position, you will:

• Collaborate with IT leadership to thoroughly analyse and monitor our asset data to ensure accuracy and reliability.
• Take ownership of developing and implementing robust asset controls, efficient processes, meticulous reconciliation procedures, and effective disposal strategies to optimise asset management throughout their lifecycle.
• Oversee the management, tracking, and maintenance of our asset budgets and financial resources.
• Proactively identify opportunities to enhance the efficiency and utilisation of our IT hardware assets, such as optimising usage, minimising downtime, and streamlining procurement processes.
• Managing and monitoring mobile phone, fleet, laptops, including tracking expenses & optimising usage.
• Manage stock controls and ordering of IT assets (laptops, mobile phones, peripherals (screens, keyboards, mice, docking stations, cables, headsets etc)
• Conduct laptop builds and other ah-hoc assistance required to the IT Operations team as required, including site visits (interstate and local).

what we are looking for

At yourtown , we believe positivity is powerful and the enabler of great things. We are all connected through our shared passion, commitment, and belief in young people, and are committed to helping young people see greatness in themselves.

• Demonstrated experience working in IT asset management, with experience in purchasing and receipting.
• Strong interest and understanding of computer systems, mobile devices, and tech products.
• Strong time management and organisational skills, with the ability to think laterally, and achieve deadlines in an environment of multiple competing priorities.
• Exceptional customer service skills, influencing, & networking skills, coupled with outstanding interpersonal skills, showcasing the ability to foster positive working relationships.
• Strong written and verbal communication skills.
