Job Summary:

We are seeking a highly skilled SOC Analyst to join our team in Brisbane. The successful candidate will be responsible for monitoring and analyzing security events across the organization's network infrastructure and responding to any potential threats or incidents. The ideal candidate should have strong technical skills and a thorough understanding of security operations center (SOC) processes.

Skills:

- Strong analytical skills

- Knowledge of LogRhythm SIEM tool

- Familiarity with cybersecurity technologies such as firewalls, IDS/IPS systems, endpoint protection etc.

- Excellent communication skills

- Ability to work well under pressure and meet deadlines

- Familiarity with compliance standards such as ISO27001, ISM, etc.

Responsibilities:

- Monitor the organization's network and server infrastructure for potential security breaches or incidents using LogRhythm.

- Analyze alerts generated by LogRhythm and investigate any suspicious activities.

- Escalate incidents to senior team members if required.

- Respond promptly to security events/incidents by initiating appropriate actions such as isolating affected systems/networks or conducting forensic investigations.

- Maintain up-to-date knowledge of emerging cyber threats and vulnerabilities that may affect the organization's infrastructure.

Qualifications:

The following qualifications are required for this position:

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field

- At least 3 years of experience working in a Security Operations Center (SOC)

- Experience using LogRhythm SIEM tool is mandatory
