Delivering Energy Today, Powering Your Tomorrow.

As a member of the Future Energy Projects team, the Digital Engineering Coordinator plays a key role to coordinate the design, implementation, maintenance, and continual improvement of an information management system for Project Delivery that utilises data from various sources through the business to streamline the effective receipt, delivery, and handover of projects by the Future Energy Projects team. Key responsibilities include:

  • Facilitate the Digital Engineering processes (including design specification, and commercial & contractual requirements) to enable the realisation of a Digital Twin approach to project delivery complying with the Queensland Government Digital Enablement for Queensland Infrastructure - Principles for BIM implementation , 2018.
  • Develop and support reporting metrics and visualisations for portfolio & project delivery using information management systems including;
    • Power BI
    • 4D & 5D BIM
    • Digital Twins


About You

To be successful in this role you will have the following skills and experience:

  • Significant experience working in a digital engineering environment, including a relevant university or technical qualification in engineering, project management or similar.
  • Extensive experience in information management roles in complex organisations.
  • Experience using tools such as Application Programming Interfaces, Power BI, 4D & 5D BIM, Digital Twins.
  • Significant experience in most phases of the project delivery lifecycle.
  • Demonstrated effective written and verbal communication in addition to the ability to effectively influence and negotiate as needed to achieve mutually beneficial outcomes.
  • Proven ability to manage competing priorities to meet deadlines and responsively provide information as requested.
  • Solutions orientated with the ability to think critically and outside the box to achieve outcomes within a regulated environment.
