GuestPoint has a dynamic, diversified team with big plans for the future. We power our employees’ potential and strive to create opportunity and security for every member of the team. If you are ready for something bigger, more challenging then you might be the Junior Full Stack Developer we are looking for.

  • Build and maintain high-quality, secure, and scalable web applications using the company technology stack
  • Review the code submitted by peers for accuracy, reusability, performance and security
  • Be an active and collaborative member of the team
  • Develop documentation including justification of design choices
  • Remain up to date with the terminology, concepts and best practices for coding

  • At least 1 years of proven experience as a .Net Developer
  • At least 1 years experience using React, TypeScript / JavaScript
  • Proficiency in Go as well is an advantage
  • Experience in creating integrations using the API specifications of 3rd parties
  • Experience working with SQL Server and/or Postgres
  • Good understanding of Agile methodologies
  • Experience with tools such Bitbucket (or Git), and ticketing systems such as Jira
  • High level of curiosity with the ability to translate it into new approaches to problem identification and solution
  • Good English communication skills
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering, Information Technology or any related field
  • Can attend our office in Brisbane office for minimum 3 days per week.

About GuestPoint

GuestPoint is a powerful yet simple to use Property Management System designed to streamline and automate the multitude of daily tasks completed by a motelier. For over 20 years GuestPoint (formerly Motelier) have been designing, developing and supporting software for the hospitality industry. Today, our all-in-one solution covers website, direct bookings, OTA integrations, GDS, Payments, Point of Sale as well as front and back office operations.
