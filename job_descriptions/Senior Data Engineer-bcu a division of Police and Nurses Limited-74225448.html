About the role;

The Senior Data Engineer will actively engage with stakeholders and will be required to identify requirements, produce designs, implement solutions, and test the results.  They will support the operation of the data solutions in production while driving operational excellence and provide mentorship to the remainder of the team.

Working as part of an inclusive and collaborative team your key responsibilities will include:

  • Designs and develops data pipelines including orchestration, ingestion, transformation, and delivery - across streaming, event and batch-style processing
  • Develop and enhance data warehouse and reporting capabilities. Develop the data warehouse architecture, standards, and data models
  • Apply engineering knowledge to solve one-off problems and edge cases that require scripted solutions.
  • Conduct reviews of Data assets and controls as part of monitoring capability to improve data management practices
  • Apply industry standard modelling techniques to create trusted data sets.
  • Participates in the on-call rotation and works to prioritise and address production issues. Coordinates responses to critical incidents.
  • Always considers security and privacy of data, ensuring these concerns are included consciously and by design.
  • Acts as a senior and experienced member of the team, assisting other team members and driving operational excellence. Actively participates in architectural bodies, design workshops and change management boards as a technical expert.



What will you bring?

  • Extensive, hands-on professional experience in a mix of cloud and on-premises data stores, data processing and/or general software engineering
  • Advanced knowledge of cloud data platforms and respective ecosystems.
  • Ability to use open source and commercial data pipeline tools and operate these on SAAS, Windows and Unix-based platforms.
  • Required Systems knowledge:
o Experience with Snowflake, AWS and dbt will be highly regarded
o Columnar cloud data warehousing
o Cloud data integration
o ELT style data transformation tooling
o SQL
o Python and shell scripting
  • Experience designing multi-layer data pipelines covering extraction, staging, transformation, and presentation.
  • Familiarity with Kimball, Data Vault, Lambda architecture and other industry standard techniques.
  • Bachelor’s Degree in information technology, Computer Science or associated discipline.
