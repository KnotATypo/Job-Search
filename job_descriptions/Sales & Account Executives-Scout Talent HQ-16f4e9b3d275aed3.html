The Opportunity

Exciting opportunities for full-time Sales & Account Executives await at our global riverside head office in Milton, Brisbane .

Join our highly motivated sales team, where passion for sales and client success thrive. As a Sales & Account Executive at Scout Talent, you'll be embraced by a supportive and celebratory environment, with company-wide shout-outs, awards, and quarterly town halls recognising your personal and team triumphs. Read about our values here!

Act as a trusted Salesperson, fostering strong client relationships and showcasing our long-term value as their recruitment partner.

And the best part? You will also be backed by in-house teams of recruitment specialists (recruitment marketers, shortlisters, and client success specialists) who will provide & execute the actual recruitment solutions, and even collaborate with your sales strategies!

When joining our team you will also have the opportunity to work closely with and sell our new revolutionary AI Suite! The future of recruitment software is here and you can be part of it!

Responsibilities Include:

  • Connect with prospects and engage in impactful conversations;
  • Consistently meet and exceed sales and KPI targets;
  • Identify and leverage value propositions to secure new business;
  • Collaborate with specialist teams to tailor optimal solutions for clients;
  • Manage the cross-sell and up-sell process from start to finish;
  • Cultivate strong relationships, drive renewals, and expand client portfolio; and
  • Increase lifetime value and maximise platform usage on the Scout Talent platform.
