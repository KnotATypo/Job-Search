Job Description

Planner / Programmer for a premium national advisory firm. Planner is primarily responsible for providing technical support to the constructability, engineering and estimating process through provision of programming, program analysis and reporting and development of construction methodology.

Who will this position suit best?

A 10 year+ Planner from a large infrastructure project background with exposure to both contractor and consultancy / client-side operations.

Responsibilities

  • Prepare programs (using P6 Software) and accompanying methodology assigned projects.
  • Assist Estimators with estimate development.
  • Assist with forensic programming for actual construction projects.
  • Undertake detailed analysis e.g. critical path for construction programs.
  • Obtain specialist market pricing / technical advice to support estimate methodologies and program productivity.
  • Resource load programs.
  • Assist / undertake estimates or prepare aspects of an estimate using Expert Estimation examples to include time-based cost aspects within the estimate.

What do you need?

  • Tertiary qualification in Quantity Surveying, Engineering or Construction Management
  • 10 years of experience in the construction sector ideally progressing from a site-based role to a Planner / Programmer; ideally Client Side and Contractor background.
  • Experience with Programming (P6) and Estimating Software
  • Ability to work in multidisciplinary teams as well as individually
