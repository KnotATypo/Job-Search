The Role

We are seeking a highly motivated Asset Management Consultant to join our Brisbane team. As a key member of our consulting team, you will work closely with our clients to provide a range of services, including requirements analysis, configuration, training, issues resolution, testing support, and system enhancement projects.

We are looking for a team player with a strong understanding of asset management, business analysis, process improvement, and strategic asset management concepts. The successful candidate will have excellent core consulting skills, including problem-solving, attention to detail, and developed written and verbal communication skills. TechnologyOne product experience is desirable but not required.

If you are passionate about delivering outstanding client service and thrive in a dynamic, team-oriented environment, we would love to hear from you. This is a unique opportunity to work with a young, dynamic team of professionals committed to delivering exceptional service and helping our clients achieve their goals.

Key responsibilities:

  • Assist in the implementation and configuration of the TechnologyOne system for our clients.
  • Work with our team of consultants to gather client requirements, conduct gap analyses, and consider solutions to enhance financial, maintenance or strategic asset management processes.
  • Provide subject-matter expertise, best practices, technical approaches, and recommendations.
  • Support the development and execution of test plans to ensure system functionality and accuracy.
  • Provide end-user training and support on the effective use of TechnologyOne.
  • Contribute to the documentation of system configurations, processes, and user guides.
  • Work with clients post implementation for user testing, debugging, support and maintenance.
  • Drive continuous improvement processes and implement automation initiatives.

The successful candidate:

  • Is a team player, committed to helping both our clients and other team members achieve their goals.
  • Has a passion for learning and continuous improvement and can share their knowledge with others.
  • Has experience in consulting or product support in Enterprise Resource Planning systems (TechnologyOne experience preferred).
  • Has in depth knowledge and understanding of business processes.
  • Has an understanding and appreciation for information security within systems and user devices.

Desirable criteria:

  • Tertiary qualification in Engineering, Commerce, or similar.
  • Attainment or practical experience with Project Management methodologies.
  • Previous experience with regards to asset management, particularly in relation to infrastructure assets.
  • Previous experience with regards to implementation and use of computer-based asset or financial management systems.
  • Some previous experience working in Advisory, Asset Management, Engineering and Consulting.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Excel and similar tools.
  • Previous experience/exposure to JRA Asset Management or TechnologyOne although not essential.

Applicants must be a permanent resident or citizen of Australia / NZ. Employer visa sponsorship is not available for this position.
