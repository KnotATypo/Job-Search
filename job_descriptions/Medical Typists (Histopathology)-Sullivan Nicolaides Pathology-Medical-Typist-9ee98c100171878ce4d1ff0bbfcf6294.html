About the Opportunity

Sullivan Nicolaides Pathology is currently offering part-time Medical Typist positions within the Bowen Hills Histopathology Department . This position is for day shifts on Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday from 10:00 am to 6:00 pm along with participation in a rotating Sunday roster. The position will primarily involve the typing of macroscopic descriptions of specimens, with the ability to cover other administrative duties as required.

A good working knowledge of medical terminology is desirable, with experience in histological office procedures highly regarded. A typing speed of 50 words per minute is the minimum entry level.

Applicants should possess good communication skills together with an enthusiastic and flexible attitude to the working environment.

Please note Sullivan Nicolaides Pathology are committed to ensuring a healthy work/life balance for all staff.
