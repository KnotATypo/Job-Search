The Independent Liquor Group (ILG) has been servicing the liquor industry since 1975 and is one of Australia's leading liquor wholesalers committed to providing choice and flexibility to better service our family's diverse business models with our distinct banner groups in Bottler, Super Cellars, Fleet Street and The Liquor Co-op.

ILG is a member owned Co-operative Wholesaler that services over 1500 hotels, bottle shops, licensed clubs, bars and restaurants across NSW, QLD and Vic.

The Role

Reporting to the IT Infrastructure Manager, this role is part of our IT team and located in our Richlands office in QLD.  In the role you will be responsible for assisting the business and ensuring the operation of the companies’ Network and Server Infrastructure for 150+ users.

You will have an opportunity to work on a wide variety of tasks covering network devices, cyber security, on-prem and cloud server environments, as well as IT projects helping to drive change throughout the organisation.

The role is ideally suited to an IT Engineer with a minimum of 3 years’ experience in a similar role.

You will be responsible for:

  • Deploying, configuring, managing, and monitoring network devices and solutions
  • Assisting in the planning and implementation of IT infrastructure projects.
  • The support and administration for all on-premise IT technologies and virtualised infrastructure.
  • Assisting in the development and review of IT documentation and policies.
  • Working with the team and ensure the security of systems and data.
  • Reporting on the environment and escalating critical issues.
  • Providing some end user support.


To be successful in this role you will have:

  • 3+ years’ experience across a range of on-premise technologies in a similar role.
  • The ability to work autonomously.
  • Effective multi-tasking, prioritisation and logical problem-solving abilities
  • Hands on admin experience across the Microsoft Enterprise Products including, Microsoft Office 365 and Windows Server.
  • Excellent knowledge of networking – troubleshooting and understanding (routing, switching, wireless, VPN, SDWAN and firewalls)
  • Experience with Cisco, VMware, Azure and AWS
  • Cyber security knowledge, including policy and risk mitigation processes.
  • Experience working on a variety of infrastructure projects.
  • Creating, updating, and maintaining documentation for the networking solutions as they evolve
  • Afterhours maintenance activities.
  • Clear and articulate communication skills

Additional Desirable experience includes:

  • Network certifications like CCNA
  • Experience with Fortinet Firewalls
  • Experience with Linux operating systems
  • Experience with Veeam
  • Experience PowerShell & automation.
