About the Role

Sime Darby Berhad is currently recruiting for a Safety Culture Facilitator to join our award-winning branch in Archerfield, QLD on a 12 months Fixed Term position.

Reporting to the Manager-Culture Capability, the Cultural Change Facilitator 's primary responsibility will be to develop and facilitate programs that build and continuously improve safety culture across Sime Darby Berhad (SDB). This involves coaching and mentoring frontline leaders and teams on safety leadership behaviours that align with principles underpinning "No Harm".

As a professional in your field, you will ensure safety and management systems are utilised for self and peers and that agreed methods and measures are followed for reliable results for customers.

Key responsibilities :

This role will involve the following key duties and responsibilities:

  • Facilitate the delivery of safety culture change and safety leadership program across SDB
  • Assist in the development and implementation of training frameworks and engagement with the operations team
  • Provide coaching to support frontline leaders to upskill them on safety leadership practices
  • Assist in the deployment and promotion across multiple organisations
  • Develop and implement role specific training packages, aligned with individual roles and company requirements
  • Travel to all locations across Sime Darby Group to facilitate programs and provide leadership coaching

Why Sime Darby?

With a strong presence in the industrial, motors, Aerospace, insurance and logistics sectors Sime Darby is a partner of choice for some of the most renowned brands including but not limited to BMW, Volvo, Porsche, Jaguar, Caterpillar and Ramsay Healthcare.

With operations in 18 countries across Asia Pacific over the past 100 years, we continue to grow, delivering sustainable value to our stakeholders through operational excellence, high performance standards and good corporate governance.
