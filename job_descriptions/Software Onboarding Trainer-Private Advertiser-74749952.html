About us

aXcelerate, a leading SaaS provider that powers millions of learners' course and competency records throughout Australia, has been recognised as a Great Place to Work®. This recognition is based on employee feedback, and it means that aXcelerate is a company where employees feel valued, respected, and supported.

We are looking for a dedicated Software Onboarding Trainer to join our team and support our clients in their onboarding journey. You will play a crucial role in ensuring a smooth onboarding process, addressing client inquiries, and delivering high-level training to optimise their use of our software.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Own and manage customer relationships from the point of sale through to successful implementation.
  • Complete product training and webinars to ensure that customers are supported during the onboarding process.
  • Create a Project Plan for the successful onboarding of our clients.
  • Coordinate with internal teams and provide regular progress updates.
  • Be an aXcelerate expert and discuss or educate on any nuance of the product internally and externally.
  • Establish and nurture strong client relationships.
  • Quickly pick up and disseminate any updates to the product, both to your customers and to your internal teams, as needed.

What we're looking for:

  • Experience in software training for end users.
  • A self-starter who has experience in software implementation.
  • SaaS industry knowledge is looked upon favourably.
  • Strong communication and client service skills.
  • Ability to work independently and in a team-oriented environment.
  • Excellent problem-solving and prioritisation abilities in high-pressure situations.
  • Client-focused mindset and dedication to delivering exceptional service.
  • Ability to rapidly acquire knowledge of the aXcelerate software.

What we offer:

  • A vibrant, supportive and team-oriented culture, with regular team activities and social events.
  • Hybrid work arrangement opportunity.
  • A focus on professional development, with internal and external training and a personal $2000 professional development budget each year.
  • Access for you and your family to an Employee Assistance Program and resources.
  • Opportunities for career advancement, we nurture our talent and provide career pathways across the business.

Application process:

  1. Initial phone screen with one of our Human Success representatives
  2. In-person interview
  3. Online presentation - 15-minute Software Training
