St Aidan's Anglican Girls' School and St Margaret's Anglican Girls School are two of Queensland's leading independent schools, fostering in students confidence, compassion, academic excellence and a lifelong love of learning. We look for all staff to be committed to and in harmony with the Anglican ethos of the schools.

St Aidan’s and St Margaret’s Anglican Girls Schools have a shared services model for key business functions across the schools. The purpose of the shared services model is to provide the Schools with high quality business and customer service, whilst ensuring continuous delivery and effective and efficient use of resources.

We are currently inviting applications for the role of:

IT Technical Support Officer

This is a full-time role and will be based predominately at St Aidan's Anglican Girls School in Corinda, with the need to work from St Margaret's Anglican School in Ascot as required.

The IT Technical Support Officer works across the School community at St Aidan's Anglican Girls School to support and maintain software, hardware and IT equipment within the School. Core objectives of the role are:

  • Providing service excellence to the Staff and Students of the Schools in relation to all ICT matters
  • Supporting the development and maintenance of desktop, laptop and iPad hardware
  • Assisting the Chief Information Officer in the effective running of the School's IT related systems including network, server and printing solutions.

About You

With a strong interest and knowledge in IT, you will have:

  • Service Desk experience with the ability to provide a high level of customer service
  • Exceptional communication skills and be able to communicate well with a range of audiences including students, staff and parents.
  • Demonstrated, excellent knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite (including O365), Windows 10\11 and Windows Server 2012 onwards.
  • Experience in the use of Microsoft System Centre Configuration Manager (SCCM) for SOE deployment.
  • Experience in the following applications (advantageous): TASS (Student Management System), Papercut (Print Management) and Working knowledge of Apple iPads with a preference towards JAMF
