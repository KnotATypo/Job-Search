About Aspec Engineering

Due to high demand for our services, Aspec Engineering (ASPEC) is looking for a Graduate Structural Engineer to join our Brisbane Structural Team in 2024. Join our team to gain valuable knowledge from ASPEC’s experienced engineering team while assisting to solve real-world problems for ASPEC’s port, resources, infrastructure, and heavy industry clients.

ASPEC offers a lifestyle friendly culture, flexible working hours, professional development, and competitive remuneration for a challenging and rewarding career where engineers develop engineering and management skills to take on the toughest problems. We have a unique offering that enables ASPEC to attract and retain the best talent.

ASPEC is a multi-disciplinary engineering firm established in 2003 that provides specialist services to the port, resources, heavy industry and infrastructure sectors. ASPEC provides expertise in the design and assessment of mechanical structures (such as stackers, reclaimers and shiploaders for materials handling, and even surf parks). From our offices in Brisbane, Perth, Newcastle and Wollongong, ASPEC has developed close ties with major clients, and provides a comprehensive service in our core business areas of Engineering Projects, Materials Handling and Structural Integrity.

About the Rol e

ASPEC is seeking a motivated Graduate Structural Engineer based in Brisbane, wishing to accelerate their structural engineering career.

In the role you will have opportunities to:

  • Work in a multidisciplinary team environment alongside experienced Structural and Mechanical Engineers and Designers
  • Be mentored by ASPEC’s experienced Structural and Mechanical Engineers
  • Gain a broad range of experience that will enable you to become a chartered engineer and a registered professional engineer in Queensland
  • Undertake challenging structural design, analysis and investigation projects
  • Gain hands-on experience in the field through visits to our client’s sites across Queensland and interstate
  • Develop skills in a broad range of engineering software packages and develop a detailed understanding of Australian Standards, technical literature and industry guidelines to support engineering design and analysis.

About You

The following skills, experience and attributes would be highly regarded:

  • Seeking full time work from early 2024
  • Highly motivated to learn and contribute
  • Interested in structural engineering
  • Report writing and communication skills
  • Formal engineering qualifications (degree in Civil or Structural Engineering)
    • Applicants with a Major in Structural Engineering and/or prior learning in structural analysis, steel/concrete design and Finite Element Analysis (FEA) will be highly regarded in the application process.
    • Applicants graduating in mid-2024 will also be considered for a role with an opportunity to start as a Student Engineer.
