Atlassian can hire people in any country where we have a legal entity. Assuming you have eligible working rights and a sufficient time zone overlap with your team, you can choose to work remotely or from an office (unless it’s necessary for your role to be performed in the office). Interviews and onboarding are conducted virtually, a part of being a distributed-first company.

Join Atlassian as an intern and spend your summer with us having an impact on how millions of users collaborate and use software.

As a Software Engineer intern, you will use your foundation in computer science and awesome skills in writing software with minimal bugs to tackle hard problems. Whilst passionate about your work and delivering amazing quality, you're still able to go fast, which means using your time wisely for your workload.

This role is suitable for students looking to work on front end, back end, full stack and mobile development.

We will look to align you to the best team fit prior to your start date.

This program is open to penultimate-year university students with Australian or New Zealand citizenship or Permanent Residency. The internship dates are late November 2024 to late February 2025, full-time for 12 weeks.

On the first day, we'll expect you to have:

  • Be currently enrolled in a Bachelors or Masters program in Software Engineering, Computer Science or other related technical field and completing your studies before January 2026
  • Experience with Java, Python, JavaScript or other related object-oriented programming languages

It’s great, but not required, if you:

  • Have a real passion for software engineering, as demonstrated by previous internships, work experience, projects, or publications
  • Deep understanding of data structures, in particular how they are implemented and how to apply them to solve problems
  • Familiarity with application of common design patterns

Unfortunately, this role is not eligible for visa sponsorship.
