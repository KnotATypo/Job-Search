Join this national non-profit organization, dedicated to providing access to allied health services and seamless inclusion to clients with disabilities in the Central Brisbane area. The organization is well-established and committed to its mission, with a focus on improving the lives of its clients through high-quality care and support.

They are seeking a dedicated and enthusiastic Speech Pathologist to join their team, with an uncapped professional development allowance and mentoring support to help you reach your career goals. Take advantage of this opportunity to grow your skills and experience in a supportive and collaborative environment.

Options for part time OR full time as well as flexible working hours.

Your responsibilities will include:
  • Providing evidence based therapy services to Adult NDIS clients around central Brisbane
  • Work from their office located in Fortitude Valley to attend team meetings, see clients and collaborate with team members
  • Assist adult clients in achieving clearer speech & pronunciation, swallowing, improving social communication, achieving literacy/learning goals, communication and more.
  • Caseload will be tailored to your experience and preferred interest area
  • Service clients in a limited radius near your location
  • Ensure customers receive tailored services that are individualised & outcomes focused
  • Conducts relevant therapeutic assessment with clients developing the resulting reports & plans
  • Deliver a variety of one-on-one or group based activities in a range of settings including onsite and in the community
  • Record accurate and timely notes & billing relating to activities & outcomes for clients
  • Meet billable hours targets of 5 hours per day after induction (includes report writing, face to face and travel)
The Support Structure:
  • Uncapped professional development allowance to upskill in the areas that interest you
  • Clinical supervision (weekly or fortnightly depending on your needs)
  • Administration team to take care of your bookings and billings
  • Regular in house training with your team
  • A structured onboarding program to ensure you ease into your role and develop your confidence
