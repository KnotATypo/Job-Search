Full job description

Opengear is currently looking to expand our engineering teams working on our next generation networking appliances. The successful applicant will utilise their Linux, C & Python skills to contribute to the embedded software on Opengear devices. With our products being used by some of the world's largest IT companies, you can take your career to the next level.

This role is ideally suited for someone who is a few years into their career. The successful applicant will be joining an established team of seasoned engineers with plenty of opportunity to learn.


For those who enjoy working in an office, our modern near-city location in Milton provides many benefits, including free parking, snacks, soft drinks and a games room. For those who prefer to work from home, we facilitate regular social activities so teams can still enjoy that personal interaction. The choice is yours.


What We Offer:

  • Challenging software development environment on our own in-house designed hardware devices
  • A collaborative, international Agile environment
  • Being part of a growing company with in-demand products
  • Upskill in modern networking technologies
  • Freedom to choose your working environment of home, office or hybrid
  • Flexible work practices ensuring you have a genuine work life balance
  • Quarterly bonuses based on company performance
  • Rewards program with points you can accrue and spend at Amazon and many other retailers
  • Paid parental leave and volunteer days
  • Optional compressed 9-day fortnight

What You Will Do:

  • Work as part of an Agile team building the software of our next generation hardware devices
  • Be part of the whole software process, from design right through to testing and deployment
  • Champion good development practices and contribute to our CI test suite by writing high quality automated tests
  • Deliver highly reliable embedded software for our data center grade networking appliances

Who You Are:

Required

  • Have at least 2 years’ commercial experience in software development with Python on Linux
  • Have some commercial experience in software development with C on Linux
  • Good knowledge of Linux
  • Have experience working in an Agile team environment.

Bonus

  • Experience with networking technologies
  • JavaScript web application development
  • Operating system architecture
  • Yocto


About Opengear

Opengear is a rapidly growing global supplier of sophisticated data centre, network and remote site management products. Our customers include tech unicorns and Fortune 100 companies. We are the leading supplier of out-of-band solutions for IT infrastructure management and are actively exploring new solutions for network automation and management. We are part of Digi, a global company with offices in Australia, the US, and Europe.
