About us

At Auto & General (A&G), we provide great products and services to safeguard our customers in their time of need. Our range of general insurance products protect customers on the road, at home and on holiday with various Car, Motorcycle, Home, Contents, Pet products as well as Roadside Assistance.

Our culture of ‘high performance with high integrity’ underpins our values and the way we interact with our customers, the community and each other. We’re excited about the future and we’re always on the lookout for talented, passionate individuals who can help us achieve our goal of being Australia’s best insurer! If this sounds like you, apply today.

Purpose of the Role

Join Auto & General's dynamic marketing team to innovate solutions for business challenges in a stimulating environment. Support a skilled digital analytics team in developing cloud-based solutions and optimizing performance. Drive enhancements via cloud automation, marketing analytics code development, and A/B testing techniques.

Position Accountabilities

  • Enhance our digital analytics capability within Australia’s premier marketing team
  • Utilise APIs to extract and manipulate data from SAAS platforms like Google Analytics and the Google Marketing Platform
  • Assist in developing intricate A/B tests for our insurance quote and sales processes
  • Proactively seek automation and optimization opportunities using Cloud tools
  • Address ad-hoc business challenges through code development, cloud engineering, and data analysis
  • Enhance implementations of Google Analytics and Acoustic Tealeaf
  • Research and recommend suitable technologies
  • Foster relationships with third-party vendor product managers
  • Ensure compliance with organizational policies and statutory requirements

Qualifications and Experience:

  • Proficient in Cloud tools, especially Google Cloud Platform components like BigQuery, PubSub, Datastore, and Cloud Functions.
  • Experience with APIs of third-party vendors (e.g., Google, Meta) for data extraction and management within SAAS platforms like Google Analytics, Google Ads, and Google Search Ads 360.
  • Familiarity with Tag Managers, especially Google Tag Manager, and server-side implementations. •
  • Experience with A/B testing platforms for test deployment and management.
  • Advanced front-end development skills including HTML, CSS, JavaScript (jQuery).
  • Strong SQL skills, preferably with Google BigQuery.
  • Experience with enterprise reporting and analysis platforms like Tableau.
  • Familiarity with Flutter, Python, and R is advantageous.
  • Collaboration with internal testing teams to ensure code validity and compliance.
  • Prior experience with GA4 implementation and analysis is beneficial.
  • Excellent problem-solving abilities and willingness to learn new software.
  • Effective prioritization skills and project status communication.
  • Understanding of Git version control is advantageous.
  • Knowledge of business and marketing principles.
