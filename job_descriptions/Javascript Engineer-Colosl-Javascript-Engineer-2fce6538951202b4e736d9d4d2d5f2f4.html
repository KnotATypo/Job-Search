About Colosl

Colosl is a Software Consultancy, hell bent on creating world class digital products for organisations that are committed to good causes. It hits us right in the jellies when we see our solutions solving problems for user’s, saving them time, putting smiles on their dials and even saving lives.

We work closely with our clients, often in embedded teams to create human centric and research based solutions. We live and die by the fail fast, fail early mentality by prototyping and testing with users before developing for production. This gives us confidence that we are solving the right problems and lets our engineers build rock solid features without fear of last minute amends and scope creep.
