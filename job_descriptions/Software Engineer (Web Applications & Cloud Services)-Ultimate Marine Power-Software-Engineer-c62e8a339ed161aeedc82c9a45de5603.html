Company Overview:

Ultimate Marine Power are a leader in the supply and support of Innovative Marine Equipment throughout Australasia. Our MISSION is to fulfill our customers' needs with an ultimate solution delivering exceptionally high-end products, integrated systems, and outstanding customer service. .

Position Summary:

As a Software Engineer at Ultimate Marine Power, your primary responsibility will be the development, documentation, and implementation of cutting-edge web applications. These applications are crucial for enabling interactions between our internal staff, clients, and IoT edge devices. This role demands a high level of autonomy, requiring you to generate ideas, design architecture, and evolve systems over time within a small team setting. You will leverage AWS services to build and integrate applications that operate seamlessly and efficiently, while also focusing on the challenges posed by 4G/5G network connections and edge device integration.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Software Development:
    • Independently design, document, and implement web applications for IoT edge device interfacing, with a strong focus on backend and frontend integration.
    • Utilize AWS services such as Iot Core, Greengrass, Cognito, DynamoDB, AppSync, Amplify, and API Gateway to develop scalable, efficient applications.
    • Approach development with a self-guided mindset, generating ideas and architecting solutions that can evolve to meet future requirements.
  • Maintenance and Enhancement:
    • Take proactive ownership of existing edge device plugins, with a focus on enhancing robustness and maintainability through comprehensive documentation and strict type checking.
    • Design and oversee CI/CD pipelines, facilitating automated testing and smooth deployment of updates and new features.
    • Version control using Git in a team environment.
  • Collaboration and Reporting:
    • Work closely with a small, dynamic team to integrate solutions with other company systems effectively.
    • Regularly report progress and challenges, ensuring continuous improvement through transparent communication.

Skills and Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated experience in software development, with a particular focus on web application development and cloud services.
  • Proficiency in AWS services and experience with building multi-tenant applications featuring complex access control mechanisms.
  • Familiarity with IoT technologies, edge devices, and the specific challenges associated with 4G/5G network connections.
  • Strong skills in TypeScript, JavaScript, and Cloud Development Kit (CDK) are highly regarded.
  • Exceptional documentation abilities, capable of creating clear, comprehensive guides and reports.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and a strong, independent work ethic, with the ability to thrive in a small team environment.

Work Environment and Culture:

  • Join a team of passionate professionals in a collaborative and innovative atmosphere, where autonomy and creativity are highly valued.
