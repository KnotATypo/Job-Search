Company Description


We are 'relentlessly guest obsessed' at Novotel Brisbane South Bank. Everything we do, we do to help our guests and our people feel welcome and feel valued to give them the best experience possible.

Our hotel is nestled in South Bank, with direct access to The Brisbane Convention & Exhibition Centre. South Bank is Brisbane’s premier lifestyle and cultural destination on the southern banks of the Brisbane River, it is open 365 days a year and attracts an estimated 11 million visitors annually. The hotel offers 238 rooms, an on trend open kitchen and bar and meeting venues for up to 120 delegates.

There is a rare and exciting opportunity for aspiring marketing professional to join our team as Sales & Marketing Coordinator on a full-time basis.

Reporting to our Director of Commercial and Guest Experience with a dotted reporting line to the Senior Business Development Manager and Digital Marketing Manager, the successful candidate will assist the sales and marketing team with administrative tasks, as well as hotel & departmental projects to ensure the smooth running of the department.

Job Description


In this role you will:

  • Assist Meetings & Events Coordinator with coordination of events within the hotel, including ownership of social events, which includes but not limited to proposal, contracts, banquet event orders, invoicing, after event follow-up, and prospecting/telemarketing calls.
  • Assist Digital Marketing Manager with marketing projects, including but not limited to updating of hotel websites, copywriting, social media and photoshoots.
  • Assist Senior Business Development Manager with sales tasks, including but not limited to proposals, contracts, site inspections, prospecting/telemarketing calls.
  • Assist all members of the Sales & Marketing team with administrative tasks as required, including but not limited to management of PurchasePlus, ACDC, Gift Vouchers, Welcome Cards, Departmental Reporting and any projects.
  • Assist Group Reservations Coordinator with group movement tasks, including but not limited to rooming lists, group movements.
  • Assist in the maintenance of hotel database of key contacts / clients.
  • Work with the sales and marketing team to ensure simple clear policy and procedures are in place.
  • Use of hotel PMS and Sales Systems.
  • Assist with any other tasks and/or activities requested by Director of Commercial & Guest Experience.
