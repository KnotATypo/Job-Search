About Us

Titan ICT is an Australian-owned company specialising in strategic ICT advice, systems integration and technical support services. With a proud record of delivery since 2003, we have a national footprint with offices located Brisbane, Perth and Sydney.

It’s an exciting time for us as we experience organic growth. As a result, we are looking for an experienced, energetic Digital Radio specialist to join our successful and experienced team based in Brisbane.


About the Opportunity

We are seeking a Radio (RF) Communications Systems Engineers to join our team to support the Digital Land Mobile Radio System and infrastructure engineering team, with a specific focus on solutions into the resource sector.


The responsibilities of this role include but are not limited to:

  • Providing RF solutions and consultation with focus on (but not limited to) DMR, TETRA and P25 digital radio systems.
  • Liaising with other team members to provide the best solution for the customer.
  • Designing, optimising, upgrading and maintenance of radio communications systems (including antenna systems, repeaters, earthing, power, propagation analysis, dispatch, subscriber equipment programming and backbone design)
  • Assist with monitoring the overall reliability, capacity and performance of communications systems and equipment including installation/upgrade troubleshooting and maintenance support.
  • Develop technical documentation, drawings, BOMs, presentations, and training.
  • Complete site surveys and system audits, determining potential risks and solution-based infrastructure/power requirements.
  • Perform desktop analysis including propagation, intermodulation, adjacent channel, antenna system loss and RF system traffic loading analysis.
  • Support the team in all technical stages of projects.
  • Develop and perform acceptance testing and installation engineering inspections.
  • Present and discuss technical solutions with clients.


As an experienced RF Engineer you will be able to demonstrate:

  • Bachelor’s degree in electrical/electronic engineering, or communications systems engineering or similar discipline.
  • Demonstrated experience in design, installation, commissioning and maintenance of VHF and UHF digital radio equipment and standards (P25, TETRA, DMR, etc.)
  • Knowledge of design guidelines, standards and requirements associated with design, building and upgrading of radio communication sites (including ACMA, ARPANSA, AS/NZS, IEEE, etc.)
  • Understanding of network design guidelines and concepts.
  • High attention to details and excellent communication skills.
