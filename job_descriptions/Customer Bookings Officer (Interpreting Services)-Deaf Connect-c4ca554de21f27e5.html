About Us

Deaf Connect is the largest, whole-of-life service provider and social impact organisation for Deaf, deafblind and hard of hearing Australians. We stand with the Deaf community to build capacity and influence social change while paying respect to history, culture and language.

About The Role

Deaf Connect is looking for Customer Bookings Officers provide

high quality customer service and interpreter booking services.

As a Customer Bookings Officer you will be responsible for high quality customer service with the focus of delivering information access to Deaf, Deafblind and Hard of Hearing people.

The role is fast paced whereby you will following established processes ensuring timely and accurate administration and allocation of interpreter bookings is achieved.

If you are passionate about delivering high quality customer service and have great communication skills we would encourage you to apply!

Main responsibilities of the role include:
Allocate interpreter requests to permanent and casual interpreters

Liaise and negotiate with customers as to the timing of appointments

Provide advice, education and instruction on interpreter booking procedures to new clients

Answer and respond to all phone calls, emails and online requests in a professional and timely manner
