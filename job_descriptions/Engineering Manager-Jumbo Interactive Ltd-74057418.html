What will you be doing?
As an Engineering Manager, you will manage a mid-sized software development team.  You will ensure that you are tracking the team's efficiency and quality of work, and assist in regularly adjusting processes and timelines to ensure high-quality work is delivered.  As a mentor, we want you to nurture, inspire & encourage the team to perform at their best.

As well as:
Leadership
  • Foster a continuous improvement mindset amongst the team
  • Encourage the team to identify bottlenecks & ways of speeding up delivery
  • Inspire motivated and high-performing teams to continuously learn and grow
  • Coach engineer's career paths and personal growth in a way that lines up with team goals
  • Closely work to help the team form & bond
Technical
  • Understanding engineering best practices
  • Be a subject matter professional in your relevant domain
Planning & Delivery
  • Work with others to refine and shape clear outcomes for their team
  • Own the success of the deliverable outcome
What are we looking for?
The ideal person will be a technical professional with a background working in leadership, who can foster a culture of continuous improvement, encourage their team to enhance delivery efficiency, and inspire high performance and growth. This role requires ownership of project success and a focus on developing the team's skills and concentration on current tasks. We are looking for a dynamic person capable of both managing and guiding a successful software development team (role is about 50% leadership and 50% on the tools).
