About the job

At bdna, we exist to improve society. We choose to only work with clients that have a direct impact on community wellbeing, safety and improvement - which means that we get to make a difference every day!

The bdna Forensic team of business analysts, software developers, engineers, testers and forensic subject matter experts work closely with law enforcement and forensic laboratories to improve forensic service delivery through specialist products.

What you'll do

  • Participate proactively in all Agile sprint ceremonies: daily stand ups; story grooming; team retrospectives; showcases, workshops and planning sessions.
  • Proactively contribute and champion the delivery of quality improvements
  • Working with the stakeholders and the teams to ensure Acceptance Criteria and tests are strong and of value.
  • Work with the Team on elaborating stories and providing estimates to ensure Quality deliverables.
  • Create and execute manual, exploratory and automation test cases using a risk-based approach and ensuring defects are raised in the approved manner.
  • Peer review test cases of other testers.
  • Challenge ways of working to improve the quality of delivery.
  • Engage in Root Cause Analysis and Retrospectives to regularly inspect, reflect and adapt.
  • Keep Test Lead appraised of progress, risks and issues in sprint work and project planning and document accordingly.
  • Monitor the operation and performance of automated tests to ensure they are stable, performant and relevant.
  • Keep up to date with industry quality trends, tools, practices and share this knowledge.
  • Participate in pre deployment testing and rostered overnight deployments as necessary.
  • Actively contribute to post-live quality checks and monitoring as necessary

What you've got

  • Experience in testing in an Agile environment to deliver Quality solutions (including System, Integration, Acceptance, Usability testing).
  • Extensive experience in software test and quality practices e.g. preparing test artefacts (including test plans, test cases, test summary reports, defects).
  • Analytical, with attention to detail and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to work in a structured manner in line with documented procedures.
  • Proven ability to work in a small team with a shared goal, being able to use your time management skills to multi-task in a fast-paced dynamic, complex integrated environment.
  • Stakeholder/relationship management and problem-solving skills
  • Confidence to challenge technical solutions, architecture decisions and test at a very technical level.
  • Experience of using Azure Devops and / or other Test management systems.
  • Proven experience of and in CD/CI software engineering practices.
  • Strong functional, NFT, Security, exploratory and test automation experience.
  • Experience with basic programming languages e.g. TypeScript; Java; Python.
  • Experience with API testing using Postman, SoapUI, REST API etc.
  • Experience in a Government environment, particularly Public Safety and Defence is highly desirable.
