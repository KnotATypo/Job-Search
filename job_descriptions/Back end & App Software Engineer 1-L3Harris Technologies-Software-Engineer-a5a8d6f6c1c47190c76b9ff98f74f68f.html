Software Engineer - Back end & Application

Intelligence & Cyber International (ICI)

Eight Mile Plains, Brisbane

L3Harris Space and Airborne Systems Australia is a Brisbane based subsidiary of L3Harris and part of our ICI Division. We provide design, manufacture, integration and sustainment of advanced electronic warfare components and subsystems using cutting edge wideband radio frequency and photonic technology. We are a trusted, sovereign design and production partner to the Australian Department of Defence and an established exporter to global defence primes.

The Opportunity

Join a great team of talented professionals with world-class technical expertise who take a combined approach to software engineering and enjoy working together.

We are a company on the move and with forecasted growth of L3Harris Space and Airborne Systems Australia we are now seeking to hire a Software Engineer who will be responsible for developing application software and participate in the entire software development life cycle.

Candidates are required to have a good knowledge base of engineering principles, theories and techniques and a passion for relevant new technologies. We are mission focused, agile and take innovation seriously.

Responsibilities

  • Develop modular, readable and robust code for defence systems adhering to security and functional requirements in Python and Typescript/React.
  • Participate in all phases of the software development lifecycle, including requirements analysis, systems engineering, agile development, review, testing, integration, and verification.
  • Presenting design details during customer reviews.
  • Collaborate effectively with both software and other engineering disciplines as well as management, to ensure comprehensive coverage of requirements as efficiently as possible.
  • Write and maintain both internal and deliverable documentation, either directly or through supervision of contractors.
  • Provide Engineering support to manufacturing as required.
  • Participate in demo activities for the purpose of sharing knowledge.
  • Maintain a continuous improvement mindset.

What you’ll bring

To be successful in this role and to best leverage the opportunities provided by L3Harris, some skills & experience ideal applicants will bring include;

  • Minimum 3 years’ experience in similar role.
  • Experience developing in python.
  • Experience designing and implementing front end software using Typescript or Javascript, and React.
  • Ability to work independently or as part of a multi-disciplinary team, adhering to development processes.
  • Excellent computer skills.
  • Excellent communication skills in English, both verbal and written.

Desirable Requirements

  • Experience with Docker.
  • Experience with python for emulation.
  • Experience with python for complex applications.
  • Experience with python for system verification scripting.
