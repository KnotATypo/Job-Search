Responsibilities

A day in the life of this role

  • Collecting & evaluating the requirements from users, product owners, and stakeholders
  • Identifying and solving business problems with the Power Platform
  • Show a cadence of delivery through regular client & product owner interactions as part of agile delivery
  • Work with designers & clients to understand finer details of implementation across the UI and UX process
  • Design solutions and provide estimates for pipeline work
  • Build Canvas Power Apps and Model-Driven Power Apps
  • Design & implement Dataverse databases within the Power Platform
  • Build Flows & Robotic Process Automations (RPA) within Power Automate
  • Build chatbots using Power Virtual Agent
  • Build external-facing portals using Power Portals
  • Incorporating designer, user & product owner feedback into your process
  • Testing your work against the requirements and acceptance criteria
  • Contribute toward producing technical and presales artefacts
  • Helping customers on their Power Platform uplift journey by implementing governance, performing nurturing activities, and advising on best practice

Who you’ll be working with

  • An enthusiastic Modern Applications & Platforms team
  • Velrada & client business analysts & product owners to ensure the correct understanding and interrogation of requirements
  • Other Velrada developers across multiple practices and locations to ensure a succinct outcome pertaining to the requirements
  • User groups & workshop participants

A successful candidate should also be able to demonstrate the following soft skills:

  • Strong communication skills both spoken & written
  • Strong time management skills
  • Comfortable in a client facing and consulting environment
  • The willingness to contribute to the team, culture, and become a leader of your craft
  • Team player with excellent collaboration skills
  • Ability to conduct work remotely using tools such as Miro, Whiteboard, and Microsoft Teams
  • Have a passion for working with a diverse Velrada team of business & technical experts across Australia both face-to-face and remotely
  • Keen for a fast-paced environment with opportunities to learn, grow, and be exposed to a variety of problems, clients, and technologies

A successful candidate should be able to demonstrate the following technical skills:

  • 3+ years experience in industry
  • A portfolio & history of previous web development work
  • Hands on experience delivering with the Microsoft M365 & Power Platform
  • Experience in building solutions that utilise the Dataverse or demonstrate normalised database design
  • Experience in designing & developing Canvas Power Apps and Model-Driven Power Apps
  • Experience in building workflows, processes, and approvals using Power Automate
  • Exposure to the Microsoft Power Portals (formerly D365 Portals)
  • Exposure to chatbots with Power Virtual Agent

Skills that would also benefit this role

  • Experience working with a consulting type of organisation
  • Experience with best-practice development of web applications using HTML5/CSS3/JS/TS
  • Experience with any Dynamics 365 modules or Azure services
  • Light C# and PCF Control capability
