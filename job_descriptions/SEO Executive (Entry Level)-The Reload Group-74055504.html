What You’ll Be Doing

In this role, you'll be responsible for the SEO strategy for a range of clients, which will include things like:

  • Working in a small team of 6-8 people (known internally at Reload as a ‘Pod’) to assist with the coordination and implementation of digital marketing projects & campaigns.
  • Undertaking ongoing keyword research & developing the keyword strategy
  • Content ideation and creation as part of digital marketing and SEO campaigns
  • Continuously review and optimise the on-site content and improve your clients’ websites to deliver the best results to your clients
  • Undertaking website reviews, briefing web developers and completing competitor research.
  • Review Google Analytics, website sales, and performance data to pull campaign insights
  • Prepare monthly/quarterly reports with insights and recommendations in DataStudio, PowerPoint, and other reporting tools
  • Be actively involved in meetings and discussions to plan campaigns, compare results and provide insights and expertise
  • Build strong relationships with clients through regular communication and meetings
  • Deliver exceptional customer service to both clients and teammates. This means meeting deadlines, being proactive, and being flexible in your delivery.
