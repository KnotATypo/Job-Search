A Little bit about us

At the Alpha School System (TASS), we believe a quality education sets children up for greater success in life. This drives us to create innovative and reliable technology solutions, enabling schools to connect and engage with their community to foster an exceptional learning experience that supports every child's learning journey.

A Brisbane based software company, TASS develops and supports TASS.web and Edumate Student Information Systems, and the TrackOne Studio Learning Analytics Suite. The TASS.web product is a comprehensive suite of K-12 school management and community portal software products. TASS forms part of the Education vertical of the Volaris Group of companies, a subsidiary of Constellation Software Inc. (CSI). CSI is an equal opportunity employer of more than 50,000 employees world-wide with a consolidated turnover of over US$6 Billion. This represents a rare opportunity to have the stability and support of a strong global business and the autonomy to make a difference!
