This position reports directly to the Senior Power Electronics Engineer at Tritium PTY Ltd and is based at Murarrie, QLD.

About Tritium

Founded in 2001, Tritium (NASDAQ: DCFC) designs and manufactures proprietary hardware and software to create advanced and reliable DC fast chargers for electric vehicles. Tritium’s compact and robust chargers are designed to look great on Main Street and thrive in harsh conditions, through technology engineered to be easy to install, own, and use. Tritium is focused on continuous innovation in support of our customers around the world.

About the Role

This position sits within the Power Electronics team. The role is intended to support the design and development of the power electronics modules with the main focus on improving the design and of our automated test and validation capabilities. In addition, the role may call on a range of additional tasks including software and firmware development, hardware design, assembly, test and validation.

Key responsibilities for this role include, but are not limited to:
  • Work to develop world leading power modules for Tritium fast charger products
  • Improve user experience and product operation through improved software and control
  • BoM cost, reliability and serviceability are optimized as key attributes
  • Improve reporting and Software quality to improve serviceability
  • Contribute toward the product roadmap, especially the power module and technology roadmap
  • Work closely with the power electronics engineers and systems engineers to develop EMS, IQC and EOL firmware for functional testing of modules as well as for dedicated Automated Test Equipment (ATE)
  • Look for opportunities to up-skill and further improve knowledge and experience
  • Help to develop and plan the implementation of all power modules verification work
  • Create and implement the verification tests used to ensure power module performance conforms with the module TRD with a focus on power PCBA’s and ATE for production
  • Ensure the correct implementation of the verification tests are used to ensure power module performance conforms with the module TRD
  • Liaise closely with the test team (within ETS) to execute all the verification tests
  • Ensure documentation requested for Technical Authoring is delivered on-time with expected quality
  • Help to establish and continue to improve linkages within the team, out to the larger organisation and to external stakeholders
