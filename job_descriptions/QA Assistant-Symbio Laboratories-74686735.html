THE ORGANISATION

Proudly Australian owned and operated, Symbio Laboratories is a leading provider of commercial analytical testing services to the food, agricultural, water, environmental and pharmaceutical industries. With laboratories in Brisbane, Melbourne, Sydney, Perth and Wagga Wagga, Symbio performs important technical analysis on thousands of samples from the food we eat, to the water we drink.

Symbio’s test results underpin consumer confidence, and the Quality Assurance team ensures the integrity of these transactions.

THE ROLE

Symbio Laboratories is seeking a QA Assistant to join the team at our Brisbane Head Office.

Key duties:

  1. Perform daily analytical tasks in the Quality Assurance Department, whilst providing quality services to our clients;
  2. Perform calibrations and checks of laboratory equipment and test equipment;
  3. Maintain the calibration schedule, and equipment and maintenance registers;
  4. Conduct asbestos and foreign matter analysis;
  5. Maintain sample integrity and traceability;
  6. Assist with client-specific reporting requirements, proficiency test reviews, and tasks related to NATA accreditation;
  7. Conduct internal audits when required, and assist with improvement of the Quality Management System.
