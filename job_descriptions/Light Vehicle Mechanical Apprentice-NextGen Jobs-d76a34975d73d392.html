Full job description

Build your mechanical skills working with a wholesale workshop located in Eagle Farm, Brisbane. With vehicles from BMW, Mercedes-Benz and Volvo on the hoists, you will be exposed to a variety of makes and models, while learning to perform repairs and services.

About the Apprenticeship:

  • Prestige brands - working on BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Volvo and more
  • 4 Year, full-time apprenticeship - working towards a Cert III in Light Vehicle Mechanical Technology
  • Learn to repair and service vehicles to pristine working condition
  • Maintain clean, organised and safe workshop
  • Ongoing support throughout your apprenticeship from your NextGen Jobs Mentor

About You:

  • Passionate about a career in light vehicle automotive
  • Strong communication skills
  • Not afraid to problem solve and think outside the box
  • Punctual, reliable and professionally presented at all times
  • Hold a current and valid drivers licence

NextGen Jobs:

NextGen Jobs is Australia's newest group training organisation (GTO), and Australia's first GTO focused exclusively on the delivery of 'new-collar' apprenticeships and traineeships in emerging high-tech and high-growth occupations. We currently employ and train more than 650 young Australians through our national network, making us one of the largest employers of apprentices and trainees in Australia.

NextGen Jobs values and recognises diversity in the workplace. We are an equal opportunity employer and actively support applicants from a diverse range of backgrounds to apply. We offer safe and inclusive working environments where you can learn, grow and achieve your career objectives.

For more information, go to: https://www.nextgenjobs.org.au
