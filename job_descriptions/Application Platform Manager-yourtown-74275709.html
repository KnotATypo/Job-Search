yourtown is a trusted provider of services for young people, with a focus on mental health and wellbeing, long-term unemployment, prevention of youth suicide, child protection, as well as support for those experiencing domestic and family violence.

yourtown helps children and young people realise they have the ability to tackle whatever life throws at them. We help them to believe in their unique strengths, tenacity and self-determination. We recognise the courage and determination of children and young people when they reach out to yourtown for support.

your opportunity

As the Applications Platform Manager , you’ll be at the forefront of managing critical aspects of our platforms. You will bring a wealth of experience in managing Microsoft Dynamics platform and our Dynamics development team. Based at our Milton Head Office, you will oversee application governance, reporting, and major incident management. Collaborating closely with business stakeholders, you’ll lead the management of yourtown’s applications platforms throughout their entire lifecycle—from planning and development to deployment and maintenance. Your strategic vision will shape our applications platform, ensuring alignment with organisational goals and industry best practices.

In this role, you will:

• Collaborate with the Head of Technology & Security to establish the environment management framework.
• Develop and implement a strategic vision for the applications platform, aligning it with yourtown’s overall objectives.
• Provide operational reporting on the performance of yourtown’s business applications.
• Work in collaboration with leadership to manage the annual operational budget.
• Manage the entire application lifecycle, including planning, development, testing, deployment, and maintenance.
• Define and enforce application platform standards, guidelines, and best practices, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and security standards.

what we are looking for

• Possess tertiary qualifications in computer science or related fields, or equivalent practical experience. Additionally, demonstrate significant experience in leading and managing IT teams.
• Showcase a proven ability to create high-quality, scalable solutions using the Microsoft technology stack.
• Embrace a proactive approach to learning and adapting to new systems and technologies.
• Exhibit excellent organisational skills, problem-solving abilities, and interpersonal skills to engage and influence key stakeholders effectively. Prioritise tasks, multitask, and maintain a customer-focused approach in a demanding professional environment.
