About Woolpert

Woolpert is the premier architecture, engineering, geospatial (AEG), and strategic consulting firm, with a vision to become one of the best companies in the world. We innovate within and across markets to effectively serve public, private, and government clients worldwide.

Woolpert is a Global Top 100 Geospatial Company, a Top 100 ENR Global Design firm, earned six straight Great Place to Work certifications, and actively nurtures a culture of growth, inclusion, diversity, and respect. Founded in 1911 in Dayton, Ohio, Woolpert has been America’s fastest-growing AEG firm since 2015 with over 2,000 employees and more than 60 offices on five continents.

In the Asia-Pacific region, Woolpert specialises in the collection, analysis and delivery of spatial data. We capture measurement and location data by surveying the built and natural environments from the air, ground and over water. We deliver digital solutions of the physical world. Our clients use our products and spatial data to plan, design, build and manage their assets, infrastructure and environments. This is a technical industry where innovation and technologies are constantly evolving.

We are seeking multiple skilled and motivated Developers to join our team and play a pivotal role in shaping our clients’ success.

Your new role

As a key member of our Digital Delivery team, you will use your strong software development skills and technical knowhow to develop custom applications and deliver revolutionary projects for our clients.

Your key responsibilities will include:

  • Work as part of a development team in an agile environment to create, customise, and support full stack applications and services.
  • Develop enhancements for existing geospatial applications, tools, and web services.
  • Perform testing, troubleshooting, and functional evaluation to ensure product quality and release readiness.
  • Conduct performance testing, tuning, usability testing and functional testing to meet quality objectives.
  • Create and maintain technical documentation and deployment guides.

You may also be required, within your skills, qualifications, and experience, to undertake other responsibilities and perform other duties or projects from time to time as the Company may require meeting its operating needs.

What we’re looking for

To be successful in this role you will already have prior experience in a similar role together with:

  • 1+ year of experience with geospatial technology, ideally using the ArcGIS platform.
  • Strong foundational proficiency in HTML, JavaScript, ideally TypeScript and CSS
  • Experience developing and consuming RESTful API’s or services using .NET (C#), .NET core, Python or Java Frameworks.
  • Experience maintaining/reviewing existing code frameworks.
  • Competent with development tools such as Visual Studio Code, Git, GitHub, Bitbucket, Jira, Confluence
  • Experience in quality assurance, test strategies, test automation, and deployment automation to support CI/CD delivery.
  • Proven ability to be self-sufficient, prioritise tasks and meet deadlines
  • Experience with enterprise IT environments and developing for service- oriented and cloud-based technologies.
  • Experience in delivering projects on time and within the budget.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, including presentation skills.
  • Self-motivated team player with an interest in continuous learning and building software products.
  • Exceptional communication and interpersonal abilities, enabling effective collaboration with diverse teams and clients.
