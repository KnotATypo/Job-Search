Your next exciting job opportunity -

To continue our success, we are looking for enthusiastic Graduate or Experienced Graduate Flooding Engineers seeking an opportunity to advance your career in flood modelling, investigation, and floodplain management. Join one of Australia’s leading specialist flood teams, working alongside the TUFLOW development team; Learn from the best, join BMT, and be surrounded by our dynamic specialist flooding team in Brisbane working on an exciting pipeline of work.


In this role you will be able to:

  • Conduct flood risk assessments, including hydrological and hydraulic modelling, floodplain mapping, and analysis of flood impacts.
  • Perform detailed investigations of flood events, assess flood mitigation measures, and provide recommendations for flood risk reduction strategies.
  • Collaborate with multi-disciplinary teams to develop flood management plans and policies, ensuring compliance with relevant regulations and standards.
  • Utilize advanced software and tools for flood modelling, data analysis, and visualization to support decision-making processes.
  • Stay updated with the latest industry trends, research, and advancements in flood engineering, and apply innovative approaches to enhance flood resilience.

Relevant Education, Experience, and Skills:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Civil Engineering, Environmental Engineering, or a related field.
  • 0-5 years of professional experience in flood engineering, flood modelling, floodplain management, or a similar role. Role offered will be based on experience level.
  • Experience in hydrological and hydraulic modelling software (e.g., TUFLOW), GIS tools (e.g., ArcGIS, QGIS), and other relevant software applications.
  • Knowledge of flood risk assessment methodologies, floodplain zoning, flood management strategies, and relevant legislation and regulations.
  • Experience in conducting flood investigations, analysing flood data, and interpreting flood modelling results.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills, with the ability to think critically and develop innovative solutions to complex flood-related challenges.
  • Effective communication skills to liaise with clients, stakeholders, and regulatory agencies, and to present technical information in a clear and concise manner.
  • Good organisational skills, with the ability to contribute to multiple projects simultaneously and deliver high-quality work within deadlines.
