About Aphex:

At Aphex (https://www.aphex.co/), we believe that construction teams' tools to plan and communicate their work should have stayed in 1995.

That's why we are on a mission to make it easier for construction teams to get on the same page with a live, multiplayer planning platform. Instead of the current mountain of spreadsheets, whiteboards, and stone tablets, we build tools that allow anyone on a project to plan and communicate their work with complete visibility of everyone working around them.

Our customers are the largest construction contractors, building the tunnels, roads, bridges, and buildings we use daily. Our users are the engineers managing and planning these incredible projects.

We're at an exciting stage of our journey. Having launched our V1 in the UK in 2019, we power most major contractors and projects today. We entered the Australian market in 2022 and have replicated that success as the leading solution. As a team, we have found initial product market fit, have proven we can expand across international markets, and are in the early stages of scaling our go-to-market team.
