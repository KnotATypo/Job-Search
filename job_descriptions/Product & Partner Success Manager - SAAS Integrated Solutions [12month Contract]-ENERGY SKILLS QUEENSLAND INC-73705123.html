Join Our Team as a Product and Partner Success Manager!

We are seeking a dynamic and versatile professional to join our team as a Product and Partner Success Manager for our Integrated Solution, SkillPASS.

Reporting to the General Manager, SkillPASS, the ideal candidate will possess a unique blend of skills, largely focused on the development and innovation of SkillPASS, but also ensuring the Customer Success Team is proactively engaging with our largest and most valuable customers in their book of work, ensuring they guide their customers to optimal value realisation while at the same time partnering with their customers on their business needs.


The desirable talents of this role include product management, project management, data analysis, customer success management, SAAS experience, and business development. This role will be pivotal in driving the success of our integrated solutions, ensuring they meet customer needs and contribute to the overall growth of the business and the numerous opportunities we presently have on hand.

While initially a 12-month maternity leave cover, there are good prospects for continuance in the right circumstances. If you're ready to make an impact and drive growth in a fast-paced industry, we want to hear from you!

Responsibilities:

Partner Success Management:

Collaborate with customer success teams to understand customer pain points and address them through product enhancements, and strategising intelligence from client business reviews.

Develop strategies to increase customer satisfaction and loyalty for integrated solutions.

Act as a bridge between customers and internal teams to ensure a seamless customer experience.

You will be responsible for driving continuous value for the teams’ portfolio of enterprise accounts in all stages of the customer lifecycle.

You will act as a trusted advisor to your team and their customers, meeting regularly with relevant stakeholders and continuing to drive engagement.

You will onboard strategic customers focusing on maximising value while finding opportunities to increase usage and create long-term value.

You will provide resources to your team and their customers, including success plans, and customer-specific training sessions, and organise regular check-ins.

You will translate your teams’ customers’ requirements by understanding their risk management frameworks to offer a tailored solution on how best they can utilise SkillPASS and its suite of offering

You will monitor the health of the teams’ customers and intervene with proactive education when they or their customers are not effectively using SkillPASS to achieve the value they expected.

Advocate for the SkillPASS customer on all levels; Provide updates to internal cross-functional partners (Product, Sales & Marketing) on customer perspectives, risks, strategic insights, issue resolution activities, expansion & cross-sell opportunities.

Drive customer satisfaction, and product adoption for the team. And ultimately, increase lifetime customer value.

Work with your team to develop strategic account plans that clearly articulate the customer’s business, their goals, and how SkillPASS can play into their success.

Engage and collaborate in a fast-moving, multicohort, multidisciplinary team, contributing to ongoing improvements.

Product Management:

Champion and lead the end-to-end product lifecycle, from ideation to launch, for integrated solutions that meet market demands.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define product requirements, specifications, and roadmaps.

Conduct market research to identify trends, competitive landscape, and customer needs.

Project Management:

Develop and execute project plans, ensuring timely delivery of integrated solutions.

Coordinate with internal and external teams, technology partners, design, and other stakeholders to prioritise and manage project timelines.

Monitor project progress, identify potential roadblocks, and implement solutions to keep projects on track.

Data Analysis:

Utilise data-driven insights to inform product decisions and optimise the performance of integrated solutions.

Interpret system data and UI status to ensure optimisation to enhance end UX.

Work closely with analytics teams to develop and implement metrics for measuring product success.

Analyse customer feedback and usage data to drive continuous improvement.

Be proactive and operate as part of a team in the design, development and integration of an ESQ Card, or equivalent/related card, acting as the interface for SkillPASS.

SAAS Experience:

Leverage expertise in SAAS (Software as a Service) to drive the development and enhancement of our cloud-based solutions.

Stay abreast of industry trends and best practices in SAAS to inform product strategy.

Business Development:

Identify and pursue business development opportunities that align with the company's strategic goals.

Collaborate with internal and external teams to understand market demands, organic growth opportunities, and contribute to revenue growth.

Establish and maintain partnerships to expand the reach and impact of integrated solutions.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in business, project management, computer science, or a related field; MBA is a plus.
  • Proven experience in product management, project management, data analysis, and customer success management.
  • Demonstrated success in driving business development initiatives and achieving revenue targets.
  • Strong understanding of SAAS principles and experience working with cloud-based solutions.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work cross-functionally.
  • Analytical mindset with the ability to translate data into actionable insights.
  • Proactive problem-solving skills and a results-driven approach.
