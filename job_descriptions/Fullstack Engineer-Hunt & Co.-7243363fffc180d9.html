Are you a Full Stack Engineer looking to make a positive impact? Does the thought of creating software solutions for an ever-expanding organisation excite you? Are you located in Brisbane? We want to talk to you!

Our client is looking for a dedicated and experienced Full Stack Engineer to join their team as they continue to scale. After a recent acquisition, they are excited to keep growing to maintain their reputation as a leader in the Australian healthtech industry. If you want to work towards making healthcare accessible to all, then keep reading!

About You:
  • You have a strong knowledge of Azure, .NET and Angular
  • You like solving problems efficiently and effectively
  • You have excellent attention to detail
  • You have great communication and collaboration skills
  • You enjoy working with others to design and create software
  • You have a passion for writing clean code that adheres to best practices
Benefits:
  • Remote or hybrid options — they are champions of a healthy work/life balance
  • Free onsite parking at their office in South Brisbane
  • Annual training days to ensure continuous professional development
  • Strong team culture that prioritises regular celebrations and acknowledgement
The Role :
  • Work with internal and external teams to solve, deliver and engineer excellent software solutions
  • Participate in full lifecycle software development using C#, Angular, Javascript and SQL
  • Write, translate and code software according to specifications — consistency and reliability is key for our client
  • Design and develop both internal and external facing APIs
  • Design enterprise level software applications hosted in Microsoft Azure
Experience:
  • Minimum 4 years experience with C#, ASP, .NET/SQL and Javascript
  • Experience with Azure Cloud Platform
  • Experience with full-stack development projects in a commercial setting
  • Working knowledge of SQLServer databases and SQL queries
  • Ability to work with internal and external teams
  • A proven contributor to past teams in a fast-paced environment
Salary: Up to $150k+ Super
