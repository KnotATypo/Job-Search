We are seeking a skilled and experienced IT Systems Engineer to join our client's dynamic team. Reporting to the IT Infrastructure Manager, you will be responsible for the management of the organisation's infrastructure systems and security. This includes designing, planning, developing, installing, configuring, maintaining, supporting, and optimising all computer systems and applications. You will work closely with IT teams, audit, and other corporate teams to ensure business continuity, quality, and performance of IT systems.

Details:

  • Permanent, fulltime position.
  • Hybrid working (1-2 days onsite). Office based in Eight Mile Plains but open to remote candidates in AU.
  • Brisbane based applicants will naturally be preferred.
  • Reporting to IT Infrastructure Manager.
  • Must be a Permanent Resident or AU Citizen.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Build and maintain cloud-based infrastructure using Azure.
  • Liaise with vendors to assess technology capability as required.
  • Manage and monitor all infrastructure and systems installations, including configurations, testing, and maintenance.
  • Determine the appropriate configuration of hardware and software to ensure desired performance.
  • Diagnose faults with production systems, research and implement solutions, and resolution of escalated problems from the service desk.
  • Contribute to the planning and implementation of maintenance and installation work.
  • Perform project work and business as usual tasks.
  • Produce, evaluate, and maintain systems documentation.
  • Identify risks and issues regarding the use of IT systems within the organisation.
  • Provide technical expertise to enable the correct application of operational procedures.
  • Use management tools to determine infrastructure load and performance statistics.
  • Assess, analyse, develop, document, and implement changes based on requests for change.
  • Govern compliance against company policies, procedures, and security practices.

Required Knowledge and Experience:

  • Experience with Azure infrastructure and Office365 applications.
  • Experience in developing and contributing to IT system strategies.
  • Proven experience in remote support of systems and customers.
  • Understanding of industry-standard security frameworks.
  • Experience with firewalls, switches, routers, and VPNs.
  • Experience with backup infrastructure and DR planning, testing, and execution.
  • Experience with monitoring tools.
  • Experience in security incident and event management.
  • Security focus with hardening, patch, and vulnerability management.
