What you’ll be doing

Atturra, in partnership with a global-renowned organisation is looking for a driven and motivated Power BI Developer based in Brisbane, to design and build systems and applications for their clients within.

We are seeking candidates who have a passion for innovative technology and driving customer transformation. This person must be able to communicate with a variety of different audiences and must have strong presentation skills. The ability to work in a cohesive team is important for this role.

This will be an initial 6-month contract, with hybrid working requirements in their state-of-the-art Brisbane city office.

Responsibilities:

  • Establish business intelligence best practices, including architecture and governance.
  • Work with business stakeholders to understand their reporting and data needs and develop effective BI solutions.
  • Help to develop datasets that are high quality, well-modelled, and available where/when needed.
  • Assist with business uptake of BI reports, dashboards, and self-service literacy, including through workshop facilitation.
  • Perform analyses and data visualisation to uncover business insights.

What will make you successful in this role?

Technical Requirements:

  • Minimum 4 years implementing Microsoft BI solutions for clients
  • Work with end users to understand their reporting and data needs, and design and develop BI solutions to meet these needs
  • Experience in developing Power BI reports with multiple data sources
  • Experience in integrating Power BI and Power Apps

Behavioural Requirements:

  • Agile project delivery experience
  • Must have effective written and verbal communication skills, with both technical and non-technical audiences
  • Ability to effectively recognise and adapt to change
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment
  • High level of enthusiasm, a strong work ethic, and highly self-motivated
  • Strong interpersonal skills and ability to work effectively with a wide range of individuals in a diverse community
  • Applications are open Australian Citizens and Permanent Visa holders in Australia.
