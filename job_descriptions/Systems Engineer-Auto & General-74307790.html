Purpose of the Role

The Systems Engineer is responsible for delivering technology solutions and operational services that support critical workloads across on-premises infrastructure and hybrid cloud resources, system patching and upgrades with a focus on continuous improvement through the ownership of platform roadmaps.

Position Accountabilities

  • Ensure platform performance, stability, and security.
  • Drive continuous improvements for reliability, scalability, and efficiency.
  • Lead infrastructure innovation for enhanced performance and reliability.
  • Develop technical roadmaps and implement improvement opportunities.
  • Evaluate and recommend new technologies for cloud migration and cost reduction.
  • Collaborate with DevOps for automated deployment and maintenance.
  • Implement proactive monitoring and alerting tools.
  • Ensure compliance with security policies and industry standards.
  • Provide expert technical support to technology teams.
  • Contribute to knowledge base and procedure enhancement.
  • Mentor junior staff for skill development.
  • Maintain compliance with company policies and procedures.

Qualifications and Experience:

  • 3+ years of relevant experience with tertiary qualifications or industry certifications.
  • Ability to communicate technical solutions effectively.
  • Advanced problem-solving and implementation skills across technologies such as; Microsoft, VMware, vSphere, NetApp Storage, Citrix ect.
  • Excellent understanding of systems security and administration practices.
  • Proficient in stakeholder management and communication.
  • Capable of handling multiple tasks within prioritized frameworks.
  • Familiarity with project management frameworks.
  • Demonstrated track record of delivering customer-focused outcomes.
