CNC Machine Operation

Are you looking for a change from the standard CNC machining position that you have been performing for a few years now?

Are you looking to use your skills in a new environment, on Complex Products, with Cutting Edge Equipment, with Modern Manufacturing systems?

Do you want to challenge yourself, and work on the most high-end, technically complex programs on the market?

Ferra Engineering is a market leading manufacturing organisation servicing the aerospace industry.

We are a well-established Engineering Company located in Brisbane that specialises in precision CNC machining, mechanical sub assembly & Aerostructure manufacturing. We invest heavily in modern equipment, and work in a clean environment, to make some of the most challenging and complex components on the market today. We work nearly exclusively with Digital Product, using modern manufacturing systems, processes, and CAD models to manufacture and inspect parts.

We work with partners like Boeing, Lockheed Martin, Northrup Grumman, Parker Aerospace, and many others to manufacture parts for the F-35 Lightning II, MQ-28 Ghost Bat, and many other programs.

We have several positions available to support those programs in the Machining department. We would love to hear from applicants with experience with aerospace quality systems, who has worked on 5 Axis machines, or those who want to transition into an aerospace / defence environment.

Our renumeration system is based on the skills you have. The more machines you can run well, the more we will pay you. So, if your new to the trade, have only run a single machine type, there’s plenty of opportunity for you to grow, with pay increases based on number of machines you have mastered, as well as the opportunity to recognise the other skills, such as CNC programming (Mastercam or Catia) and inspection.

We can support your growth with the variety of Milling Machine, including Mazak, DMG, Doosan, Makino, Breton and HAAS. Our controllers include Heidenhain, TNC, Celos, Fanuc, Pro 3 / 6 & HAAS. There’s plenty of opportunity to run something you know as well as to learn something new.

We also run around the clock, with a Day, Afternoon and Night Shift. We have positions open on all shifts, and we pride ourselves on being flexible with our team.

We are looking for people to join our team, who are proud to produce Australian made aviation products, and who can take pride in their work when they see a jet fly by.

Responsibilities and duties will include:

  • Manufacture components to drawings and instructions as detailed on work orders using Computer Numerical Control Machines (CNC)
  • Record details on work orders and sign for completed work
  • Measure completed work using handheld gauges to ensure components conforms to shop and/or customer drawings
  • Use electronic workstations to log on and off tasks to cost centres as they are performed
  • Use of hand tools and equipment associated with the performance of work duties
  • Carry out Operator maintenance on machinery as required
  • Work as part of the team to investigate machining defects and work to prevent re-occurrence

The ideal candidate will have:

  • Good understanding of manufacturing processes
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Ability to manage their time
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Understanding of 3D models and using of CAD systems.
  • Good Computer literacy (Windows and MS Office)

Qualifications and Experience

Essential

  • Trade Qualification or Recognised Equivalent as a Metal Machinist (Certificate III in Engineering)
  • Use of measuring equipment including Vernier’s, Micrometres etc.
  • Read and understand drawings, including model-based data.
  • Read and understand ISO G Code for minor program changes.

Desirable

  • 2 years' experience in a CNC Machining Role
  • Programming CNC Machines using a CAM Package (Preference for Mastercam or Catia)
  • Reading and understanding technical engineering drawings, GD&T & specifications
  • Working within an AS9100 and ISO9001 quality management system
