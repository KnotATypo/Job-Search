Cloud Engineer

QTAC is a not-for-profit organisation that provides and operates an equitable, centralised tertiary entrance system on behalf of our member and participating institutions and to provide comprehensive information for prospective applicants. The institutions that participate in QTAC determine their own admission requirements and QTAC ensures that these admission rules and procedures are administered in a transparent, consistent and equitable manner.

Are you an experienced Cloud / DevOps practitioner looking for a change? This is your opportunity to give back to the community by joining a growing not-for-profit, community focused organisation that supports young people to access education prospects.

This exciting and varied position works closely with talented Infrastructure and project teams supporting BAU and project work.  You will provide build and deployment support to production and project systems, proactive monitoring, incident management, and issue investigation and resolution, as you enhance and update business systems and processes.

To be successful in this role you will use your experience in AWS/Azure cloud environments and use best practice methodologies to optimise our continuous integration, deployment and delivery pipelines.
You will drive scalability, automation, innovation and be an enabler for our software developers, working closely and collaborating with the project team and the infrastructure team to build and integrate modern tools and best practices into our environment.
