Job Summary:

We are seeking an enthusiastic and motivated individual to join our team as a Laboratory Assistant. The role primarily involves preparation of glassware and sampling media for field personnel, and recovery and analysis of samples using a range of laboratory techniques. You will be working in a friendly team environment and must be a self-starter able to work independently.

Specific laboratory duties including:

  • In-house lab analysis including titrations, gravimetric analysis (weighing), gas chromatography and dynamic olfactometry;
  • Administrative duties including preparation of laboratory sample reports, completion of chain of custody documents, shipping samples to external laboratories and recording sample movement.
  • Preparing of chemical solutions;
  • Sample preparation & clean-up;
  • Maintaining lab cleanliness;
  • Preparing glassware and sampling equipment / media based on monitoring team requirements;
  • Perform routine and troubleshooting, calibration and maintenance on instrumentation and equipment.
  • Working to strict schedules while maintaining product quality

Skills:

  • An interest in science, chemistry and laboratory methods.
  • Motivated and able to work independently
  • Good working ability with computers and spreadsheets
  • Good analytical and technical "hands-on" skills
  • Aptitude for practical problem solving
  • Aptitude for attention to detail to ensure a high degree of QA/QC
  • A valid driver's license

Salary will be consummate to experience and education.

Position is for immediate start.
