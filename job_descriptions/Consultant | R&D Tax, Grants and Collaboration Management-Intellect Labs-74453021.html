Intellect Labs is a boutique consulting firm with a focus on R&D Tax, Grant Strategy, Collaboration Management and Intellectual Property advice. We are driven by our purpose – to help Australian science and technology businesses innovate, grow and solve the world’s greatest challenges.

Our services lie at the crossroads of accounting, law, science, and technology and we work with incredible Australian companies across a range of industries.

The Opportunity

Join a high-calibre team of advisors offering a lifestyle-friendly culture. We are looking for people who want to work in a fast-growing, high-performing but friendly practice.

You will work directly with our Principals across our medtech, biotech, software, food and beverage, recycling and manufacturing client base. With direct coaching from our experienced team, you’ll get the skills, support and opportunity to quickly build your advisory experience and business understanding early in your career.

  • Bonus opportunities as you progress in your career.

Some other kickers:

The Role

This is a full-time role working 37.5 hours per week. Your day-to-day will include a mix of:

  • Conducting project scoping interviews with inventors and innovators to identify eligible R&D projects or match projects with other Government funding;
  • Preparing R&D Tax Incentive and grant applications to advocate projects against legislation and merit criteria and calculating R&D expenditure and tax benefits
  • Advising clients on strategies to maximise R&D Tax Incentive claims;
  • Working directly with technical and finance teams in exciting industries; and
  • Involvement with business development initiatives to continue to grow the practice.

Who You Are

The role will suit a recent graduate in a tertiary qualification like law, commerce, accounting, science, engineering or similar. This role would also suit a post-graduate researcher looking to shift to a career in consulting. Our ideal candidate is an especially good written communicator and great at synthesising information into concise and structured formats.

We are looking for someone who:

  • Asks a lot of questions and gets excited hearing about new ideas,
  • Finds inspiration in the success of others,
  • Learns from feedback, is self-motivated and sees effort as a path to mastery.
