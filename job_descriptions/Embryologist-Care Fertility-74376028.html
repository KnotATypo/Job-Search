We have an exciting opportunity for an Embryologist who is passionate about reproductive science and exceptional patient care.

Working collaboratively with our friendly and supportive team, you will perform a range of standard and complex procedures, using advanced equipment in our laboratory at Greenslopes Private Hospital.

You will be available to work from 6:30am or 7:30am Monday to Friday, public holidays and weekend rotating rosters.

Responsibilities

  • Perform standard ART procedures and other treatments undertaken in the andrology/embryology laboratory;
  • Perform (or develop proficiency in) a range of advanced ART procedures;
  • Provide scientific support at clinical procedures;
  • Participate in quality management and audit processes;

Requirements:

  • Experience working in a clinical andrology/embryology laboratory;
  • Bachelor of Science degree or equivalent;
  • Demonstrated competency at standard ART procedures (including embryo transfer and vitrification) and ICSI experience (essential);
  • Experience in embryo biopsy is preferred but not essential;
  • Knowledge of ART legislation and clinical protocols;
  • Knowledge of RTAC Code of Practice and Pathology Practice and    Accreditation (NATA)
  • Knowledge of quality management and aseptic technique.

Attributes:

  • Excellent communication, organisational and inventory skills;
  • Accuracy and attention to detail;
  • A team player with the ability to work autonomously;
  • Ability to follow policies and procedures without supervision;
  • Computer literacy and keyboard skills.
