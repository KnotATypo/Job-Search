About us

Are you looking for a career with endless opportunities? There’s more to us than banking.

P&N Group is amongst the largest customer-owned banking organisations in Australia, represented by P&N Bank in Western Australia and BCU Bank in New South Wales and southeast Queensland. Our purpose is to enrich the lives of our customers and their communities.

With over $8 billion in assets and a head office in Perth and corporate officers in Coffs Harbour and Brisbane, we service over 180,000 customers across Australia from all walks of life supported by over 600 employees in group services, retail banking, broker, and business banking roles.

We believe our culture is a real point of difference, and our values – Focus on Members, Improve & Learn and Trust & Collaborate – are fundamental to our ways of working. We offer a collaborative and inclusive workplace where our people feel a strong sense of belonging, are recognised, invested in, cared for, and are part of something bigger.

As part of our ongoing strategic customer-centric transformation, we’re focussed on making it even easier to bank with us for our customers of today and the future.


About the Role

  • Designs and develops data pipelines including orchestration, ingestion, transformation, and delivery - across streaming, event and batch-style processing
  • Understands all types of source system including SAAS applications, relational databases, semi-structured databases, flat files and APIs
  • Can apply engineering knowledge to solve one-off problems and edge cases that require scripted solutions
  • Applies industry standard modelling techniques to create trusted data sets
  • Always considers security and privacy of data, ensuring these concerns are included consciously and by design
  • Participates in the Scrum and traditional project initiatives
  • Uses and understands CI/CD pipelines and automations
  • Participates in the on-call rotation and works to prioritise and address production issues
  • Monitors and optimises the cost and performance of the production platform
  • Full-time, 12 month contract with possibility of transitioning to permanent


What will you bring?

Experience

  • Extensive hands-on professional experience with a mix of cloud and on-premise data stores, data processing and/or general software engineering
  • Familiarity with cloud data platforms and respective ecosystems
  • Ability to use open source and commercial data pipeline tools and operate these on SAAS, Windows and Unix-based platforms
  • Develop and enhance data warehouse and reporting capabilities
  • Develop the data warehouse data models and pipelines
  • Design and implement ELT processes including mappings, data flows and orchestration

Skills & Knowledge

  • Columnar cloud data warehousing (eg. Snowflake, Redshift, BigQuery, etc.)
  • Cloud data integration (eg. Fivetran, CData, Informatica, AWS DMS, AWS AppSync, Matillion, AirByte, Singer, Stitch, Python scripting, etc.)
  • ELT-style data transformation tooling (eg. SQL, dbt, Datameer, Snowflake Tasks & Streams, AWS Glue, Matillion, etc.)
  • DevOps tools (eg. GitHub Actions, Azure DevOps, BitBucket Pipelines, etc.)
  • Cloud Infrastructure expertise (eg. AWS, Azure or GCP) is highly desirable
  • Reporting tools (eg. PowerBI, Tableau, QuickSight, etc.) are highly desirable
  • Skills related to our pre-cloud legacy environment (SQL Server & SSIS) are desirable
  • SQL mastery is required
  • Basic Python or equivalent language knowledge is required
  • Basic Unix and/or PowerShell scripting is required
  • Ability to work from the command line in Windows and/or Unix-based systems is highly regarded
  • Knowledge of R, Scala, Java and/or JavaScript is highly regarded
  • Experience building on multi-stage data pipelines covering extraction, staging, transformation, and presentation
  • Familiarity with Kimball, Data Vault, Lambda architecture and other industry standard techniques
  • Excellent organisational, verbal, and written communication skills, problem solving and ability to gather and interpret requirements
  • Kimball, AWS, Informatica, dbt and/or Snowflake certifications are highly desirable
  • Bachelor Degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, associated discipline or equivalent life experience
