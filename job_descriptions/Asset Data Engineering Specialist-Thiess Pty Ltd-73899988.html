About The Role

Thiess is seeking an Asset Data Engineering Specialist to play a pivotal role in advancing our digital transformation in asset management. Based in our Brisbane Head Office, you will spearhead the development and implementation of comprehensive Asset Data solutions, supporting the execution of our business plan and transforming current practices. With Thiess, you will have the opportunity to work alongside industry experts and contribute to projects that that are reshaping asset management through advanced data engineering.

Additionally, you will:

  • Design and develop data movement pipelines and operational data warehousing solutions.
  • Incorporate extensive asset sensor-generated datasets into data engineering solutions.
  • Collaborate with data science and business intelligence teams to tailor data solutions for a range of use cases.
  • Manage and interpret data from mining or heavy industry assets
  • Develop, refine & maintain dimensional models to support multi-source enterprise reporting.
  • Advocate for and assist in the development of effective data governance processes.
  • Facilitate and maintain system integrations with existing platforms such as Entire, TyreSense, Synergy, OneStream, Salesforce, and Anaplan.
  • Identify opportunities to improve the quality and efficiency of maintenance work through leading engineering practices and new technologies.

About You

Your seamless communication, both verbal and written, adds finesse to the flow of ideas and information. You will elevate stakeholder experience through exceptional service and take the initiative to champion innovation in a collaborative, diverse team environment. You will uphold unwavering integrity and contribute to a culture of trust.

Additionally you have:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or related field required; Master’s degree preferred.
  • Minimum 2 years of experience in data engineering roles, preferably within heavy-industry sectors such as mining or construction.
  • Experience in developing and managing cloud data platforms, collaborating with Cloud and Infrastructure teams.
  • Proficiency in using the Azure data stack, SQL, Databricks or Spark, and handling various data ingestion requirements.
  • The following is not essential but will be advantageous:
    • Experience using Business Intelligence visualisation tools (e.g. Power BI, Tableau)
    • Exposure to mechanical concepts and processes relating to asset management and/or condition monitoring.  This will aid in understanding requirements from your stakeholders, the majority of whom are of a mechanical background.
