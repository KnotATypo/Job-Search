The Role

Reporting to the Managing Director, the Head of Finance and Systems holds the pivotal responsibility of overseeing financial operations, technological advancements, and innovative solutions within the organisation. This role plays a crucial part in steering the financial well-being and technological progress of the company towards sustainable growth and success, while also providing mentorship and direction to the Finance Officer and Business Services Team. The aim is to cultivate a culture of continual improvement and operational excellence. The Head of Finance and Systems is tasked with offering strategic financial counsel, fostering commercial awareness, and aligning financial strategies and systems to meet business objectives, all while enhancing operational efficiencies to elevate our organisational competitiveness.

Your responsibilities include:

  • Leading the strategic development and implementation of financial strategies and systems that support the company's growth goals.
  • Evaluating, selecting, and implementing cutting-edge financial technologies and systems to enhance efficiency, precision, and decision-making capabilities.
  • Championing a culture of innovation by encouraging creativity, experimentation, and continuous improvement in systems, processes, and practices.
  • Ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and internal policies, while managing financial risks through robust governance and controls.
  • Promoting operational excellence through process streamlining, cost control, and performance evaluation to maximise value and operational effectiveness.
  • Supervising the finance team and collaborating closely with the administration team to ensure effective and efficient delivery of business services across the organisation.
  • Cultivating and nurturing business partnerships; actively engaging with stakeholders across various departments to understand their objectives and challenges.
  • Managing the preparation of monthly management reports and fulfilling all other related reporting obligations as required.
  • Collaborating with the Leadership and Management Teams to establish budgets that support business planning initiatives.
  • Fostering a high-performance culture by providing leadership, mentorship, and development opportunities to team members, promoting collaboration, accountability, and engagement.

By embodying exemplary leadership behaviours and demonstrating a commitment to innovation and continuous improvement, you play a pivotal role in shaping the organisation's financial resilience, technological advancement, and competitive edge in the marketplace.

This is a newly established, full-time position that is currently open, as part of our organisational growth strategy.

About You

Given the strategic nature of this role and its impact on organisational success, you will possess a blend of financial acumen, leadership capabilities, and a forward-thinking mindset. Experience in managing cross-functional teams and leading transformative change initiatives would be advantageous.

You will demonstrate expertise in financial management, strategic planning, and innovation leadership. With strong analytical abilities, you will interpret complex financial data, and exhibit proficiency in implementing technological solutions to enhance business operations. A proven track record in fostering an innovative culture and driving operational excellence will be crucial for this role.

To excel in this position, you will need:-

  • A natural leadership approach, characterised by integrity and accountability
  • A collaborative approach to knowledge sharing, reflecting our company’s core principles
  • An adaptive, innovative approach to problem solving
  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Business Administration, or related field. Additional qualifications such as CPA, CFA, or MBA are preferred.
  • At least 5 years of experience in a senior finance or business leadership role, preferably within a similar industry or context
  • Integrated ERP (or multifaceted accounting program) software proficiency
  • Prior experience using Deltek Vision is highly desirable
  • The ability to manage multiple, competing priorities, under pressure
  • An agile mindset with a focus on adaptability
  • Proven ability to engage at a personal level with team members, to understand their goals, drivers, and wellbeing considerations
  • A commitment to recognising, rewarding, and celebrating individual and team achievements

Our offices are located in Brisbane, Newcastle, Townsville and Perth and we offer flexible work arrangements for our staff. Regular travel to our Brisbane office will be required for team meetings, annual strategy days, social events, training workshops and end of year celebrations.

About Us

AGE Consultants is Australia’s largest specialist groundwater environmental consultancy. Established in 1997, we exist to empower informed water decisions to help our clients, our communities, and our environment thrive. With over 35 geoscientists across our Brisbane, Newcastle, Townsville and Perth offices, and an outstanding support team, we combine water knowledge in the disciplines of hydrogeology, hydrogeochemistry, and groundwater modelling. Our vision is to be recognised throughout Australia and beyond as the go-to specialists in groundwater and environmental consulting for a changing world. We do this through:-

  • being a place people want to work
  • being good people to work with
  • building on our expertise and range of services
  • maintaining up-to-date knowledge
  • nurturing our long-term relationships
  • developing strategic partnerships
  • focusing on quality and improving our processes

AGE provides specialised groundwater management consultancy for national and international projects and we plan to open more offices throughout Australia as part of our strategic expansion plan. We have built a very successful company with a strong reputation for high quality work, and a leadership team who understand that our people are our strongest asset.
